Importing information from the POS System
Use the Import POS feature to transfer information from your Point of Sales system into MenuLink Client. Depending on how your MenuLink Client system is set up, you may be able to import specific portions of POS information into your system.
Note: Since the exact process to import information is customizable and dependent upon the type of POS system(s) in your restaurant and the specific information you import, the following instructions are for general information only. Contact your system administrator for instructions for your specific system.
To Import Information from your POS System:
1.
From the Sales Switchboard, click Import POS.
2.
In Import/Export Selection, choose the import you want to do.
Example screen. Your MenuLink Client system may display different options.
3.
If importing by date, MenuLink Client prompts you to select a date.
 
 
4.
When you click OK the following screen appears
 
5.
When you see ‘Processing Complete – No errors Detected’ click Ok and you are redirected to the Sales Switchboard.
