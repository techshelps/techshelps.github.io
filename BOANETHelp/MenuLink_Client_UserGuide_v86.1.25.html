Recalculate the Usage Per Factor
The Recalculate Usage per Factor is where you can recalculate your usage after performing a physical count. This can affect the recommended order quantity that MenuLink Client calculates when creating purchase orders.
Once you have entered or changed your inventory, click Recalc Usage Factor. The following dialog box appears where you can choose the time frame to use to recalculate the usage for ordering.
