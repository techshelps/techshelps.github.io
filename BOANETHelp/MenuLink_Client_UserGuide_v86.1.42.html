Items Where Used
Items Where Used shows you a list of all the prep item and menu item recipes that a selected item is used in and the portion of that item that each recipe uses.
To view where an item is used:
1.
On the Recipes Switchboard, click Where Used.
The Items Where Used screen appears.
 
 
2.
Select an item and then click Lookup to display the Where Used screen.
In this screen, a list of menu item and prep item recipes that the item is assigned to is displayed along with the portion of that raw item that is used in the recipe.
 
3.
Click Print to print the list of items.
