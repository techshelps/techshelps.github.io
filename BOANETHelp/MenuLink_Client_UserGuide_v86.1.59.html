Employees
The Employees screen contains several tools you can use to enter and manage employee information, and borrow or transfer employees.
 
Features in the Employee screen include:
•
Employee Maintenance — Enter and manage employee information, including name, emergency contact information, job codes, POS information, and other important, required data.
•
Employee Borrowing — Import employee information from another site to add the employee to your site.
•
Payroll Export — The Payroll Export button is reserved for future use.
•
Employee Transfers — Transfer employee information from another site to add the employee to your site.
•
Worksheets — The Worksheets button is reserved for future use.
•
Reports — View and print Employee-related reports.
