Align controls

        

  1. Select the controls to align.

  2. Select the dominant control.

  3. From the Format menu, choose Align.

  4. From the cascading menu, choose one of the following to align the specified part of each selected control with the same part of the dominant control:
    • Lefts葉o align the left edge.

    • Centers葉o align the center of each control. This means a vertical line drawn at the center of the dominant control would contain the center of every selected control.

    • Rights葉o align the right edge.

    • Tops葉o align the top.

    • Middles葉o align the center of each control. This means a horizontal line drawn at the center of the dominant control would also contain the center of every selected control.

    • Bottoms葉o align the bottom.

    • To Grid葉o align the upper left corner of each selected control with its nearest grid point. Note that this option is not based on the position of the dominant control.

Note   Each command on the menu has a small picture that shows how the controls will be aligned.
