Target Audience
 

This guide is intended for system architects, application architects, and administrators who plan to install, configure, and maintain the applications developed with the UII framework. To fully understand this guide, you should have experience with designing and implementing business solutions in a customer service data center environment.

This guide provides step-by-step procedures for installing, configuring, and administering the UII environment for development. In addition, it provides guidelines for deployment scenarios and common security considerations.
