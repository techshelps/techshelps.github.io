Modifying an Existing Setting
 

UII Administrator can use the UII general settings management features to change the setting name, value, and owner.

To modify the existing settings

  1. Using CRM administrator credentials, log on to Microsoft Dynamics CRM.
  2. In the left navigation pane, click Settings > UII Settings > UII Options.
  3. The Active General Settings list page appears, as shown in the following illustration. Double-click the setting you want to update.


  4. The General Settings Information page appears. Modify the information to meet your requirements, and then click Save or Save and Close.
