Online Training Resources QR Codes

Links to online resources are available via QR Codes placed throughout the help file.

Online resources include videos or PDF guides that can be viewed on your mobile device.

Each of the QR codes found in the help file are also conveniently located in the table below.

  • To view online resources, scan the QR codes with an app on the mobile device.
  • An Internet connection is required to view online content.
  • If the code will not scan, try a different scanner app.
