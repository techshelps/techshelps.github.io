Add or Remove a Place

Use My Places to add a list of shortcut locations to import or share access to files at any time across computers or devices.

Add a Place

  1. Select one of the following in Camtasia Studio:
    • File > Import from My Places.
    • Produce and share > Production options dropdown > Share to My Places > Click Next > Click Browse.
  2. Click the Add button.
  3. Click OneDrive for Business.
  4. Enter an email and password.
  5. Click Sign in.

The shortcut location is added to My Places.

Remove a Place

  1. Select one of the following in Camtasia Studio:
    • File > Import from My Places.
    • Produce and share > Production options dropdown > Share to My Places > Click Next > Click Browse.
  2. Click to select the Place to remove from My Places.
  3. Click the Remove button.
  4. Click Yes.

The shortcut location is deleted from My Places.
