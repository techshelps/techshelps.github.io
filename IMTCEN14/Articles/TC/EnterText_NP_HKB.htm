Note:

  • In desktop UI, if there are more than 9 candidates in the candidate window, you can press the Expand/Collapse button on the candidate window to expand or collapse the candidate window. You can also exand or collpase the candidate window by pressing the Right Arrow or Left Arrow key.

  • In Metro style UI, when you move the cursor pointer to the candidate window, arrow icons might be displayed at the beginning and/or end of the candidate window, which indicates more candidates are available. You can click the arrow icons to view other candidates.

  • If you are using a touch screen, you can also flick through the candidate window to view more candidates. You can tap the desired candidate to select and enter it.
