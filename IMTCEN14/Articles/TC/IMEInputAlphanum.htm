When you are not using Microsoft New Phonetic with the touch keyboard, you can Enter English in one of the following ways:

  • Press SHIFT to switch to English input state.
  • On the taskbar, click the icon to change it to .
  • Press Windows logo key+Spacebar to switch to US keyboard.
