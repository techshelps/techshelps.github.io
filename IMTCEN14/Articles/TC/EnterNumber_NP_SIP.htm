For some commonly used punctuations, you can enter them using the touch keyboard in Chinese/English input view. To enter numbers or some other symbols, switch to number/symbol input view.

  1. Tap the key to switch to number/symbol input view.

  2. Tap the key of the desired number or symbol to enter it.

Note

  • If the desired symbol is not in the current view, you can tap or to display other available symbols.
  • Pressing and holding a symbol or number key will display corresponding child keys. Move your finger to the desired child key to enter the corresponding symbol or number.
