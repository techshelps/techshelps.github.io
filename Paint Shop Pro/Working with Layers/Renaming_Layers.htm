Renaming Layers

As you add layers to an image, you may find it convenient to rename the layers so they are easy to identify on the Layers palette.

To rename a layer:

  1. On the Layers palette, right-click the name of the layer and choose Rename from the context menu.

  2. Type the name and press Enter.
