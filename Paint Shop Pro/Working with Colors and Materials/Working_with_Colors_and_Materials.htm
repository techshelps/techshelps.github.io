Working with Colors and Materials

Whether you are applying color to an image or adjusting photographic colors, it’s important to understand how Paint Shop Pro works with color. This chapter describes how to choose colors and materials that you can use to paint, draw, or fill. It also discusses the basics of color—how it is displayed on screen and printed—as well as image color depth.
