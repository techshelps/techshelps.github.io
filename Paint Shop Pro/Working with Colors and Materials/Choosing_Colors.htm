Choosing Colors

When you edit images in Paint Shop Pro, you often need to choose a color before you paint, draw, or fill, as well as when making manual color corrections, or when choosing a background for a new image. You choose colors as follows:

  • For images with 16 million colors, choose from the Jasc Color Picker.

  • With images with 2 to 256 colors, choose from the image palette.

  • For all images, you can choose a color from an open image or from any displayed color.

Choose a color from the Frame tab or the Rainbow tab of the Materials palette. Left-click to choose the foreground color or right-click to choose the background color.
