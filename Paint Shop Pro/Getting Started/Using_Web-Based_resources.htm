Using Web-Based Resources

On the Help menu of Paint Shop Pro, you can access a number of Web site pages dedicated to support issues. From the Help menu select one of the following Web site links:

  • Contact Information Takes you to a Customer Support page listing customer service and technical support contact information, as well as mailing addresses for US English and the other supported languages.

  • Online Support and Resources Access the Jasc Web site's Support pages where you can search for our Knowledge Base, access tutorials in the Learning Center, contact Jasc Customer Care, and look for software updates.

  • Register Product Online Register your copy of Paint Shop Pro online.

  • Check for Updates Check to see if there are updates to your copy of Paint Shop Pro.

Getting in Touch with Other Paint Shop Pro Users

Millions of people use and love Paint Shop Pro. There are many independent (non-Jasc) Paint Shop Pro communities where users share tips and tricks, ask questions, and get answers about Paint Shop Pro. There are Web sites, newsgroups, and chatrooms devoted to Paint Shop Pro. As a starting point, search online for “Paint Shop Pro.”

Popular newsgroups include forums.jasc.com as well as the Usenet newsgroup, comp.graphics.apps.paint-shop-pro. Access these newsgroups using your favorite news reader such as Microsoft Outlook Express, or an on-line news reader such as Google Groups.
