Choosing Effects

Choose Paint Shop Pro’s effects in one of two ways: through a menu command or through the Effect Browser, For more information, see Using the Effect Browser.

To choose an effect through a menu:

  1. From the main menu, choose Effects, choose one of the sub-menus listed below, and then choose an effect. For example, choose Effects > Distortion Effects > Twirl.
