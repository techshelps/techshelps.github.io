Applying Image Effects

The Image effects are Offset, Page Curl, and Seamless Tiling. To limit an effect to a specific area, make a selection before applying the effect.

Important: These effects work on greyscale and 16 million color (24-bit) images only. To increase the color depth of an image, see Increasing the Color Depth of an Image.

To apply an effect, choose it from the Effects Browser, or choose Effects > Image Effects and select one of the following effects.

Offset

Page Curl

Seamless Tiling
