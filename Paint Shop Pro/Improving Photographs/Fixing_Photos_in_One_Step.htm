Fixing Photos in One Step

Use the One Step Photo Fix command to automatically adjust the color balance, contrast, clarity, saturation, smooth edges, and sharpen an image.

To fix a photo in one step:

On the Photo toolbar, choose One Step Photo Fix to automatically fix a photo.

Note: To view the Photo toolbar, choose View > Toolbars > Photo.
