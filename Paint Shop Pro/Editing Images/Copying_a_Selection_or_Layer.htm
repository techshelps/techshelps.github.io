Copying a Selection or Layer

You can make a selection in an image, and then copy that selection into a new layer.

To copy a selection or a layer:

  1. Select the area or layer you want to copy, see Making Selections in Images.

  2. Do either of the following:

  3. To copy the selection or active layer, choose Edit > Copy or press Ctrl + C.

  4. To copy all layers of the selected area, choose Edit > Copy Merged or press Ctrl + Shift + C.
