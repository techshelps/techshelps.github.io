Cropping with a Selection

In Paint Shop Pro, you can make a selection and then crop the image using that selection.

Note: If you crop to a selection that is feathered, the current background color fills in the feathered region of the selection.

To crop using a selection:

  1. Make a selection in the image, see Making Selections in Images.

Note: The selection can be any shape. Paint Shop Pro places a crop area rectangle around irregularly shaped selections.

  1. Do either of the following:

  2. Choose Image > Crop to Selection; or

  3. Choose the Crop tool, and then click Current selection in the Snap crop rectangle to group box on the Tool Options palette. Adjust the crop area and click Apply .
