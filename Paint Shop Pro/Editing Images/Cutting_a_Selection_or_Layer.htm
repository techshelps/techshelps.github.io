Cutting a Selection or Layer

You can make a selection in an image and then cut to that selection.

To cut a selection or layer:

  1. Select the area or layer you want to cut. For information about making selections, see Making Selections in Images.

  2. Choose Edit > Cut or press Ctrl + X.
