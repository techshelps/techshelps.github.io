Rotating an Image 90 Degrees

Correct the orientation of an image by rotating it 90 degrees clockwise or counter clockwise.

To rotate all layers 90 degrees:

Choose Image > Rotate and pick Rotate 90 Clockwise or Rotate 90 Counter clockwise.
