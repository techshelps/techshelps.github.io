Customizing Toolbars and Menus

Paint Shop Pro gives you almost total control over the program’s toolbars, allowing you to:

  • Move commands from toolbar to toolbar.

  • Add almost any command to any toolbar.

  • Add your own custom scripts to toolbars or menus.

  • Create your own custom toolbar.
