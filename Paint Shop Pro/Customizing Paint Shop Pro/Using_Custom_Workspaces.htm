Using Custom Workspaces

The Paint Shop Pro workspace is made up of all the program’s pal­ettes, toolbars, and open images. You may find that the way you arrange the workspace while editing photos may differ from the arrangement while using the Web tools. Fortunately, Paint Shop Pro allows you to save any number of workspace arrangements, allowing you to load the specific workspace that best suits the work you intend to do.

Paint Shop Pro also contains sample workspaces, such as one that duplicates the Paint Shop Pro 7 workspace.
