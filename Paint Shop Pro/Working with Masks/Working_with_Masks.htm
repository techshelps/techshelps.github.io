Working with Masks

A mask is a greyscale image that is applied to a layer. Use mask layers to hide and show parts of a layer, fade between layers, and create other special effects. A mask can be created from a selection, from an alpha channel, or an existing image. Masks can completely cover a layer, or cover a layer with varying levels of opacity.
