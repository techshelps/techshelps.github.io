Editing Nodes

A node is a point on a contour that define the contour’s shape at that point. The Nodes have zero, one, or two control arms extending from them. The length and direction of the control arms determines the shape of the contour at the node. Click on the control handles to adjust control arms. The straight or curved line between two nodes is a line segment. For information about the types of nodes, see Node Types.
