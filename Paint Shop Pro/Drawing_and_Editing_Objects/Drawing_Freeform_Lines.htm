Drawing Freeform Lines

Use the Freehand tool to draw free-form lines.

To draw using free-form lines:

  1. From the Pen tool’s Tool Options palette, choose Freehand .

  2. Click and drag the cursor along the path you want the line to take. Release the mouse. Paint Shop Pro creates a contour that matches the path. To adjust how precisely the line follows your cursor, see Drawing Objects.
