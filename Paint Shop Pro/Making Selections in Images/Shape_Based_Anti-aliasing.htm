Shape-Based Anti-aliasing

Use the Shape-Based Anti-alias command to anti-alias a selection based on its shape.

To anti-alias based on shape:

  1. Choose Selections > Modify > Shape-Based Anti-alias to open the Shape-based Anti-alias dialog.

  2. For the Anti-alias mark an option:

  3. Inside to anti-alias the inside of the selection marquee.

  4. Outside to anti-alias outside of the selection marquee.

  1. Click OK to anti-alias the selection edges.
