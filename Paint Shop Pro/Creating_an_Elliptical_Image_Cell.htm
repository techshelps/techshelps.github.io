Creating an Elliptical Image Cell

To change a cell from rectangular to elliptical:

  1. In Print Layout, load a template containing at least one cell.

  2. Click the cell you want to make elliptical, and then click the Make cell elliptical button on the Print Layout toolbar, or choose Edit > Set selected cells elliptical. The cell's image will then appear through the ellipse.

Note: To make the cell rectangular again, click the Make cell rectangular button , or choose Edit > Set selected cells rectangular.
