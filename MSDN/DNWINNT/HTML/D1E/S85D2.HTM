Microsoft Consulting Services

Microsoft Consulting Services (MCS) consultants are system architects with experience and expertise in Microsoft technology, methodologies, and tools, chartered to help organizations capitalize on the benefits of the most powerful platform for client-server computing—the Microsoft Windows NT Server network operating system. MCS consultants focus on transferring knowledge and skills to corporations, government organizations, and third-party Microsoft Solution Providers worldwide. MCS, in conjunction with third-party solution providers, offers organizations a number of services customized to their unique information technology environment, including planning, design, development, integration, and implementation.
