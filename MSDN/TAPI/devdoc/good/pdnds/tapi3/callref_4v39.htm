Media Type

[This is preliminary documentation and subject to change.]

The following MediaTypes are defined:

TAPIMEDIATYPE_AudioIn
An audio media stream that is entering the computer. This media stream would typically be played on speakers, or sent to a handset device.
TAPIMEDIATYPE_AudioOut
An audio media stream that is leaving the computer. This media stream would typically be captured through a microphone or handset device.
TAPIMEDIATYPE_VideoIn
A video media stream that is entering the computer. This media stream would typically be rendered in a video window.
TAPIMEDIATYPE_VideoOut
A video media stream that is leaving the computer. This media stream would typically be captured with a video camera.
TAPIMEDIATYPE_DataModem
A data media stream that is associated with a data modem.
TAPIMEDIATYPE_G3Fax
A data media stream that is associated with G3Fax.
