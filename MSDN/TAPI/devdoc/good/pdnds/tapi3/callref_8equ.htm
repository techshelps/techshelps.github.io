ITCallTonesNotification::DTMF

[This is preliminary documentation and subject to change.]

This method is called when a DTMF is detected on the call. A DTMF character is '0'-'9', 'A', 'B', 'C', 'D', '#', and '*'.

HRESULT DTMF(
  ITCallInfo *pCall,
  unsigned char DTMF
);

Parameters

pCall
[in] Call.
DTMF
[in] DTMF character.
