Delivery

When delivering your control, use CAB files and sign your controls. Everyone should be well aware of the recent security issues that have received great attention. Advice to end users is not to download executable content unless it is signed, so if you do not sign your control you cannot expect users to download your product. Using CAB files also creates a better end-user experience because multiple files can be downloaded, with a single certificate presented to a user in advance of download. Details of this technology are in the Internet Client SDK.
