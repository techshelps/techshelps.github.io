Compiling Your Help Files

AppWizard provides you with a robust help package: .RTF files that already document the standard (AppWizard-generated) menus, a help project (.HPJ) file, a Help contents topic (.CNT) file, and the other resources needed to generate an application with a fully functional online Help system.

Suggested Reading

  • Help: Authoring Help Topics, Visual C++ Programmer’s Guide

  • Setting Directories, Visual C++ User’s Guide

You will customize the AppWizard-generated files to suit your particular application as you develop the application. When you compile and build your developing application, you must also recompile your project Help files so they continue to reflect your customizations.

See Help: Compiling and Testing Your Help File.
