Completing the DaoEnrol Tutorial

The procedures for creating the Enroll and DaoEnrol applications are identical except for a few differences in the implementation details of DAO. Because of this, you will be referred to Enroll Lesson 3 and Enroll Lesson 4 for most of the tasks, and DaoEnrol Step 2 and DaoEnrol Step 3 will only cover the DAO implementation differences.

  • DaoEnrol Step 2 and DaoEnrol Step 3 will guide you through the procedures you need to complete.

  • DaoEnrol Step 4 consists of a review of the files in the DAOENROL sample.
