OL97 ErrMsg: General Error When Sending a Fax

 Last reviewed: January 19, 1998
Article ID: Q168704
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Outlook 97

SYMPTOMS

When you try to send a fax using the Compose Fax Wizard in Microsoft Outlook 97, you may receive the following error message: 

   General Error!

WORKAROUND

You may be able to work around this problem by leaving the Subject field blank when you send a fax.

For additional information, please see the following article in the Microsoft Knowledge Base: 

   Article ID: Q140432
   Title     : Problems Sending Fax from MAPI-Aware Programs
