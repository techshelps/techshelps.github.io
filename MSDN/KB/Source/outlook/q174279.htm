OL97: Error Trying to Use Word as Your E-mail Editor

 Last reviewed: January 19, 1998
Article ID: Q174279
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Outlook 97

SYMPTOMS

When attempting to use Microsoft Word 97 as your e-mail editor in Microsoft Outlook 97, you may get the following error message: 

   This form requires Word as your e-mail editor,
   but Word is either busy or cannot be found.
Following the message above, Outlook uses the default editor for the message.

CAUSE

This problem has been reported when using the Colorgraphic Pro Lightning Accelerator card.

RESOLUTION

Delete or rename the "Mover" software that comes with the Colorgraphic Pro Lightning Accelerator card.

MORE INFORMATION

The Colorgraphic Pro Lightning Accelerator card supports switching between two different video monitors.

The Colorgraphic Pro Lightning Accelerator is manufactured by Xi Graphics, a vendor independent of Microsoft; we make no warranty, implied or otherwise, regarding this product's performance or reliability.
