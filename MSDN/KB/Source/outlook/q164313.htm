OL97: Cannot Print Secondary Tab of Outlook Forms

 Last reviewed: January 19, 1998
Article ID: Q164313

The information in this article applies to:

  • Microsoft Outlook 97

SYMPTOMS

Only the first or primary page of a form prints in Outlook 97. If you try to print another page of a form such as the Meeting Planner tab of a Meeting Request form, the primary page, Appointment tab, prints.

STATUS

Microsoft has confirmed this to be a problem in Microsoft Outlook 97. We are researching this problem and will post new information here in the Microsoft Knowledge Base as it becomes available.

MORE INFORMATION

Here is a list of affected forms and their tabs: 

Message Form:     Message (Primary tab)
                  Options

Appointment:      Appointment (Primary tab)
                  Meeting Planner
Meeting Request: Appointment (Primary tab) 
                  Meeting Planner

Task:             Task (Primary tab)
                  Status

Task Request:     Task (Primary tab)
                  Status

Contact Form:     General (Primary tab)
                  Details
                  Journal
                  All Fields
