OL97: Err Msg: Outlook Caused A GP Fault In Module RASGDI.EXE.

 Last reviewed: January 19, 1998
Article ID: Q174335
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Outlook 97

SYMPTOMS

When printing Contacts in Address Card view in Microsoft Outlook 97 using a Okidata OkiPage 4w printer, you may receive the following error message: 

   OUTLOOK caused a general protection fault in module RASGDI.EXE at
   000d:0000135b

    -or-

   OUTLOOK caused a general protection fault in module VHSDGDI.DRV at
   000d:0000135b

CAUSE

This behavior may occur with Version 3.10 03/25/97 of the Okidata Okipage 4W printer driver.

RESOLUTION

Upgrade to version 4.0 of the Okidata Okipage printer driver. You can download this driver (4we3195.exe) from the Okidata Web site at: 

   http://www.okidata.com/drivers/win95txt.html
NOTE: Remove the current driver prior to installing the updated version.

MORE INFORMATION
