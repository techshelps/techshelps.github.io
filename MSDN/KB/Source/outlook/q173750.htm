OL97: Invalid Page Fault Changing Recurring Meeting Start Time

 Last reviewed: January 19, 1998
Article ID: Q173750
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Outlook 97

SYMPTOMS

In the Day/Week/Month view of the Calendar, when you change the start time of a recurring meeting or appointment lasting less then 30 minutes by dragging the top of the Calendar item down, an illegal operation occurs. When you click Details, the following appears: 

  OUTLOOK caused an invalid page fault in module OUTLLIB.DLL.

STATUS

Microsoft has confirmed this to be a problem in Microsoft Outlook 97, versions 8.00, 8.01, and 8.02. This problem is fixed in Outlook 97, version 8.03, which is available only with Microsoft Exchange Server, version 5.5.

MORE INFORMATION

Steps to Reproduce the Problem

  1. Click the Calendar icon in the Outlook Bar.

  2. On the Calendar menu, click New Recurring Appointment.

  3. Change the duration to 15 minutes, then click OK.

  4. Click Save and Close.

  5. In the Day list, drag the top of the Recurring Appointment down to a later time. The illegal operation occurs.
