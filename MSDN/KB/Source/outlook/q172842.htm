OL97: Outlook Third Party Add-on Information

 Last reviewed: February 16, 1998
Article ID: Q172842
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Outlook 97

SUMMARY

Microsoft Outlook 97 is quickly becoming the preferred information manager for computer telephony, universal Inbox, and sales force automation solutions. With Outlook add-on applications developed by third-party vendors, you can enhance Outlook functionality to deliver these features and many others.

Outlook third-party add-ons enhance these Outlook features:

  • Telephony/Universal Inbox: faxing, paging, universal inbox, and call control.
  • Connectivity: synchronizing or sharing information between systems or devices.
  • Sales force automation: contact management, customer care, and help desk.
  • Miscellaneous: virus protection, workflow, forms, and reporting.
