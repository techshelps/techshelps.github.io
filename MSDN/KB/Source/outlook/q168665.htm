OL97: All Day Events Not Exported to Timex Data Link Watch

 Last reviewed: January 19, 1998
Article ID: Q168665
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Outlook 97

SUMMARY

In Microsoft Outlook 97, when exporting information to the Timex Data Link Watch, events that have been marked as an All Day Event are not exported. The only events that will be exported are completed Birthday and Anniversary fields on the Details tab of a Contact.

MORE INFORMATION

Only events that are related to a specific Contact are exported; these events are recorded on the Details tab of a Contact. Currently, the only events that can be exported are Birthday and Anniversary.

To export these events to the Timex Data Link Watch, use the following steps:

  1. On the File Menu, click Import/Export.

  2. Click "Export to Data Link Watch" and click Next.

  3. Click to select Anniversaries, to export Birthdays and Anniversaries.

  4. Select the number of Days of appointments (if applicable) to export and click Next.

  5. Select the number of Tasks (if applicable) to export and click Next

  6. Select the Contact phone numbers (if applicable) to export and click Next.

  7. Select the Anniversaries, Birthdays, and other Annual events you want to export and click Next.

    NOTE: Only contacts from your Contacts folder with the Anniversary or Birthday field completed appear here.

  8. Set the Alarms (if applicable) to export and click Next.

  9. Choose the Time Zone (if applicable) to export and click Next.

  10. Set the Data Link Watch to Comm Ready mode and click OK.

The Anniversaries and Birthdays for each Contact will be exported to the Data Link Watch.
