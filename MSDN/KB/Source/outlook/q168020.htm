OL97: Information About Synchronizing Outlook with PalmPilot

 Last reviewed: January 19, 1998
Article ID: Q168020
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Outlook 97

SUMMARY

This article describes how you can obtain information about synchronizing your U.S. Robotics PalmPilot organizer files with Microsoft Outlook 97 items.

MORE INFORMATION

Microsoft does not manufacture programs, to synchronize PalmPilot files with Outlook files. Several other companies do provide software for this purpose. The following list includes some of the companies that provide synchronization software for the PalmPilot organizer:

Company: DataViz, Inc. Product: Desktop-To-Go

Company: Chapura, Inc. Product: Pilot Mirror

Company: Puma Technology, Inc. Product: IntelliSync for Pilot/PalmPilot
