PROFS: Why the Gateway PC Needs the Correct Date/Time

 Last reviewed: October 20, 1997
Article ID: Q87203
3.00 MS-DOS kb3rdparty

The information in this article applies to:

  • Microsoft Mail Gateway to IBM PROFS and OfficeVision, version 3.0

The LAN administrator of the Microsoft Mail Gateway to IBM PROFS and OfficeVision must make sure that the gateway PC always has the correct date/time, as this information is used to "stamp" every mail item passing through the gateway.

For mail coming from the host to the LAN, this date/time stamp determines where the item is displayed in your mailbag. For example, if a host mail item was delivered to your mailbag, but it was date-stamped incorrectly, it could end up at the bottom of your mailbag. If you have many items in your mailbag, you may never see the new piece of host mail.

The same scenario is possible when sending a message from MS Mail to a host user. The host user may not be aware of the reciept of a new PROFS note or a new file in the Reader.
