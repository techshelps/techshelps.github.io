PC WRmt: Messages Waiting in Outbox Not Sent

 Last reviewed: October 31, 1994
Article ID: Q99202
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Mail Remote for Windows, version 3.2

SYMPTOMS

Version 3.2 of Microsoft Mail Remote for Windows may display messages waiting in the Outbox that never get sent.

CAUSE

These messages are most likely associated with a different mail driver than the one you currently are using.

RESOLUTION

Switch back to the driver you used when you created those messages and they will be sent the next time you connect.

Before switching mail drivers, send all your messages to make sure your Outbox is empty.

REFERENCES

This situation is documented in the Mail Remote for Windows "User's Guide" in Chapter 2, page 15, "Making the Mail Remote Driver Current."
