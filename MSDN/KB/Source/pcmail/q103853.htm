ATT: DocErr: AT&T Support Number Incorrect in Admin Guide

 Last reviewed: October 20, 1997
Article ID: Q103853
1.00 MS-DOS kb3rdparty kbdocerr

The information in this article applies to:

  • Microsoft Mail Gateway to AT&T Mail, version 1.0

The support number for AT&T is listed incorrectly on page 55 of the version 3.2 Microsoft Mail Gateway to AT&T Mail "Administrator's Guide."

The manual incorrectly states that the phone number for the AT&T Customer Support Center is: 

   (800) 625-5672
The correct number is: 

   (800) 624-5672
NOTE: The number is correctly listed on page 10 of the manual.
