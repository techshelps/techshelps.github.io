PC Win: Selected Order of Messages Open Out of Order

 Last reviewed: October 29, 1994
Article ID: Q81552
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Mail for Windows, versions 3.0, 3.0b, and 3.2

SYMPTOMS

Messages in the Message Center Viewer (MCV) sometimes cannot be opened in the order in which they are displayed.

Messages that are visible and highlighted in the MCV are opened in their appropriate order, but selected messages that you have scrolled past in the MCV are not. This same sort of behavior exists if you selectively highlight random messages (hold hold the CTRL key and click with the mouse button) and scroll past the initial MCV view.

STATUS

Microsoft has confirmed this to be a problem in versions 3.0, 3.0b, and 3.2 of Mail for Windows. We are researching this problem and will post new information here in the Microsoft Knowledge Base as it becomes available.
