PC Win: How to Recover Missing Columns in Message Center Viewer

 Last reviewed: October 31, 1994
Article ID: Q101935
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Mail for Windows, versions 3.0, 3.0b, and 3.2

SYMPTOMS

The Message Center Viewer (MCV) may become corrupted and the user can lose his Subject and/or Date columns. Rebuilding the Mail message file (MMF) may not restore the columns properly.

RESOLUTION

  1. Close the MCV that does not have the columns.

  2. Select New Window.

  3. Exit Mail (this step is important because this saves the configuration).

  4. Restart Mail.
