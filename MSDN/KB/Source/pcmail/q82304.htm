PC OS2: Incorrect Minimize/Maximize Buttons

 Last reviewed: October 12, 1994
Article ID: Q82304
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Mail for PC Networks, OS/2 workstation, version 3.0

SYMPTOMS

In version 3.0 of Microsoft Mail for OS/2, when you maximize the Message Center Viewer (MCV), the minimize and maximize buttons will be positioned right beside the Help menu rather than to the right edge of the Mail window as it is under Microsoft Windows.

STATUS

This is a design limitation of version 1.0 of the Windows Libraries for OS/2 (WLO) dynamic-link libraries (DLLs).
