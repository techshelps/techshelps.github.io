Intel SatisFAXtion 400i Driver Support for Windows NT 3.51

 Last reviewed: May 1, 1996
Article ID: Q142908
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Windows NT Workstation version 3.51
  • Microsoft Windows NT Server version 3.51

The drivers (Intel SatisFAXtion 100 and Intel SatisFAXtion 400e) provided in Windows NT 3.51 does not support the Intel SatisFAXtion 400i internal modem. The Intel SatisFAXtion 400i is not in the Windows NT 3.51 Hardware Compatibility List (HCL).

To obtain the drivers for the Intel SatisFAXtion 400i modem, contact Pure Data Technical Support at (905) 731-4533 or download NT351.EXE (self- extracting file) from one of the following electronic services:

  • CompuServe 

          GO PCVENF
      Search for NT351.EXE
      Display results and download
  • Pure Data Bulletin Board Service (BBS) 

          Dial (905) 731-4679 to connect to the BBS
      Download NT351.EXE
