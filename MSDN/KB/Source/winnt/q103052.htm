Dysfunctional Browsing on Wide Area Network (WAN)

 Last reviewed: May 8, 1997
Article ID: Q103052

The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Windows NT Advanced Server version 3.1

Wide are network (WAN) browsing may fail when there have been two domain controllers present for some amount of time. This usually occurs when promoting a server to a domain controller; there is usually a short amount of time when there are two domain controllers in the domain.
