Monitor Does Not Sync with Orchid Fahrenheit Video Adapter

 Last reviewed: May 8, 1997
Article ID: Q102174

The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Windows NT operating system, version 3.1
  • Microsoft Windows NT Advanced Server, version 3.1

SYMPTOMS

Your monitor does not synchronize with your Orchid Fahrenheit video adapter.

CAUSE

The default switch settings on the video adapter are not configured properly for the video driver's scan rate.

RESOLUTION

Change the switch settings to match those used by the video driver.
