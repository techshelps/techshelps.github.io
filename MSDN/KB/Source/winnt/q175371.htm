Hidden Shared Folders Do Not Show in SBS Console

 Last reviewed: March 5, 1998
Article ID: Q175371
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft BackOffice Small Business Server version 4.0

SYMPTOMS

Shared folders that are created (those with a dollar sign ($) at the end of the name) do not show up in the Console page Manage Shared Folders.

CAUSE

This behavior is because hidden shares remain hidden throughout the system.

RESOLUTION

To be able to see the share, remove the dollar sign ($) after the name to prevent it from being hidden.
