First Publisher Requires Mouse Driver Version 6.24

 Last reviewed: September 16, 1996
Article ID: Q31549

SUMMARY

Mouse drivers earlier than Version 6.24 may not work correctly with First Publisher Version 1.00. Gray dots may appear where the mouse cursor should be. First Publisher recommends a mouse driver update to version 6.24 or later.

This information applies to the Microsoft Mouse driver versions 6.24 and earlier for MS-DOS systems.
