Microsoft Mouse Hardware Specifications

 Last reviewed: September 16, 1996
Article ID: Q83344
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Mouse, version 1.0

The following is specific hardware information about the Microsoft Mouse: weight, size, temperature range, and humidity range. This information applies to the 400 ppi "Dove Bar" style mouse, which is available in both bus and serial/PS2 versions. 

Weight:                 Approximately 5.5 oz (170 grams +-20)

Size:                   Approximately 10 cm long, 5.25 cm wide, and
                        3 cm high

Temperature range:      Operating environment specification is 0 to +40
                        degrees Celsius

                        Storage environment specification is; -30 to +60
                        degrees Celsius

Humidity:               Operating range is 8 to 80 percent relative
                        humidity

                        Storage range is 5 to 95 percent relative
                        humidity
