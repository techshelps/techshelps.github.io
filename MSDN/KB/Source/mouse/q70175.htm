PS/2 Mouse Port Does Not Function on Austin Computer

 Last reviewed: September 16, 1996
Article ID: Q70175
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft BallPoint Mouse, version 1.0

The Microsoft BallPoint Mouse does not work on the mouse port of Austin computers. If the BallPoint Mouse is connected to the serial port, the mouse works correctly.

The Austin products included here are manufactured by a vendor independent of Microsoft; we make no warranty, implied or otherwise, regarding these products' performance or reliability.
