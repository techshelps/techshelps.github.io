How to Specify Full Headers in a Reply To Group Message

 Last reviewed: September 3, 1997
Article ID: Q154402
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Internet Mail and News version 1.0 for Windows 95

SUMMARY

When you click Reply To Group in Internet News, the default message addressing options are:

  • Newsgroups
  • Cc
  • Subject

This article describes how to see additional message addressing options.

MORE INFORMATION

To see additional message addressing options, follow these steps:

  1. Click Reply To Group.

  2. On the View menu, click Full Headers. The following message addressing options are shown: 

        - Newsgroups
    - Followup-To
    - Cc
    - Reply-To
    - Distribution
    - Keywords
    - Subject

These message addressing options remain available until you click Full Headers on the View menu again.
