Internet News May Not Download All Messages

 Last reviewed: September 10, 1997
Article ID: Q161640
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Internet Mail and News version 1.0 for Windows 95

SYMPTOMS

When you connect to some InterNetNews (INN) servers, Internet News may be unable to download some of the messages.

CAUSE

Internet News version 1.0 does not issue the "mode reader" command that is required by some INN servers to retrieve messages.

RESOLUTION

Support for the "mode reader" command is included in the updated version 1.0 of Internet Mail and News.

For information about obtaining the updated version 1.0 of Internet Mail and News for Windows 95 and Windows NT 4.0, please see the following article in the Microsoft Knowledge Base: 

   ARTICLE-ID: Q154457
   TITLE     : Availability of Microsoft Internet Mail and News
