Availability of Microsoft Internet Mail and News for Macintosh

 Last reviewed: September 10, 1997
Article ID: Q159681
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Internet Mail and News version 1.0 for Macintosh

SUMMARY

Microsoft has made Microsoft Internet Mail and News for Macintosh available.

MORE INFORMATION

Microsoft Internet Mail is an Simple Mail Transfer Protocol (SMTP)/Post Office Protocol 3 (POP3) mail client that enables you to work offline, sort and store mail any way you want, and view messages quickly in the preview pane. Internet News enables you to connect to multiple mail servers and automatically decodes binary files, and supports the same preview pane and filtering offered by Internet Mail.

You can obtain Internet Mail and News for Macintosh from the following Microsoft Web site: 

   http://www.microsoft.com/ie/
