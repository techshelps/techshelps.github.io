WD97: Items Missing from Recently Used File List

 Last reviewed: August 25, 1997
Article ID: Q172961
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Word 97 for Windows

SYMPTOMS

The recently used file list (sometimes referred to as the most recently used list or "MRU") for Microsoft Word may not be accurate if you use WordMail as your e-mail editor. In some circumstances one or more of the files last used may not appear in the list.

The recently used file list is the list of files at the bottom of the File menu.

WORKAROUND

In Microsoft Outlook, follow these steps to turn off WordMail as your e- mail editor:

  1. On the Tools menu, click Options.

  2. Click the E-mail tab.

  3. Click to clear the "Use Microsoft Word as the e-mail editor" check box.

For additional information about how to turn WordMail on or off, please see the following article in the Microsoft Knowledge Base:

   TITLE     : WD97: How to Enable, Disable Microsoft Word as the E-Mail
               Editor

STATUS
