WOPR: Two-By-Four Macro for Booklets and Side-by-Side Printing

 Last reviewed: July 30, 1997
Article ID: Q97116
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Word for Windows, versions 2.0, 2.0a, 2.0a-CD, 2.0b, 2.0c

SUMMARY

Woody's Office POWER Pack version 2.0 (WOPR) is a third-party add-on package for Word for Windows. This package includes a macro, called Two-by-Four 2.0, that prints a booklet in the following layout formats:

  • Duplex (prints on both sides of the paper)
  • Side-by-Side (two pages, reduced in size, printed on the same page)
  • Side-by-Side Duplex (same as Side-by-Side, printed on both sides of the paper, for a total of four pages on each sheet of paper).
