Footnotes Not Available in Header/Footer in Word for Windows

 Last reviewed: July 30, 1997
Article ID: Q66041
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Word for Windows, versions 1.0, 1.1, 1.1a, 2.0, 2.0a, 2.0a-CD, 2.0b, 2.0c, 6.0, 6.0a, 6.0c

It is not possible to place a footnote reference mark within either the header or footer portion of a Microsoft Word for Windows document.

The header and footers are handled as separate entities from the body of the document and are not currently designed to allow the inclusion of footnote references.

REFERENCES

"Microsoft Word for Windows User's Reference" pages 126-130.
