WD: Can't Print Word Document Duplex from Office Binder

 Last reviewed: February 5, 1998
Article ID: Q145416
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Word for Windows 95, versions 7.0, 7.0a
  • Microsoft Word 97 for Windows

SYMPTOMS

You cannot use the duplex priting option when you print a Word document from Office Binder.

CAUSE

Office Binder re-sets the duplex printing option to none.

STATUS

Microsoft has confirmed this to be a problem in Microsoft Office Binder versions 7.0 and 7.0a for Windows 95 and Microsoft Office Binder 97. We are researching this problem and will post new information here in the Microsoft Knowledge Base as it becomes available.

WORKAROUND

To work around this problem, use one of the following methods.

Method 1: Print the section using the Print command on the Section menu. 

          a. Open the Word document in Office Binder.

          b. On the Section menu, click Print.

          c. Click the Properties button.

          d. Click the Paper tab, click More Options, select the duplex
             printing option you want to use, and click OK.

          e. Click Apply.

          f. Click OK.
Method 2: Print the section using the Selected Sections option in the Print 
          dialog box.

          a. Open the Word document in Office binder.

          b. On the File menu, click Print.

          c. Under Print What, choose Selected Sections.

          d. Click the Properties button.

          e. Click the Paper tab, click More Options, select the duplex
             printing option you want to use, and click OK.

          f. Click Apply.

          g. Click OK.
Method 3: Change the properties for the printer. 

          a. Click the Windows Start button, point to Settings, and click
             Printers.

          b. Right-click the printer icon, and then click Properties.

          c. Click the Paper tab, click More Options, select the duplex
             printing option you want to use and click OK.

          d. Click Apply.

          e. Click OK.
When you return to Office Binder and print the document, Office Binder will use the duplex printing option.
