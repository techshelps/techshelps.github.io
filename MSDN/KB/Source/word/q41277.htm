WD: Printing from Outline View

 Last reviewed: February 2, 1998
Article ID: Q41277
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Word for the Macintosh, versions 3.0, 3.01, 3.02, 4.0, 5.0, 5.1, 6.0
  • Microsoft Word 98 Macintosh Edition

SUMMARY

In outline view, Word prints only those paragraphs that are displayed when you click Print.

MORE INFORMATION

If only the first line of body text is displayed in outline view, the entire paragraph will be printed, not just the first line that appears in outline view with Show Body Text on.

Word does not print body or headings text that is collapsed (not visible in Outlining).
