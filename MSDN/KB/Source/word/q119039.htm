The information in this article applies to:

SUMMARY

This article contains the directory of the 5.25-inch, high-density (1.2 MB) Disk #8 (disk 8 of 11) for version 6.0a of Word for Windows. The article includes the decompressed filename for each file, which you must provide when you use the DECOMP.EXE utility to manually decompress a file.

MORE INFORMATION

The date and time of each file is 01-26-94, 4:2x PM, unless otherwise indicated (where "2x" is 21, 22, 23, 24, or 25).
