Disk Contents for Version 4.0

 Last reviewed: July 30, 1997
Article ID: Q58582
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Word for the Macintosh, version 4.0

SUMMARY

Microsoft Word version 4.0 is shipped with the following three disks: 

   Program
   Utilities 1
   Utilities 2
The contents of these disks are listed below.
