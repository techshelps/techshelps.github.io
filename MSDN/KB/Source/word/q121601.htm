How to Obtain a Replacement Keyboard Template for Word 6.0

 Last reviewed: February 6, 1998
Article ID: Q121601
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Word for Windows, versions 6.0, 6.0a, 6.0c

To obtain a keyboard template (sometimes called a "tent card") for Word 6.0 for Windows, contact the Microsoft Sales Information Center at (800) 426-9400 or call the Microsoft Order Desk at (800) 360-7561 . The part number is 51167

The keyboard template contains Word Function Key descriptions, Keyboard Shortcuts, and WordPerfect/Word Key Equivalents.
