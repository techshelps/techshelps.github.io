Caption Buttons for Document Window Appear as Black Boxes

 Last reviewed: August 5, 1997
Article ID: Q135284
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Word for Windows 95, versions 7.0, 7.0a

SYMPTOMS

The caption buttons for minimize, maximize, and close appear as black boxes.

CAUSE

You modified your display properties and applied Bold formatting to the Menu item font. (To locate this option, open the Windows Control Panel, double-click the Display icon, and then click the Appearance tab.)

STATUS

Microsoft has confirmed this to be a problem in the Word versions listed previously. This problem was corrected in Microsoft Word 97 for Windows.

WORKAROUNDS ==========-

Method 1: Remove the bold formatting for the Menu item. To do this: 

          a. Click the Windows Start button, point to Settings, and click
             Control Panel.
          b. Double-click the Display icon.
          c. Click the Appearance tab.
          d. In the Item list, click Menu.
          e. Click the Bold button to turn off bold formatting.
          f. Click Apply.
          g. Click OK.
Method 2: Use a font that is already bold. To do this: 

          a. Click the Windows Start button, point to Settings, and click
             Control Panel.
          b. Double-click the Display icon.
          c. Click the Appearance tab.
          d. In the Item list, click Menu.
          e. In the Font list, click a font such as Arial Black
             or Arial Rounded MT Bold.
          f. Click Apply.
          g. Click OK.
