WD98: Word 5.1 Menu Commands Do Not Match Word 5.1 Order

 Last reviewed: February 17, 1998
Article ID: Q177354
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Word 98 Macintosh Edition

SYMPTOMS

When you use the Word 5.1 menus (On the View menu, click Word 5.1 menus), some menu commands do not follow the order of the original Word 5.1 menus.

CAUSE

This is by design to ease the transition to the Word 98 Macintosh Edition menu order. The Word 5.1 menu view order is the same as the Word 98 Macintosh Edition view.
