WD: MacWord Doesn't Support Suitcase Dynamic Font Menu Updating

 Last reviewed: November 17, 1997
Article ID: Q99027

The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Word for the Macintosh, versions 3.0, 3.01, 3.02, 4.0, 5.0, 5.1, 6.0

SUMMARY

Suitcase 2.0, a font utility for managing fonts and sounds on the Macintosh, allows you to easily change the fonts available to your applications. Some programs react to these changes instantly and change their menus as Suitcase changes the font situation. Word does not currently support this capability. To see Suitcase's font changes in Word, you must quit Word and restart the program.

This feature is under review and will be considered for inclusion in a future release.
