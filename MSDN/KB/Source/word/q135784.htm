WD6X: Fax Drivers Tested With Word 6.0.1 for the Macintosh

 Last reviewed: January 12, 1998
Article ID: Q135784
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Word for the Macintosh, version 6.0.1

SUMMARY

This article lists fax drivers and products that have been tested with Microsoft Word 6.0.1 for the Macintosh.

MORE INFORMATION

Global Village TelePort and Global Village PowerPort 

   When you click the File menu, Word may produce a "Type 3" error or it
   may stop responding (hang) if you are using Global Village products and
   the Office 4.2x Update for Power Macintosh, but this is easily
   corrected by obtaining the updated extension. For additional
   information, please see the following article in the Microsoft
   Knowledge Base:

      ARTICLE-ID: Q134909
      TITLE     : Known Conflicts with Version 1.0 of the Office Extension

MacComCenter and QuickLink II 

   Word acts as if it's spooling the information to the fax driver, but
   MacComCenter does not activate itself, and no fax is sent. Updating to
   6.0.1 resolves the problem. For additional information, please see the
   following article in the Microsoft Knowledge Base:

      ARTICLE-ID: Q129244
      TITLE     : Can't Fax with MacComCenter or QuickLink II from
                  Word 6.0

   Smith-Micro (QuickLink II and MacComCenter)
   51 Columbia
   Aliso Viejo, CA 92656
   (714) 362-5800
Prometheus MaxFax 

   When you send a fax from Word with Prometheus MaxFax, pages may fax
   blank or entirely black. Additionally, canceling from the fax's Address
   dialog box may cause Word to hang, crash, or "Unexpectedly Quit."

      ARTICLE-ID: Q129243
      TITLE     : Prometheus MaxFax Faxes Blank or Black Pages

   Prometheus
   9524 SW Tualatin-Sherwood

Delrina MacFax Pro 

   When you attempt to fax a document from Word version 6.0 for the
   Macintosh, the print job may spool, but nothing faxes. Updating to
   6.0.1 resolves the problem. For additional information, please see the
   following article in the Microsoft Knowledge Base:

      ARTICLE-ID: Q128179
      TITLE     : Word Doesn't Fax with Delrina's No Chooser Extension
