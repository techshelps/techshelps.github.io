WD97: Known Video Driver Conflicts in Word 97

 Last reviewed: March 18, 1998
Article ID: Q166703
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Word 97 for Windows

SUMMARY

This article contains information about known video driver issues with Word 97. It also includes contact information for video driver vendors.

Word 97 Hangs

When you use a Trident 9660/968X Linear Accelerated for PCI v. 1.0a video driver, Microsoft Word 97 will suddenly freeze when working in a document. The resolution to this is to change the video driver to VGA or to obtain an updated video driver from Trident.
