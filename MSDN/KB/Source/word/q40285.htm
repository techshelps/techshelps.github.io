Word: History of Updates and System Compatability

 Last reviewed: July 30, 1997
Article ID: Q40285
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Word for the Macintosh, versions 3.0, 3.01, 3.02, 4.0, 5.0, 5.1
