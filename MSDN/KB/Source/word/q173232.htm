WD95: Title/INFO Title Field Causes Invalid Page Fault

 Last reviewed: August 29, 1997
Article ID: Q173232
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Word for Windows 95, versions 7.0, 7.0a

SYMPTOMS

When you insert a TITLE or INFO Title field into the header or footer of a Microsoft Word document, you may receive the following error message: 

   This program has performed an illegal operation and will be shut
   down. If the problem persists, contact the program vendor.
When you click Details, the following error message is displayed: 

   WINWORD caused an invalid page fault in module WINWORD.EXE at
   0137:50019794.

   -or-

   WINWORD caused an invalid page fault in module WINWORD.EXE at
   0137:50019796.

WORKAROUND

To work around this problem, type a Title description into the document properties, and then insert the Title field into the header or footer.

To add a Title description to your document, follow these steps:

  1. In your Word document, click Properties on the File menu.

  2. Click to select the Summary tab.

  3. In the Title box, type your Title description, and then click OK.

To insert the Title field, follow these steps:

  1. In your Word document, click Header And Footer on the View menu.

  2. On the Header And Footer toolbar, click the "Switch Between Header and Footer" button to change to the header or the footer.

  3. On the Insert menu, click Field.

  4. Under Field Names, click to select Title, and then click OK.

    NOTE: Do not add text to the Title field. The Title field will use the Title text that you typed in the document Properties dialog box. You may also use the INFO Title field instead of the Title field.

STATUS

This problem was corrected in Microsoft Word 97 for Windows.
