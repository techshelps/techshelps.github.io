IA: Title Bar, Status Bar Flash Downloading Files, Images

 Last reviewed: February 5, 1998
Article ID: Q130214
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Internet Assistant for Word, versions 1.0, 1.0z
  • Microsoft Word for Windows, versions 6.0a, 6.0c

SYMPTOMS

The Word Title bar and Status bar flash while you are downloading images or files using Internet Assistant.

CAUSE

The Internet Assistant is changing the focus from Word to the Status bar and back to Word again. This behavior is by design.

STATUS

This was changed in Internet Assistand version 2.0.
