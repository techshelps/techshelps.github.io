WinWord 6.0: Contents of Disk #10 (5.25/1.2 MB)

 Last reviewed: July 30, 1997
Article ID: Q108860
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Word for Windows, version 6.0
  • Microsoft Windows operating system version 3.1

SUMMARY

This article contains the directory of the 5.25-inch, high-density (1.2 MB) Disk #10 (disk 10 of 11) for version 6.0 of Word for Windows. The article includes the decompressed filename for each file, which you must provide when you use the DECOMP.EXE utility to manually decompress a file.
