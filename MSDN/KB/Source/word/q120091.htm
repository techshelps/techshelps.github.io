Hidden Outlining Toolbar Appears Even After You Turn It Off

 Last reviewed: February 5, 1998
Article ID: Q120091
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Word for Windows, versions 6.0, 6.0a, 6.0c
  • Microsoft Word for the Macintosh, version 6.0

SYMPTOMS

If you turn off the Outlining toolbar, Word automatically turns it back on under the following conditions:

  • You are in outline view, and you turn off the Outlining toolbar, switch to print preview, and then click Close.

    -or-

  • You turn off the Outlining toolbar in one window, switch to another document window, and then switch back to the first document window.

(To hide the Outlining toolbar when you are in outline view, click Toolbars on the View menu and click to clear the Outlining check box in the Toolbars list.)

STATUS

We are researching this problem and will post new information here in the Microsoft Knowledge Base as it becomes available.
