WD97: Some Files Remain After "Remove All"

 Last reviewed: July 31, 1997
Article ID: Q164502
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Word 97 for Windows

SUMMARY

Setup leaves certain files because they are shared by other programs. This article lists those folders and files that remain after you select "Remove All" during Word Setup.

The following folder is not removed: 

   Program Files\
