Incorrect or No Printout Mail Merging to Avery Label Printer

 Last reviewed: February 5, 1998
Article ID: Q115612
6.00 6.00a WINDOWS

The information in this article applies to:

  • Microsoft Word for Windows, versions 6.0, 6.0a

SYMPTOMS

When you try to perform a mail merge from Word 6.0 or 6.0a to an Avery Personal Laser Printer, the printer produces incorrect results or fails to print. (Merging to this printer from Word 2.x works correctly.)

CAUSE

Word 6.0 or 6.0a and the Avery Personal Laser Printer version 1.0 software are incompatible. The Avery 1.0 software includes macros designed for mail merging from Word 2.x. These macros don't work correctly from Word 6.0 and 6.0a.

STATUS

Avery has updated these macros for use with Word 6.0 and 6.0a. The macros are available in version 1.0D.

RESOLUTION

Upgrade to version 1.0D of the Personal Laser Printer. To order this software, contact Avery Consumer Service at (800) 642-8379. For more information, contact Avery Technical Support at (214) 888-2699.
