WD: Word 6.0 for Windows&Macintosh Converter Available Online

 Last reviewed: February 5, 1998
Article ID: Q143414
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Word for Macintosh, versions 6.0, 6.0.1

SUMMARY

The "Word 6.0 for Windows&Macintosh" converter is now available on online services. This converter serves as a conversion tool for Word 5.x for Macintosh users who need to convert Word 6.0 or 7.0 documents and do not have access to Word. This capability remains unchanged from the version of the converter released with Word 6.x for the Macintosh.
