List of Fonts Installed with Word 6.0 for the Macintosh

 Last reviewed: February 6, 1998
Article ID: Q117772

The information in this article applies to:

  • Microsoft Word for Macintosh, version 6.0, 6.0.1

SUMMARY

Microsoft Word 6.0 for the Macintosh comes with TrueType fonts for use with Word, Equation Editor 2.0, WordArt and file conversions to other formats. The following font lists are grouped by the setup option which installs them. All fonts are TrueType unless otherwise denoted.

NOTE: All fonts are installed with the Custom/Complete installation. No fonts are installed with the Minimum or Typical installation.

Word Cross-Platform Fonts

Arial Arial Bold Arial Italic Arial Bold Italic Courier New Courier New Bold Courier New Italic Courier New Bold Italic Times New Roman Times New Roman Bold Times New Roman Italic Times New Roman Bold Italic Wingdings

Equation Editor 2.0 Fonts

MT Extra MT Extra 12 (screen) MT Extra 24 (screen) MT Extra 48 (screen) MTExt (Type 1 PostScript font)* 

   * font is installed into the Extensions folder

WordArt Fonts

Arial Narrow Arial Narrow Bold Arial Narrow Italic Arial Narrow Bold Italic Arial Rounded MT Bold Bauhaus 93 Bookman Oldstyle Bold Braggadocio Britannic Bold Brush Script MT Century Gothic Colonna MT Desdemona Footlight MT Light Impact Kino MT Matura MT Script Capitals Mistral Playbill Stencil Vivaldi Wide Latin

Conversion Fonts

MS LineDraw
