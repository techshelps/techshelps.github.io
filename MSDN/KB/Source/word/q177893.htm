WD98: Table Sum Rounded Incorrectly on Large Numbers

 Last reviewed: February 17, 1998
Article ID: Q177893
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Word 98 Macintosh Edition

SYMPTOMS

When you sum a number in a table that is greater than 15 decimal places, the number is rounded to the nearest 10.

For example, the following 16 digit number rounds to the nearest 10th. 

   6553565535655351 + 1 = 6553565535655350

CAUSE

This is a limitation of the floating-point processor. The exact behavior of a floating-point calculation is hardware dependent. 

       Table, Formula, OK
