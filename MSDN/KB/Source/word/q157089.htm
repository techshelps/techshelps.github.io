WD97: Limitations of Converting from WordPerfect 6.x to Word 97

 Last reviewed: March 4, 1998
Article ID: Q157089
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Word 97 for Windows

SUMMARY

In Microsoft Word 97, you can use the WordPerfect 6.x converter to open WordPerfect 6.x for Windows or MS-DOS documents. You cannot save a Word 97 document in WordPerfect 6.x format. However, you can save the Word 97 document in WordPerfect 5.x format and then open the WordPerfect 5.x document in WordPerfect 6.x.

For additional information on the limitations of converting between Word 97 and WordPerfect 5.x, please see the following article in the Microsoft Knowledge Base: 

   ARTICLE-ID: Q157085
   TITLE     : Limitations of Converting from Word 97 to WordPerfect 5.x
