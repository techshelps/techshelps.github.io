WD: Margins Shift When Printed on Personal LaserWriter 300

 Last reviewed: February 2, 1998
Article ID: Q110960
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Word for the Macintosh, versions 4.0, 5.0, 5.1, 6.0, 6.0.1

SYMPTOM

When you print a document to a Personal LaserWriter 300, the margin shifts left approximately .5 inch.

CAUSE

You selected the Personal LaserWriter LS printer driver in the Chooser instead of the Personal LaserWriter 300 driver.

RESOLUTION

To print the document with the correct margins do the following:

  1. From the Apple menu, choose Chooser.

  2. Select the Personal LaserWriter 300 icon.

  3. Close the Chooser.

The document should now print correctly using the Personal LaserWriter 300 printer.
