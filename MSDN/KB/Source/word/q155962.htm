WD97: Some Features Disabled After Setup

 Last reviewed: July 31, 1997
Article ID: Q155962
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Word 97 for Windows

SUMMARY

When you install Word 97 on a computer that has less than 12 megabytes (MB) of RAM, some background features are automatically disabled.

CAUSE

Some features are automatically disabled to improve performance on computers with lower amounts of RAM.

MORE INFORMATION

If your computer has less than 8 MB of RAM, the following background features are automatically turned off during Setup.

  • AutoFormat As You Type
  • Check Grammar With Spelling
  • Check Grammar As You Type

If your computer has less than 12 MB of RAM, the following background features are automatically turned off during setup:
  • Check Grammar As You Type
