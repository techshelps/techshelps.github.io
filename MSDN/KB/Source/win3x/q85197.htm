Setup Runs in Black and White on 8514/a Video Systems

 Last reviewed: November 21, 1994
Article ID: Q85197
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Windows & MS-DOS 5 for IBM PS/2

The Microsoft Windows & MS-DOS 5 for IBM PS/2 Setup program will run in black and white on 8514 video systems. This is also true of 8514/a compatible cards such as the ATI Ultra.

Microsoft Windows & MS-DOS 5 for IBM PS/2 will set up correctly, and Windows 3.1 will run correctly in color with the 8514/a video drivers after the setup is complete.
