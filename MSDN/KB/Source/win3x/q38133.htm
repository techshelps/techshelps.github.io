Hewlett-Packard Font Cartridges "A" Through "G" Description

 Last reviewed: November 30, 1994
Article ID: Q38133
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Windows operating system versions 1.x, 2.x, 3.0, 3.0a, 3.1, 3.11

SUMMARY

Following is a description of the capabilities of the HP Font Cartridges "A" through "G".

The font cartridges support a print resolution of 300 x 300 dots per inch and have an average lifetime of 500 insertions per cartridge. Separate fonts are required for portrait and landscape. Some fonts may not be available in both orientations on a specific cartridge.

For further information relating to these font cartridges, please contact Hewlett-Packard.
