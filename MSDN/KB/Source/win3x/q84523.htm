OmniPage Pro 2.0 Does Not Display Scanned Image at High Res

 Last reviewed: November 23, 1994
Article ID: Q84523
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Windows operating system versions 3.0, 3.0a, 3.1, 3.11

SYMPTOMS

OmniPage Pro 2.0 may not display a scanned image when using a display driver with a resolution higher than 800 x 600 x 16.

RESOLUTION

OmniPage Pro version 2.1 corrects this problem.

MORE INFORMATION

You may be able to view the scanned image at high resolutions by resizing the image window. To do this, move the Size Bar at the lower right up. This will allow you to reduce the image window to one-fourth or one-third of its full size.

Contact OmniPage Technical Support for information about obtaining OmniPage Pro version 2.1.
