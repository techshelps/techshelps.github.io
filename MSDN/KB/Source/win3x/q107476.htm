Video Seven VRAM II Video Adapter Hangs WFWG 3.11 Setup

 Last reviewed: November 21, 1994
Article ID: Q107476
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Windows for Workgroups version 3.11

SYMPTOMS

If you use the Video Seven VRAM II video adapter and the VGA, Super VGA, or Super VGA (256 color) video driver with Microsoft Windows for Workgroups, your computer stop responding (hang) during Setup.

CAUSE

The Video Seven VRAM II video adapter is incompatible with these drivers in Windows for Workgroups 3.11.

RESOLUTION

Run Setup and select Custom Installation. Select the appropriate Video Seven display driver to install.

STATUS

Microsoft has confirmed this to be a problem in Microsoft Windows for Workgroups version 3.11. We are researching this problem and will post new information here as it becomes available.
