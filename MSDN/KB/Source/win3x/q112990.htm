Zoom F9624 Fax Modem Requires ROM Revision 1.15 or Later

 Last reviewed: November 22, 1994
Article ID: Q112990
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Windows for Workgroups version 3.11

The Zoom model F9624 fax modem requires ROM revision of 1.15 or later to work correctly with Microsoft At Work PC Fax.

In Microsoft Windows for Workgroups Terminal, you can determine the ROM revision of your modem by typing "ATI3" (without the quotation marks). This command should echo the revision number.
