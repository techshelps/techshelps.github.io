Cannot Use Reply All Button with Received DCX Faxes

 Last reviewed: November 22, 1994
Article ID: Q111928
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Windows for Workgroups version 3.11

SUMMARY

If you receive a fax in facsimile format and then choose the Reply All command, Microsoft At Work PC Fax does not send your reply to all recipients of the original fax. You can reply only to the person who sent the original fax.

MORE INFORMATION

When you receive a fax in facsimile format, you cannot reply to the people listed on the carbon copy (CC) line by choosing the Reply All button. The fax numbers associated with those names are received in the fax header as a bitmap and cannot be extracted. The only number you can reply to using the Reply All button is the number of the fax sender.
