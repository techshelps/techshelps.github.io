Internet Explorer Plus 4.0 Third-Party Vendor Phone Numbers

 Last reviewed: February 13, 1998
Article ID: Q174451
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Internet Explorer Plus version 4.0

SUMMARY

This article lists the phone numbers of the third-party vendors whose programs or services are included on the Internet Explorer Plus 4.0 CD-ROM.

MORE INFORMATION

Microsoft does not provide support for third-party programs or services included on the Internet Explorer Plus 4.0 CD-ROM. To obtain support for a third-party program or service, please contact the vendor.
