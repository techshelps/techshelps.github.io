How to Hide and Restore the Title on a Toolbar

 Last reviewed: September 29, 1997
Article ID: Q165957

The information in this article applies to:

  • Microsoft Internet Explorer version 4.0 for Windows 95
  • Microsoft Internet Explorer version 4.0 for Windows NT 4.0

SUMMARY

This article decribes how to remove and restore a toolbar's title in Internet Explorer 4.0.

MORE INFORMATION

To remove a toolbar's title, follow these steps:

  1. Press down and hold the SHIFT key while you use the right mouse button to click the toolbar's title.

  2. Click the Show Title command on the menu that appears to remove the check mark.

NOTE: The title can be removed and restored for the Desktop toolbar, the Quick Launch toolbar, or any custom toolbar. You cannot remove the title of the Address or Links toolbars.

To restore a toolbar's title, use the steps outlined above, but in step 1 use the right mouse button to click the toolbar's gripper (because the title is not visible).
