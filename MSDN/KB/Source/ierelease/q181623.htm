Channels Are Missing After You Rename the Channels Folder

 Last reviewed: February 27, 1998
Article ID: Q181623
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Internet Explorer version 4.01 for UNIX on Sun Solaris

SYMPTOMS

If you rename the Channels folder and then restart Internet Explorer, your channels may be missing. Note that by design, you should not be able to rename the Channels folder.

MORE INFORMATION

To avoid this issue, do not rename the Channels folder.

STATUS

Microsoft has confirmed this to be a problem in Internet Explorer for UNIX on Sun Solaris. We are researching this problem and will post new information here in the Microsoft Knowledge Base as it becomes available.
