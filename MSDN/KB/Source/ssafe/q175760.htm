INFO: Contents of Analyze Window Limited to ~30,000 Characters

 Last reviewed: October 28, 1997
Article ID: Q175760
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Visual SourceSafe, 32-bit, for Windows, versions 4.0a, 5.0

SUMMARY

The buffer length for the edit window in the Analyze.exe utility is limited to approximately 30,000 characters. For large databases or situations where lots of error messages are displayed, messages at the beginning of the buffer may not be viewable in the edit window.

All errors should be in the Analyze.exe log file (named Analyze.log by default) because it is not truncated due to the edit window buffer limit.
