XADM: Storage Limit Warning Not Generated

 Last reviewed: February 17, 1998
Article ID: Q177780
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Exchange Server, versions 4.0, 5.0, and 5.5

SYMPTOMS

Notification messages are not sent to mailbox owners or public folder contacts who have exceeded the maximum amount of space allotted for their mailbox or public folders when the Issue Warning limit has been set by the administrator.

CAUSE

The date and time that notification messages are sent to mailbox owners or public folder contacts who have exceeded the maximum amount of space allotted for their mailbox or public folder are not configured to Always.

For more information please refer to the online Help.

RESOLUTION

To work around this problem, change the Storage Warning property page to a time more frequent than the currently selected time(s) or change it to Always, which will send the notification messages every 15 minutes.
