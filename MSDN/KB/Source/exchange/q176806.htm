XGEN: How to Order Exchange 4.0 and 5.0 Manuals

 Last reviewed: February 20, 1998
Article ID: Q176806
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Exchange Server, versions 4.0, 5.0

SUMMARY

This article contains information about ordering manuals, Exchange Kits, Supplemental Replacement manuals, and parts for Microsoft Exchange.

Items are subject to change based on their availability. Please call the Microsoft Sales Information Center at (800) 426-9400 for the latest part numbers and prices.

To place your order for Supplemental Replacement parts, please call (800) 360-7561.

NOTE: When you place your order, you must provide the nine-digit assembly number on the white sticker on the back of the product compact disc case.
