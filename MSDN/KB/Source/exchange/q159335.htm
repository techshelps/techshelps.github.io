XFOR: IMC Perfmon Counters Available Only When IMC is Running

 Last reviewed: April 3, 1997
Article ID: Q159335

The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Exchange Server, version 4.0

SUMMARY

The Internet Mail Connector (IMC) performance counters are available in Performance Monitor only when the IMC service is started. This is different from the MTA and Information Store counters; these counters are available in Performance Monitor even when the service is not started.

MORE INFORMATION

This behavior is by product design.
