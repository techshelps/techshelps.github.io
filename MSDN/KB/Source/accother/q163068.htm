ODE97: Sources for "Getting Results With Office 97" Manual

 Last reviewed: October 20, 1997
Article ID: Q163068
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Office 97 Developer Edition Tools
  • Microsoft Office 97 Professional Edition

SUMMARY

Novice: Requires knowledge of the user interface on single-user computers.

This article summarizes where you can find the "Getting Results with Microsoft Office 97" book.

MORE INFORMATION

Some of the materials in the Microsoft Office 97 Developer Edition Tools (ODE) contain a reference to the "Getting Results with Microsoft Office 97" manual; however, a hard copy of that manual is not included with the ODE.

To obtain pre-sales information about new or updated Microsoft products, call the Microsoft Sales Information Center at (800) 426-9400. If you are outside the United States, contact the Microsoft subsidiary for your area. To locate your subsidiary, see the Microsoft World Wide Offices Web site at: 

   http://www.microsoft.com/worldwide/default.htm
Order part number 269-00600; the price of the book is $14.95.

You can also view the manual from Microsoft's World Wide Web site on the Internet at the following address: 

   http://www.microsoft.com/OfficeReference/GettingResults/default.htm
