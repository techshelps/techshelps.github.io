Cirrus Logic Card w/ 5402 Chip Set Does Not Work w/ 256 Colors

 Last reviewed: September 13, 1995
Article ID: Q123695
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Windows 95

Cirrus Logic has a video card with a 5402 chip set. This card is no longer manufactured or supported by Cirrus Logic, nor are they not providing new drivers for it.

The Windows 95 standard VGA driver can be used with this card; however, the driver does not work with 256 colors (or more).
