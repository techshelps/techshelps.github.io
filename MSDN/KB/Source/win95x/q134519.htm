Adjusting Color Intensity in Picture Publisher Has no Effect

 Last reviewed: February 12, 1998
Article ID: Q134519

The information in this article applies to:

  • Microsoft Windows 95

SYMPTOMS

When you print from Micrografx Picture Publisher 4.0, adjusting the color intensity seems to have no effect.

CAUSE

Picture Publisher 4.0 dithers the image to 4 bits per pixel before passing the image to the printer.

RESOLUTION

Contact the program's manufacturer to inquire about obtaining an updated version of the program.
