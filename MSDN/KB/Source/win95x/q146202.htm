AT&T Globalyst TPC Modems Not Detected Properly

 Last reviewed: February 15, 1996
Article ID: Q146202
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Windows 95

SYMPTOMS

TPC modems in certain AT&T Globalyst computer models are not detected properly in Windows 95.

RESOLUTION

Contact AT&T Technical Support for updated drivers for the TPC modem.

MORE INFORMATION

All AT&T Globalyst computers with model numbers in the "3348-23xx-xxxx" series require updated drivers to operate correctly with Windows 95.
