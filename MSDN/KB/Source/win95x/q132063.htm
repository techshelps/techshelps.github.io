Hard Disk Activity After Shutdown

 Last reviewed: June 26, 1996
Article ID: Q132063
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Windows 95

SUMMARY

Brief disk activity may occur after you see the "It's now safe to turn off your computer" screen.

MORE INFORMATION

The disk activity that occurs after you see the "It's now safe to turn off your computer" screen does not write any data to the hard disk and is not a source of potential data loss. All disk data in memory is written to the hard disk before you see the "It's now safe to turn off your computer" screen. After you see this screen, any additional disk activity is the result of multiple hard disk reset commands.
