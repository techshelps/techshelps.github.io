Title Bar Appears at the Bottom of a Window

 Last reviewed: August 21, 1995
Article ID: Q134495
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Windows 95

SYMPTOMS

A window's title bar, which should appear at the top of the window, may be at the bottom of the window and contain garbled text.

CAUSE

Programs that exhibit this behavior are designed to paint their own title bars instead of letting Windows handle this function for them.

RESOLUTION

Contact the program's manufacturer to inquire about an updated or corrected version of the program.
