ScanDisk Marks Numerous Bad Sectors with XtraDrive

 Last reviewed: August 28, 1995
Article ID: Q130714
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Windows 95

SYMPTOMS

ScanDisk for Windows marks numerous bad sectors on an XtraDrive 3.0 compressed drive during a thorough test (surface scan).

CAUSE

ScanDisk's thorough test is incompatible with XtraDrive 3.0.

RESOLUTION

XtraDrive should repair the compressed volume file automatically after you restart your computer.

MORE INFORMATION

You can use ScanDisk's standard test to check for file allocation table (FAT) errors. Please contact Integrated Information Technology for information about surface scan utilities that are compatible with XtraDrive.
