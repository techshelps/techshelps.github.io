Paradox Coaches Button is Not Displayed

 Last reviewed: August 30, 1995
Article ID: Q135229
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Windows 95

SYMPTOMS

When you run Paradox, you cannot gain access to the Coaches button in the lower left-hand corner of the screen.

CAUSE

The Windows 95 taskbar is covering the Coaches button.

WORKAROUND

To work around this behavior, move the taskbar to the left or right side, or to the top of the screen.
