Chips & Technologies 65550 Refresh Rate Changes after Restart

 Last reviewed: December 19, 1996
Article ID: Q161239
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Windows 95 OEM Service Release versions 2, 2.1

SYMPTOMS

When you change the refresh rate settings for your monitor on a computer using a Chips & Technologies 65550 video chip set, the settings may change back to the default settings when you restart the computer.

RESOLUTION

To resolve this problem, follow these steps:

  1. In Device Manager, double-click the Display Adapters branch to expand it.

  2. Double-click the listed display adapter.

  3. Click the Resources tab, and then click the Use Automatic Settings check box to clear it.

  4. Click OK, and then click OK.

  5. When you are prompted to restart your computer, do so.
