Cannot Change Font Used in Printed Online Help Topic

 Last reviewed: October 30, 1995
Article ID: Q138513
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Windows 95

SUMMARY

When you print from Windows 95 online Help, the font that is used is 8-point MS Sans Serif. The font and font size cannot be changed.

MORE INFORMATION

You can change the size of the font that is displayed on the screen in online Help, but you cannot change the font that is printed. To change the size of the font on the screen when you are viewing a Help topic, click Options, point to Font, and then click Small, Normal, or Large.

To change the font that is printed, you can copy the Help topic text and then paste it into a word processor. Then, change the font in the word processor and print the document. To copy Help topic text, click Options, and then click Copy.
