Mustek TwainScan CG-8400 Hand Scanner Does Not Work

 Last reviewed: October 5, 1995
Article ID: Q134354
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Windows 95

SYMPTOMS

After you upgrade to Windows 95, your Mustek TwainScan CG-8400 hand scanner does not work.

CAUSE

There is no DMA channel available for the hand scanner's card to use.

RESOLUTION

Mustek has a new scanner card available that does not require a DMA channel. To resolve the problem described in this article, obtain the new scanner card from Mustek.
