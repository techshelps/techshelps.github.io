SAMPLE: PortIO.exe Demonstrates How to Reserve Ports

 Last reviewed: February 17, 1998
Article ID: Q181278
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Win32 Device Driver Kit (DDK) for Windows NT, versions 3.5, 3.51, 4.0

SUMMARY

PortIO.exe is a sample minimal driver that demonstrates how to reserve ports and how to read (IN) and write (OUT) to it.
