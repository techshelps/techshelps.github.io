SUMMARY

Below is a non-exhaustive list of Visual Basic User Groups throughout the world including their name, contact name, address, and if applicable their voice phone and FAX phone ID numbers. The list is broken down into the following three geographic regions:

  1. United States
2. Canada 3. International Countries/Regions

The geographic regions are sorted alphabetically by state, province, or country within their region.
