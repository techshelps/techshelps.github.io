Visual Basic 4.0 Reading List

 Last reviewed: June 19, 1996
Article ID: Q152564
The information in this article applies to:
  • Standard, Professional, and Enterprise Editions of Microsoft Visual Basic, 16-bit and 32-bit, for Windows, version 4.0

SUMMARY

This article contains a list of books that may be useful to the Visual Basic for Windows developer and is by no means definitive. The article does not constitute an endorsement by Microsoft of any of these books.
