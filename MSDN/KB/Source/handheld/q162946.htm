Cell References Not Updated in Pocket Excel

 Last reviewed: April 14, 1997
Article ID: Q162946
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Pocket Excel version 1.0

SYMPTOMS

When you cut information from a cell or group of cells in Pocket Excel and then paste the information into a different cell or group of cells, you may experience one or more of the following symptoms:

  • Any cell that references the original cell is not updated with the new cell location.
  • Cell references in formulas are not updated properly.

RESOLUTION

This problem does not occur when you use the Copy command to copy information from a cell or group of cells and then paste the information into a different cell or group of cells. To work around this problem, copy the information from the original location, paste the information in the new location, and then delete the information from the original location.

STATUS

Microsoft is researching this problem and will post new information here in the Microsoft Knowledge Base as it becomes available.
