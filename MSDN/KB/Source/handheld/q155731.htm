Windows CE Does Not Display SRAM PC Card Storage Information

 Last reviewed: February 10, 1998
Article ID: Q155731
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Windows CE version 1.0

SUMMARY

Neither Windows CE nor H/PC Explorer displays the storage capacity of an SRAM or Solid State PC Card in the PC Card socket of your Handheld PC (H/PC).

MORE INFORMATION

If you place an SRAM or Solid State PC Card in the PC Card socket of your H/PC and then view the System properties for your H/PC, the memory information does not include the storage capacity of the PC Card. If you view the PC Card folder in HPC Explorer, the free space listed on the status bar reflects the amount of free storage space on your H/PC, but does not include the memory or storage capacity of the PC Card.
