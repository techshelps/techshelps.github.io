Error Message: One or More Items Could Not Be Deleted

 Last reviewed: March 20, 1998
Article ID: Q177297
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Windows CE, version 2.0

SYMPTOMS

When you delete an e-mail message from Pocket Outlook, you may receive the following error message: 

   One or more items could not be deleted.

CAUSE

This behavior can occur for any of the following reasons:

  • You tried to delete the e-mail message while an attachment in the message was open.
  • You use an Internet Mail information service that has a different name than the information service you used to receive the e-mail messages from your Internet service provider (ISP).

RESOLUTION

To resolve this behavior, follow these steps:

  1. Synchronize your Handheld PC (H/PC) to your desktop computer. To do so, use the following steps:

    a. Connect your H/PC to your desktop computer.

    b. On your H/PC, tap Start, tap Programs, tap Communication, and then 

          tap ActiveSync.

    c. Tap Sync Now, and then tap Disconnect.

    d. Test to see if the behavior still occurs. If the behavior is not 

          resolved, continue to the next step.

  2. Add an Internet Mail information service that has the same name you used to receive the e-mail messages from your ISP. To do so, follow these steps:

    a. On the H/PC, double-tap Inbox.

    b. On the Compose menu, tap Options.

    c. On the Services tab, tap Add.

    d. Tap Internet Mail, and then tap OK.

    e. In the Enter A Unique Name box, type the same name you used to 

          receive the e-mail messages that do not open, and then tap OK.

    f. Type the appropriate information in the Service Definition dialog 

          box, and then tap Next. If you do not know the appropriate
      information, please contact your Internet service provider (ISP).

    g. Configure the features you want in the General Preferences dialog 

          box, and then tap Next.

    h. Configure the features you want in the Inbox Preferences dialog box, 

          and then tap Finish.

    i. Tap OK.
