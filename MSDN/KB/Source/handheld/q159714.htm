Images May Not Be Visible in Pocket Word Outline View

 Last reviewed: April 14, 1997
Article ID: Q159714
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Pocket Word version 1.0

SYMPTOMS

After you change from Outline view to Normal view, images are no longer visible in Pocket Word. However, you can still see an image outline if you tap in the vicinity of a missing image.

CAUSE

The heading level in Outline view is not set to All Levels. The heading level allows you to select the highest level you want displayed.

RESOLUTION

In Outline view, tap All Levels on the headings list on the toolbar.

STATUS

Microsoft is researching this problem and will post new information here in the Microsoft Knowledge Base as it becomes available.
