SBFM: Displaying the Legal Disclaimer After Setup

 Last reviewed: January 8, 1997
Article ID: Q153482
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Excel for Windows 95, versions 7.0, 7.0a
  • Microsoft Small Business Financial Manager for Microsoft Excel, version 1.0

SUMMARY

The Legal Disclaimer agreement is displayed during the initial setup of the Microsoft Small Business Financial Manager for Microsoft Excel (SBFM). After you click the "I Agree" button, SBFM continues with the installation of the program. It is also possible to display the Legal Disclaimer agreement after the setup has been completed.

MORE INFORMATION

To display the Legal Disclaimer agreement after setting up SBFM, do the following:

  1. Start SBFM.

  2. On the Help menu, click "About Financial Manager."

The following is the Legal Disclaimer agreement that is displayed after setup: 

   Note: This template has been prepared to give you ideas and examples
   of how you might use the product for business analysis. It may not,
   however, be appropriate for analysis of your particular business due
   to differences in accounting principles, tax laws and other regulations
   and standards applicable in your area. This template is not intended to
   be a substitute for professional advice. Accordingly, you may wish to
   consult your financial/legal advisors regarding its use. Microsoft and
   its suppliers are not responsible for any action you take based on the
   use of this template.
NOTE: The Legal Disclaimer agreement that appears during setup of SBFM also includes the following sentence: 

   Companies, names, and/or data used in screens and sample output are
   fictitious unless otherwise noted.
