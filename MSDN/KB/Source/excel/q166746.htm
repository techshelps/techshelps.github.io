SBFM97: Files Installed with Small Business Financial Manager

 Last reviewed: February 12, 1998
Article ID: Q166746
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Excel 97 for Windows
  • Microsoft Small Business Financial Manager 97 for Microsoft Excel 97

SUMMARY

This article lists the names and folder locations for the files that are installed by the Microsoft Small Business Financial Manager 97.

MORE INFORMATION

Files Installed During Setup

If you changed the default folder locations or names when you ran Setup, some files may be installed in folders with different names on your computer. The folder names listed in this section are the default names.
