XL5: Tabs Displayed Incorrectly with ATI Ultra Driver

 Last reviewed: March 4, 1997
Article ID: Q108004
5.00 WINDOWS kbusage

The information in this article applies to:

  • Microsoft Excel for Windows, version 5.0

SYMPTOMS

When you use the ATI Ultra 800x600 video driver dated 4/22/92, tab- delimited text may be displayed incorrectly.

For example, if you enter the followng text 

   ="Test1"&CHAR(9)&"Test2"
where CHAR(9) is the tab character, the text will be displayed as: 

   Test1T[]est2
This text should be displayed as Test1[]Test2, where [] is the tab character.

RESOLUTION

To correct this problem, use a version of the driver dated later than 4/22/92. To obtain a later verison of this driver, contact ATI Technical Support at (905) 882-2626.
