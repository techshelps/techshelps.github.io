Excel: Password Protection Not Supported with WK3 Format

 Last reviewed: November 1, 1994
Article ID: Q74616

SUMMARY

Microsoft Excel versions 2.2 and 3.0 do not support saving a document in the Lotus 1-2-3 formats when document protection has been turned on. The document must be unprotected prior to saving in these formats.

MORE INFORMATION

This feature is under review and will be considered for inclusion in a future release.
