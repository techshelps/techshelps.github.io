Copy Field Names to Extract Range Procedure Is Incorrect

 Last reviewed: November 2, 1994
Article ID: Q71719

SUMMARY

Page 39 of the "Microsoft Excel Getting Started" manual has a section labeled "Copy field names to the extract range". Step 5 states: 

   Use the horizontal scroll bar or press the LEFT ARROW key to scroll
   through the worksheet until you can see columns F through I.
This is a documentation error. The statement should read: 

   Use the horizontal scroll bar or press the RIGHT ARROW key to
   scroll through the worksheet until you can see columns F through I.

REFERENCES

"Microsoft Excel Getting Started." Version 3.00, page 39.
