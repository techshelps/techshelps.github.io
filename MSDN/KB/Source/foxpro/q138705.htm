Files Installed by Visual FoxPro Professional in Windows 3.x

 Last reviewed: October 29, 1995
Article ID: Q138705
The information in this article applies to:
  • Professional Edition of Microsoft Visual FoxPro for Windows, version 3.0

SUMMARY

This article lists locations for the files installed for Visual FoxPro professional edition and what component each of the files belongs to.
