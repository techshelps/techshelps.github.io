Disk Space Requirements of FoxPro for MS-DOS

 Last reviewed: April 18, 1995
Article ID: Q115886
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft FoxPro for MS-DOS, version 2.6

SUMMARY

A complete installation of FoxPro for MS-DOS, including the Standard (16- bit) and Extended (32-bit) versions of FoxPro, the Help files, and all optional components, requires approximately 18,500 kilobytes (K), or 18.1 megabytes (MB) of free disk space.

NOTE: The exact amount of disk space required may vary slightly, depending on disk storage factors, such as disk partitioning or compression.

MORE INFORMATION

The individual FoxPro components require the following amounts of free disk space: 

   Component          Kilobytes
   ----------------------------

   Standard FoxPro    6,472
   Help files         2,600
   Extended FoxPro    6,448
Distribution Kit

Maximum installation requires a minimum of 7060K of free disk space.

Standard version EXE support library    1426

Connectivity Kit

The Connectivity Kit requires approximately 150K of free disk space.

Library Construction Kit

Maximum installation requires approximately 785K of free disk space.
