Fox Mouse Support Numbers

 Last reviewed: June 26, 1995
Article ID: Q95721
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft FoxPro for MS-DOS, versions 2.0, 2.5, and 2.5a
  • Microsoft FoxPro for Windows, versions 2.5 and 2.5a

Fox Software marketed two different types of mice. One was a Dexxa mouse that did not have a Fox logo on it. The other mouse is a Logitech mouse; this mouse has a Fox logo on it.

Hardware support issues for these mice are to be directed to Logitech Technical Support at (510) 795-0427. (The Dexxa mouse is manufactured and supported by Logitech.)

The mice included here are manufactured by a vendor independent of Microsoft; we make no warranty, implied or otherwise, regarding these products' performance or reliability.
