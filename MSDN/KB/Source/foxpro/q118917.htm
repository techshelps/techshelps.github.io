FoxPro for UNIX Screens Have MS-DOS Objects

 Last reviewed: August 28, 1995
Article ID: Q118917
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft FoxPro for UNIX, version 2.6

SUMMARY

When a FoxPro for UNIX screen is generated and browsed, MS-DOS objects are visible.

MORE INFORMATION

This behavior is by design. UNIX objects do not exist in FoxPro for UNIX. Since both FoxPro for MS-DOS and FoxPro for UNIX are character-based applications, the only differences between the two products are differences specific to the operating systems. Therefore, the objects used in both applications are the same.
