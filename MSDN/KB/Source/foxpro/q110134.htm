FIX: Compile Thermometer Not Updating in FoxPro for Mac

 Last reviewed: October 20, 1997
Article ID: Q110134
2.50b MACINTOSH kbprg kbfixlist kbbuglist

The information in this article applies to:

  • Microsoft FoxPro for Macintosh, version 2.5b

SYMPTOMS

When you are compiling more than one program (.PRG) at a time, the Compile thermometer will not refresh properly, and does not display the actual status of the compilation process.

Specifically, the thermometer travels all the way to the right after compiling one program, but does not reset itself when it starts to compile another.

STATUS

Microsoft has confirmed this to be a problem in the Microsoft products listed at the beginning of this article. This problem was corrected in Visual FoxPro 3.0b for Macintosh.
