How to Place Insertion Point at Far Left of Field on Entry

 Last reviewed: October 20, 1995
Article ID: Q132677
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Visual FoxPro for Windows, version 3.0

SUMMARY

When entering data, a user may click a field and end up placing the insertion point into the middle of the field instead of the beginning. This article describes a simple method a developer can use to ensure that the insertion point is always placed at the far left of a data entry field anytime it is entered.

MORE INFORMATION

In the Click Event of the object where this functionality is desired, type the following as a User Procedure: 

   KEYBOARD '{HOME}'
This causes the insertion point (cursor) to always go to the far left upon entry of this object.
