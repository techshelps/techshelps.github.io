DOCERR: GotFocus Event Documented Incorrectly in VFP Help

 Last reviewed: October 29, 1996
Article ID: Q157191
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Visual FoxPro for Windows, versions 3.0, 3.0b, 5.0

SUMMARY

The third paragraph in the "Remarks" section of the Visual FoxPro Help topic for GotFocus method states the following: 

   A form receives the focus only when the form has no controls or when
   all its controls are disabled or invisible.
This is incorrect. The GotFocus Event of the form occurs even when the form has no controls or when all its controls are disabled or invisible.
