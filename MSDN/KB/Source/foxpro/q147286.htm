Install Options for Visual FoxPro 3.0 Standard & Professional

 Last reviewed: February 23, 1996
Article ID: Q147286
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Visual FoxPro for Windows, version 3.0

SUMMARY

This article lists the installation options available in Visual FoxPro 3.0 for Windows. It lists the installation options available in the Standard and Professional editions of Visual FoxPro for CD-ROM and disk media.
