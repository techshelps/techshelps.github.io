 Last reviewed: April 30, 1996
Article ID: Q109602
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft FoxPro for MS-DOS, versions 1.02, 2.0, 2.5, 2.5a, and 2.5b
  • Microsoft FoxPro for Windows, version 2.5, 2.5a, and 2.5b

SUMMARY

This article lists the Fox user groups in the U.S. and Canada. This list was furnished by various user groups and is subject to change without notice.
