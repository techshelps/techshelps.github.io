PRB: Online Help Does Not Match Manual for ON KEY LABEL

 Last reviewed: August 28, 1995
Article ID: Q121417
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft FoxPro for Windows, versions 2.5x, 2.6, 2.6a

SUMMARY

The online Help information for the ON KEY LABEL command does not match the information on page L3-709 of the "Language Reference" manual.

The difference occurs in the first four entries of the "Keycap Identification" table. The "Language Reference" manual is correct.

MORE INFORMATION

The top four entries of the table in the ON KEY LABEL Help topic are reversed horizontally; that is, what is on the left should be on the right, and vice versa.

Furthermore, even after the left and right columns are reversed, the FOXHELP.HLP Help topic indicates that LEFT ARROW, RIGHT ARROW, UP ARROW, and DOWN ARROW are the proper key labels for their respective keys. This is incorrect. The correct key labels, as indicated in the manual, are LEFTARROW, RIGHTARROW, UPARROW, and DNARROW.

In the FOXHELP.DBF Help topic, after the columns are reversed horizontally, the key labels are correct except for the use of DOWNARROW in place of DNARROW.
