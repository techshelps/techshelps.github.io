Libraries and Development Tools for FoxPro

 Last reviewed: January 24, 1996
Article ID: Q92959
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft FoxPro for MS-DOS, versions 2.0, 2.5x, 2.6, 2.6a

SUMMARY

This article lists FoxPro library products and programming tools that are available from vendors other than Microsoft. The products are listed in alphabetical order by product name. None of the products is recommended over any other, or over any products not included in the list.

If you are aware of other products that should be included in this list, please notify us.

The products discussed in this article are manufactured by vendors independent of Microsoft; we make no warranty, implied or otherwise, regarding these products' performance or reliability.
