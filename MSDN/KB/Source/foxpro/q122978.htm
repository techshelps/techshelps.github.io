Files Installed from Different Custom Setup Choices

 Last reviewed: May 16, 1996
Article ID: Q122978
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft FoxPro for Macintosh, version 2.5b

SUMMARY

This article lists the files that are decompressed with each option in the Custom Setup option.

MORE INFORMATION

During Custom Setup, several components of FoxPro may be installed or not installed. Each component has its own check box and one or several files in each.
