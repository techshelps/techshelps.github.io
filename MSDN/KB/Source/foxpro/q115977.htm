BUG: Pressing D Key Activates dBASE Help Instead of Diary

 Last reviewed: April 18, 1995
Article ID: Q115977
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft FoxPro for MS-DOS, version 2.6

SYMPTOMS

The Diary cannot be accessed from the System menu by pressing D. Both the uppercase and lowercase letter "d" activate dBASE Help.

CAUSE

Both the Diary and dBASE Help have the letter "d" listed as their shortcut key.

STATUS

Microsoft has confirmed this to be a problem in FoxPro version 2.6 for MS-DOS. We are researching this problem and will post new information here in the Microsoft Knowledge Base as it becomes available.
