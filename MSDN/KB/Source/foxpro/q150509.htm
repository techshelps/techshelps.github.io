List of Problems Solved by FoxPro 2.6a for Macintosh

 Last reviewed: May 16, 1996
Article ID: Q150509
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft FoxPro for Macintosh, version 2.6a

SUMMARY

Below is a list of the problems in FoxPro version 2.5c for Macintosh that were corrected in FoxPro version 2.6a for Macintosh.

MORE INFORMATION

This list contains the numbers and titles of Microsoft Knowledge Base articles that fully describe the problems corrected in FoxPro 2.6a for Macintosh:
