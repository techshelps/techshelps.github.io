PRB: Enhanced Keyboard Arrow Keys Unavailable w/NUM LOCK On

 Last reviewed: April 17, 1995
Article ID: Q86912
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft FoxPro for MS-DOS, versions 1.02 and 2.0

SYMPTOMS

When FoxPro is running on a system and NUM LOCK is on, the arrow keys on an enhanced keyboard are unavailable.

CAUSE

The system has a DTK BIOS (basic input/output system) version 3.15 or 3.16.

RESOLUTION

Contact your dealer or the manufacturer of your computer equipment for information about a BIOS upgrade. BIOS version 3.2 should support the enhanced keyboard arrow keys with NUM LOCK on.
