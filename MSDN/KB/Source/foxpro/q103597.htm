@ ... SAY BITMAP ISOMETRIC CENTER Not Working as Expected

 Last reviewed: June 27, 1995
Article ID: Q103597
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft FoxPro for Windows, versions 2.5 and 2.5a

Using both the ISOMETRIC and CENTER clauses with the same @ ... SAY command does not cause the picture to be scaled and centered within the scaled area, as one might expect. Although the bitmap is displayed in the area defined by the clause, the exact scaling and positioning of that bitmap is unpredictable.

The ISOMETRIC and CENTER clauses can't be used together.

Additional reference words: FoxWin 2.50 BITMAP ISOMETRIC CENTER graphic
