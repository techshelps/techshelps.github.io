US & Canadian FoxPro User Groups & Authorized MS Trainers

 Last reviewed: October 25, 1995
Article ID: Q138458
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Visual FoxPro for Windows, version 3.0

SUMMARY

This article tells you where to get in touch with user groups and authorized Microsoft training and education centers around the United States and Canada.

MORE INFORMATION

Affinity Publishing, located in Seattle, Washington, publishes the "FoxPro Resource Guide," which lists third-party products, services, and user group information. To order a copy of this reference, call Affinity Publishing at 206-281-0089. The cost is $7 per copy.

To find a Solution Provider or Authorized Training Center in your area, please contact Microsoft at 1-800-765-7768.
