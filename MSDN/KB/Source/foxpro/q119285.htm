APPICON.PCT Does Not Exist

 Last reviewed: May 23, 1996
Article ID: Q119285
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft FoxPro for Macintosh Distribution Kit, versions 2.5x, 2.60a

Page 14 of the "User's Guide" for the FoxPro for Macintosh Distribution Kit incorrectly refers to a file called APPICON.PCT. This file does not exist.

Additional reference words: FoxMac 2.50b 2.50c 2.60a PICT dk
