FIX: Extra Linefeeds Sent to PostScript Printers

 Last reviewed: September 22, 1997
Article ID: Q88710
2.00 MS-DOS kbprg kbfixlist kbbuglist

The information in this article applies to:

  • Microsoft FoxPro for MS-DOS, version 2.0

SYMPTOMS

Extra linefeeds are sent to the printer when using the PostScript printer driver shipped with some of the early (prior to 9/26/91) releases of FoxPro 2.0 for MS-DOS.

CAUSE

Different problems have been associated with GENPD.APP, the printer support application supplied with releases dated prior to September 26, 1991, of FoxPro 2.0 for MS-DOS.

STATUS

Microsoft has confirmed that this problem occurs in releases dated prior to September 26, 1991, of Microsoft FoxPro version 2.0 for MS-DOS. Microsoft has confirmed that this problem was corrected in the release dated September 26, 1991, of Microsoft FoxPro version 2.0 for MS-DOS.
