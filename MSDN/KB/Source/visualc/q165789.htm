DOC: Tab Text is too Small to Read

 Last reviewed: October 9, 1997
Article ID: Q165789
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Visual C++, 32-bit Editions, version 5.0

SUMMARY

The text in the tabs for debug windows, such as the Output and Locals windows, is too small to read.

MORE INFORMATION

The tab size is controlled by the desktop's scrollbar size. To change the scrollbar size, right-click the desktop and choose properties, or from the Taskbar click Start, Settings, Control Panel, and double-click the Display icon. Choose the Appearance tab and then change the Item selection to Scrollbar.
