DOC: Registration Card Reports Wrong Support Policy

 Last reviewed: June 26, 1997
Article ID: Q156132
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Visual C++, Enterprise Edition, version 4.2

SUMMARY

The registration card for Visual C++ Enterprise Edition states that Standard Support for this product is 30 days from the time of the first call to product support.

This information is incorrect.

The card should state the current support policy for Microsoft development tools, which follows: 

   Microsoft Technical Support Standard support provides no-charge support
   from Microsoft support engineers via a toll call between 6:00 A.M. and
   6:00 P.M. Pacific time, Monday through Friday, excluding holidays. In
   Canada, support engineers are available via a toll call between 8:00
   A.M. and 8:00 P.M. Eastern time, Monday through Friday, excluding
   holidays. Every Microsoft development tools product includes 2 no-charge
   support incidents that may be used for the current version of the
   product.
