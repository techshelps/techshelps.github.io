DOCERR: Divison Rather than Multiplication

 Last reviewed: November 3, 1994
Article ID: Q28226
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Macro Assembler for MS-DOS, versions 5.0, 5.1, and 5.1a
  • Microsoft Macro Assembler for OS/2, versions 5.1 and 5.1a

On Page 315 of the "Microsoft Macro Assembler 5.0 Programmer's Guide" and on page 315 of the "Microsoft Macro Assembler 5.1 Programmer's Guide" the last sentence of the first paragraph incorrectly states "After the multiplication the result..."

The sentence should read "After the division the result..."

Additional reference words: 5.00 5.10 5.10a
