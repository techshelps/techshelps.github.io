SUMMARY

This article lists the disk contents, file names, and their sizes along with the files that each of the .CAB files contain for Microsoft Office Standard version 7.0b for Windows 95. This article lists disks (21-23 and the three unnumbered PowerPoint viewer disks) for the 3.5-inch DMF disks.
