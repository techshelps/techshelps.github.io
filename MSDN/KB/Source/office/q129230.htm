RAM Doubler Not Shipped with Office Version 4.2.1

 Last reviewed: November 17, 1997
Article ID: Q129230
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Office for the Macintosh, version 4.2.1

SUMMARY

Connectix RAM Doubler, the utility that was shipped with Microsoft Office version 4.2 for the Macintosh, is not shipped with Microsoft Office version 4.2.1 for the Macintosh.

MORE INFORMATION

Connectix RAM Doubler was shipped with Microsoft Office version 4.2 for the Macintosh for a limited time as a special offer. This free offer expired on March 31, 1995, and the offer does not continue beyond that date. Note that if you purchase a copy of Microsoft Office version 4.2 for the Macintosh that contains Connectix RAM Doubler, you may install and use the utility on your machine free of charge.

If you purchase a copy of Microsoft Office version 4.2 for the Macintosh that does not have Connectix RAM Doubler on Disk 1, or if you purchase Microsoft Office version 4.2.1 for the Macintosh, then you do not qualify for a free copy of Connectix RAM Doubler.

The only way to receive Connectix RAM Doubler after March 31, 1995, is to order it through mail-order, through a software retailer, or directly from Connectix. You can contact Connectix as follows:
