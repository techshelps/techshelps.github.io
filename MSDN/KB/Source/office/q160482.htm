OFF97: Toolbar Animation Help Topic Is Not Available

 Last reviewed: June 9, 1997
Article ID: Q160482
97 WINDOWS kbusage kbtool

The information in this article applies to:

  • Microsoft Office 97 for Windows

SUMMARY

Information about the toolbar animation feature is not available in Help.

MORE INFORMATION

You can find information about menu animation in the Customize dialog box for toolbars. To see information about toolbar animation, do the following:

  1. On the View menu, click Toolbars and click Customize.

  2. In the Customize dialog box, click the Options tab.

  3. Right-click the Menu Animations box, and then click What's This?

You can also click Help in the lower left corner on the Options tab. Clicking this button displays the Microsoft Office Assistant. Then, click "Help with this feature."

To animate toolbars, do the following:

  1. On the View menu, click Toolbars and click Customize.

  2. In the Customize dialog box, click the Options tab.

  3. In the Menu Animations box, click an animation type. (To disable
animation, click "(None)" in the box.)

NOTE: Toolbar animation is set globally. Changes that you make to the animation in one program affect the animation in all Microsoft Office programs.
