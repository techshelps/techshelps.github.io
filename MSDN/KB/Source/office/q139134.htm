  1. BEFORE USING THE BUSINESS DATABASE

EXITING YOUR DATABASE: Always exit the database by choosing the Exit button on the Business Database toolbar. If you choose Exit from the File menu or use the Control-menu box, the Business Database may not update your last transaction.

OPENING AND PRINTING FORMS IN YOUR DATABASE: Always open the Small Business Pack forms and reports from either the Small Business Pack toolbar or the Small Business Pack Help. Opening multiple forms from the Forms window in Access can deplete system resources, resulting in degraded performance or errors.

Before printing forms or reports, complete all fields on the form; otherwise, what you've printed may not match the state of the database.

  1. PRINTING LARGER DOCUMENTS TO POSTSCRIPT PRINTERS

The Small Business Pack Business Plan requires substantial PostScript printer memory (more than 1 MB) to print completely. If you try to print but your printer has too little memory, the print job may fail unpredictably--- usually with a PostScript error after a few pages print successfully. To avoid this failure, try printing a few pages at a time.

The entire document will print correctly to a PCL-compatible printer. If a PCL printer is available, print to it. If you must print to a PostScript printer, try using a generic PostScript printer driver (e.g., PSCRIPT.DRV) provided with your Windows system.

You can verify the memory actually available in your printer at least two ways: --Choose the Device Options tab in the Properties dialog 

  box for your printer
--Print a printer test page, which may include this 
  information. For instructions, refer to the manufacturer's
  manual.

  • VIEWING AND PRINTING TAB LEADERS

    If your document contains tab leaders, they may display and print in slightly different locations. If this happens with your document, you should switch to spaces for tab leaders before printing.

    1. VIEWING CELL NOTES IN WORKBOOKS

    To view cell notes in Excel workbooks, choose Options on the Tools menu, and make sure you have chosen the Note Indicator on the View tab.

    1. PRINTING CHARTS

    Some charts may display properly, yet print incorrectly. For example, a chart may print to two pages, instead of the expected one page. If this happens with your printer, you should adjust the scaling factor in Page Setup before printing.

    1. AUTOMATIC CALCULATION

    Some of the Small Business Pack Excel templates calculate totals automatically. To ensure this calculation is performed correctly, choose Options from the Tools menu, and select Automatic on the Calculation tab.

    1. BUSINESS ANALYZER, MICROSOFT MAIL, AND MICROSOFT WORD

    If you want to see the Narrative Report, you should first close all mail messages that have been opened by Microsoft Word, if any. Then, press the Narrative Report button.

  • PARTITIONED AND MULTIPLE HARD DRIVES

    The Office Small Business Pack will not support partitioned drives or systems with multiple hard drives. Here is a summary of conditions and suggestions:

    I. If you have installed Office 95 on Drive C: and Small 

       Business Pack on Drive D:

   When you attempt to launch any of the Word, Excel or
   PowerPoint templates, you may receive an error,
   "Cannot Find or Run the file or program
   \mydocu~1\<folder name>\<filename>". The path is always
   displayed in 8.3 file format.

      WORKAROUNDS
      ===========

      1) Access the templates through the following Small
         Business Pack folder shortcuts on the Office Shortcut bar:

         Financial
         Management
         Marketing
         Operations
         Planning
         Sales

      2) Launch one of the Access databases from the help file
         in the following help path from the Small Business Pack
         toolbar:

         a) Click on the Small Business Pack Help button on the
            Small Business Pack toolbar
         b) Choose the Answer Wizard tab and type in "Job Listings"
            without the quotes.
         c) Choose the "Search" button and then select "Job Listings"
            from the help topics list.
         d) Choose the "To Open the Job Listings form now, click
            <button>" in this help screen.
         e) Once the database launches and asks the user to Attach
            a database, choose Close to close the database.
         f) Now the template links should work. For example, if
            you choose the Small Business Pack Help button, Contents
            tab, choose "Using the Documents", choose Financial,
            choose Accounts Payable, then choose the link button at
            the bottom, the link should now work.
    II. If you have installed both Office 95 and Small Business Pack on 
        Drive D:

    You will not immediately get the error, "Cannot Find or Run the
    file or program \mydocu~1\<folder name>\<filename>" when
    attempting to open the templates via the links in the help file.
    However, you will eventually run into this error message and then
    none of the help links to the Word, Excel or PowerPoint templates
    will work. The Access links will work but you will need to
    reinstall the Small Business Pack to get the links to work again for
    the templates.

       WORKAROUNDS
       ===========

       1) Access the templates through the following Small
          Business Pack folder shortcuts on the Office Shortcut bar:

             Financial
             Management
             Marketing
             Operations
             Planning
             Sales

       2) Start the Small Business Pack setup and choose the
          "Remove All" option to remove the Small Business Pack.
          Then, restart setup and reinstall. However, this may not
          permanently remove the problem and it may appear again later.
          See Workaround (1) above.
    III. If you have installed Office 95 on Drive D: and Small 
         Business Pack on Drive C:

     When you attempt to launch any of the Word, Excel or
     PowerPoint templates from the help file or Answer Wizard,
     you will get an error, "Cannot Find or Run the file or
     program /mydocu~1/<folder name>/<filename>".  The path is
     always displayed in 8.3 file format. For example, you may see
     an error message like this:

     "Cannot find or run the file or program
     /mydocu~1/financ~1/accoun~1.lnk"

     WORKAROUND
     ==========

     Start the Small Business Pack setup and choose the "Remove All"
     option to remove the Small Business Pack. Then, restart setup
     and reinstall. However, this may not permanently remove the problem
     and it may appear again later. See Workaround II, (1) above.
