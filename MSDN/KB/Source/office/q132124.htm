Disk Contents (DMF): MS Office 4.2c for Windows (Disks 1-10)

 Last reviewed: September 16, 1996
Article ID: Q132124
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Office for Windows, version 4.2c

SUMMARY

This article lists the .CAB filenames and sizes and the files that the .CAB files contain for Microsoft Office version 4.2c 3.5-inch disks 1-10 (20 disks total). Disk 1 and disk 20 are standard 1.44 megabyte (MB) formatted disks; disks 2 through 19 are DMF formatted.
