Office 4.3c-CD: Support for Office Demos on the Disk 2 CD

 Last reviewed: September 16, 1996
Article ID: Q122350
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Office for Windows, version 4.3c-CD

SUMMARY

Microsoft Office ships with a collection of application demonstrations, such as Microsoft Money, and Visio Express. These product demos, contained on the Disk 2 CD, are not supported by Microsoft Product Support.

For the demos created by vendors other than Microsoft, contact the individual companies listed below for any problems that you encounter when you run the demo.

The Microsoft demos are not supported. Each demo contains explicit messages as to why it will not run on your computer, or what steps to take to fix the configuration problem that is preventing the demo from running.

NOTE: No files are modified on your computer as a result of running any of the demos.
