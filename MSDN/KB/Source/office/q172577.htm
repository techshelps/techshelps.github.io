OFF97: How to Update the Small Business Edition to SR-1

 Last reviewed: October 24, 1997
Article ID: Q172577
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Office 97 Small Business Edition for Windows

SUMMARY

A separate Service Release 1 (SR-1) version of the Small Business Edition of Microsoft Office 97 will not be available.

To update the Microsoft Office 97 programs included in the Small Business Edition, install the Microsoft Office 97 for Windows Service Release 1 Patch.

MORE INFORMATION

The following table lists the programs that are included in Microsoft Office 97 Small Business Edition for Windows and indicates which programs can be updated with the Microsoft Office 97 SR-1 Patch. 

   Program                                       Updated by the Patch
   ------------------------------------------------------------------

   Microsoft Excel 97 for Windows                Yes

   Microsoft Outlook 97 for Windows              Yes

   Microsoft Publisher 97 for Windows            No

   Microsoft Word 97 for Windows                 Yes

   Microsoft Small Business Financial Manager    No

   Microsoft Automap Street Plus                 No

   Microsoft Internet Explorer                   No
