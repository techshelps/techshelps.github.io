The information in this article applies to:

SUMMARY

The purpose of this article is to provide a listing of the files and folders that are available from the Office 97 CD-ROM disk.

This is a directory listing of the Aamsstp, Autorun, Cdonline, Queries, Sounds, and Srvpack folders and their sub-folders.
