What Is the Small Business Pack for Microsoft Office 95

 Last reviewed: November 24, 1997
Article ID: Q139721
1.00 WINDOWS kbusage kbref kbtool

The information in this article applies to:

  • Microsoft Office Small Business Pack for Office 95, version 1.0
  • Microsoft Office Professional for Windows 95, version 7.0

SUMMARY

The Microsoft Office Small Business Pack for Office 95 (OSBP) is an add-on product for Microsoft Office Professional for Windows 95. The OSBP provides task-based tools such as business templates, forms, and reports to help customers plan, manage, and analyze their businesses.

MORE INFORMATION

Minimum System Requirements to Run Microsoft Office Small Business Pack

  • Personal computer with a 386DX or higher processor
  • 8 megabytes (MB) of RAM with Microsoft Office Standard, or running two applications simultaneously 12 MB of RAM with Microsoft Office Professional, running three applications simultaneously, or running Microsoft Access
  • 20 MB of available hard disk space
  • Microsoft Mouse or compatible pointing device
  • VGA or higher-resolution video adapter
  • One 3.5-inch high-density disk drive
  • Microsoft Office Professional for Windows 95 installed

Integration with Microsoft Office Professional Programs

You can access the OSBP templates from the Office Shortcut Bar as well as from within Microsoft Office Professional programs. Because OSBP is integrated with Office Help, you can also access the OSBP templates through the Answer Wizard.

Setup

Setup of the Microsoft Office Small Business Pack does not support network installations.
