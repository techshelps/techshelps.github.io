OFF97: Small Business Edition Doesn't Include Binder or OSB

 Last reviewed: March 13, 1998
Article ID: Q165453
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Office 97 for Windows, Small Business edition

SUMMARY

The following programs are not included in the Small Business edition of Microsoft Office 97: 

   Microsoft Office Shortcut Bar
   Microsoft Office Binder

MORE INFORMATION

The Small Business edition of Microsoft Office 97 includes the following programs: 

   Microsoft Automap Streets Plus       Microsoft Publisher 97
   Microsoft Excel 97                   Microsoft Outlook 97
   Microsoft Internet Explorer          Microsoft Word 97
   Microsoft Small Business Financial Manager 97
In Microsoft Office 97, you use an integrated Setup program to install all of the programs included in the package, including the Microsoft Office Shortcut Bar and Microsoft Office Binder.

However, in the Small Business edition of Microsoft Office 97, there is no integrated Setup program; each program in the package has its own Setup program. Because of this, the Microsoft Office Shortcut Bar and the Microsoft Office Binder are not included with the Small Business edition of Microsoft Office 97.

To install Microsoft Office Shortcut Bar and/or Microsoft Office Binder on your computer, you must install the Standard edition or the Professional edition of Microsoft Office 97.
