How to Obtain the Multimedia Jumpstart CD

 Last reviewed: February 15, 1996
Article ID: Q124824
The information in this article applies to:
  • Multimedia Jumpstart CD-ROM Disc version 2.0

As of 8/15/95, Multimedia Jumpstart is no longer distributed on CD-ROM.

All of the updated software contained on the Jumsptart CD-ROM to date is available for immediate download on Microsoft s FTP server on the Internet. 

   ftp ftp.microsoft.com
   cd developr/drg/multimedia/Jumpstart
The content and distribution of this product is currently being revised and reconsidered in an ongoing effort by Microsoft to continue to bring you the most up-to-date tools for multimedia developing on Windows 95. Please refer to MSDN Level II for the latest development information. You can subscribe by calling 1-800-759-5474 in the US and Canada, (402) 691-0173 for international calls, or 31.10.258.8864 from within Europe. You can visit the MSDN Offramp web site at http://www.microsoft.com/MSDN.

We will post new information when the revised edition becomes available.
