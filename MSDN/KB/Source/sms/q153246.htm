Sms.exe Fails to Start When Attaching Sites

 Last reviewed: March 11, 1997
Article ID: Q153246

The information in this article applies to:

  • Microsoft Systems Management Server versions 1.0, 1.1 and 1.2

SYMPTOMS

SMS.EXE will fail to launch when Despooler fails to read "mini job to transfer inventory info" sent from the primary site before the central site's inventory information has been loaded into the central site's database.

MORE INFORMATION

After the Despooler fails on the "mini job to transfer inventory info," SMS.EXE will fail to launch when accessing the central site database even though accessing the primary site database is successful. The central site will be rendered unusable when an attempt to attach to the central site is performed before the central site has the chance to inventory and update its own inventory information.
