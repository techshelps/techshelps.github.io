SMSINST: Changes to Wizard Dialogs Do Not Appear Immediately

 Last reviewed: June 30, 1997
Article ID: Q168773
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Systems Management Server, version 1.2
  • Microsoft Systems Management Server Installer, version 1.0

SYMPTOMS

Changes made to the Wizard dialog boxes, such as adding a radio button or making a font change, are not reflected during the setup.

WORKAROUND

The changes to the dialog boxes will be seen the next time you launch the Systems Management Server Installer.
