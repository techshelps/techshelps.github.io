NdisOpenAdapter

NdisOpenAdapter sets up a binding between the calling protocol and a particular underlying NIC driver or NDIS intermediate driver.

Syntax

VOID NdisOpenAdapter(OUT PNDIS_STATUS Status,
OUT PNDIS_STATUS OpenErrorStatus,
OUT PNDIS_HANDLE NdisBindingHandle,
OUT PUINT SelectedMediumIndex, IN PNDIS_MEDIUM MediumArray,
IN UINT MediumArraySize, IN NDIS_HANDLE NdisProtocolHandle,
IN NDIS_HANDLE ProtocolBindingContext,
IN PNDIS_STRING AdapterName, IN UINT OpenOptions,
IN PSTRING AddressingInformation);
