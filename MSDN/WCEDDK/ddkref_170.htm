NdisMResetComplete

NdisMResetComplete returns the final status of a reset request for with the NIC driver previously returned NDIS_STATUS_PENDING.

Syntax

VOID NdisMResetComplete(IN NDIS_HANDLE MiniportAdapterHandle,
IN NDIS_STATUS Status, IN BOOLEAN AddressingReset);
