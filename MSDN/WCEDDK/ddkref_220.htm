NdisTransferData

NdisTransferData forwards a request to copy data received on the underlying NIC into a protocol-supplied packet.

Syntax

VOID NdisTransferData(OUT PNDIS_STATUS Status,
IN NDIS_HANDLE NdisBindingHandle,
IN NDIS_HANDLE MacReceiveContext, IN UINT ByteOffset,
IN UINT BytesToTransfer, IN OUT PNDIS_PACKET Packet,
OUT PUINT BytesTransferred);
