PC Card Window Speeds

Window speed masks specify the access speed for a memory or I/O window. The following window speed masks are defined:

WIN_SPEED_EXP_MASK
Exponent mask
WIN_SPEED_EXP_1NS
1 nanosecond
WIN_SPEED_EXP_10NS
10 nanoseconds
WIN_SPEED_EXP_100NS
100 nanoseconds
WIN_SPEED_EXP_1US
1 microsecond
WIN_SPEED_EXP_10US
10 microseconds
WIN_SPEED_EXP_100US
100 microseconds
WIN_SPEED_EXP_1MS
1 millisecond
WIN_SPEED_EXP_10MS
10 milliseconds
WIN_SPEED_MANT_MASK
Mantissa mask
WIN_SPEED_MANT_10
1.0
WIN_SPEED_MANT_12
1.2

4.0
