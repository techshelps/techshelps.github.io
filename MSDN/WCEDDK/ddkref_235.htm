DrvCopyBits

DrvCopyBits translates between device-managed raster surfaces and GDI standard-format bitmaps.

Syntax

BOOL DrvCopyBits(OUT SURFOBJ *psoDst, IN SURFOBJ *psoSrc,
IN CLIPOBJ *pco, IN XLATEOBJ *pxlo, IN RECTL *prclDst,
IN POINTL *pptlSrc);
