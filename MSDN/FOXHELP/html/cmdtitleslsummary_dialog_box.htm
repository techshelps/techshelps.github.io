Title/Summary Dialog Box

See Also

Lets you specify whether a Title and/or Summary band is included in the report.

Dialog Box Options

Report Title

Title Band

Includes a title band for information that prints once at the beginning of the report.

New Page

Ejects a page after the title band information is printed. You can only check this if you check Title Band first.

Report Summary

Summary Band

Includes a Summary band for information that prints once at the end of the report.

New Page

Ejects a page before the Summary band information is printed. You can only check this if you check Summary Band first.

For more information, see "Modifying Your Layout" and "Customizing Your Layout" in Chapter 7, Designing Reports and Labels in the User's Guide and "Refining Page Layout" in Chapter 12, Adding Queries and Reports in the Programmer's Guide.
