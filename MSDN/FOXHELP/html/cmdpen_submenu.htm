Pen Submenu

See Also

Sets point size and the design pattern of lines and outlines for rectangles and rounded rectangles on your label or report.

These commands are available when you work with a report or label. To enable them, create a line control, select the line control, and then select the Pen command.

Submenu Commands

Hairline

Creates a one-pixel-width outline.

1-Point

Creates a one-point-width outline.

Note   Depending on your monitor's resolution, a point could be one or several pixels wide.

2-Point

Creates a two-point-width outline.

4-Point

Creates a four-point-width outline.

6-Point

Creates a six-point-width outline.

None

Creates no outline.

Dotted

Creates dotted-line outline.

Dashed

Creates dashed-line outline.

Dash-dot

Creates an outline of alternating dashes and dots.

Dash-dot-dot

Creates an outline of dashes alternating with two dots.

For more information, see "Adding Lines, Rectangles, and Circles" in Chapter 7, Designing Reports and Labels in the User's Guide.
