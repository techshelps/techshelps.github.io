Application Wizard (5.0): Step 4 – Configure Menu

See Also

Allows you to select what menus and menu options appear in the application. Menus and menu options that have the word (excluded) after their names won't appear on the menu. Choose Exclude to remove an item from a menu; choose Include to re-add an item.

Note   If you exclude a menu name, all menu options under that menu are also excluded. If you exclude the Forms or Reports menus, you will not be able to access from the menu the forms or reports and labels that were added to the application.
