TickStyle Fields

Fields Description
BOTH Specifies that tick marks appear on both sides of the control.
BOTTOMRIGHT Specifies that tick marks appear on the bottom of a horizontal control or on the right of a vertical control.
NONE Specifies that no tick marks are visible in the control.
TOPLEFT Specifies that tick marks appear on the top of a horizontal control or on the left of a vertical control.
