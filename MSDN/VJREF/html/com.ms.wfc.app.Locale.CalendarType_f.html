Locale.CalendarType Fields

Fields Description
ERA_EMPEROR Specifies the Era: Year of the Emperor (Japan) calendar.
ERA_REPUB_CHINA Specifies the Era: Year of the Taiwan Region calendar.
ERA_TANGUN Specifies the Tangun Era (Korea) calendar.
GREGORIAN_ENGLISH Specifies the Gregorian (using English strings) calendar.
GREGORIAN_US Specifies the Gregorian (as used in the United States) calendar.
