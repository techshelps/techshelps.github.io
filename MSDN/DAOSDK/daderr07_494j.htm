Btrieve — out of resources. (Error 3241)

While working with a Btrieve file, your application ran out of resources.

To increase the number of resources

  1. Exit your application.

  2. Start the Registry Editor, and navigate to the \HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SOFTWARE\Microsoft\Jet\3.5\Engines\Btrieve key.

  3. Increase one or more settings in the Btrieve key of the Windows Registry.

  4. Restart your application, and then try the operation again.

For more information, refer to your Btrieve documentation.
