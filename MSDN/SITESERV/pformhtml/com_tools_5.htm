Changing the Order of Components in a Pipeline Stage

You can move a component up or down within its stage. If only one component exists in a stage, you cannot move it. (However, you can delete it and then insert the same component into another stage.)

To move a component in the Win32-based Pipeline Editor
  1. In the Win32®-based Pipeline Editor, open the pipeline.
  2. Right-click the name of the component, and then on the shortcut menu, click either Move Down or Move Up.
To move a component in the ASP-based Pipeline Editor
  1. In the ASP-based Pipeline Editor, open the pipeline.
  2. Locate the component you want to move, and then click its up or down button.
