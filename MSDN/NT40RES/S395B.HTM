Chapter 1 - Deployment Strategy and Details

The deployment process for Windows NT Workstation 4.0 consists of several distinct phases, including the following:

  • Assemble executive and planning teams
  • Review Windows NT Workstation 4.0
  • Identify the preferred network-client configuration
  • Prepare the planning and support teams
  • Perform lab tests of the client configuration
  • Plan the pilot rollout
  • Conduct the pilot rollout
  • Finalize the rollout plan
  • Roll out Windows NT Workstation 4.0

Not all of these tasks may be necessary for your organization.
