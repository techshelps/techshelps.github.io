Explain your assertions

Perhaps the only thing more annoying than finding an assertion statement in another programmer’s code and having no idea why it’s there is finding a similar assertion statement in your own code. Document your assertions. A simple one- or two-line comment will normally suffice—you don’t need to write a dissertation. Some assertions can be the result of quite subtle code dependencies, so in your comment try to make it clear why you’re asserting something, not just what you’re asserting.
