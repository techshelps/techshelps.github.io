Data Encapsulation

The single most important aspect of any successful report development is ensuring that data and retrieval logic is encapsulated into business objects. If the user is unaware of a physical database behind the scenes, you have achieved the right mix of data and business logic. This mix makes it much simpler for users and developers to select and manipulate data, allowing you to cut down on excess coding and to ensure a uniform approach to data retrieval no matter what technology is being used. Both an ActiveX control displaying stock price information in a Visual Basic form and an embedded ActiveX EXE document displaying stock level information in Internet Explorer should use the same business object internally.
