User Criteria Selection

Two formats are available for user selection: predefined criteria selection screens (Figure 3-1 on the following page), which are best suited to beginning and intermediate users; and free-format criteria selection (Figure 3-2), which is for advanced users. Free-format criteria selection can be placed in a text box or into the report output medium.
