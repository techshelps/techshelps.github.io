Why Convert Your Program to Visual Basic 5?

There are many reasons why you should convert from earlier versions of Visual Basic to Visual Basic 5. Some are technical, some motivational, and some personnel-related, but all of the reasons have one thing in common: they all enable you and your people to develop better applications faster. In this section, I will look at the reasons to convert. I have split the reasons into three areas: language features, improvements to the Integrated Development Environment (IDE), and other, less easily categorized, reasons.
