11.2.3 Print Job Status

The Print Job Status can return the following values.

String

Description

Paused

The job is paused from processing

Error

An error has occured while the job was being processed.

Deleting

The job is being deleted from the queue.

Spooling

The job is being spooled into the queue.

Printing

The job is being printed.

Off-line

The job has stopped processing since the print device is offline.

Paper Out

The job has stopped processing because the printer is out of paper.

Printed

The job has completed printing.

Unknown

The job status is unknown, but an error has not occurred.
