Glossary

License

The certificate or permission to run. This may be in the form of a file and/or printed document. At minimum, the license contains the publisher name, the product name, version, four secrets, and a license policy.

Secret

A reticent value contained on the license. An LS-API compliant license contains room for at least four secrets, with each secret being 4 bytes long.

License Policy

The terms under which an application is granted permission to run. Concurrent use, node-locked, and time-restricted are examples of license policy.
