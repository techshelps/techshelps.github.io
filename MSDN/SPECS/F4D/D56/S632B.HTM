Introduction

This article describes Microsoft's technical requirements and recommendations for creating TrueType Font files (TTFs) and TrueType Collection files (TTCs) which are compatible with Far East editions of Windows 95 and Windows NT 3.51.

The primary audience for this document is developers creating Far East TrueType fonts, or others interested in learning about Far East TrueType in depth. The information will also be useful for developers who create font-editing and font-manipulation tools.

Some of the information in this document may be of interest to developers who create typographically-advanced applications for Far East markets.
