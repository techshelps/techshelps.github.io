4. Conformance

Products are required to meet all applicable sections of this specification to claim conformance with PnP SCSI. Host adapters are also required to satisfy requirements of the applicable Plug and Play host bus, including ISA, PCI, PCMCIA, etc. See Appendix B: Compliance Checklist for a summary of requirements.

An exemption to section 6.3 of this specification requiring automatic switching of exit-point termination is allowed until July 1, 1994. Any PnP products using this exemption shall have the exit-point terminator configurable via a software utility.
