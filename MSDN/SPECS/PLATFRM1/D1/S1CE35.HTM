Pixel data format

As of 4/6/94, the decision was finalized to use a slight modification of Intel's currently defined YVU9 planar format, with an appended BYTE pointer specifying skip blocks, as the IF09. Pixel data in the IF09 will be arranged as shown in Figure 1 for an example bitmap of 160 x 120 pixels.
