Sound

Sound should not be the sole means of conveying information. These guidelines should be followed to ensure that sound is used effectively:

  • If you provide information by sound alone, present it visually when the ShowSounds flag is set.
  • If you generate sounds, provide a way to turn them off.
  • Support system sound events, which play optional sounds when the corresponding actions are carried out.
  • Define and use as many application-specific sound events as possible.
