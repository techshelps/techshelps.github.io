Smart Card Drivers Overview

This overview describes the development of WDM and VxD smart card drivers. It describes:

  • The IOCTL interface for a smart card driver
  • The development of WDM and VxD smart card drivers
  • The use of the WDM and VxD smart card driver libraries

The following topics are covered:

  • Smart Card Driver Environment
  • Smart Card Driver IOCTL Codes
  • Smart Card Driver Data Structures
  • Smart Card Driver Libraries
  • WDM Smart Card Driver Registry Entries
  • VxD Smart Card Driver Registry Entries
  • Smart Card Driver Device Names
  • Smart Card Driver Debugging
