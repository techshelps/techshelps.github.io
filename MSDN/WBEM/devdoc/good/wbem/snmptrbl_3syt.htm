SNMP Troubleshooting Guide

[This is preliminary documentation and subject to change.]

This guide is designed to help users retrieve information from an Simple Network Management Protocol (SNMP) device. The guide describes common errors that can occur during operations performed by the SNMP providers, and suggests solutions for correcting the problems.

The error descriptions and solutions are grouped according to the following categories:

  • Installation and registration
  • Transport protocol operation
  • SNMP Management Information Base (MIB) mapping
