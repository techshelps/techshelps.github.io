qualifier
A modifier containing information that describes a class, an instance, a property, a method, or a parameter. There are three categories of qualifiers: those defined by the Common Information Model (CIM), those defined by WBEM (standard qualifiers), and those defined by developers.
qualifier flavor
A WBEM-defined flag that governs the use of a qualifier. WBEM flavors describe rules that specify whether a qualifier can be propagated to derived classes and instances and whether or not a derived class or instance can override the qualifier's original value.
