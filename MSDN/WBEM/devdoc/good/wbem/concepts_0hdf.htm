Terms and Concepts

[This is preliminary documentation and subject to change.]

The terms and concepts used to support the Web-Based Enterprise Management (WBEM) technology originate from many sources. Many were conceived by the Desktop Management Task Force (DMTF) to describe managed objects. Others are standard Component Object Model (COM) terms and concepts. Still others are specific to the WBEM SDK.

For additional information about WBEM architecture, see Architectural Overview.
