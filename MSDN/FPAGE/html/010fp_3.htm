Marquees

A marquee is a region on a page that displays a horizontally scrolling text message, much like a theater marquee or stock ticker. Use a marquee to draw a user’s attention to a line of text, such as a headline or attention-grabbing announcement.

You create a marquee on the Insert menu by pointing to Active Elements and then clicking Marquee. In the Marquee dialog box, you supply the marquee text, background color, type of marquee effect, marquee speed, and other properties.

As with other active page elements, the best way to configure a marquee is to create one and preview it, trying various effects and color schemes until it matches your needs. To preview a marquee, use the FrontPage Editor’s Preview tab (if you have Microsoft Internet Explorer, version 3.0 or higher, installed) or choose Preview in Browser on the FrontPage Editor’s File menu.
