FrontPage Explorer Views

Views in the FrontPage Explorer provide different ways of looking at your FrontPage web, so that you can effectively manage your site.

You can choose the view that best suits your needs — from the early steps of web creation, to the moment your FrontPage web is ready to be published on the World Wide Web.

You can choose to display:

  • the file and folder structure of your FrontPage web

  • a complete listing of all the files in your FrontPage web

  • the navigation layout of your FrontPage web

  • the hyperlink relationships in your FrontPage web

  • the status of internal and external hyperlinks

  • the gallery of FrontPage themes that can be applied

  • a list of web tasks for the current FrontPage web

The seven buttons on the FrontPage Explorer’s Views bar provide a quick way to switch the display of your FrontPage web.
