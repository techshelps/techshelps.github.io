Welcome to the World Wide Web

The World Wide Web is the graphical Internet service that provides a network of interactive documents and the software to access them. It is based on documents called pages that combine text, pictures, forms, sound, animation, and hypertext links called hyperlinks. To navigate the World Wide Web, users “surf” from one page to another by pointing and clicking on hyperlinks in text or graphics.

The World Wide Web is many things to its millions of users. It is used as a marketplace, art gallery, library, community center, school, publishing house, and whatever else its authors, including you, create.
