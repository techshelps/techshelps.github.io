Hit Counters

A Hit Counter is a FrontPage component that keeps a count of how many users have visited the page it is on. By putting a Hit Counter on the Home Page of your FrontPage web, you can keep track of how many users have visited your Web site.

You create a Hit Counter on the Insert menu by pointing to Active Elements and then clicking Hit Counter. In the Hit Counter Properties dialog box, select a counter style. This is a GIF image containing the digits zero through nine. Reset a hit counter by clicking Reset Counter To and supplying the number to reset the counter to.

To supply a custom GIF image containing a customized set of the digits zero through nine, enter the relative URL of the image in the Custom Image field. If you supply a custom image, be sure to space the ten digits evenly.
