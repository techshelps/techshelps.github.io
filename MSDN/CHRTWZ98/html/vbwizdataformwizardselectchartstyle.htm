Data Form Wizard — Select Chart Style

Allows you to pick a style of chart that most effectively displays your data.

Options

Chart Styles

Displays a list of available chart styles from which you can make your selection. As you select a style, the picture changes to display a picture of that type of chart.

Help

Displays the help topic for this step. You can also press F1 for help.

Cancel

Cancels your previous actions and closes the Data Form wizard.

Back

Moves you to the previous step.

Next

Moves you to the next step.

Finish

Creates a form with a chart of your data based on the settings you selected.
