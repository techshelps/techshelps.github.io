Data Form Wizard — Select Appearance Properties

Allows you to determine how the chart will appear when it is built.

Options

Show Legend

When checked places a legend on the finished chart. A legend identifies the patterns of colors you use for the data series or categories in your chart. When you choose this option, a legend appears with the chart.

Show Markers

When checked shows the markers in the chart. Markers are graphic elements that represent numerical values or a single data point in your original data. The chart to the right changes to show your option.

Stack Series

When checked causes multiple series to be stacked. If you have only one series, checking this option has no effect.

Note   You will see multiple series only if you select a field in the z axis that has more than one unique value.

Set Degree of Rotation

Allows you to drag the slider to rotate your chart. The degree of rotation appears at the far right of the slider and changes as you move the slider. The chart to the right rotates as you change this option. This option is only available for 3-D style grids.

Help

Displays the help topic for this step. You can also press F1 for help.

Cancel

Cancels your previous actions and closes the Data Form wizard.

Back

Moves you to the previous step.

Next

Moves you to the next step.

Finish

Creates a form with a chart of your data based on the settings you selected.
