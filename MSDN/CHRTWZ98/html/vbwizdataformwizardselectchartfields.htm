Data Form Wizard — Select Chart Fields

Allows you to choose the fields containing the data that you want to display graphically. You can determine how you want to group and summarize your data.

Options

X Axis List

Allows you to choose the field you want to group by on the X axis. Required.

Z Axis List

Allows you to choose the field you want to group by on the Z axis. Optional.

Y Axis Summary Field List

Allows you to choose the field to summarize on the Y axis.

Aggregate Function List

Allows you to choose the aggregate function to use when summarizing the Y axis.

Help

Displays the help topic for this step. You can also press F1 for help.

Cancel

Cancels your previous actions and closes the Data Form wizard.

Back

Moves you to the previous step.

Next

Moves you to the next step.

Finish

Disabled in the step.
