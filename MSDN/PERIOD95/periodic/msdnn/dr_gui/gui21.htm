The Doctor's Bag

Every good doctor has a black bag, at least in movies from the 1950s. I too have a black bag. It is a mail folder labeled "Dr. GUI." As I watch messages in various e-mail groups, I take those items that seem interesting. The problem, though, is that I seldom get to use this information to solve your problems. So I thought that I would devote Dr. GUI #21 to technical questions and answers propagated within the hallowed walls of Microsoft.
