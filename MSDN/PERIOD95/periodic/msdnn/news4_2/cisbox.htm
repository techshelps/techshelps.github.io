Tap into the Developer Network CompuServe Forum

March 10, 1995

What happens in the Microsoft Developer Network forum (GO MSDN) on CompuServe?

Lots! For example, you can jump into the Peer Help/Chat Messages section to post questions or answer inquiries from other developers. In Library 1, "New Uploads," you'll find things that didn't make the last issue of the Development Library or Platform, such as the international terminology glossaries. You can also find the MSDN FAQ, a compilation of answers to the most frequently asked questions we receive.

We also sometimes post "flashes" highlighting items of importance to developers. If there is a flash, you'll automatically get it upon entering the MSDN forum. We also use the CompuServe forum to obtain your feedback and responses to surveys, particularly when we face hard decisions. For example, your input was invaluable in figuring out how—or even if—we should go to two CDs for the Development Library. When we post surveys, you are notified via flashes. You can also talk to us via the MSDN forum. Let us know if we're doing well or abysmally, and how we can improve.
