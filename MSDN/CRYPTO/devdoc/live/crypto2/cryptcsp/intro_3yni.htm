Related Documentation

Additional documents that may help you understand cryptography and the associated security issues include:

  • Schneier, Bruce Applied Cryptography, John Wiley & Sons, 1996.
  • The Microsoft Platform SDK, CryptoAPI 2.0, Microsoft , 1997, located at http://www.microsoft.com.
  • Stinson, D. R. Cryptography: Theory and Practice, CRC Press 1995.
  • Anderson, R. "Why Cryptosystems Fail," Communications of the ACM, v. 37, n. 11, Nov 1994, pp. 32-40.
  • RSA Laboratories, Public-Key Cryptography Standards, RSA Data Security, November 1993.
  • Cormen, Thomas H.; Charles E. Leiserson; Ronald L. Rivest. Introduction to Algorithms, MIT Press, 1990.
  • Davies, D.W. and W.L. Price. Security for Computer Networks, John Wiley & Sons, 1989.
  • Denning, Dorothy E., Cryptography and Data Security, Addison-Wesley, 1982.
