RSA/Full CSP Reference Overview

This reference contains detailed information about all of the Microsoft® RSA/Full Cryptographic Service Provider (CSP) functions.

This information is divided into the following sections:

  • Service Provider Functions

    Covers connecting to (and disconnecting from) CSPs and customizing their operation.

  • Key Generation and Exchange Functions

    Covers creating, configuring, and destroying cryptographic keys, as well as exchanging them with other users

  • Data Encryption Functions

    Covers encryption and decryption operations

  • Hashing and Digital Signature Functions

    Covers the computation of hashes (also known as message digests), as well as the creation and verification of digital signatures
