PR_ARRIVAL_TIME

The PR_ARRIVAL_TIME property contains the date and time a specific message arrived at a gateway.

Quick Info

Header file: EDKMDB.H
Applies to: Message transfer envelope objects (information store objects)
Identifier: 0x665f
Property type: PT_SYSTIME
Access: Read/write

Remarks

The PR_ARRIVAL_TIME property is used by developers to track the arrival time of messages.
