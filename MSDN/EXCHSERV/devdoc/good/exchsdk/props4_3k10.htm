PR_MEMBER_ENTRYID

The PR_MEMBER_ENTRYID property contains the directory object entry identifier of a member of the ACL table.

Quick Info

Header file: EDKMDB.H
Applies to: Public folder and mailbox objects (information store objects)
Identifier: 0x0fff
Property type: PT_BINARY
Access: Read/write

Remarks

The PR_MEMBER_ENTRYID property is used by the IExchangeModifyTable interface to uniquely identify a person or role to whom the ACL applies.

After a member is created in the ACL table, ENTRYID cannot be changed. To change it, you must delete the table member and recreate it with a different ENTRYID.
