PR_EMS_AB_AUTOREPLY_MESSAGE

The PR_EMS_AB_AUTOREPLY_MESSAGE property contains the message that is sent when the Out of Office rule is applied and the Out of Office Assistant is set.

Quick Info

Header file: EMSABTAG.H
Applies to: Mail-Recipient objects (directory objects). These objects appear to MAPI as messaging user objects, which are accessible through the MAPI IMailUser interface.
Identifier: 0x800a
Property type: PR_EMS_AB_AUTOREPLY_MESSAGE PT_TSTRING

PR_EMS_AB_AUTOREPLY_MESSAGE_A PT_STRING8

PR_EMS_AB_AUTOREPLY_MESSAGE_W PT_UNICODE
Access: Read/write
Unicode: Yes

Values

1
Minimum length
1024
Maximum length
