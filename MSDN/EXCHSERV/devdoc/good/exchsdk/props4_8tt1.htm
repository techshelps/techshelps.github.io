PR_OOF_STATE

The PR_OOF_STATE property contains whether the Out of Office Assistant is enabled.

Quick Info

Header file: EDKMDB.H
Applies to: Message store objects (information store objects)
Identifier: 0x661d
Property type: PT_BOOLEAN
Access: Read-only

Values

0
FALSE = Out of Office Assistant is disabled.
1
TRUE = Out of Office Assistant is enabled.
