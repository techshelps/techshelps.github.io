PR_EMS_AB_TRANSFER_TIMEOUT_NORMAL

The PR_EMS_AB_TRANSFER_TIMEOUT_NORMAL property contains the maximum time per kilobyte to transfer a normal message.

Quick Info

Header file: EMSABTAG.H
Applies to: Mail-Gateway and MTA-Cfg objects (directory objects). These objects appear to MAPI as messaging_user objects, which are accessible through the MAPI IMailUser interface.
Identifier: 0x814b
Property type: PT_LONG
Access: Read/write
