Documenting New Property Pages

You need to provide documentation to your customers to help administrators plan the application's configuration by identifying and choosing options needed to meet the organization's messaging requirements. To select the appropriate combination, administrators need to understand the benefits, effects, and relationship of these options.

Administrators should be able to plan for and implement:

  • Required messaging and network topology options.
  • Application operating options required for their organization. Depending on the application's functionality, these options could include message addressing, message and attachment conversion, service accounting, and user access to the application.
