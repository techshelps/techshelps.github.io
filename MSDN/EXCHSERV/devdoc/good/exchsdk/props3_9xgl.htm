PR_EMS_AB_ORGANIZATION_NAME

The PR_EMS_AB_ORGANIZATION_NAME property is for X.500 compatibility and is not used by Microsoft Exchange Server.

Quick Info

Header file: EMSABTAG.H
Applies to: Application-Entity, Device, Group-Of-Names, and Organization objects (directory objects). These objects appear to MAPI as messaging_user objects, which are accessible through the MAPI IMailUser interface.
Identifier: 0x8101
