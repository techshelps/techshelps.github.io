PR_EMS_AB_OOF_REPLY_TO_ORIGINATOR

The PR_EMS_AB_OOF_REPLY_TO_ORIGINATOR property determines whether Out of Office replies will be sent to the user who sends mail to a distribution list.

Quick Info

Header file: EMSABTAG.H
Applies to: Distribution-List objects (directory objects). These objects appear to MAPI as messaging_user objects, which are accessible through the MAPI IMailUser interface.
Identifier: 0x80ff
Property type: PT_BOOLEAN
Access: Read/write

Values

0
FALSE = Out of Office replies will not be sent.
1
TRUE = Out of Office replies will be sent.
