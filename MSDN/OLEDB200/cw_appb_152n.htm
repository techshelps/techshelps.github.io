Operator= Used with COLUMNINFO

COLUMNINFO& operator=(
const COLUMNINFO& other)
COLUMNINFO& operator=(
const DBCOLUMNINFO& other)

Comments

Overloaded assignment operator. The left side of the COLUMNINFO structure contents are cleared before the assignment is made. The right side of the structure is left intact. If the assignment succeeds, both structures will be synonymous (COLUMNINFO to COLUMNINFO). In addition, the DBCOLUMNINFO portion to the left of COLUMNINFO is synonymous to the right of DBCOLUMNINFO; and additional data members of the left COLUMNINFO structure will be zero initialized.
