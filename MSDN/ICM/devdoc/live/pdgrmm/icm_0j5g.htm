About Color Management

[This is preliminary documentation and subject to change.]

To understand how ICM 2.0 is used, a working knowledge of color management concepts is required. This overview presents enough color management background to provide a definition of the color management terms and concepts that appear in the ICM 2.0 Programmer's Reference in the Platform Software Development Kit (SDK) documentation. It is not exhaustive in its coverage. The topics in this section cover the major areas of color management, such as:
