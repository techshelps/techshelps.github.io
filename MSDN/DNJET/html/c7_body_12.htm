Briefcase Reconciler

The Briefcase reconciler (MSRclr35.dll) is a special utility that is automatically executed when Windows 95 or Windows NT Server or Workstation 4.0 Briefcase is used, or from Microsoft Access when synchronizations through the Synchronizer are not explicitly requested. The reconciler ensures that a database is replicable and manages the merging of updates between the Briefcase (local replica) and the target replica.
