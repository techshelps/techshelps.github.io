IAudio::Claim

HRESULT Claim(void);

Acquires the multimedia wave device for use by the audio object.

· Returns NOERROR if successful, or one of these values:

E_FAIL
E_OUTOFMEMORY
AUDERR_ALREADYCLAIMED
AUDERR_BADDEVICEID
AUDERR_HANDLEBUSY
AUDERR_INVALIDHANDLE
AUDERR_NEEDWAVEFORMAT
AUDERR_NODRIVER
AUDERR_WAVEDEVICEBUSY
AUDERR_WAVEFORMATNOTSUPPORTED
AUDERR_WAVENOTENABLED

Claim opens the multimedia wave device and generates an IAudioSourceNotifySink::AudioStart notification. The device remains open until the IAudio::UnClaim member function is called.
