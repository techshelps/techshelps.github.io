[Switch]

The [switches] section provides human readable descriptions of the option switches, with one switch per line. The format is:

<number> = <1 or 0> "name" "description"

The <1 or 0> sets the default state to on or off.

The switches section for the default Voice Dictation CFG looks something like this:

1 = 0 "Two spaces between sentences" "This puts two spaces between sentences."

2 = 1 "Acronyms" "Removes spaces between a sequence of letters."

3 = 1 "Digits" "Removes spaces between a sequence of digits."

4 = 0 "Period in acronyms" "Puts periods in acronyms, like F.E.I.F."
