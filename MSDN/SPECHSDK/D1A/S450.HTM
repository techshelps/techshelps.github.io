Interfaces and Member Functions

The following interfaces and member functions are common to two or more speech objects:

· The IEnumX member functions Clone, Next, Reset, and Skip are included in the following interfaces: IVCmdEnum, ISREnum, IEnumSRSHARE, and ITTSEnum.

· The ILexPronounce interface is supported by the speech-recognition and text-to-speech engine objects. This interface is optional.

· The IUnknown interface is supported by all speech objects.
