ISRSpeaker::Revert

HRESULT Revert(TCHAR *pszSpeakerName);

Restores training to its original state at the beginning of the session, which is the last time the speaker information was loaded (either by calling the ISRAttributes::SpeakerSet or ISRSpeaker::Select member function).

· Returns NOERROR if successful, or one of these error values:

E_INVALIDARG
E_OUTOFMEMORY

pszSpeakerName

[in] Address of a string that contains the name of the speaker.

A session begins when a speaker is selected with a call to the Select member function (unless that speaker was already selected.) An application can use Revert to cancel undesirable changes, such as those caused by extraneous noise or unrelated conversations during a training session.
