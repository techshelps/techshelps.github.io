About Power Management

To save power and ensure that no data is lost when a computer or device is turned off or placed in a low power state, the system provides power management support. The power management functions and messages retrieve the system power status, notify applications of power management events, and notify the system of each application's power requirements. This overview contains the following topics:

  • OnNow Power Management
  • System Power Status
  • System Power States
  • Power Management Events
  • Power Management Policy
  • System Sleep Criteria
  • System Wake-up Events
  • Message-Waiting Indicators
