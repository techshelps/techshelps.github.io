Low-Level Access Control Structures

The following structures are used with low-level access control.

ACCESS_ALLOWED_ACE
ACCESS_DENIED_ACE
ACCESS_ALLOWED_OBJECT_ACE
ACCESS_DENIED_OBJECT_ACE
ACL_REVISION_INFORMATION
ACL_SIZE_INFORMATION
SECURITY_DESCRIPTOR_CONTROL
SYSTEM_AUDIT_ACE
SYSTEM_AUDIT_OBJECT_ACE

The following structures are reserved for future use.

SYSTEM_ALARM_ACE
SYSTEM_ALARM_OBJECT_ACE
