Acknowledgment and Report Messages

MSMQ generates acknowledgment and report messages. They are sent to administration queues and report queues, respectively.

Note  Acknowledgment and report messages can also be created by MSMQ Connector applications.

Acknowledgment messages are sent to the administration queue specified by the sending application.

Report messages are sent to the report queue created by the MSMQ Administrator.
