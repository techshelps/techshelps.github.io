Selecting Foreground or Background Colors

Home Page (Graphics Editor) |  Overview |  How Do I ... Topics

Except for the eraser, tools on the Graphics toolbar draw with the current foreground or background color when you press the left or right mouse button, respectively.

To select a foreground color

  • With the left mouse button, click the color you want on the Colors palette.

To select a background color

  • With the right mouse button, click the color you want on the Colors palette.
