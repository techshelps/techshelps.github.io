What Happened to Recording and Playing Back Keystrokes?

Home Page (Text Editor) |  Overview |  How Do I ... Topics |  FAQ |  Reference

You can record and playback keystrokes and other actions, such as command executions, by using the macro options available in Visual C++.

To record a macro

  • Select Macro or Record Quick Macro from the Tools menu.

The Macro option provides you the ability to name, save, and later edit the macro you record. The Quick Macro option by-passes the Macro interface, immediately recording your actions and storing them so that you can play them back, until you record another Quick macro.

For more information, see the Developer Studio Objects Home Page.
