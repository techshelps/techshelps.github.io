How Do I ... Topics: ClassView

Home Page (Working with Classes) | Overview

ClassView, similar to WizardBar, provides simple and direct ways to perform common tasks with your application classes. From the ClassView pane, you can easily navigate among class members, modify interfaces, set breakpoints for the integrated debugger, or generate a new form for your project. You can also use ClassView to add the initial code for classes and members. Additionally, you can modify the ClassView display itself.
