Hiding or Displaying the ClassView Pane

Home Page (Working with Classes) |  Overview |  How Do I ... Topics

You may prefer to work with only a few views on any regular basis. You can customize your workspace by hiding one or more views.

To hide or display ClassView

  1. With your mouse cursor pointed at a tab along the bottom edge of the Project Workspace, click the right mouse button.

  2. From the list of views, select or clear ClassView to toggle its display.

You also can hide the Project Workspace.
