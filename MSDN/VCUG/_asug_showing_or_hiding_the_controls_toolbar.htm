Showing or Hiding the Dialog Editor Toolbars

Home Page (Dialog Editor) |  Overview |  How Do I ... Topics

When you are in the Dialog editor, you can toggle the display of the Controls toolbar or the Dialog toolbar by selecting them from the list of available toolbars and windows.

To show or hide a Dialog editor toolbar

  • From within the Dialog editor, right-click in an empty part of the menu bar and check or uncheck Controls or Dialog.
