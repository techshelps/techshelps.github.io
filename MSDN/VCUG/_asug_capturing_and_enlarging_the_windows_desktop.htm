Capturing and Enlarging the Windows Desktop

Overview |  How Do I ... Topics

To use ZoomIn

  1. Click the Start button on the Windows taskbar and highlight Programs.

  2. On the resulting Programs submenu, highlight Microsoft Visual C++ 6.0.

  3. Highlight Microsoft Visual C++ 6.0 Tools then click ZoomIn.

  4. Click within the ZoomIn window's client area and drag the rectangle over the target area you want to enlarge.

    This target area can be anywhere in the Windows graphical desktop area. The area over which you center the ZoomIn rectangle appears in the ZoomIn window's client area.

  5. Release the mouse button when the desired target area is visible in the ZoomIn window.

To enlarge the image, use the scroll bar to scroll down. To reduce the image to its original size, use the scroll bar to scroll up. Each successive click on the scroll bar enlarges or shrinks the image.
