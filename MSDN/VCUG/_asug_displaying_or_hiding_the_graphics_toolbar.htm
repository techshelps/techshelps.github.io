Displaying or Hiding the Graphics Toolbar

Home Page (Graphics Editor) |  Overview |  How Do I ... Topics

Since many of the drawing tools are available from the keyboard, sometimes it is useful to hide the Graphics toolbar. To find out which drawing commands are available from the keyboard, select the Keyboard command from the Help menu, then click Image in the drop-down list.

To show or hide the Graphics toolbar

  1. Place the mouse pointer over the toolbar area and click the right mouse button.

From the pop-up menu, choose Graphics.
