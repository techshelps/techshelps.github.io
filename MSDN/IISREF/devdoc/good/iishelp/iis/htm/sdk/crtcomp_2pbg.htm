Selecting a Threading Model

Whether it is used over the Internet or an intranet, your component will need to deal with simultaneous calls. How it deals with simultaneous calls is largely determined by the threading model you choose. This choice will have significant implications on your component's responsiveness, particularly in large volume Internet scenarios:

Your options for threading models are:

  • Single (not recommended)
  • Apartment (recommended for components that have page scope)
  • Free (not recommended)
  • Both (recommended for all components)

The threading recommendations outlined in Component Design Guidelines summarize the technical implications for choosing between the threading model options.
