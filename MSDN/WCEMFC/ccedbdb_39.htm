CCeDBDatabase::m_hHandle

This data member contains the handle to an open database.

HANDLE m_hHandle;

At a Glance

Header file: Wcedb.h
Platforms:
Versions: 2.0 and later

Remarks

When you call the Open member function, it sets the m_hHandle data member to a non-NULL value.
