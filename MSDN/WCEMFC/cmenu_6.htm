CMenu::CMenu

Use this constructor to construct a CMenu object. The menu is not actually created until you call one of the create or load member functions of CMenu:

  • CreateMenu
  • CreatePopupMenu
  • LoadMenu
  • Attach

Syntax

CMenu( );
