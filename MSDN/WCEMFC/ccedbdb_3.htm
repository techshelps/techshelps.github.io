CCeDBDatabase::CCeDBDatabase

This constructor constructs an empty database object.

Syntax

CCeDBDatabase( );

At a Glance

Header file: Wcedb.h
Platforms:
Versions: 2.0 and later

Remarks

After constructing a database, you have to open it before you can call any member functions on it, other than Create and Exists.
