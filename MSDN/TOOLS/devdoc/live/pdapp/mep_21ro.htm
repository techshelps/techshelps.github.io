Cursor Movement

Description Function Key
Move left one character. Left CTRL+S
Move right one character. Right CTRL+D
Move up one line. Up CTRL+E
Move down one line. Down CTRL+X
Move to beginning of next line. Newline unassigned
Move to first character in line. Begline unassigned
Move past last character in line. Endline END
Move left one tab stop. Backtab SHIFT+TAB
Move right one tab stop. Tab TAB
Move back by paragraphs. Mpara CTRL+UP
Move forward by paragraphs. Ppara CTRL+DOWN
Move back by words. Mword CTRL+LEFT
Move forward by words. Pword CTRL+RIGHT
Move back by pages. Mpage CTRL+R
Move forward by pages. Ppage CTRL+C
Move to upper-left corner. Home unassigned
Move to top of file. Begfile CTRL+PGUP
Move to end of file. Endfile CTRL+PGDN
