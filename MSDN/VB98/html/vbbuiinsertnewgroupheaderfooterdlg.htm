Insert New Group Header/Footer Dialog Box (Microsoft Data Report)

Inserts a new Group Header and new Group Footer and lets you position it on the report. Group Headers and Group Footers can only appear between the detail section and the Page Header and Page Footer sections.

Options

List of sections

Lists the sections in the order in which they appear on the report. The new Group Header and Group Footer sections appear below the current Group Headers and Group Footers. Use the arrow keys to position the selected New Group Header and New Group Footer where you want them.

Moves the selected Group Header and Group Footer one position away from the detail section each time you click it. The Group Header moves until it is positioned below the Page Header section and the Group Footer move until it is positioned above the Page Footer section. When you cannot move them further the button becomes inactive.

Moves the selected Group Header and Group Footer one position toward the detail section each time you click it. The Group Header moves until it is positioned above the detail section and the Group Footer moves until it is positioned below the detail section. Then you cannot move them further and the button becomes inactive.
