Align Commands (Microsoft Data Report Shortcut Menu)

Aligns selected Data Report designer controls with each other.

Lefts

Horizontally aligns the position of the selected controls, putting the leftmost edges in line with that of the leftmost control.

Centers

Horizontally aligns the position of the selected controls, putting the center of each control in line with that of the others.

Rights

Horizontally aligns the position of the selected controls, putting the rightmost edges in line with that of the rightmost control.

Tops

Vertically aligns the position of the selected controls, putting the top of each control in line with that of the topmost control.

Middles

Vertically aligns the position of the selected controls, putting the middle of each control in line with that of the others.

Bottoms

Vertically aligns the position of the selected controls, putting the bottom of each control in line with that of the bottommost control.

To Grid

Aligns the top left of the selected controls to the closest grid. The control is not resized.
