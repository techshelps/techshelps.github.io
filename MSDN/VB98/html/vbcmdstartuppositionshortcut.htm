Startup Position Commands (Form Layout Window Shortcut Menu)

   

Displays the startup position of the form over which your cursor was placed when you got the shortcut menu and allows you change the startup position of this form.

Manual

Allows you to manually move the forms around the Form Layout Window. The form has a colored bar at the top and a single border around it.

Center Owner

Places the form in the center of the monitor with another window underneath it. The additional window represents the host or owner window. Both form and host window have a colored bar at the top.

Center Screen

Places the form in the center of the monitor. This form has an extra border around it.

Windows Default

Places the form in the upper left quadrant of the monitor with an extra border around it.
