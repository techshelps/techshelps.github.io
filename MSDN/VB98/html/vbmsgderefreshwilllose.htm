Refresh will lose all user-defined information in fields or parameters associated with this Command object. Are you sure you wish to continue?

Refreshing a Command object rebuilds all field and parameter information. Any user-defined property settings are lost during the refresh. For Fields, the Control and Caption properties are reset to their defaults. For Parameters, the Name, Direction, DataType, Precision, Scale, Size, and HostDataType properties are reset to their defaults. Once the user-defined property settings are discarded, they cannot be retrieved.
