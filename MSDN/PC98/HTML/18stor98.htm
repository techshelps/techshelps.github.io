Chapter 18: Storage and Related Peripherals

This section presents the PC 98 requirements for storage and related peripherals, including DVD devices. Specific requirements for SCSI, IDE, and ATAPI peripherals are defined in the related chapters in Part 3 of this guide.

For specific information about implementation details related to storage devices under the Windows and Windows NT operating systems, see the articles at http://www.microsoft.com/hwdev/devdes/.
