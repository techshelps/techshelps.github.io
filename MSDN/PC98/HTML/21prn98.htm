Chapter 21: Printers

This chapter presents the PC 98 requirements and recommendations for printers. Printers and other devices attached to parallel ports should be capable of high-speed, bi-directional data transfers. The design criteria for parallel devices follows the design criteria for parallel ports as described in the “I/O Ports and Devices” chapter in Part 4 of this guide.

The goal of the PC 98 requirements for printers and parallel ports is to ensure the following:

  • Maximum speed for transfer of parallel data between the system and the peripheral

  • A true Plug and Play experience for users
