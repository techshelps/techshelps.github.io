Session Goal

As consultants, many of us make our living explaining to client companies how to achieve increases in productivity and effectiveness by using technology. But like the cobbler's kids, often we do not take our own advise. Consulting companies and software developers can gain a significant competitive advantage by implementing advanced technology into their business.

This session's goal is to establish information system objectives for the software development process and to provide a detailed description of how a solution was developed to reach these objectives.

What You Will Learn

  • Establish the requirements of a Software Development Information System (SDIS).

  • Understand how Microsoft® Mail can be used as an enabling technology to capture, synthesize, and disseminate information.

  • Understand how Microsoft's Workgroup Strategy can be embraced for the development of solutions.
