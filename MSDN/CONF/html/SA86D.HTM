Application Structure

The application is composed of the following macros:

  • AutoNew—initializes a new document

  • AutoOpen—determines if the creator or a contributor opened the document and shows appropriate commands/toolbars

  • CheckInbox—checks inbox for incoming drafts

  • Constants—defines constants necessary for MAPI

  • DistributeSection—sends a section to a contributor

  • InsertSection—inserts a new section into the master document

  • ReturnSection—returns a section to the master document creator
