Books

  • DNS and BIND
    • All-in-one tutorial style DNS reference for those using BIND. Excellent for beginning to intermediate DNS administrators; lacks coverage of advanced topics.

Published: October 1992, reprinted March 1993 with minor corrections.

Authors: Paul Albitz and Cricket Liu

Publisher: O'Reilly & Associates

ISBN: 1-56592-010-4

  • TCP/IP Network Administration
    • Contains a self-contained overview of DNS as just one of the standard network services in a TCP/IP environment.

Published: August 1992, reprinted January 1994 with minor corrections.

Author: Craig Hunt

Publisher: O'Reilly & Associates

ISBN: 0-937175-82-X

  • UNIX System Administration Handbook, Second Edition
    • An update to Nemeth and Snyder's classic, now features Chapter 16 dedicated to DNS. Especially good for the feature comparison of currently shipping vendor variants.

Published: 1995

Authors: Evi Nemeth, Garth Snyder, Scott Seebass, Trent R. Hein

Publisher: Prentice Hall

ISBN: 0-13-151051-7

  • TCP/IP Illustrated, Volume 1

For network and protocol issues, see Chapter 14.

Published: 1994

Author: W. Richard Stevens

Publisher: Addison-Wesley Publishing Company

ISBN: 0-201-63346-9

  • Firewalls and Internet Security: Repelling the Wily Hacker

Includes some advice on DNS in a firewall environment.

Published: 1994

Authors: William R. Cheswick and Steven M. Bellovin

Publisher: Addison-Wesley Publishing Company

ISBN: 0-201-63357-4

  • Building Internet Firewalls
    • Good all-round security book with a practical approach. Chapter 8 includes a detailed section on DNS in a firewall environment.

Published: September 1995

Authors: D. Brent Chapman and Elizabeth D. Zwicky

Publisher: O'Reilly & Associates

ISBN: 1-56592-124-0
