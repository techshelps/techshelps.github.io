Annotation

While reviewing the Briefing Book, users can press the Annotate button and capture the current page of the book. Annotations can then be placed on the captured page.

Figure 16: Briefing Book Annotation.

Annotations can include:

  • Highlighter - A highlighter can mark special areas of interest.

  • Red Arrow - Arrows can point to important information.

  • Text - Text notes can be added.

  • Red Line - Lines can be drawn around points of interest.

  • Sound - Embedded sound can be included.
