Day 2 (Tuesday, November 5)

Day 2 expands its focus to applications running on top of DCOM and DCOM security. The recommended sessions include a discussion of the new Microsoft Message Queue architecture, DCOM security, and Viper.

Two optional sessions, Developing Distributed Applications (Part 1 and Part 2), may be especially interesting for those who would like to see how easy it is to create distributed transaction applications using Viper.
