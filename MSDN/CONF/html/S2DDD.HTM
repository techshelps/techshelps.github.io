How MMC Can Work With Control Panel Applications

Today's control panel is not well-structured. Some Control Panel applets (such as Color and Schemes) are intended for the typical user, and others (such as Devices and Services) are targeted at the administrator. In future releases, the Windows team will simplify the control panel by migrating the administrative control panel applets to become MMC Snap-Ins. In the process, these management tools will also then support remote administration.
