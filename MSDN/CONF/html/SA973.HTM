One-To-Many Report

One of the most powerful new features of FoxPro 3.0 is the ability to display data from two tables which have a one-to-many relationship in a single form or report. Data from the one side is displayed in controls on the report and data from the many side is displayed in a grid. This Wizard steps users through choosing the tables on the one and on the many side and then helps users set the relationship between the report and the grid.
