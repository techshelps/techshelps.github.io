Providing Compatibility with Cisco L2F

As noted earlier, the PPP Extensions working group agreed to the PPTP Forum proposal at the June 1996 IETF meeting in Montreal. This includes converging PPTP and Cisco's Layer 2 Forwarding approach to tunneling. Microsoft and the other PPTP Forum companies are working to provide mutual compatibility between PPTP and Cisco's Layer 2 Forwarding (L2F) tunneling protocol. This is good news for users because regardless of which tunneling protocol they initially use, the other will be supported.
