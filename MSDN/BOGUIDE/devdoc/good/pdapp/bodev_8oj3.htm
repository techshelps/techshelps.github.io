Licensing the Designed for Microsoft BackOffice Logo

The BackOffice logo program provides a way to demonstrate that a product has been tested and integrated with the BackOffice family. Displaying the Designed for Microsoft BackOffice logo informs customers that your product is designed to give them extra benefits when used with BackOffice.

To carry the Designed for Microsoft BackOffice logo, a product must meet a set of technical criteria as described at http://www.microsoft.com/backoffice/designed.
