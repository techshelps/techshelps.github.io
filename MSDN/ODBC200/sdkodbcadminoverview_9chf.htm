Viewing Drivers

To configure data sources, you must have installed at least one driver on your system. The ODBC Drivers tab in the Administrator displays a list of the drivers installed on your computer, including the name, version, company, file name, and file creation date of each driver. You must use the driver’s Setup program to add or delete a driver from your system.

To view installed drivers and driver details

  1. Make sure the ODBC Administrator is running. (See “Running the ODBC Administrator.”)

  2. On the Drivers tab, a list displays the drivers installed on your system and information about the drivers. The date displayed is the creation date of the driver.
