FileAOCEReplyMail

Syntax

FileAOCEReplyMail

Remarks

Adds a mailer for replying to the active letter and specifies the sender of the original letter as the recipient. The subject text is copied from the original letter and prefixed with "Re> ". FileAOCEReplyMail is available only on the Macintosh and only if PowerTalk is installed.
