FileAOCEForwardMail

Syntax

FileAOCEForwardMail

Remarks

Adds a mailer for forwarding the active letter. The subject text is copied to the new mailer and prefixed by "Fwd> ". FileAOCEForwardMail is available only on the Macintosh and only if PowerTalk is installed.
