ToolsSpellingRecheckDocument

Syntax

ToolsSpellingRecheckDocument

Remarks

Rechecks the entire document for spelling errors, ensuring that words that have already been reviewed and corrected are checked for spelling errors again. In Word version 6.0, ToolsSpellingRecheckDocument is unavailable and generates an error.
