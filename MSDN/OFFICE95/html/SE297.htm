Microsoft Product Support Services

Microsoft offers a variety of support options to help you get the most from your Microsoft product. For more information about Microsoft Product Support Services, see Getting Results with Microsoft Excel for Windows 95 or Getting Results with Microsoft Office for Windows 95.

Outside the United States, contact Microsoft Product Support Services at the Microsoft subsidiary office that serves your area. Microsoft subsidiary offices and the countries they serve are listed in Getting Results with Microsoft Excel for Windows 95 and Getting Results with Microsoft Office for Windows 95.
