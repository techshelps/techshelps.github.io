FileAOCEReplyAllMail

Syntax

FileAOCEReplyAllMail

Remarks

Adds a mailer for replying to the active letter and specifies the sender and all recipients of the original letter as recipients. The subject text is copied from the original letter and prefixed with "Re> ". FileAOCEReplyAllMail is available only on the Macintosh and only if PowerTalk is installed.
