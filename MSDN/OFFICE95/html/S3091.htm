ClearAddIns

Syntax

ClearAddIns RemoveFromList

Remarks

Unloads all global templates and Word add-in libraries (WLLs) that appear in the list of global templates and add-ins in the Templates And Add-ins dialog box (Templates command, File menu).

Argument

Explanation

RemoveFromList

Specifies whether to remove the global templates and add-ins from the list in addition to unloading them:

0 (zero) Global templates and add-ins remain in the list.

1 Global templates and add-ins are removed from the list (except for those in the Startup folder).
