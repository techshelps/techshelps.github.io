ToolsBulletListDefault

Syntax

ToolsBulletListDefault

Remarks

Adds bullets and tab characters to the selected paragraphs and formats the paragraphs with a hanging indent. Bullets are inserted as SYMBOL fields.

Note

The ToolsBulletListDefault statement corresponds to the Bulleted List button on the Toolbar in Word for Windows version 2.x. In Word version 6.0, the Bullets button is on the Formatting toolbar and its corresponding WordBasic statement is FormatBulletDefault.
