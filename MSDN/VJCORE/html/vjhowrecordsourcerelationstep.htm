Record Source Relation Step

If you selected the Master/Detail layout in the Form step of the Data Form Wizard, the Record Source Relation step allows you to specify the one-to-many relationship between the master and detail record sources.

To specify the master/detail relationship

  1. In the Master list, select the related field. This field should uniquely identify each record in the master record source.

  2. In the Detail list, select the related field.

  3. Click Next to add additional controls to the form.
