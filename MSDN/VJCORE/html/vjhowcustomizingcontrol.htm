Customizing a Control

In addition to defining your control's members, you can define its functionality by customizing it. In this section, you can find information about:

  • Determining the Control's Display   How to manage the visual representation of your control.

  • Adding a Bitmap for a Control   How to associate a bitmap with your control to be displayed in the Toolbox.

  • Creating Control Customizers   How to create Customizer objects to add functionality such as design-time hit tests and verbs to your control.
