Summary Step

In the Summary step of the Data Form Wizard, you can save and review your wizard settings.

To save and review the wizard settings

  1. To save your settings to an existing profile, select the profile in the drop-down list. To save your settings to a new profile, click the ellipses button (...) to specify a file name. (For more information about profiles, see Introducing Wizards and Builders.)

  2. To review your settings, click View Report. To save the report, click Save in the View Report dialog box.

  3. Click Finish to add the form to your project.

The Data Form Wizard provides a quick start to creating a form with controls bound to the fields in a database. To bind data without the wizard, you can directly use the DataBinder control or the DataGrid control.
