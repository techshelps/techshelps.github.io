BOPPOPCUSTOM: Custom Bar of Pie/Pie of Pie Chart Options (1067h)

This record stores options for a custom bar of pie or pie of pie chart; these are two of the pie chart subtypes.

Record Data—BIFF8

Offset

Name

Size

Contents

4

cxi

2

Count of pie slices in the bar of pie or pie of pie chart

6

rggrbit

var

Array of bytes; each byte contains a bit field that describes the individual point positioning in the series. If a slice is on the secondary pie or bar chart, the corresponding bit is set to 1 (one); otherwise the bit is 0 (zero).
