CONTINUE: Continues Long Records (3Ch)

Records that are longer than 8228 bytes (2084 bytes in BIFF7 and earlier) must be split into several records. The first section appears in the base record; subsequent sections appear in CONTINUE records.

In BIFF8, the TXO record is always followed by CONTINUE records that store the string data and formatting runs.
