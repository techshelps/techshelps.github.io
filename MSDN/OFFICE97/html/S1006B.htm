XCT: CRN Record Count (59h)

For BIFF8, an XCT record precedes a CRN record. The XCT and CRN records are grouped with their associated SUPBOOK record.

For BIFF7 and earlier, the XCT record stores the number of CRN records (type 5Ah) in the file. The CRN records immediately follow the XCT record.
