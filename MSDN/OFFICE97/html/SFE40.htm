Constant Improvements

Writing high-quality add-ins requires constant vigilance on the part of the developer, as the state of the art continues to advance. Because add-ins are a subset of writing macros, these tips apply to all code development. You can visit the various Web sites dedicated to Microsoft Excel/Office development for additional tips and tricks. Look for examples of real-world code, since samples are often simplified to allow a specific technique or concept to be made obvious.
