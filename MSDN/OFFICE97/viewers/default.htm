  Microsoft® Office File Viewers let users share their work with people who don't have Office or specific Office applications. For example, a Word Viewer allows people who don't have Word to view and print Word documents exactly as they appear in Word. Likewise, a Microsoft PowerPoint Viewer lets people who don't have PowerPoint to see or deliver a presentation.

Office users can easily expand their audience to the online world by using Viewers as another way to deliver their work to customers, prospective clients, and teammates.
