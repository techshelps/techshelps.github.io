Section A: General

This section applies to all products.

If your product is modal, read the Modal Products section of Office Compatible 97 Testing Requirements, and verify that it complies.

If your product is a mainframe connectivity product, read the Mainframe Connectivity Products section of Office Compatible 97 Testing Requirements, and verify that it complies.

If your product is a multi-module product, read the Multi-module Products section of Office Compatible 97 Testing Requirements, and verify that it complies.

Verify that your product contains no software errors that impair the normal functionality of Office or its component applications. Read The Scope of Office Compatible 97 Testing: A Word About Bugs In Office Compatible Products section of Office Compatible 97 Testing Requirements.
