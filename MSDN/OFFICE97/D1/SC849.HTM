Application Specific Entries In The Office Compatible 97 Main Menu Bar

In addition to the standard main menu entries discussed above, most Office Compatible 97 products will include non-standardized entries, which correspond to the product's distinctive functionality. These application specific items must be located between Tools and Window on the main menu bar. If Tools or Window are not supported, then the application-specific entries must be located to the left of Help and to the right of all other standard Office Compatible 97 main menu entries.
