General Appearance and Function of the Office Compatible 97 Toolbar Configuration Dialog Box

The Toolbar Configuration dialog box should be substantially similar to the design shown in Figure 6. Minor differences in size, location, or color will not affect testing. Other deviations from this design will not affect testing if they are driven by a consideration of the product's functionality, but arbitrary design changes are not acceptable. In any case, developer's should obtain Microsoft's approval of their design changes before submitting their product for testing.
