Standard Toolbar

Verify that your product's standard toolbar conforms to the requirements listed in Table 2 of Office Compatible 97 Testing Requirements. Read Office Compatible 97 Standard Toolbar for more information. In particular, verify that your toolbar meets the requirements for the following attributes:

  1. Location, Size, & Appearance per Office Compatible 97 standards
  2. Small-format buttons supported by default
  3. Large-format buttons optionally available
  4. ToolTips
  5. Toolbar Configuration Dialog Box per Office Compatible 97 standard
  6. New, Open, Save, Print, Cut, Copy, Paste, & Help buttons
  7. Location of application specific buttons
  8. Location of toolbar button separators
  9. Proper support for MIS toolbar buttons designated by MIS and MDA in Table 2 of Office Compatible 97 Testing Requirements.
