Accessing the Office Compatible 97 Help Topic

Office Compatible 97 products may list the Office Compatible 97 Help topic in the application specific region of their Help dropdown menu. Alternately, a link to the topic should be displayed if the customer searches for "Microsoft" "Office" or "Office Compatible" (or if they look for these keywords within the product's Help index). In addition, Office Compatible vendors are strongly encouraged to include a "Microsoft Office Compatible 97" entry within their Help contents, but this is not mandatory.
