Section E: Host-Required Applications

Verify that your product complies with the Windows guidelines described in the Office Compatible 97 Testing Requirements For Host-Required Products section of Office Compatible 97 Testing Requirements.

If your product inserts buttons on Office's standard toolbar, verify that it complies with item 2 in the Office Compatible 97 Testing Requirements For Host-Required Products section of Office Compatible 97 Testing Requirements.

If your product inserts entries on Office's main menu bar, verify that it complies with item 3 in the Office Compatible 97 Testing Requirements For Host-Required Products section of Office Compatible 97 Testing Requirements.

If your product inserts entries on Office's dropdown menus, verify that it complies with item 4 in the Office Compatible 97 Testing Requirements For Host-Required Products section of Office Compatible 97 Testing Requirements.

If your product replaces the standard Office toolbar or any of its buttons, verify that it complies with item (5) in the Office Compatible 97 Testing Requirements For Host-Required Products section of Office Compatible 97 Testing Requirements.

Verify that your product does not unnecessarily or disruptively alter the Office user interface, per item (7) in the Office Compatible 97 Testing Requirements For Host-Required Products section of Office Compatible 97 Testing Requirements.

If your product can be run in "full window" mode, verify that it complies with item (9) in the Office Compatible 97 Testing Requirements For Host-Required Products section of Office Compatible 97 Testing Requirements.

If your product is a template, verify that it complies with item (10) in the Office Compatible 97 Testing Requirements For Host-Required Products section of Office Compatible 97 Testing Requirements.

If your product is a host-required content product, verify that it complies with item (11) of the Office Compatible 97 Testing Requirements For Host-Required Products section of Office Compatible 97 Testing Requirements.
