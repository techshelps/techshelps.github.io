Sound (2022)

Sound consists of:

PST_CString (instance 0): name of sound (e.g. "crash")

PST_CString (instance 1): type of sound (e.g. ".wav")

PST_CString (instance 2): reference id of sound in sound collection

PST_CString (instance 3): built-in id of sound, for sounds we ship. This is the id that's in the reg file.
