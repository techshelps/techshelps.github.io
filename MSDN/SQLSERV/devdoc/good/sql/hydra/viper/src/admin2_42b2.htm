Transactions Shown

Use the Transactions Shown slider to control how long a transaction must be active before being displayed on the graphical interface. These are the values on the slider:

  • Show 5 minute old transactions. (Shown as "Very Old" on the slider.)
  • Show 1 minute old transactions.
  • Show 30 second old transactions (default).
  • Show 10 second old transactions.
  • Show 1 second old transactions. (Shown as "New + Old" on the slider.)
