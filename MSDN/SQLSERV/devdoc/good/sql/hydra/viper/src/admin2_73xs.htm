The Aggregate Group

For aggregate values, refer to these statistics:

  • Committed¾The cumulative total of committed transactions. This number does not include forced (manually resolved) commits.
  • Aborted¾The cumulative total of aborted transactions. This number does not include forced (manually resolved) aborts.
  • Forced Commit¾The cumulative total of committed transactions that were manually committed.
  • Forced Abort¾The cumulative total of aborted transactions that were manually aborted.
  • Total¾The cumulative total of all transactions.
