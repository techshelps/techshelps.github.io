Publishing

Data is published by creating a publication, selecting the tables to be included in the publication, and making the publication available to subscription servers. A table included in a publication is called an article. An article can be the entire table, or a subset of columns and/or rows from the table.

The topics that follow explain how to create and modify publications and articles.
