Integrating with Setup Computer Wizard

[This is preliminary documentation and subject to change.]

You can simplify the details of installing your application on Microsoft® Small Business Server by integrating your Setup program with the Setup Computer Wizard (SCW). By integrating with SCW, you can have your application available for deployment and installation on client computers throughout the Small Business Server Network.
