IPropertyPageImpl::GetPageInfo

This method fills the pPageInfo structure with information contained in the data members. GetPageInfo loads the string resources associated with m_dwDocString, m_dwHelpFile, and m_dwTitle.

Syntax

HRESULT GetPageInfo( PROPPAGEINFO* pPageInfo );
