IgnoreChange ControlEvent

[This is preliminary documentation and subject to change.]

The IgnoreChange control event is published by the DirectoryList control when a folder is highlighted but not opened in the current directory.

Published By

DirectoryList

Argument

None.

Action on Subscribers

None.

Action by Publisher When Subscriber Activated

None.

Typical Use

Typically the DirectoryCombo control subscribes to the IgnoreChange control event.
