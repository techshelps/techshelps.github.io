Changing a NetBEUI Binding

You can manually change the NetBEUI bindings. The changes you can make are:

  • Enable a binding
  • Disable a binding
  • Move the order (priority) of the bindings up or down

To change a NetBEUI binding

1. Click Start, point to Settings, and click Control Panel.

2. Double-click Network.

3. Click the Bindings tab.

4. In Show Bindings for, click all protocols.

5. Double-click NetBEUI Protocol to display a list of bindings.

6. Select the binding you want to change and then click the appropriate action button.

7. Click OK.
