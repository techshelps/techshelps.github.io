XFORMOBJ

The XFORMOBJ structure describes an arbitrary linear two-dimensional transform, such as for geometric wide lines. The transform can be downloaded to the driver. Functions are also provided to apply the transform to driver-supplied data.

There are no public members in the XFORMOBJ structure.
