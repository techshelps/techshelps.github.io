3.1.3 Filling In NDIS_MINIPORT_CHARACTERISTICS

Since some of the handlers passed in NDIS_MINIPORT_CHARACTERISTICS are optional this section describes the criteria for choosing when to register such an optional MiniportXxx function. The optional MiniportXxx functions are:

  • MiniportISR

  • MiniportEnableInterrupt and MiniportDisableInterrupt

  • MiniportCheckForHang

  • MiniportSend versus MiniportSendPackets

  • MiniportTransferData versus MiniportReturnPacket

  • MiniportAllocateComplete
