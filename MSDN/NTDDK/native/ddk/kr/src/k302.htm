Chapter 2 SCSI Miniport Driver Routines

All SCSI miniport drivers must have some of the routines described in this chapter. Whether any particular miniport driver has others depends on the nature of its HBA(s) or on that miniport’s designer.

This chapter describes miniport routines in alphabetical order.

A miniport’s HwScsiXxx routines can have any name chosen by the driver writer. DriverEntry is a required name.
