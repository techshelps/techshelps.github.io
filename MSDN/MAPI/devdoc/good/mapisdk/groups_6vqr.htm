Display Table Structures

The following structures are used by clients and service providers to describe tabbed pages, list boxes or labels and a variety of dialog boxes along with controls, check boxes or buttons to be used in dialog boxes:

DTBLBUTTON
DTBLCHECKBOX
DTBLCOMBOBOX
DTBLDDLBX
DTBLEDIT
DTBLGROUPBOX
DTBLLABEL
DTBLLBX
DTBLMVDDLBOX
DTBLMVLISTBOX
DTBLPAGE
DTBLRADIOBUTTON
DTCTL
DTPAGE
