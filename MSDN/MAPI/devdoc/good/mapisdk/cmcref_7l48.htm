CMC_X_MS_ATTACH

CMC_X_MS_ATTACH data structures are used by the CMC_X_MS_ATTACH_DATA and CMC_X_MS_ATTACH_MESSAGE extensions to support message attachments.

Quick Info

Header file: XCMCMSX2.H
