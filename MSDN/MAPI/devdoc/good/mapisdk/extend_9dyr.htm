Recipient Properties for Delivery Status Reports

The following properties are present for delivery status reports for recipients. PR_DELIVER_TIME is not used on nondelivery reports. PR_NDR_DIAG_CODE and PR_NDR_REASON_CODE are used only on nondelivery reports.

PR_DELIVER_TIME

PR_DISPLAY_NAME

PR_ENTRYID

PR_NDR_DIAG_CODE

PR_MESSAGE_CLASS

PR_NDR_REASON_CODE

PR_ORIGINAL_DISPLAY_NAME

PR_ORIGINAL_ENTRYID

PR_ORIGINAL_SEARCH_KEY

PR_RECIPIENT_TYPE

PR_REPORT_TEXT

PR_REPORT_TIME

PR_SEARCH_KEY

PR_DISPLAY_TYPE

PR_OBJECT_TYPE
