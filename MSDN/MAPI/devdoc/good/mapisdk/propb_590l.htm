PR_TITLE

The PR_TITLE property contains the recipient's job title.

Quick Info

Header file: MAPITAGS.H
May be exposed by: Messaging user objects
Identifier: 0x3A17
Property type: PT_TSTRING
Property tag: 0x3A17001E (0x3A17001F for Unicode)

Remarks

The PR_TITLE property is one of the properties that provide identification and access information for a recipient. These properties are defined by the recipient and the recipient's organization. For more information on this group of properties, see IMailUser::IMAPIProp.

PR_TITLE is commonly used to indicate the recipient's formal job title, such as Senior Programmer, rather than occupational class, such as programmer. It is not typically used for "suffix" titles such as Esq. or DDS.

Common PR_TITLE values include: Managing Director, Programmer II, Associate Professor, and Development Lead.
