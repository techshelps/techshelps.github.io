Notification Functions

The following functions deal with event notification:

HrAllocAdviseSink
HrDispatchNotifications
HrThisThreadAdviseSink
NOTIFCALLBACK prototype
ScCopyNotifications
ScCountNotifications
ScRelocNotifications

Note Except for HrDispatchNotifications and HrThisThreadAdviseSink, these functions may not be supported in future versions of MAPI.
