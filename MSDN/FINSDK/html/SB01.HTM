Introduction

Our intention with this document and the contents of the companion disk is to provide you with tools and information that will help in the development, testing and certification of your OFC server. We are providing a set of tests in that will allow us to certify your OFC server.

The certification tests that we will provide will not encompass the idiosyncratic behavior of your OFC server and customers. You will be required to test this behavior and help to ensure the goals of developing an OFC server.
