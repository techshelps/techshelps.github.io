Introduction

Financial Institutions choosing to offer Online Services with MS Money may take part in a unique branding program offered by Microsoft. This program offers partner financial institutions a flexible and dynamic way to maintain and enhance their financial services, while building a strong identity with their online customers. This program allows the institution to incorporate graphics as well as other information—such as a welcome message or important phone numbers specific to your institution—into the customer's customized view of Microsoft Money.

The Branding Toolkit has been provided with the OFC SDK so that developers and product managers may have access to this information during the server development process.
