Manipulating Relationship Collections

You cannot add or delete a relationship collection after it is created. A relationship collection might consist of zero relationships, but the collection still exists. If you retrieve such a relationship collection, the Repository returns an interface pointer to a relationship collection, just as it would return for any other relationship collection.
