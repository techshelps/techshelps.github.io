Video Control

[This is preliminary documentation and subject to change.]

In Broadcast Architecture, you can use the Microsoft® ActiveX® control for video (the Video control, Vid.ocx) to manage and display multimedia streams in Web pages and applications. Although the Video control can also be used to display video in enhancement pages, it is usually more convenient to use the Enhancement control. For more information, see Which Video Control Should I Use?
