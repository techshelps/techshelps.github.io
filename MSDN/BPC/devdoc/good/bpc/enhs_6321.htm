Additional Stream Authoring Functionality

[This is preliminary documentation and subject to change.]

This topic discusses additional functionality that you can add to your stream authoring tool to make it easier to use and more robust. While the features described below are not essential to a stream authoring tool, they add considerable value.

  • Merging and Including Streams
  • Custom Comments and Syntax
  • Graphical Time Line
  • Indication of Bandwidth Utilization
  • Wizards
  • Templates
