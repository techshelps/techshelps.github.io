Enhancement Stream Language Reference

[This is preliminary documentation and subject to change.] 

The reference information of the Enhancement Stream language is divided into the following three sections. For more information, click on the topic that is of interest to you.

Syntax Conventions.   This section explains the lexical rules that you must follow to write valid Enhancement Stream language statements.

Reserved Variables.   The reserved variables define a number of the characteristics of the show, such as the name, the title, the bandwidth you are broadcasting on, and the IP (Internet Protocol) addresses you will use for the broadcast—to name just a few.

Event Statements.   These statements direct the activity of the assets and enhancements. They instruct announcements to be sent, direct files to be broadcast at specific times, and then summon those files to display at the appropriate place on the screen, at the appropriate time.
