Keywords Syntax

[This is preliminary documentation and subject to change.] 

In addition to the basic syntax of event statements, the behavior of many events can be further customized through the use of keywords.

For more information, click on the keyword of choice in the following table:

Before.   Indicates that the event in which the keyword appears should be completed before the time indicated.

Now.   Indicates that the event in which the keyword appears should take place immediately.

Only.   Indicates that any assets or enhancements named in the event in which the keyword appears should be handled alone; their associated dependencies should not also be acted upon by the event.

Repeat.   Indicates that the event in which the keyword appears should be repeated at the interval indicated or the number of times specified.
