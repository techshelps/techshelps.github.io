Microsoft TechNet

Microsoft TechNet is the comprehensive worldwide information service designed for those who support or educate users, administer networks or databases, create automated solutions, and recommend or evaluate information-technology solutions. For an annual TechNet subscription fee, subscribers receive two compact discs (CDs) per month containing Microsoft Knowledge Base, Resource Kits, up-to-date drivers, and other information. TechNet also maintains a CompuServe forum (go technet) for up-to-the-minute news. To subscribe in the United States and Canada, using your credit card, call (800) 344-2121, weekdays, between 6:30 a.m. and 5:30 p.m. Pacific time. For international orders, call (303) 684-0914 (in the United States) for contacts in your area.
