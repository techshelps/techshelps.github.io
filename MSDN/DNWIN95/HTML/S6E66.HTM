Part 1, Corporate Planning Guide.

Provides an overview for MIS managers and technical support personnel of the process for testing and deploying Windows 95 on the corporate network, plus details about how to make decisions and how to plan, test, and prepare for a major rollout of Windows 95.

Note

Be sure to read Chapter 1, "Deployment Planning Basics" and Chapter 2, "Deployment Strategy and Details" before attempting to deploy Windows 95 in your organization. These chapters provide essential information for developing, testing, and carrying out a deployment plan that will result in a successful company-wide installation of Windows 95.
