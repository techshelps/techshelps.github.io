General

[This is preliminary documentation and subject to change.]

These are guidelines that relate to providing consistent, up-to-date support for Microsoft® Windows® in general:

  • Meet all requirements on the latest releases of Windows NT and Windows 95. (Required)
  • Support multiple platforms. (Required)
  • Make your application stable and fully functional. (Required)
  • Make your application 32-bit. (Required)
    • Identify 16-bit legacy versions clearly. (Required)
  • Support multiTasking. (Required)
  • Support standard system settings. (Required)
    • Support additional standard system settings. (Recommended)
    • Use standard system settings for multi-monitor support. (Best Practice)
  • Support Windows Sockets 2.0. (Recommended)
  • Detect and handle slow or dead network links. (Best Practice)
