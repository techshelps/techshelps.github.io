Pretest the Install Process (Required)

[This is preliminary documentation and subject to change.]

Before submitting your product to VeriTest for testing, you are required to pretest its installation procedure.

To pretest your product's installation, use the Installation Analyzer found at: http://www.veritest.com/installation_analyzer.htm.

To save yourself from having to retest, it is also a good idea to perform all relevant pretests listed in Pretesting.
