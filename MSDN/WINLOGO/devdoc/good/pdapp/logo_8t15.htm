Bundled Applications Are Tested Individually

[This is preliminary documentation and subject to change.]

Requirement   (Designed for Microsoft® Windows® Logo)

For a bundled package of applications (commonly known as a suite) to use the Logo, each individual product in that suite must be tested and pass the Logo requirements. If you want the Logo to go on a product (from the suite) that is available separately, that product must be retested for compliance with Logo installation and uninstallation requirements only.
