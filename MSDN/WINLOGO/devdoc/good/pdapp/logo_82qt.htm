Add-On Products Included in a Suite Are Tested Both Individually and With the Suite

[This is preliminary documentation and subject to change.]

Requirement   (Designed for Microsoft® Windows® Logo)

If separately marketed add-on products are also included in a suite of applications, they must be tested both separately and with the suite for the add-on product to qualify for the Designed for Microsoft Windows Logo.
