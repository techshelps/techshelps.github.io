Take Advantage of COM+ Run-Time Interfaces

[This is preliminary documentation and subject to change.]

Best Practice   (Designed for Microsoft® Windows® Logo)

Your application supporting COM+ objects does not have to implement any of the following interfaces supplied by the COM+ run time:

  • IUnkown
  • IDispatch
  • IProvideClassInfo
  • IProvideErrorInfo
  • IClassFactory
  • IConnectionPointContainer
  • IConnectionPoint

See Also
