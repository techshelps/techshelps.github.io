Be Easy to Install and Remove

[This is preliminary documentation and subject to change.]

Installation is one of the most important and sensitive areas for a user or administrator. First impressions are lasting, and a trouble-free installation is the best kind of first impression to make.

Also, a user or administrator needing to remove an application expects to be provided an easy, automated way to do so.

The guidelines in this section apply to two areas:

  • Installing and removing entire applications.
  • Installing and removing individual components.
