Testing

[This is preliminary documentation and subject to change.]

Software applications are tested by an independent laboratory, VeriTest, Inc. For a current fee schedule and more information, see the VeriTest Web site at: http://www.veritest.com/microsoft.htm. You can also send e-mail, to logolab@veritest.com.

Note that all testing fees are to be paid to VeriTest, not to Microsoft.

When you submit your product to VeriTest, you must include all of the following:

  • Your software product (on floppy disk or CD-ROM).
  • The results of a pretest you have run on your product (see Pretesting).
  • A signed paper copy of the VeriTest Testing Agreement (see Paperwork).
  • A signed paper copy of the Windows NT® and Windows® 95 Logo License Agreement (see Paperwork).

We recommend that you write the tracking number that you received from VeriTest, after submitting the online Vendor Questionnaire, on the items you submit (see the section called Paperwork).
