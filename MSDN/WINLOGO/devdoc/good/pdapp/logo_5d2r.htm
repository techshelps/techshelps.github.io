Locate COM Objects in the Class Store Transparently in COM and DCOM Clients

[This is preliminary documentation and subject to change.]

Recommendation   (Designed for Microsoft® Windows® Logo)

COM and DCOM clients should look up COM objects in the class store. This process requires no special action on the part of the client.
