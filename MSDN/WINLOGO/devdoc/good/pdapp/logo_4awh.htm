Cooperate with Administrators

[This is preliminary documentation and subject to change.]

As more and more software is used in large networked enterprise settings, it is increasingly important to make your application easy to manage. Designed for Microsoft® Windows® Logo requirements that relate to making your application manageable are listed below.

This list is just a summary. To qualify for the Designed for Windows Logo, you must comply with all stipulations of the requirement reference text to which the list refers.

  • Enable policy settings to uninstall and unadvertise your application.
  • Provide an .ADM system policy file for your application.
  • Provide for disabling Run and Find dialogs.
  • Support NoViewContextMenu in shell extensions.
  • Call ShellExecute instead of CreateProcess.
