Do Not Write InprocServer32 Entries for Components Directly to HKCR

[This is preliminary documentation and subject to change.]

Best Practice   (Designed for Microsoft® Windows® Logo)

Components should not write entries directly to the registry for HKEY_CLASSES_ROOT under their class ID (CLSID) for the InprocServer32 entry.
