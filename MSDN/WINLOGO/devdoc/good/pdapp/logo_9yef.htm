Pretesting

[This is preliminary documentation and subject to change.]

To receive the Designed for Microsoft® Windows® Logo, you are required to pretest your product's installation process, as described in Pretest the Install Process.

To ensure the best results and avoid retests, consider pretesting your product for compliance with other logo requirements as well. The pretesting techniques suggested in the following sections are designed to help your product pass on its first submission, to save you time and money.

It may also be helpful to check the Top Ten Reasons Why Applications Fail Logo Testing on the First Try at http://www.veritest.com/nt95top10.htm.

  • Pretesting for stability on Windows 95
  • Pretesting for stability on Windows NT
  • Pretesting high-contrast support
  • Pretesting exposure of keyboard focus
  • Pretesting UNC and LFN support
  • Pretesting ACPI/OnNow support
