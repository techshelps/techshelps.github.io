ADS_SEARCHPREF_INFO

The ADS_SEARCHPREF_INFO structure specifies the query preferences.

    ADSVALUE vValue;
    ADS_STATUS dwStatus;
} ADS_SEARCHPREF_INFO, *PADS_SEARCHPREF_INFO, *LPADS_SEARCHPREF_INFO;
