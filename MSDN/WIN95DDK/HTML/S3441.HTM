Sys_Critical_Exit2 


include vmm.inc

mov     eax, Sys_Critical_Exit2
VMMcall System_Control

Similar to Sys_Critical_Exit, except that Sys_Critical_Exit2 is sent only to virtual devices marked version 4.0 and it is sent in reverse device initialization order. Sys_Critical_Exit2 is sent immediately after Sys_Critical_Exit.

See also Sys_Critical_Exit, Sys_Critical_Init, Sys_VM_Terminate
