Instant Support for Some IR Hardware in Individual VxDs

The following devices are supported by the IR communications driver binaries supplied in this DDK:

  • ACTiSYS ACT-200L Infrared Wireless Interface (ACT200L.VXD)
  • ACTiSYS ACT-220L Infrared Wireless Interface (ACT220L.VXD)
  • Adaptec(tm) AIRport APA-9320 External Infrared Adapter; this adapter is also called the Adaptec AIRport 2000 (ADAPTEC.VXD)
  • Adaptec AIRport 1000 (ADAPTEC.VXD)
  • AMP PhasIR Serial Adapter (CRYSTAL.VXD)
  • Extended Systems JetEye PC Infrared PC Interface, ESI-9680 (ESI.VXD)
  • Parallax IR Adapter LiteLink PRA9500A (PARALLAX.VXD)

For any limitations of the adapters listed above, see Troubleshooting.

Microsoft has tested the Infrared Communications driver binaries that are in the DDK with the following laptop computers that have built-in IR devices:

  • Digital® HiNote Ultra CT475
  • Gateway® 2000 Liberty
  • Gateway Solo
  • HP® Omnibook™ 600CT
  • HP Omnibook 4000C
  • IBM® ThinkPad® 701C (Butterfly)
  • IBM ThinkPad 755 (most configurations)
  • Midwest Micro Elite
  • Midwest Micro Elite p90
  • Sharp® PC 3050
  • TI® TravelMate™ 5000
  • Toshiba® Satellite Pro 400 CDT

For any limitations of the laptop computers listed above, see Troubleshooting.

The IR communications driver binaries have also been tested with the IrDA-compatible HP 5P printer.
