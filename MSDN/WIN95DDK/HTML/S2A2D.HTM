XformID

Used in the serial wave device INF only. Specifies the serial wave format used.

Use one DWORD for this value. For example, 


HKR,Config,XformID,     1, 01, 00, 00, 00

The valid values, and their meaning, are listed in the following table.

XformID

Format

1

4 bit Rockwell ADPCM 7200Hz

2

4 bit IMA ADPCM 4800 Hz

3

4 bit IMA ADPCM 7200 Hz

4

4 bit IMA ADPCM 8000 Hz
