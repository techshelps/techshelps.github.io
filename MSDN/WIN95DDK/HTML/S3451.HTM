VM_Suspend2 


include vmm.inc

mov     ebx, VMHandle
mov     eax, VM_Suspend2
VMMcall System_Control

Similar to VM_Suspend, except that VM_Suspend2 is sent only to virtual devices marked version 4.0 and it is sent in reverse device initialization order. VM_Suspend2 is sent immediately after VM_Suspend.

See also VM_Resume, VM_Suspend
