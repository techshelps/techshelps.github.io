How to Get a Virtual Device Driver Identifier Number

To get a unique VxD identifier, you need to fill out the form below and return it to Microsoft. The form can be sent to the Internet address: vxdid@microsoft.com.

Another way to get an identifier is to fax this information to Windows DDK Product Support, at 1-206-93MS-FAX or mail the information to:

Windows DDK Product Support Services

Microsoft Corporation

One Microsoft Way

Redmond, Washington 98052-6399


(E-mail is preferred and will probably provide faster turnaround time.)
