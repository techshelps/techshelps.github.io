IOS_Requestor_Service 


VxDCall IOS_Requestor_Service, <<OFFSET32 IRS>>

Carries out requested IOS services, such as retrieving information about the devices. If the service is successful, the IOS sets the IRS_result member of the IRS structure to indicate success.

  • No return value.

IRS

Address of an IRS structure that contains the information about the IOS service to carry out.

See also IRS
