Troubleshooting Jobs

As described in "Monitoring Jobs" earlier in this chapter, you monitor jobs by checking the job status and status details displayed in the Jobs window, the Jobs Status dialog box, and the Jobs Status Details dialog box. If you observe a problem with a job, you can troubleshoot the job by checking the following:

  • Events in the Events window and in the Windows NT event log.
  • Status of the files that are created, modified, copied, and deleted during the job process.
  • Trace logs for the services that manage the job process.
