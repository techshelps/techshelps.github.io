ISIDIS.EXE: IDIS_NT Checker

ISIDIS checks whether the IDIS video driver, IDIS_NT.DLL, is installed on Windows NT 3.51 for remote control. IDIS_NT.DLL enhances video performance for Windows NT 3.51 remote control.
