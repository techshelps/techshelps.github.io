About the Resource Guide

This guide includes the following chapters.

Chapter 1, "Deploying Software with SMS," Describes how to use SMS to deploy Windows NT 4.0 and Office 97 throughout your organization.

Chapter 2, "Working with Systems Management Server," Describes a variety of tips for working with SMS. Includes information on unattended installation of SMS clients, interpreting SNMP traps, and SMS registry entries. Also includes information on the new PCM service for SMS 1.2 clients.

Chapter 3, "Troubleshooting Remote Control," Provides many useful tips for working with SMS Remote Control.

Chapter 4, "Tools," Describes tools included with this BackOffice Resource Kit.
