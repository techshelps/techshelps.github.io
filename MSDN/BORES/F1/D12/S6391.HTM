Creating Simple Applications Without Programming

Programming creates a major obstacle to developing many custom solutions. Although programming is required for more complex applications, it hinders users who are willing to build simpler systems but lack programming skills.

If users could build their own solutions without writing programs, IS departments would have more time to focus their resources on building organization-wide systems. Users at the departmental and group levels wouldn't have to wait as long to get what they need. At the same time, programmers would benefit from a development environment that can support advanced and highly specialized solutions designed to solve more demanding problems.
