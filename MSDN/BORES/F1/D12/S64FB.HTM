6.0 Directory Structure of the Microsoft BackOffice Resource Kit: Part Two CD-ROM

The following is the directory structure of the Microsoft BackOffice Resource Kit: Part Two CD-ROM. The subdirectory names on the CD-ROM correspond to the categories you can choose from during the Setup process. When installed, the files for the tools that you selected during Setup will be installed on your hard disk under the directory you specified during installation or one of its subdirectories.
