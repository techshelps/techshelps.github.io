Installing Buddy Dropper

To install Buddy Dropper

From the Exchange\<operating system\platform>\Admin\Dropper subdirectory on the Microsoft BackOffice Resource Kit, Part Two compact disc, copy Dropper.exe and Droprm.exe to your computer.

Copy all dynamic-link libraries (DLLs) to %systemroot%\system32 for Windows NT, or to %systemroot%\system for Windows 95.
