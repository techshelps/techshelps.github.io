Implementing Multi.xls

You can use the macro as is, or you can expand the code to suit your needs.

To run Multi.xls

On the Tools menu in Excel 97, click Macro, and then click Macros.

On the Macros in drop-down list box, select Multi.xls, and then click Run.

To edit Multi.xls

On the Tools menu in Excel 97, click Macro, and then click Macros.

On the Macros in drop-down list box, select Multi.xls, and then click Edit.
