Publishing Data on the Internet

Integrating Microsoft SQL Server with your Internet site or intranet ensures an active, robust Web presence.

Thousands of companies are discovering the potential of internal corporate networks as logical and powerful extensions of their external publication platforms. Microsoft SQL Server enables organizations of all sizes to employ the power of this technology to the greatest possible advantage. By integrating SQL Server with your Internet or intranet site, you can build an active, robust Web presence by publishing real-time information, providing for interaction and customization, and conducting business securely and reliably—all with confidence in the integrity of your data. This article discusses:

  • Databases on the Web.
  • SQL Server and the active Internet.
  • Optimizing SQL Server for the active Internet.

The Internet has introduced new technologies associated with global communications and wide-area public networks. Microsoft's strategy is to work openly with customers, other companies, and industry groups to realize the full potential of the Internet. The next generation of powerful Internet applications promises to make the Internet more exciting and useful by seamlessly integrating audio, video, three-dimensional animation, and more.
