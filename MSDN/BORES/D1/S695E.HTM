Entering Extended Characters with the U.S. Keyboard

Turn on Num Lock and use the number pad on the right side of the keyboard.

To enter ANSI extended characters

  • Press ALT+0, followed by the ASCII code.

To enter OEM extended characters

  • Press ALT followed by the ASCII code.

    For example, to enter a mu sign (m), press ALT and then 0181 for the ANSI 1252 extended character, or press ALT and then 230 for the OEM 437 extended character.
