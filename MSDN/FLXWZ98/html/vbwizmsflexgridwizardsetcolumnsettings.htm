Data Form Wizard—Set Column Settings

Allows you to set the order and widths of the columns of your MSHFlexGrid. To change column order, drag and drop the columns on the lower half of the wizard. To change column width, drag the column borders.

Help

Opens the Help topic for this step. You can also press F1 for help.

Cancel

Cancels your previous actions and closes the Data Form Wizard.

Back

Moves you to the previous step.

Next

Moves you to the next step.

Finish

Accepts any selections you entered, then creates the MSHFlexGrid form.
