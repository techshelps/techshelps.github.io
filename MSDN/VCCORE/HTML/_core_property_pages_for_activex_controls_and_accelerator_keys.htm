Property Pages for ActiveX Controls and Accelerator Keys

Home |  Overview |  How Do I

Property pages control the appearance and the behavior of resources and differ according to their purpose. Use the following information as a guideline for the property pages you will encounter for ActiveX controls and for accelerator keys.

ActiveX Controls

ActiveX controls supplied by independent vendors may come equipped with their own property pages and characteristics. ActiveX controls always have General and All pages, plus whatever the vendor has attached to a particular control.

Accelerator Keys

Accelerator keys have many legal entries in the key box on the property page. For more information, see Setting Accelerator Properties.
