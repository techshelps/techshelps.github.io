The MFC AppWizard (DLL version): Step-by-Step

Home |  Overview |  How Do I |  Details |  Sample

The MFC AppWizard (DLL version) series has only one step. You can also move backward to change your project options.

Help is available for every option in the MFC AppWizard (DLL version). Right-click on the control for more information about each option.

To complete Step 1

  1. Choose what type of DLL you would like to create.

  2. Choose the Automation and/or Windows Sockets features you would like to include in your DLL.

  3. Choose Yes if you would like AppWizard to generate comments in the source file that will guide you in writing your program.

  4. Click Finish and then click here to prepare your MFC program for use.
