Content Formats

The Media Player control acts as a universal player for rendering most of today's standard multimedia formats. This includes stored and live content in network streaming formats, as well as non-streaming formats. Network streaming formats supported by the player include:

  • Advanced Streaming Format (ASF).
  • Video On Demand (VOD).
  • Motion Picture Experts Group standard one, two, and three (MPEG-1, MPEG-2, and MPEG-3)

In addition to the standard formats listed above, the player supports playback of RealAudio (RA) and RealVideo (RV) content as well.

Non-streaming (local playback) formats supported by the control include all of the network streaming formats previously listed and the following:

  • Audio-Video Interleaved (AVI)
  • Quicktime (MOV)
  • Musical Instrument Digital Interface (MIDI)
  • Indeo 5
  • Waveform Audio (WAV)
  • Sound File (SND)
  • UNIX audio (AU)
