Undo Check Out

See Also

To undo a checkout:

  1. Click the item(s) you want in VSS Explorer.

  2. On the SourceSafe menu, click Undo Check Out to display the Undo Check Out dialog box.

  3. Click OK.

Warning   If you choose the Undo Check Out command, you will lose any changes you have made to the local copy of the file or project.
