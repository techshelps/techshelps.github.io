How to Change Your Password

To change your password:

  1. From the Tools menu, click Change Password to display the Password dialog box.

  2. In the Old Password box, type your current password.

  3. In the New Password box, type your new password.

    Note   A password can consist of any combination of 1 to 15 valid characters. As you type, the characters are displayed as asterisks. Microsoft VSS passwords are not case-sensitive.

  4. In the Verify box, retype your new password to confirm the change.

  5. Click OK.
