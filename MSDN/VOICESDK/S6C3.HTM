1.1 VoiceView Protocol

Radish Communications Systems, Inc. of Boulder, Colorado licenses the VoiceView protocol to hardware manufacturers for use in their communications equipment. Most modem chipset and hardware manufacturers have licensed this protocol for inclusion in their product. Software developers create applications that use VoiceView-enabled hardware, and they need not license the protocol. The features of VoiceView-enabled hardware and VoiceView-aware software operate to achieve specific communications goals.
