I can't find the Design Master.

The Design Master might have been moved from its original location or might be damaged. To find the new location:

  1. Open a replica in the replica set.

  2. On the Tools menu, point to Replication, and then click Synchronize Now.

  3. Click Browse to locate other members of the replica set on your computer or network.

  4. Select a member of the replica set. If the Make 'Filename' The Design Master check box appears, the member you are synchronizing with is the Design Master.
