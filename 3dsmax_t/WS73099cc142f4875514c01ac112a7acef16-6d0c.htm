Adjusting the Biped to the Model
 
 
 

This tutorial shows you how to use the Physique modifier to associate a biped with a mesh and fine-tune the association.

In this tutorial, you will learn how to:

  • Customize the biped structure.
  • Pose the biped to fit the mesh.
  • Adjust vertex assignments with envelope parameters.
  • Use control points to fine-tune the shapes and sizes of envelopes.
  • Adjust envelopes in shoulder and hip areas for the best deformation.

Skill level: Beginner

Time to complete: 2.5 hours

Files for This Tutorial

All the files necessary for this tutorial are provided on the program disc in the \tutorials\character_animation\physique_basics directory. Before starting the tutorials, copy the \tutorials folder from the disc to your local program installation.
