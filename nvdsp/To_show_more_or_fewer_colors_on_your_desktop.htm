To show more or fewer colors on your desktop

  1. From the NVIDIA Control Panel navigation tree pane, under Display, click Change resolution to open the Change Resolution page.

  2. If applicable, select the display for which you want to change the resolution.

  3. If multiple choices are available, click the Connector list arrow and then select the connector type for your display.

  4. Click the Color depth list arrow and then select the color depth you want to set on your desktop.

  5. Click Apply when done.

Note:  Increasing color quality lets you view more photo-realistic images and is recommended for most desktop publishing and graphics illustration applications.
