Connecting a Single 5K Monitor - Quadro K Series

 

These connection instructions apply to Quadro K series workstation solutions with 2 DisplayPort 1.2 connectors.

Select your application:

  • Single Quadro Card

  • Multiple Quadro Cards with SLI Enabled

  • Multiple Quadro Cards with SLI Disabled or Not Supported

 

 

 

Single Quadro Card

Connect DP1 and DP2 from the 5K monitor to two DisplayPort connectors on the Quadro card.

NOTE: Images are for illustrative purposes, and may not match your particular card.

 

Configuration

Notes

Single GPU

You can configure the 5K monitor with a non-5K monitor in extended or clone mode.

Multiple Quadro Cards with SLI Enabled

Connect DP1 and DP2 from the 5K monitor to the two DisplayPort connectors on the master SLI GPU.

NOTE: Images are for illustrative purposes, and may not match your particular card.

 

Configuration

Notes

2-way SLI

 

 

Connect the 5K monitor to the master SLI GPU.

 

Clone/Extended Mode

To configure with a non-5K monitor in clone or extended mode, connect the second display to the master SLI GPU.

Multiple Quadro Cards with SLI Disabled or Not Supported

Connect DP1 and DP2 from the 5K monitor to the two DisplayPort connectors on any single Quadro card. Do not connect DP1 to one Quadro card and DP2 to another Quadro card.

NOTE: Images are for illustrative purposes, and may not match your particular card.

 

Configuration

Notes

Two Quadro Cards

 

 

Connect the 5K monitor to any card.

 

Clone Mode

To configure with a non-5K monitor in clone mode, connect the second display to the same GPU as the 5K monitor.

Extended Mode

To configure with a non-5K monitor in extended mode, connect the second display to any GPU.
