To compensate for poor lighting conditions when viewing your desktop

  1. From the NVIDIA Control Panel navigation tree pane, under Display, click Adjust Desktop Color Settings to open the page.

  2. If applicable, select the display on which you want to improve the sharpness of images.

  3. Move the Digital Vibrance slider to increase (or decrease) the crispness/richness/intensity of your desktop colors.
