TV and HDTV Settings

You can find the TV and HDTV settings controls in the NVIDIA Control Panel Display category pages.

  • Adjust  television color settings controls are now located in the Adjust Desktop Color Settings page.

  • For help, see Color Settings.

  • TV and HDTV signal and resolution format controls are now located in the Change Resolution page.

  • For help, see TV/HD Format.  

  • The digital color format controls are now located in the Change Resolution page.

  • For help, see TV/HD Format.

  • The controls for changing the desktop size and position on a TV and HDTV are now located in the Adjust Desktop Size and Position page.

  • For help, see Desktop Size and Position.

  • The controls for fine tuning the HDTV pixel number or selecting progressive or interlaced scanning modes are now located in the Change Resolution page.

  • For help, see Screen Resolution.
