Connecting Multiple 5K Monitors - Quadro K Series

 

To connect two or more 5K monitors, you need at least two supported Quadro cards.

Select your application:

  • Multiple Quadro Cards with SLI Enabled

  • Multiple Quadro Cards with SLI Disabled or Not Supported

 

 

 

 

 

 

Multiple Quadro Cards with SLI Enabled

 

Connection Rules

  • Connect DP1 and DP2 from each 5K monitor to two DisplayPort connectors on each Quadro card. Do not connect DP1 to one Quadro card and DP2 to another Quadro card.

  • If the Quadro card has more than two DisplayPort connectors, then you can connect the 5K monitor to any two DisplayPort connectors on the card.

  • DP1 and DP2 from each 5K monitor must be connected to the same connector positions on each Quadro card.

For example:

DP1 is connected to connector 1 and DP2 is connected to connector 2 on the master SLI GPU.

The same connections must be made for the remaining SLI GPUs.

NOTE: Images are for illustrative purposes, and may not match your particular card.

 

Configuration

Mosaic, Clone, Extended Mode Notes

2-way SLI

 

You can connect a maximum of two 5K monitors and configure them in 2-way Mosaic mode.

 

 

Clone/Extended Mode

Clone or extended mode with non-5k monitors can be enabled only with displays connected to the master SLI GPU.

3-way SLI

 

You can connect a maximum of three 5K monitors and configure them in 3-way Mosaic mode.

 

 

Clone/Extended Mode

Clone or extended mode with non-5k monitors can be enabled only with displays connected to the master SLI GPU.

 

4-way SLI

 

You can connect a maximum of four 5K monitors and configure them in 4-way Mosaic mode.

4-way Mosaic is limited to the following topologies:

- 2x2 (landscape or portrait)

- 4x1 (landscape only)

- 1x4 (portrait only)

 

 

Clone/Extended Mode

Clone or extended mode with non-5k monitors can be enabled only with displays connected to the master SLI GPU.

Multiple Quadro Cards with SLI Disabled or Not Supported

 

Connection Rules

  • Connect DP1 and DP2 from each 5K monitor to two DisplayPort connectors on each Quadro card. Do not connect DP1 to one Quadro card and DP2 to another Quadro card.

  • If the Quadro card has more than two DisplayPort connectors, then you can connect the 5K monitor to any two DisplayPort connectors on the card.

NOTE: Images are for illustrative purposes, and may not match your particular card.

 

Configuration

 Notes

Two GPUs

Supports a maximum of two 5K monitors. Connect each monitor to the ports on the same GPU.

Clone Mode

To configure with a non-5K monitor in clone mode, connect the second display to the same GPU as the 5K monitor.

Extended Mode

To configure with a non-5K monitor in extended mode, connect the second display to any GPU.

Three GPUs

Supports a maximum of three 5K monitors. Connect each monitor to the ports on the same GPU.

Clone Mode

To configure with a non-5K monitor in clone mode, connect the second display to the same GPU as the 5K monitor.

Extended Mode

To configure with a non-5K monitor in extended mode, connect the second display to any GPU.

Four GPUs

Supports a maximum of four 5K monitors. Connect each monitor to the ports on the same GPU.

Clone Mode

To configure with a non-5K monitor in clone mode, connect the second display to the same GPU as the 5K monitor.

Extended Mode

To configure with a non-5K monitor in extended mode, connect the second display to any GPU.
