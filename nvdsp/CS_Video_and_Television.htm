Video

Video features are similar to those for analog and digital displays but include features specific to video.

For example, you can:

  • Adjust Video Color Settings

  • Adjust Video Image Settings  

 

NOTE: Optimus technology systems:

On systems with NVIDIA Optimus™ technology, the NVIDIA Control Panel video settings are effective only for videos that are decoded by the NVIDIA GPU.  When running, these are listed in the NVIDIA GPU Activity icon.

To adjust video for content that is not decoded by the NVIDIA GPU, use the Windows Control Panel or the controls provided by your integrated graphics vendor.
