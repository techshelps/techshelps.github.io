To adjust the red-blue balance of your video

  1. From the NVIDIA Control Panel navigation tree pane, under Video, click Adjust video color settings to open the page.

  2. If applicable, select the display on which you are playing the video.

  3. Under How do you make color adjustments, select With the NVIDIA settings.

  4. Click the Color tab and then move the Hue slider to adjust the red-blue color balance of your video image.  

  5. Click Apply when done.
