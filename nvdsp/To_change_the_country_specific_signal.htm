To change the country-specific signal format for your standard television

  1. From the NVIDIA Control Panel navigation tree pane, under Display, click Change resolution to open the associated page.

  2. Set the Connector list to your television's connector, such as Composite or S-Video.

  3. From the TV format list, select the signal format based on your country location.
