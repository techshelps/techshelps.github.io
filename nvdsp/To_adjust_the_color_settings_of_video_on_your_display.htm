To adjust the color settings of video on your display

  1. From the NVIDIA Control Panel navigation tree pane, under Video, click Adjust video color settings to open the page.

  2. If applicable, select the display on which you are playing the video.

  3. Refer to the following sections for the type of color adjustment you want to make:

    • Adjust the brightness of video on my display

    • Adjust the contrast of video on my display

    • Adjust the red-blue balance of my video

    • Adjust the color intensity of my video

    • Adjust the contrast of my video dynamically

    • Enhance the color quality of my video

    • Adjust the brightness of mid-tones (gamma) video on my display

    • Improve details in the extreme black and white areas of the video
