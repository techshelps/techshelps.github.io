To adjust your screen resolution

  1. From the NVIDIA Control Panel navigation tree pane, under Display, click Change resolution to open the Change Resolution page.

  2. Click the image that represents the display you want to affect.

  3. If multiple choices are available, click the Connector list arrow and then select the connector type for your display.

  4. In the Resolution list box, scroll to locate the resolution that you want to set and then click the resolution.

  5. Click Apply when done.

Note: Increasing the display resolution shows more information but in a smaller size. Decreasing the display resolution shows less information but in a larger size.
