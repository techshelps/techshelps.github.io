Connecting a Single 5K Monitor - GeForce 900 Series

 

These cards typically have more than two DisplayPort connectors. You can connect the 5K monitor using any two of the DisplayPort connectors as long as they are on the same GPU. Do not connect DP1 to one GPU and DP2 to a different GPU.

Select your application:

  • Single GeForce Card

  • Multiple GeForce Cards with SLI Enabled

  • Multiple GeForce Cards with SLI Disabled or Not Supported

 

 

 

Single GeForce Card

 

Connect DP1 and DP2 from each 5K monitor to two DisplayPort connectors on the GeForce card.

If the GeForce card has more than two DisplayPort connectors, then you can connect the 5K monitor to any two DisplayPort connectors on the card.

Multiple GeForce Cards with SLI Enabled

Connect DP1 and DP2 from each 5K monitor to two DisplayPort connectors on the master SLI GPU.

If the GeForce card has more than two DisplayPort connectors, then you can connect the 5K monitor to any two DisplayPort connectors on the card.

NOTE: Images are for illustrative purposes, and may not match your particular card.

Notes

Multiple GeForce Cards with SLI Disabled or Not Supported

Connect DP1 and DP2 from each 5K monitor to two DisplayPort connectors on any single GeForce card. Do not connect DP1 to one GeForce card and DP2 to another GeForce card.

If the GeForce card has more than two DisplayPort connectors, then you can connect the 5K monitor to any two DisplayPort connectors on the card.

NOTE: Images are for illustrative purposes, and may not match your particular card.

To configure with a non-5K monitor in clone mode, connect the second display to the same GPU as the 5K monitor.

To configure with a non-5K monitor in extended mode, connect the second display to any GPU.
