Available only under Windows XP, the Television Setup Wizard guides you through the steps to configure your television for use with your computer.

To follow guided steps for setting up your TV

  1. From the NVIDIA Control Panel navigation tree pane, under Display, click Run television setup wizard.

  2. Follow the instructions in the wizard to

  • Select the connector type

  • Select the nView display mode
