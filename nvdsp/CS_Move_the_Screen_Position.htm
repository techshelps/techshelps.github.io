Move CRT Screen Position

The adjust screen position controls appear on the Adjust Desktop Size and Position page when you click the icon that represents your analog (CRT) display.

Use the arrows buttons to adjust the screen position.

Click the  button to move the desktop or image to the left on your analog display. Reasons for use:

    •  The left side of the screen has a black border.

    •  You would like the desktop or image to appear more centered on the screen.

    •  The right side of the desktop or image extends beyond the screen.

 Click the  button to move the desktop or image to the right on your analog display. Reasons for use are:

    •  The right side of the screen has a black border.

    •  You would like the desktop or image to appear more centered on the screen.

    •  The left side of the display screen has a black border.

Click the button to move up the image or desktop on your tanalog display. Reasons for use are:

    • The top of the screen has a black border.

    • You would like the desktop or image to appear more centered on the screen.

    • The bottom of the desktop extends beyond the screen.

 Click the button to move down the image or desktop on your analog display. Reasons for use are:

    • The bottom of the screen has a black border.

    • You would like the desktop or image to appear more centered on the screen.

    • The bottom of the screen has a black border.
