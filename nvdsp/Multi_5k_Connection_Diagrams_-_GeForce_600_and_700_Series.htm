Connecting Multiple 5K Monitors - GeForce 600 and 700 Series

 

With GeForce cards that have only one DisplayPort connector, four cards in 4-way SLI mode are required to support two 5K monitors.

Connection Rules

From each 5K monitor, connect DP1 to one GeForce card, and DP2 to another GeForce card.

In the diagram below, connectors identified with the same color indicate that the two cards are driving the same 5K monitor.  

NOTE: Images are for illustrative purposes, and may not match your particular card.

 

Configuration

Surround, Clone, and Extended Mode Notes

4-way SLI

Surround

You can connect a maximum of two 5K monitors and configure them in 2-way Surround.

 

 

Clone/Extended Mode

Clone or extended mode with non-5k monitors can be enabled only with displays connected to the master SLI GPU.
