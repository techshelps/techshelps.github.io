Image Options Tab Overview

The Image Options tab enables you to modify the appearance of your printed documents by changing the following options:

Note: Some features are only supported by specific printers, operating systems, or driver types (PostScript or PCL). You may see more features or options listed than what your specific system supports.

  • Lightness
  • Defaults button: Returns all options on the tab to the default settings.

The following items are located on the bottom of the tab:

  • Status area and More Status button
  • Earth Smart Settings button
  • Saved Settings
