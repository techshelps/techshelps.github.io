Transparency Separators Dialog Box

On the Paper/Output tab.

Note: Some features are only supported by specific printers, operating systems, or driver types (PostScript or PCL). You may see more features or options listed than what your specific system supports.

Appears when you select Paper > Transparency Separators.

Use this dialog box to have the printer insert a separator (a divider page) between each printed transparency in your print job.

Transparency separators are not included in your job when your job is printed on any other media type. They are also not included if you select to print from a specific tray even when that tray contains transparencies.

  • Separator Options:
    • No Separators: Does not insert separators.
    • Blank Separators: Inserts a blank sheet of paper between the transparencies.
    • Printed Separators: Inserts a printed sheet of paper between the transparencies. The printed sheet contains the same image as the transparency.
  • Source: Select the paper tray to use for the separators from the list. The separators must be in a different paper tray than the tray that feeds the transparencies.

Note: These transparency separator settings override any transparency separator settings in the application. Transparency separators cannot be specified with Folding, Saddle Stapling, or Booklet output.
