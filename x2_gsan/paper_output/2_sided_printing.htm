2-Sided Printing

On the Paper/Output tab and the Layout/Watermark tab (under Page Layout Options).

You can have your job automatically printed on both sides of a sheet of paper.

Select one of the following options:

  • 1-Sided Print: Prints on one side of the paper or transparency. Use this option when printing on transparencies, envelopes, or labels.
  • 2-Sided Print: Prints on both sides of a sheet of paper. The images are printed so the job can be bound on the long edge of the sheet. The following illustrations show the results for documents with portrait and landscape orientations respectively.

    • Note: 2-Sided Printing is also part of the Earth Smart settings.

  • 2-Sided Print, Flip on Short Edge: Prints on both sides of a sheet of paper. Prints the images so the job can be bound on the short edge of the sheet. The following illustrations show the results for documents with portrait and landscape orientations respectively.

Note: Some media types, sizes, weights, and booklet layouts cannot be automatically printed on both sides. See the User Guide for more information.
