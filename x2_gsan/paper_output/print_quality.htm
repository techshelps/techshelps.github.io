Print Quality

On the Paper/Output tab.

The Print Quality menu affects the appearance of your prints and the time they take to print. Select one of the following print-quality modes:

  • High Resolution: Provides the best overall image quality for detailed line drawings such as CAD files. Recommended for printing photos.

    • Note: The print job may take longer to process when printed in High Resolution mode.

  • High Speed: Fastest mode, useful for many images and previewing your work. Rapidly produces review documents; useful for rush jobs. Not recommended for documents containing small text, fine detail, or large areas of bright solid fill.
