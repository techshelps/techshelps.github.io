Fax Confirmation Dialog Box

Appears when you print your job as a fax.

If the fax is scheduled to be sent at a later time, the send time is shown on the first line.

The Fax Recipients table shows the recipients' names and fax numbers. It may also show their organizations, phone numbers, and email addresses.

To change the recipients or other fax options, click to return to the Fax dialog box.

To continue with the fax job, click the OK button.
