Fax: Options Tab

Use the Options tab to select the following options for your fax job:

Note: Some features are only supported by specific printers, operating systems, or driver types. You may see more features or options listed than what your printer supports.

  • Confirmation Sheet: Select whether you want your printer to print a confirmation sheet to confirm that the fax was successfully sent.
  • Send Speed: Select the appropriate fax transmission rate. If you live in an area where the phone lines are inadequate for high speed fax/data transmission, select a lower transmission rate to reduce the chance of transmission errors.
  • Fax Resolution: Select the resolution for the fax. The resolution setting affects the amount of detail that is reproduced during the fax transmission. Higher resolution fax transmissions consume more memory and take longer to send.

    Note: If the recipient's fax does not support your resolution, your printer automatically changes the resolution to match the other fax's capability.
  • Send Time: Select one of the following options:
    • Send Now: To transmit the fax immediately (when you print your job as a fax).
    • Send At: To delay the fax and send it at a specified time within the next 24 hours, select this option, and then specify the time to send.
  • Dialing Prefix: To access an outside phone line, select this check box, and then type the prefix. For example, many corporate phone systems require you to dial "9" to access an outside line.
