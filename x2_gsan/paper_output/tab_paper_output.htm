Paper/Output Tab Overview

The Paper/Output tab contains the following items for selecting the paper you want to use and the output options:

  • Job Type
  • Paper summary and paper selection menu, which accesses the Paper Size and Advanced Paper Selection dialog boxes.
  • 2-Sided Printing
  • Finishing summary and finishing selection menu, which accesses the Booklet Creation dialog box. It also access Stapling and Hole Punching options.
  • Print Quality
  • Output Destination

The following items are located on the bottom of the tab:

  • Status area and More Status button
  • Earth Smart Settings button
  • Saved Settings
