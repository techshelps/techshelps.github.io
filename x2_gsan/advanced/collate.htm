Collate

On the Advanced tab (in the Paper/Output folder).

Collate activates after you select two or more copies in the:

  • Print dialog box.
  • Copies field, on the Advanced tab.

To determine the order that pages print, select either:

  • Collated: The printer prints the pages consecutively and finishes one copy of the whole document before printing the pages for the next copy of the document.

  • Uncollated: The printer prints copies of each page before printing the next page.
