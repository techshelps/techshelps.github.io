Job Identification

On the Advanced tab (in the Paper/Output folder).

A banner sheet is a separator page that is printed with the job. The banner sheet has information about the print job, including the network user name of the user who submitted the job, and the document name.

If you do not want to print banner sheets, you can print job identification information in the margins of your document instead. Settings made here are in effect for all print jobs unless overridden.

You can choose one of the selections from the Job Identification menu:

  • Disable Job ID: Do not print any job identification information, either on a banner sheet or in the margins of the document.
  • Print ID on a Banner Sheet: Job identification is printed on a separate banner sheet.
  • Print ID in Margins - First Page Only: Job identification is printed in the top, left margin of the first page of the document.
  • Print ID in Margins - All Pages: Job identification is printed in the top, left margin of all the pages of the document.

Notes:

  • Banner Sheets must also be enabled on the printer to print banner sheets.
  • The ability to disable Banner Sheets or print document identification information in page margins is part of the Earth Smart settings.
