Create a calculated field by using an expression

  1. In the Data pane, click a blank column heading.
  2. Type the expression for the calculation, and then press ENTER. Do not begin the expression with an equal sign (=).

    For example, if employees receive a commission that equals 15 percent of their total sales, you can calculate each employee's commission by adding the sales for each employee and then multiplying the total by 0.15.
