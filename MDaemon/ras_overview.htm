RAS Dialup Settings

Click the "Setup » Default Domain/Servers » RAS" menu selection to configure your RAS Dialup settings. This dialog will only be available if you have Remote Access Services installed on your system. It is used by MDaemon when you need to dial up your ISP just prior to a Remote Mail processing event.

The RAS Dialup contains the following three screens:

·RAS
·Logon
·Processing
