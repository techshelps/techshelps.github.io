Internal Browser

Portable Offline Browser incorporates the Internal browser panel for quick and easy browsing of downloaded Web sites.

By default, when you select Browse from the context menu, the downloaded page will be opened in Internal browser.

You can easily navigate Internal browser using its toolbar.

Internal browser may be turned on or off using the Ribbon - View tab.

You may open multiple tabs to browse several pages at once. Click the V button to see the full list of all open tabs.

To browse a Project in a new tab, right-click the Project and select "Browse in a new tab".
