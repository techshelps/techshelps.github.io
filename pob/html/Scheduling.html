Scheduling Project Downloads

Downloads may easily be scheduled. Every Project can be started automatically at specified time intervals. To set scheduling, select the desired Project and select Advanced | Scheduling from the Properties dialog.

Table 1. 

Manual:No schedule.
Daily:Start the download every day at the specified time.
Weekly:Start the download once a week on the selected Weekday at the specified time.
Monthly and Yearly:Start the download at the specified time every month or year.
Every:Start the download at a specific time (every Hour : Minute).
Only once:Download only once at the specified time and then reset the Schedule to the Manual state.
