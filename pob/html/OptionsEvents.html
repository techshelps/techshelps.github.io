Options dialog: Internet - Disconnect and Exit

This page contains settings to hang up dialup connections or shut down the computer after the download is complete.

  • Disconnect modem connection

    When this setting is checked, Portable Offline Browser will hang up the modem connection after all downloads are complete. If you want to close connections at some specified time, use the Stop downloading and disconnect at setting.

  • Shutdown computer

    When this setting is checked, Portable Offline Browser will exit and turn off the computer after the download is complete.

    Note: If other programs are running, they may request that their data be saved when shutting down the computer. This will prevent Portable Offline Browser from completing its shutdown. Please ensure that all other programs are closed before placing Portable Offline Browser in the automatic shutdown mode. For complete power off, your computer motherboard should support that feature (ATX motherboard standard.)
