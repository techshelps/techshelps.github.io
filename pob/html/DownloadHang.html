Why Portable Offline Browser stops during downloading?

Sometimes you may notice that Portable Offline Browser stops loading files, but the queue is not empty and there are many files to load.

This may happen because of the very high downloading speed. Some Web servers or routers may simply hang when processing the huge amount of requests Portable Offline Browser generates.

As a simple workaround, please open the Options dialog, set a one second delay between downloads and click OK.
