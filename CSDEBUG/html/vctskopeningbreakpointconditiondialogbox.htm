Opening the Breakpoint Condition Dialog Box

To specify or change a breakpoint condition when creating a new breakpoint

  1. In the Breakpoints window, select the breakpoint you want.
  2. On the toolbar at the top of the window, click the Properties tool.
  3. In the New Breakpoint dialog box, click the Conditions button.

To specify or change a breakpoint condition when creating a new breakpoint from a source or Call Stack window

  1. Right-click the breakpoint and choose Properties from the shortcut menu.
  2. In the Breakpoint Properties dialog box click the Conditions button.
