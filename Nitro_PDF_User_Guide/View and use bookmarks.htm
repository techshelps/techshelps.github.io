View and use bookmarks

Bookmarks are displayed in the Bookmarks pane, which appears to the left of the document area.

To open the Bookmarks pane:

  1. From the navigation tabs (displayed along the left side of the Nitro Pro window), click the Bookmark tab

To increase or decrease the font size displayed in the Bookmarks pane:

  1. Click the File menu button , and then click Preferences
  2. Click Interface, and in the Bookmark text size list, click the text size
  3. Click OK
