Send PDF documents by email

Nitro Pro 7 makes an easy task out of sending a PDF file via email, by creating a new message in your default email client and automatically attaching the currently open PDF file.

To send a PDF as an email attachment

  1. Open your PDF document in Nitro Pro 7

  2. In the File menu, click Email as PDF attachment

  3. An email is composed in your default email client, with your PDF document included as an attachment

  4. Write your message in the body of the email, and click Send
