Extract pages

Nitro Pro 7 enables you to extract one or more pages from a PDF document, quickly creating a new document containing those pages. Optionally, you can also delete the pages from the original PDF file once they are extracted.

To extract pages:

  1. On the Edit tab, in the Pages group, click Extract
  2. In the Extract Pages dialog, select the pages to extract
  3. Optionally, click to select the Delete pages after extraction checkbox
  4. Click Extract
