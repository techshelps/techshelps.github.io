To switch to the big Full Navigation wheel

Do one of the following:

  • Click View tabNavigate panelSteeringWheels flyoutFull Navigation Wheel. At the Command prompt, enter navswheelmode. When prompted for a new value, enter 2 and then start the navswheel command.
  • Right-click on the wheel, and click Full Navigation Wheel.
 Command entry: NAVSWHEEL
 Shortcut menu: Right-click over the drawing window and click SteeringWheels
 Toolbar: Status bar SteeringWheels
