To use the Properties palette to adjust the underlay contrast, fade, monochrome, and background color setting

  1. Double-click the underlay to display the Properties palette.
  2. In the Properties palette, do one of the following:
    • Adjust the contrast by entering a new value.
    • Adjust the fade by entering a new value.
    • Turn Monochrome On or Off.
    • Set Adjust Colors for Background to Yes or No.

The appearance of the underlay is updated in real-time as you make adjustments in the Properties palette.

 Shortcut menu: Right-click an underlay to display the Properties Palette.
