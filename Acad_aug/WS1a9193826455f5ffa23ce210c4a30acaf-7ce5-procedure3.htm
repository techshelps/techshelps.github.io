To apply a material by layer

  1. At the Command prompt, enter materialattach.
  2. In the Material Attachment Options dialog box, drag a material onto a layer.

    The material is applied to all objects on the layer whose Material property is set to BYLAYER. BYLAYER is the default for the Material property when you create an object.
