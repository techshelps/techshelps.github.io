Commands

CONVTOSURFACE

Converts objects to 3D surfaces.

EXPLODE

Breaks a compound object into its component objects.

PLANESURF

Creates a planar surface.

System Variables

DELOBJ

Controls whether geometry used to create 3D objects is retained or deleted.

SMOOTHMESHCONVERT

Sets whether mesh objects that you convert to 3D solids or surfaces are smoothed or faceted, and whether their faces are merged.
