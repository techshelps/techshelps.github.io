ConceptProcedure
 
 
 

Motion path animations, such as 3D animated walk-throughs of a model, allow you to visually demonstrate a model to both a technical and non-technical audience. You can record and play back a navigation to communicate your design intent dynamically.

Topics in this section

  • Control a Camera Motion Path

    You control the camera motion, and therefore the animation, by linking the camera and its target to a point or a path.

  • Specify Motion Path Settings

    You determine the format of the animation file of a motion path animation by specifying settings in the Motion Path Animation dialog box.

  • Record a Motion Path Animation

    You can preview the animation before you record it and then save it in the desired format.
