To turn notification for standards violations on or off

  1. Click Manage tab Cad Standards panel Configure. At the Command prompt, enter standards.
  2. In the Configure Standards dialog box, click Settings.
  3. Do one of the following:
    • To turn off standards notifications, select Disable Standards Notifications.
    • To be notified by alert, select Display Alert upon Standards Violation.
    • To be notified by the display of an icon in the status bar, select Display Standards Status Bar Icon.
  4. Click OK.
