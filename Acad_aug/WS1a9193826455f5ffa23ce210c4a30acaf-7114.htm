Several tools are available that you can use to restrict or lock the movement of your cursor.

Topics in this section

  • Adjust Grid and Grid Snap

    To enhance drawing speed and efficiency, you can display and snap to a rectangular grid. You can also control its spacing, angle, and alignment.

  • Use Orthogonal Locking (Ortho Mode)

    You can restrict cursor movement to horizontal and vertical for convenience and precision when creating and modifying objects.

  • Use Polar Tracking and PolarSnap

    Polar tracking restricts cursor movement to specified angles. PolarSnap restricts cursor movement to specified increments along a polar angle.

  • Lock an Angle for One Point (Angle)

    You can specify an angle override that locks the cursor for the next point entered.
