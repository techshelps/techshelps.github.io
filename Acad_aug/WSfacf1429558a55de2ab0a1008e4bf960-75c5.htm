You can magnify the details in your drawing for a closer view or shift the view to a different part of the drawing. If you save views by name, you can restore them later.

Topics in this section

  • Pan or Zoom a View

    You can pan to reposition the view in the drawing area or zoom to change magnification.

  • Pan and Zoom with the Aerial View Window

    In a large drawing, you can pan and zoom quickly in a window that can display the whole drawing.

  • Save and Restore Views

    When you save specific views by name, you can restore them for layout and plotting or when you need to refer to specific details. You can create and save views using the VIEW or the CAMERA command.

  • Control the 3D Projection Style

    You can view both parallel and perspective projection of a 3D model.

  • Shade a Model and Use Edge Effects

    Hiding lines enhances the drawing and clarifies the design. The addition of shading produces a more realistic image of your model.
