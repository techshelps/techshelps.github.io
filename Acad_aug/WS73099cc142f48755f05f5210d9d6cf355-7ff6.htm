When you annotate your drawings, you can use certain tools and properties to make working with annotations easier.

Topics in this section

  • Overview of Annotations

    Annotations are notes or other types of explanatory symbols or objects that are commonly used to add information to your drawing.

  • Scale Annotations

    You can automate the process of scaling annotations in various layout viewports and in model space.

  • Set Orientation for Annotations

    Annotative blocks and text can be set so that their orientation matches the orientation of the layout. The orientation of annotative hatches always matches the orientation of the layout.
