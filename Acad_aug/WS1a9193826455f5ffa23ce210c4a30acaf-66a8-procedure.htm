To adjust a section object using grips

  1. In the drawing area, click the section plane.

    Depending on the current section plane state, different grips are displayed.

  2. Hover over the grip with the cursor until it turns red. Then drag the grip to a new location.
  3. To adjust the height, width, or depth of a section plane, click the Menu grip and select Section Volume or Section Boundary.
