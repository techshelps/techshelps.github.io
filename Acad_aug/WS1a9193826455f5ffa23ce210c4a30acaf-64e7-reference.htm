Commands

LEADER

Creates a line that connects annotation to a feature.

MLEADER

Creates a multileader object.

QLEADER

Creates a leader and leader annotation.

System Variables

DIMASSOC

Controls the associativity of dimension objects and whether dimensions are exploded.

DIMGAP

Sets the distance around the dimension text when the dimension line breaks to accommodate dimension text.

DIMLDRBLK

Specifies the arrow type for leaders.

MLEADERSCALE

Sets the overall scale factor applied to multileader objects.
