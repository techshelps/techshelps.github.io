Having saved your rendered image, you can redisplay that rendering at any time.

Having saved your rendered image, you can view that rendering at any time by opening it in the Windows Image Viewer.

If you don’t want to use an external program, you can also view the image by inserting it into the drawing with the IMAGEATTACH command.
