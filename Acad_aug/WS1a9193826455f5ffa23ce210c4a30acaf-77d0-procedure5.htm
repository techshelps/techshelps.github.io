To display the area of the grid limits by zooming

  • Click View tab Navigate panel All. At the Command prompt, enter zoom, then enter a (All).

The limits of the drawing grid fill the current viewport or the drawing area. If there are any objects outside the grid limits, they are also included.

 Command entry: ZOOM
 Toolbar: Standard
