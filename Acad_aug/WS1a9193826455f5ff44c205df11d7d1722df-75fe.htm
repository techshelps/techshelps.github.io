You can control the display of layers, use object snaps, and adjust display settings with attached underlays.

Topics in this section

  • Control the Display of Layers in an Underlay

    You can turn layers on and off in an underlay.

  • Modify the Position, Scale, or Rotation of an Underlay

    You specify an underlay’s position, scale, or rotation when you attach an underlay file. You can also modify these settings later during the drafting process.

  • Use Object Snaps with Underlays

    Use object snaps to draw or edit objects relative to a precise location.

  • Adjust Underlay Contrast, Fade, Monochrome, and Colors for the Background

    You can modify the contrast, fade, and monochrome settings of an underlay. Also, so that the underlay is visible, you can adjust the underlay colors based on the AutoCAD background colors.

  • Clip Underlays

    You can use a clipping boundary to clip an underlay.

  • Hide and Show Underlay Frames

    You can display and plot a border around the underlay or the clipping boundary.
