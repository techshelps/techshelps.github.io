To specify a new UCS with three points

  1. Click View tabUCS panel3 Point.At the command prompt, enter ucs.
  2. Specify a new origin point. This is the point (0,0,0) on the new UCS.
  3. Specify a point on the positive X axis of the new UCS.
  4. Specify a point on the positive XY plane of the new UCS.
