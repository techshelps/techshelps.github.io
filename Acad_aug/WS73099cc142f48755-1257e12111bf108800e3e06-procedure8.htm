To apply a tangent constraint

  1. Click Parametric tab Geometric panel Tangent. Enter geomconstraint at the Command prompt.
  2. Select the two objects to be made tangential.

    The second object maintains a point of tangency with the first object.

 Toolbar: Parametric, Geometric Constraints
 Command entry: GEOMCONSTRAINT
