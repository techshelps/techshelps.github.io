You can create your own visual styles by changing the face and edge settings and using shadows and backgrounds.

Topics in this section

  • Shade and Color Faces

    Shading and color effects control the display of faces in a model.

  • Display Backgrounds and Shadows

    The visual style also controls the display of backgrounds and shadows in the viewport.

  • Control the Display of Edges

    Edges of different types can be displayed using different colors and linetypes. You can also add special effects such as jitter and overhang to edges.
