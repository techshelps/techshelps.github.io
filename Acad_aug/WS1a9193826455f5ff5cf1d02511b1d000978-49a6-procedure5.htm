To enable tooltips for wheels

  1. Click View tab Navigate panel SteeringWheels flyout click one of the available SteeringWheels.
  2. Right-click on the wheel and click SteeringWheel Settings.
  3. In the SteeringWheels Settings dialog box, under Display, click Show Tooltips.

    Tooltips are displayed for each wedge and buttons on a wheel when the cursor hovers over the wheel.

  4. Click OK.
