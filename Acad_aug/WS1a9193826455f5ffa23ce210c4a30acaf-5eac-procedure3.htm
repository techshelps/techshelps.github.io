To remove a sheet from a drawing set for publishing

  1. Click Output tabPublish panelPublish. At the command prompt, enter publish.
  2. In the Publish dialog box, ensure the sheets that you want to remove are listed.
  3. Under Sheets, select one or more sheets to remove. Click the Remove Sheets button.
 Toolbar: Standard
