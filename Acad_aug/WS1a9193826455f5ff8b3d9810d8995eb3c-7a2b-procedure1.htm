To show or hide light glyphs

  1. Click Render tab Lights panel Light Glyph Display.

    Turns on and off the display of glyphs that represents lights in the current viewport.

NoteThe display setting does not control plotting of light glyphs.
 Command entry: LIGHTGLYPHDISPLAY
