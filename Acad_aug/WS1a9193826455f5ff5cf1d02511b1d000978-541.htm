The Pan tool repositions the current view of the model by panning.

When the pan tool is active, the Pan cursor (a four-sided arrow) is displayed. Dragging the pointing device moves the model in the same direction. For example, dragging upward moves the model up while dragging downward moves the model down.

TipIf the cursor reaches the edge of the screen, you can continue panning by dragging further to force it to wrap around the screen.
