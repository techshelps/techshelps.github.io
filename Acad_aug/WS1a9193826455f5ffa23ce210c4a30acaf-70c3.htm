To specify a new point location, you can combine coordinate values from several points or you can specify offsets from existing objects.

Topics in this section

  • Combine Coordinate Values (Coordinate Filters)

    You can use coordinate filters to extract one coordinate value at a time from locations on existing objects.

  • Track to Points on Objects (Object Snap Tracking)

    You can draw objects at specific angles or in specific relationship to other objects along specified directions called alignment paths.

  • Track to Offset Point Locations (Tracking)

    You can use tracking to specify a point by offsetting vertically and horizontally from a series of temporary points.
