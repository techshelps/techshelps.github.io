Commands

JOIN

Joins similar objects to form a single, unbroken object.

SPLINEDIT

Edits a spline or spline-fit polyline.

REVERSE

Reverses the order of vertices of the selected lines, polylines, splines, and helixes.

System Variables

PLINECONVERTMODE

Specifies the fit method used in converting splines to polylines.

SPLFRAME

Controls the display of splines and spline-fit polylines.
