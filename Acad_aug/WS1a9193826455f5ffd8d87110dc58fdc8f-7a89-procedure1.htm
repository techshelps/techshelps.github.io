To remove a diffuse map from a material

  1. Click Render tab 3D Palettes Materials.
  2. In the Materials window, select a material.
  3. In the Materials window, in the Maps section, clear the Dffuse map selection.
  4. Click the Delete Map Information from Material icon to remove the diffuse map from the material.

    You are prompted to delete all nested maps.

  5. Click Yes.

    The material Diffuse map and nested maps are deleted from the material.

 Toolbar: Render 
 Command entry: MATERIALS
