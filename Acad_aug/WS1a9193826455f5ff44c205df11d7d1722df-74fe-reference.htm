System Variables

DGNFRAME

Determines whether DGN underlay frames are visible or plotted in the current drawing.

DWFFRAME

Determines whether DWF or DWFx underlay frames are visible or plotted in the current drawing.

FRAME

Turns the display of frames on and off for all external references, images, and DWF, DWFx, PDF, and DGN underlays.

PDFFRAME

Determines whether the PDF underlay frame is visible.
