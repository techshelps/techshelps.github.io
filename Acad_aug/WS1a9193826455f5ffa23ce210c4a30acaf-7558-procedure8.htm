To make a layout current

Do one of the following to make a layout current:

  • Click the layout tab for the layout that you want to make current.
  • Press CTRL+Page Down to cycle through the layout tabs from left to right or CTRL+Page Up to cycle through the layout tabs from right to left. Stop when you get to the tab of the layout that you want to make current.
