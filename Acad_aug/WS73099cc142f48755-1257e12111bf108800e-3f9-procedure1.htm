To dock a toolbar

  1. Position the cursor on the name of the toolbar or in any blank area, and hold down the button on your pointing device.
  2. Drag the toolbar to a docking location at the top, bottom, or either side of the drawing area.
  3. When the outline of the toolbar is displayed in the docking area, release the button.

    To place a toolbar in a docking region without docking it, hold down Ctrl as you drag.
