You can modify a camera’s lens length, change its front and back clipping planes, name a camera, and turn the display of all cameras on or off in a drawing.

When you select a camera, the Camera Preview dialog box opens to display the camera's view.

There are several ways to change camera settings:

  • Click and drag grips to resize or relocate a lens length or field of view.

  • Use the Dynamic Input tooltip to enter X,Y, Z coordinate values.

  • Modify camera properties in the Camera Properties palette.
