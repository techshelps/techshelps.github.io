To create an email hyperlink

  1. In the drawing area, select one or more graphical objects to attach the hyperlink to.
  2. Click Blocks & References tabData panelHyperlink. At the command prompt, enter hyperlink.
  3. In the Insert Hyperlink dialog box, under Link To, click E-mail Address.
  4. Enter the recipient's address in E-mail Address.
  5. (Optional) Specify a subject for the email message in Subject.
  6. Click OK.

    When you open an email hyperlink, your default email application creates a new email message with the address and subject (if specified) filled out. You can then enter message text and send it by email.

 Command entry: HYPERLINK
