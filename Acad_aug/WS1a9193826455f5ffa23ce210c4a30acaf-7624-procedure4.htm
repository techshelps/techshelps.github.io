To change a camera’s target

  1. If cameras are not already displayed in the drawing, click Render tab Camera panel Show Cameras.
  2. Click the camera glyph whose target you want to change.
  3. Click the Target Distance grip tool (the blue grip in the center), drag it to the new location, and click to place the target.
  4. Press Enter.
 Command entry: CAMERADISPLAY
