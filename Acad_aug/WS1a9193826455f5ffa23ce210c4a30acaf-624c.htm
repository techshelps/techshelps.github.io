The numeric values displayed in dimensions can appear in several formats. You can also control how numeric distances are represented.

Topics in this section

  • Control the Display of Dimension Units

    The numeric values of dimensions can be displayed as a single measurement or in two measurement systems. In either case, you can control details of how the numeric values are presented.

  • Round Off Dimension Values

    You can round off the numeric values in dimensions and lateral tolerances.

  • Suppress Zeros in Dimensions

    You can suppress leading and trailing zeros in the numeric portion of dimension text. You can also specify the sub unit for the dimension distance.

  • Display Lateral Tolerances

    Lateral tolerances are values indicating the amount a measured distance can vary. You can control whether lateral tolerances are displayed and you can choose from several styles of lateral tolerances.

  • Control the Display of Fractions

    You can control the format of the fraction displayed in dimensions.
