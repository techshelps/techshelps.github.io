To change the viewing position in the Position Locator window

  1. Do one of the following:
    • Click View menu Walk and Fly Walk.At the Command prompt, enter 3dwalk.
    • Click View menu Walk and Fly Fly.At the Command prompt, enter 3dfly.
  2. In the Position Locator window, Preview area, click the position indicator (a colored dot) and drag it to a new position.
  3. If the target indicator is displayed, click it and drag it to the new target.
  4. In the General section, make any changes to the current settings.
  5. Continue walking or flying through the model.
 Toolbar: Walk and Fly
 Command entry: 3DWALK, 3DFLY
