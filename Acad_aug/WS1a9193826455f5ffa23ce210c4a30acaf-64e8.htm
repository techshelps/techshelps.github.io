You can create, modify and add content to a leader object.

Topics in this section

  • Overview of Leader Objects

    A leader object is a line or a spline with an arrowhead at one end and a multiline text object or block at the other.

  • Create and Modify Leaders

    A leader object typically consists of an arrowhead, an optional horizontal landing, a leader line or curve, and either a multiline text object or block.

  • Work with Leader Styles

    The appearance of a leader is controlled by its multileader style. You can use the default multileader style, STANDARD, or create your own multileader styles.

  • Add Content to a Leader

    Leaders can contain multiline text or blocks to label parts of your drawing.
