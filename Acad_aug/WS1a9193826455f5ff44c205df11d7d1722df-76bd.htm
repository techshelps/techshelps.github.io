You can display DWF, DWFx, PDF, and DGN files as underlays in your drawing.

Topics in this section

  • Overview of Underlays

    You can underlay and snap to 2D geometry stored in DWF, DWFx, DGN, and PDF files.

  • Attach, Scale, and Detach Underlays

    You can add or remove references to underlays within drawing files, or you can change their relative size.

  • Work with Underlays

    You can control the display of layers, use object snaps, and adjust display settings with attached underlays.

  • Manage and Publish Drawings Containing Underlays

    You can view and manipulate underlays and change paths to underlays in the External References palette.
