To continue an arc with a tangential line

  1. Complete the arc.
  2. Click Home tabDraw panelLine. At the command prompt, enter line.
  3. Press ENTER at the first prompt.
  4. Enter the length of the line and press ENTER.
 Toolbar: Draw 
 Command entry: LINE
