To reorient a view by moving towards or away from the model

  1. Display the big Tour Building wheel.
  2. Click and hold down the Forward wedge.

    The Drag Distance indicator is displayed.

    NoteIf you click the Forward wedge once, the model moves forward 50% of the distance between the current location and the pivot point.
  3. Drag the cursor up or down to change the distance from which you view the model.
  4. Release the button on your pointing device to return to the wheel.
 Command entry: NAVSWHEEL
 Shortcut menu: Right-click over the drawing window and click SteeringWheels
 Toolbar: Status bar SteeringWheels
