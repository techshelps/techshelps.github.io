To turn all wipeout frames on or off

  1. Click Annotate tabMarkup panelWipeout. At the command prompt, enter wipeout.
  2. At the command prompt, enter f (Frames).
  3. Enter on or off and press ENTER.
 Command entry: WIPEOUT
