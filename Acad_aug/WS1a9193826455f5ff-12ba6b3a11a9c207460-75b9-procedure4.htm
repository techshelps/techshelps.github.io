To automatically fit the model after a view orientation

  1. Click View tab Views panel ViewCube. At the Command prompt, enter navvcube.
  2. Right-click the ViewCube tool, and click ViewCube Settings.
  3. In the ViewCube Settings dialog box, under When Clicking on the ViewCube, click Zoom to Extents After View Change.

    Clicking a predefined area of the ViewCube tool reorients the model and fits the model to the window.

  4. Click OK.
 Command entry: NAVVCUBE
