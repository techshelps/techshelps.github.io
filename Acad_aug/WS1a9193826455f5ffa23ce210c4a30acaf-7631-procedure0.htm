To create a camera

  1. Click Render tab Camera panel Create Camera.
  2. Click in the drawing to specify the camera’s location.
  3. Click again in the drawing to specify a target location.
  4. Do one of the following:
    • If you are finished setting up the camera, press Enter.
    • To further define camera properties, right-click and select from the list of options. Then press Enter to finish setting up the camera.
     Command entry: CAMERA
     Toolbar: View
