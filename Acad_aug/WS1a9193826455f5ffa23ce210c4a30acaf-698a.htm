Grips are small squares that are displayed at strategic points on objects that you have selected with a pointing device. You can drag these grips to edit objects directly and quickly.

Topics in this section

  • Use Grip Modes

    You can drag grips to perform any stretch, move, rotate, scale, or mirror operations. The editing operation you choose to perform is called a grip mode.

  • Make Multiple Copies with Grips

    You can create multiple copies of objects as you modify them with any of the grip modes.

  • Control Grips in Blocks

    You can specify whether a block displays a single grip or multiple grips.
