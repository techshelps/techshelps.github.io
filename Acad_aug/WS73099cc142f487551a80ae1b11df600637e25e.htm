Add a Radial Constraint Parameter

ConceptProcedureQuick Reference
 
 
 

A radial constraint parameter controls the radius of a circle, arc, or polyline arc segment.

While a radial dimension represents the radius of a circle or arc,, a radial constraint parameter defines and controls the radius.

In the example below, a radial constraint parameter has been applied to the polyline arc in the block definition on the left. The polyline has been manipulated in the block definition on the right. As a result, the radius of the arc remains fixed while the polyline segments around it change.
