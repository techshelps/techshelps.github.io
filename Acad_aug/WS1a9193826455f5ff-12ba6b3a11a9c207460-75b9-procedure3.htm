To use animated transitions when reorienting a view to a preset orientation

  1. Click View tab Views panel ViewCube. At the Command prompt, enter navvcube.
  2. Right-click the ViewCube tool, and click ViewCube Settings.
  3. In the ViewCube Settings dialog box, under When Clicking on the ViewCube, select Use View Transitions when Switching Views.

    Transitions from one view to another appear animated when you click a predefined area on the ViewCube tool.

  4. Click OK.
 Command entry: NAVVCUBE
