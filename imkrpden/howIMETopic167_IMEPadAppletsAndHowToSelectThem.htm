IME Pad Applets and How to Select Them

IME Pad has the following three applets:

  1. Stroke Count Applet
    Search for Hanja by stroke count.
  2. Radical Applet
    Search for Hanja by radical.
  3. Handwriting Applet
    Search for Hanja by drawing its strokes.

  1. On the Language Bar, click IME Pad (), then click the applet you wish to use.

  2. To switch to a different applet, select the desired applet from the Applet Bar.
    Alternatively: click the IME Pad menu (), select Applet Menu, and click the desired applet.

Tips

  • If the applet you want to use is not displayed, try adding the applet to the Applet Bar.
