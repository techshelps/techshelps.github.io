About IME Pad

Microsoft(R) IME Pad (hereafter referred to as IME Pad) is the generic name for various character input tools.
Below are some of the things you can do using IME Pad:

  • Enter Hanja when you do not know its phonetic reading by drawing, stroke count, radical, etc...
    Enter characters using a mouse, a pen, etc...

Tips

  • The Handwriting applet lets you look up Hanja without its phonetic reading by drawing its strokes. You can also input non-Hanja characters by drawing them.
