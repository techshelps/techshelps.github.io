Common Alarm Switch Valid Level Configuration(9~16)

Parameter Name

Common Alarm Switch Valid Level Configuration(9~16)

Description

This parameter specifies the level specifications of special Boolean alarms (numbered 9 to 16). There are two specifications: high level alarm and low level alarm. The bit 0 indicates high level alarm and the bit 1 indicates low level alarm. By default, all the alarms belong to low level alarm.

GUI Value Range

[Alarm Switch 16 (Low Level), Alarm Switch 15 (Low Level), Alarm Switch 14 (Low Level), Alarm Switch 13 (Low Level), Alarm Switch 12 (Low Level), Alarm Switch 11 (Low Level), Alarm Switch 10 (Low Level), Alarm Switch 9 (Low Level)]
