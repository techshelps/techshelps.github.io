Mean Number of Blocked Channels in Cells of Different Types

Measurement Counter

AR3570:CELL_CH_BLOCK_NUM_SD_AVR

AR3571:CELL_CH_BLOCK_NUM_FR_AVR

AR3572:CELL_CH_BLOCK_NUM_HR_AVR

Description

This measurement provides the average number of blocked channels (SDCCH/TCHF/TCHH) in a cell controlled by the BSC in the granularity period.

Unit

Integer number or integer.

Measurement Point

In the granularity period, the counters are calculated as follows.

Formula

Mean Number of Blocked Channels (SDCCH) = [Total Number of Blocked Channels (SDCCH)]/[Number of Attempts to Scan Channel States]

Mean Number of Blocked Channels (TCHF) = [Total Number of Blocked Channels (TCHF)]/[Number of Attempts to Scan Channel States]

Mean Number of Blocked Channels (TCHH) = [Total Number of Blocked Channels (TCHH)]/[Number of Attempts to Scan Channel States]
