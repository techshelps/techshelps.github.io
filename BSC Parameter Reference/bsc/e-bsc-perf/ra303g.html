Success Rate of Call Setup (Immediate Assignment)

Measurement Counter

RA303G: CELL_ESTB_IND_SUCC_RATE_IMM_ASS

Description

This counter provides the ratio of the EST IND messages received by the BSC from the BTS to the CHAN RQD messages received by the BSC when the access reason of the MS in the measured cell is not Packet Service.

Unit

%.

Measurement Point

None.

Formula

Success Rate of Immediate Assignments = [Call Setup Indications (Circuit Service)] x {100}/[Channel Requests (Circuit Service)]
