Rate of Transmitted Error Frames

Measurement Counter

RL9A08: CELL_GABIS_TRAU_TRAN_ERR_FR_RATE

Description

This measurement provides the proportion of error TRAU frames to the total TRAU frames during uplink transmission in a granularity period. The number of error frames includes the number of received out-of-synchronization TRAU frames and the number of received check error TRAU frames. The counter indicates the transmission quality. Usually, the satellite and microwave transmission may cause a high rate of transmitted error frames.

Unit

%.

Measurement Point

After a granularity period, this counter is measured on the basis of the related original counter. The calculation method is as follows: (Number of error frames in the uplink/Total number of frames in the uplink) x 100. For details, see the following section.

Formula

Rate of Transmitted Error Frames = ([Number of Received Out-of-Synchronization TRAU Frames] + [Number of Received Check Error TRAU Frames]) x 100/([Number of Received Out-of-Synchronization TRAU Frames] + [Number of Received Check Error TRAU Frames] + [Number of Received Normal TRAU Frames] + [Number of Received Information TRAU Frames])
