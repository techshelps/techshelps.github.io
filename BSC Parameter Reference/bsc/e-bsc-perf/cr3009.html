Number of Initially Configured Channels (CBCH)

Measurement Counter

CR3009: CELL_CH_CFG_NUM_CBCH

Description

CR3009:CELL_CH_CFG_NUM_CBCH provides the number of configured CBCHs (BCCH+CBCH or SDCCH+CBCH) in the cells on all frequency bands controlled by the BSC at each sampling point.

Unit

Integer number or integer.

Measurement Point

In the granularity period, the counters are calculated as follows.

Formula

Number of Initially Configured Channels (CBCH) =

[Number of Initially Configured Channels (CBCH) (900/850 cell)] +

[Number of Initially Configured Channels (CBCH) (1800/1900 cell)]
