Failed Reconnections to Old Channels upon External Outgoing Cell Handover Failure per BSC

Measurement Counter

ZCH932C:BSCRPT_INTERBSC_OUTCELL_HO_FAIL_RECONN_FAIL

Description

This measurement provides the number of outgoing external inter-cell handover failures due to T8 expiry in all the cells controlled by a BSC. The channel involved in the measurement can be the SDCCH, TCHF, or TCHH.

After the source BSC sends an HO CMD message to the MS through the source cell, the T8 timer is started and waits for a CLR CMD message sent by the MSC.

According to the counters External Outgoing Cell Handover Requests per BSC and External Outgoing Cell Handover Commands per BSC, the outgoing external inter-cell handover per BSC can be well understood. According to the counters related to incoming external inter-cell handover, the incoming external inter-cell handover per BSC can be well understood.

The handover can be performed between GSM900/850 and GSM900/850, between GSM1800/1900 and GSM1800/1900, between GSM900/850 and GSM1800/1900, or between GSM1800/1900 and GSM900/850.

The handover between different signaling points in a BSC is also called outgoing external inter-cell handover.

Unit

Integer number or integer.

Measurement Point

This counter is measured according to the following formula when the measurement period ends. For details on the measurement point, see "Measurement Point" of each counter in the formula.

Formula

Failed Reconnections to Old Channels upon Internal Outgoing Cell Handover Failure per BSC=

Failed Outgoing Internal Inter-Cell Handovers (Timer Expired) (SDCCH) (Excluding Directed Retry)+

Failed Outgoing Internal Inter-Cell Handovers (Timer Expired) (TCHF) (Signaling Channel)+

Failed Outgoing Internal Inter-Cell Handovers (Timer Expired) (TCHH) (Signaling Channel)+ Failed Outgoing Internal Inter-Cell Handovers (Timer Expired) (TCHF) (Traffic Channel)+

Failed Outgoing Internal Inter-Cell Handovers (Timer Expired) (TCHH) (Traffic Channel)+

Failed Outgoing Internal Inter-Cell Handovers (Timer Expired) (Directed Retry)
