Average Number of Online EGPRS MSs

Measurement Counter

AS9349: CELL_USER_AVG_EGPRS_USERS

Description

This measurement provides the average number of online EGPRS MSs in a cell in a granularity period. This counter indicates the usage of PS services in a cell.

Unit

Integer number or integer.

Measurement Point

None.

Formula

Average Number of Online EGPRS MSs = [Number of Online EGPRS MSs]/[Sampling Times of Online MSs]
