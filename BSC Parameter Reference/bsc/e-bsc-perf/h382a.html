Failed Cell Incoming Handovers

Measurement Counter

H382A:CELLCELL_INCELL_HO_FAIL_CONG

H382M:CELLCELL_INCELL_HO_FAIL_OTHER

Description

In the incoming inter-cell handover procedure, if the handover fails, this counter is measured based on the cause of the handover failure. Along with Cell Incoming Handover Requests, this counter indicates the performance of incoming inter-cell handovers. Along with the counters related to the outgoing inter-cell handover, this counter indicates the performance of inter-cell handovers.

The handover failure can be caused by congestion or others.

In the measurement of the counter "H382M: Failed Cell Incoming Handovers (Other Causes)", the cause includes directed retry.

Unit

Integer number or integer.

Measurement Point

The originating cell and the target cell are GSM cells. For the measurement point, refer to Failed Internal Intra-Cell Handovers, Failed Incoming Internal Inter-Cell Handovers, and Failed Incoming External Inter-Cell Handovers. If the cause of the handover failure is Radio Resource Unavailable, the counter H382A: CELLCELL_INCELL_HO_FAIL_CONG is measured; otherwise, the counter H382M: CELLCELL_INCELL_HO_FAIL_OTHER is measured.

Formula

This is an original counter without involving any formula.
