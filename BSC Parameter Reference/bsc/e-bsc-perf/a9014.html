Total Duration of Uplink GPRS TBF (ms)

Measurement Counter

A9014:UP_GPRS_TBF_TOTAL_TIME

Description

This measurement provides the total duration of uplink GPRS TBF (ms) in a granularity period.

Unit

ms.

Measurement Point

The activation duration of a GPRS TBF refers to the period that starts when the GPRS TBF is successfully established and ends when the GPRS TBF is normally released. The measurement value is obtained by accumulating the activation duration of all uplink GPRS TBFs that are normally released in a granularity period.

Formula

This is an original counter without involving any formula.
