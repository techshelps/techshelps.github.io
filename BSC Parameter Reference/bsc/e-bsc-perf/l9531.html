Maximum Downlink Throughput of GPRS Users LLC PDU

Measurement Counter

L9531: CELL_LLC_TX_DOWN_GPRS_LLC_PDU_THRPUT_RATE_MAX

Description

This measurement provides the maximum downlink rate at the LLC layer of GPRS users in a granularity period.

Unit

Kbit/s.

Measurement Point

Each time the service transmission is complete on the downlink TBF of GPRS users, the transmission rate of LLC PDUs is calculated. The transmission rate is equal to the number of transmitted bytes divided by the transmission duration. In a granularity period, the maximum transmission rate that is calculated each time is taken as the maximum throughput of downlink GPRS users LLC PDU.

Formula

This is an original counter without involving any formula.
