Failed Queuing Attempts

Measurement Counter

R3136:CELL_QUEUE_FAIL_TIMES_PRE-EMPTION

R3137:CELL_QUEUE_FAIL_TIMES_TIMEOUT

R3138:CELL_QUEUE_FAIL_TIMES_DYNADJ_TIMEOUT

Description

In an assignment procedure or incoming-BSC handover procedure, the MSC requests for a TCH, but the BSC finds no TCH available for assignment. If both the BSC and the call support preemption, the BSC performs the preemption procedure. If the preemption is successful and the system supports queuing, the queuing procedure is performed. Meanwhile, the related timer is triggered to wait for an idle TCH. When the timer expires, the measurement of the counter R3136:CELL_QUEUE_FAIL_TIMES_PRE-EMPTION is triggered.

In the assignment procedure or incoming-BSC handover procedure, the MSC requests for a TCH, but the BSC finds no TCH available for assignment. If the BSC does not perform the preemption procedure or dynamic adjustment procedure and if the BSC supports queuing, the call queuing procedure is performed. Meanwhile, the related timer is triggered to wait for an idle TCH. When the timer expires, the measurement of the counter R3137:CELL_QUEUE_FAIL_TIMES_TIMEOUT is triggered.

In the assignment procedure or incoming-BSC handover procedure, the MSC requests for a TCHF or a TCHH, but the BSC finds no TCHF or TCHH available for assignment. If the BSC supports dynamic adjustment procedure and if the conditions for dynamic adjustment are met, the BSC performs the dynamic adjustment procedure. If the BSC also supports queuing, the call queuing procedure is performed. Meanwhile, the related timer is triggered to wait for an idle TCHF or TCHH. When the timer expires, the measurement of the counter R3138:CELL_QUEUE_FAIL_TIMES_DYNADJ_TIMEOUT is triggered.

Unit

Integer number or integer.

Measurement point

In an assignment procedure or incoming-BSC handover procedure, the measurement of the counter R3136:CELL_QUEUE_FAIL_TIMES_PRE-EMPTION is triggered when the preemption and call queuing procedures are performed and when the timer that is triggered to wait for an idle TCH expires.

In an assignment procedure or incoming-BSC handover procedure, the system does not perform the preemption procedure or the dynamic adjustment procedure. If the call queuing procedure is performed, the measurement of the counter R3137:CELL_QUEUE_FAIL_TIMES_TIMEOUT is triggered when the timer that is triggered to wait for an idle TCH expires.

In an assignment procedure or incoming-BSC handover procedure, the system performs the dynamic adjustment procedure or call queuing procedure. The measurement of the counter R3138: CELL_QUEUE_FAIL_TIMES_DYNADJ_TIMEOUT is triggered when the timer that is triggered to wait for an idle TCHF/TCHH expires.

Formula

This is an original counter without involving any formula.
