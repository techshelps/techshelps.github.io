Number of BTS Loopback Failures after the Handover during the BSC Loopback

Measurement Counter

R382c:CELL_LOCALSWITCH_BSCLOOP_HO_BTS_LOOP_FAIL

Description

A handover is initiated in the stable BSC loopback state (BSC loopback is successful and the calling and called MSs are in loopback state). After the handover is complete, the BTS loopback procedure is restarted. The loopback, however, fails because of various reasons. This measurement provides the number of BTS loopback failures.

Unit

Integer number or integer.

Measurement Point

A handover is initiated when the calling and called MSs are in the stable BSC loopback state. After the handover is complete, the BTS loopback procedure is restarted. In other words, the loopback start conditions are determined, the BTS local switch call detection is performed, and the BTS loopback start request message is sent. The loopback, however, fails because of various reasons (No Loopback Policy Available, Invalid State, Failures on the BTS Side, Territorial Resource Request Failure, Timer Expired, Not Support by the BTS, and other causes). In this case, this counter is measured in the cell where the loopback is initiated after the handover.

Formula

This is an original counter without involving any formula.
