Downlink Throughput of GPRS Users LLC PDU (Kbit/s)

Measurement Counter

AL9529: CELL_LLC_TX_DOWN_GPRS_LLC_PDU_THRPUT_RATE_AVG

Description

This measurement provides the downlink throughput of LLC PDUs of the GPRS MSs in a granularity period.

Unit

Kbit/s.

Measurement Point

In a granularity period, Downlink Throughput of GPRS Users LLC PDU (Kbit/s) is equal to Throughput of Downlink GPRS Users LLC PDU divided by Total Bytes of Downlink GPRS Users LLC PDU.

Formula

Downlink Throughput of GPRS Users LLC PDU (Kbit/s) = [Throughput of Downlink GPRS Users LLC PDU]/([Total Bytes of Downlink GPRS Users LLC PDU] x 8/1024)
