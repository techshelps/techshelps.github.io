Number of Cell Ping-Pong Reselections by MS

Measurement Counter

H9009: MOBILE_PP_CELL_RESELECT_RATE

Description

This measurement provides the number of times ping-pong cell reselection is performed by an MS in a granularity period.

Unit

Integer number or integer.

Measurement Point

When the MS is handed over from cell A to another cell, and is then handed over from another cell to cell A within 20s, the counter is incremented by one.

Formula

This is an original counter without involving any formula.
