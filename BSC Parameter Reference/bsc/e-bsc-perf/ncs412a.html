Number of MRs on Downlink TCHF (Receive Quality Rank N)

Measurement Counter

NCS412A:TRX_FR_DOWN_RX_QLTY_0_NEW

NCS412B:TRX_FR_DOWN_RX_QLTY_1_NEW

NCS412C:TRX_FR_DOWN_RX_QLTY_2_NEW

NCS412D:TRX_FR_DOWN_RX_QLTY_3_NEW

NCS412E:TRX_FR_DOWN_RX_QLTY_4_NEW

NCS412F:TRX_FR_DOWN_RX_QLTY_5_NEW

NCS412G:TRX_FR_DOWN_RX_QLTY_6_NEW

NCS412H:TRX_FR_DOWN_RX_QLTY_7_NEW

Description

The measurement of the preceding counters provides the number of times in the measurement report with the downlink receive quality rank ranging from 0 to 7 over the TCHF.

Unit

Integer number or integer.

Measurement Point

After receiving the CONNECT ACK message, the BSC measures these counters based on the downlink receive level (0-7) on the TCHF when receiving the valid MR or pre-processing MR, and stops to measure these counters when receiving the DISCONNECT or CLEAR COMMAND.

Formula

This is an original counter without involving any formula.
