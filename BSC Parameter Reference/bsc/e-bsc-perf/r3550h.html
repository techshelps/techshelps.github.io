Traffic Volume on Signaling Channels (SDCCH) per Service Type

Measurement Counter

R3550H:CELL_SD_CH_TRAF_ERL_SPEECH

R3550I:CELL_SD_CH_TRAF_ERL_SMS

R3550J:CELL_SD_CH_TRAF_ERL_USSD

R3550L:CELL_SD_CH_TRAF_ERL_NOT_CERTAIN

Description

This measurement provides the traffic volume of different services on the signaling channels (SDCCH) in the granularity period. These services include speech service, short message service, USSD service, and other unknown services.

Unit

Erl.

Measurement Point

In the granularity period, this counter is calculated according to the following formula.

Formula

Traffic Volume of Speech Service on SDCCH = [Number of Busy SDCCH Channels for Speech Service]/[Number of Attempts to Scan SDCCH States]

Traffic Volume of Short Message Service on SDCCH = [Number of Busy SDCCH Channels for Short Message Service]/[Number of Attempts to Scan SDCCH States]

Traffic Volume of USSD Service on SDCCH = [Number of Busy SDCCH Channels for USSD Service]/[Number of Attempts to Scan SDCCH States]

Traffic Volume of Unknown Service on SDCCH = [Number of Busy SDCCH Channels for Unknown Service]/[Number of Attempts to Scan SDCCH States]
