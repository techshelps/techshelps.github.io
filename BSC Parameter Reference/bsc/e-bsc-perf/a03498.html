Attempted VGCS TCH Handovers for Uplink Quality

Measurement Counter

A03498: CELL_GCC_HO_REQ_UL_QLTY

Description

After the originating BSC makes the handover decision, the originating BSC analyzes the reason for the handover. This measurement provides the number of handovers for uplink quality. Along with Successful VGCS TCH Handovers for Uplink Quality, this measurement reflects the performance of the handovers for uplink quality.

Unit

Integer number or integer.

Measurement Point

The measurement point is specified in the following three situations:
  • (1) In the case of the intra-cell handover, this counter is incremented by one when the originating BSC sends a HANDOVER REQUIRED message to the MSC for uplink quality. The measurement point is A, as shown in Figure 1.
  • (2) In the case of the inter-cell handover, this counter is incremented by one when the BSC determines to initiate the internal inter-cell handover based on the measurement report for uplink quality. The measurement point is A, as shown in Figure 2.
  • (3) In the case of the inter-BSC handover, this counter is incremented by one when the originating BSC sends a HANDOVER_REQUIRD message to the MSC for uplink quality. The measurement point is A, as shown in Figure 3.
