Measurement of Incoming Inter-RAT Inter-Cell Handover Requests

Measurement Counter

H3600:CELL_INTERRAN_INCELL_HO_REQ_SD

H3609:CELL_INTERRAN_INCELL_HO_REQ_TCH

Description

When a BSC receives an HO REQ message from the MSC for an MS in a neighboring cell from another network (such as UMTS or TD-SCDMA), an inter-RAT inter-cell handover is initiated. The number of incoming inter-RAT inter-cell handover requests is measured based on the type of the target channel. Together with Incoming Inter-RAT Inter-Cell Handover Responses and Failed Incoming Inter-RAT Inter-Cell Handovers, this counter indicates the performance of the incoming inter-RAT inter-cell handover.

This measurement covers the handovers on the SDCCH and the TCH.

Unit

Integer number or integer.

Measurement Point

As shown in Figure 1, this counter is measured at A if the BSC receives an HO REQ from the MSC and determines an inter-RAT inter-cell handover (TDD).

Figure 1 Measurement of the counters related to the incoming inter-RAT inter-cell handover

A

Measurement point of Incoming Inter-RAT Inter-Cell Handover Requests

Measurement point of Incoming Inter-RAT Inter-Cell Handover Detection Messages

Formula

This is an original counter without involving any formula.
