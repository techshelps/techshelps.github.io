Successful Internal Inter-Cell Handovers per BSC (900/850 to 1800/1900)

Measurement Counter

ZCH903Y:BSCRPT_INTRABSC_INTERCELL_HO_SUCC_900_1800

Description

This measurement provides the number of successful internal inter-cell handovers of all the cells controlled by a BSC (900/850-1800/1900).

The channel involved in the measurement can be the SDCCH or the TCH.

According to the counter Attempted Internal Inter-Cell Handovers per BSC (900/850 to 1800/1900), the internal inter-cell handovers per BSC can be well understood.

The handover frequency band range is 900/850 to 1800/1900.

Unit

Integer number or integer.

Measurement Point

This counter is measured according to the following formula when the measurement period ends. For details on the measurement point, see "Measurement Point" of each counter in the formula.

Formula

Successful Internal Inter-Cell Handovers per BSC (900/850 to 1800/1900) = ( Incoming Internal Inter-Cell Handover Requests (SDCCH) (900/850-1800/1900) + Incoming Internal Inter-Cell Handover Requests (TCH) (900/850-1800/1900)) -

( Failed Incoming Internal Inter-Cell Handovers (SDCCH) (900/850-1800/1900) + Failed Incoming Internal Inter-Cell Handovers (TCH) (900/850-1800/1900))
