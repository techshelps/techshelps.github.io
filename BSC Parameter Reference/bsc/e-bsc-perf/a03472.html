Unsuccessful Internal Inter-Cell VGCS TCH Handovers (Timer Expired)

Measurement Counter

A03472: CELL_GCC_INTRABSC_INTERCELL_HO_FAIL_EXP

Description

After the internal inter-cell VGCS TCH handover procedure is started, the originating cell sends a HANDOVER COMMAND message to the MS. After the MS successfully accesses the target cell, the target cell requests the originating cell to clear the original resources. Thus, the internal inter-cell handover procedure is complete. If any timer expires during the internal inter-cell VGCS TCH handover procedure, the handover procedure fails. This measurement provides the number of unsuccessful internal inter-cell VGCS TCH handovers due to timer expiration.

Unit

Integer number or integer.

Measurement Point

This counter is incremented by one when the internal inter-cell VGCS TCH handover procedure fails due to the timer expiration. The measurement point of this counter is A in the unsuccessful internal inter-cell VGCS TCH handover procedure, as shown in Figure 1.

Figure 1 Unsuccessful internal inter-cell VGCS TCH handover procedure

Formula

This is an original counter without involving any formula.
