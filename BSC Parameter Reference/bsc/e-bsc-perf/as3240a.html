Average MS Power Level of AMR Call

Measurement Counter

AS3240A:CELL_AMR_MS_POWER_LEVEL_AVE

Description

This measurement provides the average MS power level of the AMR calls in the cell per granularity period.

Unit

Integer number or integer.

Measurement Point

In the granularity period, this counter is calculated according to the following formula.

Formula

Average MS Power Level of AMR Call = [MS Power Level of AMR Call]/[Total Number of Measurement Reports for AMR with MS Power Level]
