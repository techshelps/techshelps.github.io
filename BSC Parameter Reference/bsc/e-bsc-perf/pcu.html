PCU Measurement(PCU)

Overview

PCU Measurement provides the measurement on the number of messages sent from the PCU to the BSC. The measurement can be performed only when the external PCU is configured (the BSC is connected to the external PCU through the Pb interface, and the PCU performs the PS processing).
