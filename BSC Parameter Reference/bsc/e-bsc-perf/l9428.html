Maximum Uplink Throughput of EGPRS Users LLC PDU (Kbit/s)

Measurement Counter

L9428: CELL_LLC_TX_UP_EGPRS_LLC_PDU_THRPUT_RATE_MAX

Description

This measurement provides the maximum uplink throughput of LLC PDUs sent by an EGPRS MS in a granularity period.

Unit

Kbit/s.

Measurement Point

Every time the uplink TBF of EGPRS MS completes service transmission, the throughput of LLC PDUs is calculated. The throughput is equal to the number of transmitted bytes divided by the transmission duration. In a granularity period, the maximum calculated throughput is used as the value of the counter.

Formula

This is an original counter without involving any formula.
