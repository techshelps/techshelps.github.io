Uplink Throughput of EGPRS Users LLC PDU (Kbit/s)

Measurement Counter

AL9426: CELL_LLC_TX_UP_EGPRS_LLC_PDU_THRPUT_RATE_AVG

Description

This measurement provides the uplink throughput of LLC PDUs of the EGPRS MSs in a granularity period.

Unit

Kbit/s.

Measurement Point

In a granularity period, Uplink Throughput of EGPRS Users LLC PDU (Kbit/s) is equal to Throughput of Uplink EGPRS Users LLC PDU divided by Total Bytes of Uplink EGPRS Users LLC PDUs.

Formula

Uplink Throughput of EGPRS Users LLC PDU (Kbit/s) = [Throughput of Uplink EGPRS Users LLC PDU]/(Total Bytes of Uplink EGPRS Users LLC PDUs x 8/1024)
