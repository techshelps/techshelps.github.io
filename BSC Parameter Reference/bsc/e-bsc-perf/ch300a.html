Number of Intra-BSC Intra-Cell Handover Successes During BSC Loopback

Measurement Counter

CH300a: CELL_INTRACELL_HO_SUCC_BSC_LOOP

Description

This measurement counter indicates the number of intra-BSC intra-cell handover successes during BSC loopback. It is equal to the Internal Intra-Cell Handover Requests During BSC Loopback minus the Failed Internal Intra-Cell Handovers During BSC Loopback.

Unit

Integer number or integer.

Measurement Point

None.

Formula

Number of Intra-BSC Intra-Cell Handover Successes During BSC Loopback = Internal Intra-Cell Handover Requests During BSC Loopback - Failed Internal Intra-Cell Handovers During BSC Loopback
