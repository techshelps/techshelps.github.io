Average Downlink BER Probability

Measurement Counter

S4733B:CELL_DL_SIG_MEANBEP_AVR

Description

During a granularity period, every time the BSC receives an EMR or pre-processed EMR from the BTS, the BSC accumulates the MEAN_BEP in the EMR. At the end of the granularity period, the value of this counter is the result of dividing the accumulative value of MEAN_BEP by the number of EMRs.

Unit

None.

Measurement Point

In the granularity period, the counter is calculated as follows.

Formula

Average Downlink BER Probability = [Downlink BER Probability]/([Number of EMRs on the TCHF] + [Number of EMRs on the TCHH])
