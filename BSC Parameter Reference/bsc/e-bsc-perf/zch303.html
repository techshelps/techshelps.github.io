Successful Internal Intra-Cell Handovers per BSC

Measurement Counter

ZCH303:BSCRPT_INTRACELL_HO_SUCC

Description

This counter measures the number of successful internal intra-cell handovers per BSC. The channel involved in the measurement can be the SDCCH, TCHF, or TCHH.

The handover involved in the measurement can be intra-cell handover, intra-cell handover due to channel type conversion, or intra-cell handover due to IUO cell conversion.

According to the counter Internal Intra-Cell Handover Requests per BSC, the internal intra-cell handovers per BSC can be well understood.

Unit

Integer number or integer.

Measurement Point

This counter is measured according to the following formula when the measurement period ends. For details on the measurement point, see "Measurement Point" of each counter in the formula.

Formula

Successful Internal Intra-Cell Handovers per BSC = Successful Internal Intra-Cell Handovers

(That is, the total of the original counter Successful Internal Intra-Cell Handovers in all the cells of the BSC.)
