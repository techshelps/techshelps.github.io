CHAN ACT NACK Messages Sent by BTS in External Incoming Cell Handover Procedure per BSC

Measurement Counter

ZCR9350B:BSCRPT_INTERBSC_INCELL_HO_CH_ACT_NACK

Description

This measurement provides the number of CHAN ACTIV NACK messages sent by the BTS in all the cells controlled by a BSC in the incoming external inter-cell handover procedure.

In the incoming external inter-cell handover procedure, the target BSC sends a CHAN ACTIV message to the BTS. If the BTS sends a CHAN ACTIV NACK message to the BSC, it indicates that the handover fails. The BSC collects the number of CHAN ACTIV NACK messages sent by the BTS in the incoming external inter-cell handover procedure in the target cell according to the channel type. The channel involved in the measurement can be SDCCH or TCH. The TCH can be a traffic channel or a signaling channel.

Unit

Integer number or integer.

Measurement Point

BSC counters are calculation counters. This counter is measured according to the formula below when the measurement period ends. For details on the measurement point, see "Channel Activation Measurement per Cell".

Formula

CHAN ACT NACK Messages Sent by BTS in External Incoming Cell Handover Procedure per BSC =

CHAN ACT NACK Messages Sent by BTS in External Incoming Cell Handover Procedure per BSC +

CHAN ACT NACK Messages Sent by BTS in External Incoming Cell Handover Procedure per BSC +

HAN ACTIV NACK Messages Sent by BTS in Incoming External Inter-Cell Handover Procedure (TCHH) per BSC
