Number of Downlink Frames Among the HR Frames in the Customized MR

Measurement Counter

S4555: TRX_EXT_HR_TCH_DN_NUM

Description

When decoding the customized extended MR or pre-processed customized extended MR from the BTS, the BSC checks whether the EI_FRAME cell is contained in the MS. If the cell is contained, and the cell shows that the frame type is HR, then the BSC decodes the number of downlink frames and this counter is incremented by the number of downlink frames.

Unit

Integer number or integer.

Measurement Point

After receiving a customized extended MR or pre-processed customized extended MR from the BTS, the BSC decodes the MR. This counter is measured when the EI_FRAME cell is contained in the MS and the number of HR downlink frames is contained in the EI_FRAME cell.

Formula

This is an original counter without involving any formula.
