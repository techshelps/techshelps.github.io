LAPD Link Measurement

Description

The LAPD link is the signaling transfer channel between the BSC and the BTS, and between the BSC and the PCU.

LAPD Link Measurement is used to measure the number of Q921 protocol frames related to the LAPD link on the BSC side.

Measurement Object

LAPD Link
