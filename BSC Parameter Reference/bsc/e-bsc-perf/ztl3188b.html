RACH Overloads due to CS Service Counted Through the Indications from the Abis Interface per BSC

Measurement Counter

ZTL3188B:BSCRPT_CCCH_LOAD_RACH_OVERLOAD_ABIS

Description

This measurement provides the number of RACH overloads due to the CS service counted through the indications from the Abis interface per BSC, that is, the total of RACH overloads due to the CS service counted through the indications from the Abis interface in all cells.

Unit

Integer number or integer.

Measurement Point

This counter is measured according to the following formula when the measurement period ends. For details on the measurement point, see "MSG CCCH LOAD IND (RACH) Messages Sent on Abis Interface".

Formula

RACH Overloads due to CS Service Counted Through the Indications from the Abis Interface per BSC = MSG CCCH LOAD IND (RACH) Messages Sent on Abis Interface
