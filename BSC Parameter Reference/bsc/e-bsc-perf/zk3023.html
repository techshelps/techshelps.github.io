Successful TCH Seizures per BSC (Signaling Channel)

Measurement Counter

ZK3023:BSCRPT_KPI_TCH_SUCC_TIMES_SIG

Description

In the very early TCH assignment procedure, this measurement provides the number of successful attempts to activate the TCHs assigned by the BSC.

Unit

Integer number or integer.

Measurement Point

This counter is measured according to the following formula when the measurement period ends. For details on the measurement point, see "Successful TCH Seizures (Signaling Channel)".

Formula

Successful TCH Seizures per BSC (Signaling Channel) = Successful TCH Seizures (Signaling Channel)

(That is, the total of the original counter Successful TCH Seizures (Signaling Channel) in all the cells of the BSC.)
