Mean Signal Strength Difference Between Serving Cell and Neighbor Cell

Measurement Counter

AS362:CELL_SCELL_NCELL_DIFF_LEV_AVR

Description

The system accumulates the receive level difference between the serving cell and each neighbor cell contained in the MRs from the MS. At the end of a granularity period, the accumulated numbers are divided by the number of MRs containing the corresponding neighbor cells. Then, the counters are obtained.

Unit

dBm.

Measurement Point

In the granularity period, this counter is calculated according to the following formula.

Formula

Mean Signal Strength Difference Between Serving Cell and Neighbor Cell = ([Strength of Signals in serving Cells] - [Strength of Signals in Neighbor Cells])/[MRs of Neighbor Cells].
