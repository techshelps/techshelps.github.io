Total Frame Erasion Rate of the AMR FR in the Customized MR

Measurement Counter

S4512A: TRX_EXT_AMR_TCHF_ERASE_FRAME_TOTAL_RATE

Description

This measurement provides the total frame erasion rate of the customized MRs over the AMR TCHF.

Frame erasion rate reflects the bit error rate of the frames received by the DSP on the TRX of the BTS.

Unit

None.

Measurement Point

After receiving a customized extended MR or pre-processed customized extended MR from the BTS over the AMR TCHF, the BSC decodes the MR. This counter is incremented by the value of the DelFrame when the DelFrame cell is contained in the MS and the BSC decodes the value of the DelFrame.

Formula

This is an original counter without involving any formula.
