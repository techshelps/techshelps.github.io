Successful SDCCH Seizures per BSC

Measurement Counter

ZK3003:BSCRPT_KPI_SD_SUCC

Description

In an SDCCH immediate assignment procedure, the BTS sends a CHANNEL ACTIVATION ACKNOWLEDGE message to the BSC. After receiving this message, the BSC regards that the MS successfully seizes the SDCCH. In an SDCCH handover procedure, the BTS sends a HANDOVER DETECTION message to the target cell. After receiving this message, the BSC regards that the MS successfully seizes the SDCCH.

This measurement involves the two cases mentioned above.

Unit

Integer number or integer.

Measurement Point

This counter is measured according to the following formula when the measurement period ends. For details on the measurement point, see "Successful SDCCH Seizures".

Formula

Successful SDCCH Seizures per BSC = Successful SDCCH Seizures

(That is, the total of the original counter Successful SDCCH Seizures in all the cells of the BSC.)
