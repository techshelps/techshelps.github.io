Ratio (RQI Greater Than or Equal To 55)

Measurement Counter

S4439E: TRX_RQI_NUM_GT_55_RATE

Description

This measurement provides the ratio of MRs with the RQI greater than or equal to 55 to all the MRs with RQI in the granularity period.

Unit

%.

Measurement Point

In the granularity period, this counter is measured by using the following formula.

Formula

Ratio (RQI Greater Than or Equal To 55) = [Number of Measurement Reports (RQI Greater Than or Equal To 55)]/([Number of Measurement Reports (RQI = 0 to 1)] + [Number of Measurement Reports (RQI = 1 to 2)]

+ [Number of Measurement Reports (RQI = 2 to 3)] + [Number of Measurement Reports (RQI = 3 to 4)] + [Number of Measurement Reports (RQI = 4 to 5)]

+ [Number of Measurement Reports (RQI = 5 to 6)] + [Number of Measurement Reports (RQI = 6 to 7)] + [Number of Measurement Reports (RQI = 7 to 8)]

+ [Number of Measurement Reports (RQI = 8 to 9)] + [Number of Measurement Reports (RQI = 9 to 10)] + [Number of Measurement Reports (RQI = 10 to 11)] + [Number of Measurement Reports (RQI = 11 to 12)] + [Number of Measurement Reports (RQI = 12 to 13)] + [Number of Measurement Reports (RQI = 13 to 14)]

+ [Number of Measurement Reports (RQI = 14 to 15)] + [Number of Measurement Reports (RQI = 15 to 25)]

+ [Number of Measurement Reports (RQI = 25 to 35)] + [Number of Measurement Reports (RQI = 35 to 45)] )
