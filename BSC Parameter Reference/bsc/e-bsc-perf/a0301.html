SGSN-Initiated Paging Requests for CS Service

Measurement Counter

A0301: BSC_SGSN_PAGING_REQ_CS

Description

When an MS is called for CS service, the MSC sends the CS paging request to the MS over the Gb interface from the SGSN. This counter provides the total number of CS paging requests received by the BSC from the MSC, including the number of repeated paging requests. Refer to GSM Rec. 08.08.

Measurement object: BSC

NOTE:

This counter is valid only when the Gs interface between the MSC and the SGSN is available.

Unit

Integer number or integer

Measurement Point

This counter is measured when the BSC receives the PAGING REQUEST (CS) from the external PCU or directly from the SGSN when a built-in PCU is configured, at A2 as shown in Figure 1.

Figure 1 CS paging procedure

A1

Measurement point of MSC-Initiated Paging Requests for CS Service received by the BSC from the MSC over the A interface

A2

Measurement point of SGSN-Initiated Paging Requests for CS Service received by the BSC from the MSC over the Gs interface

A3

Measurement point of Delivered Paging Messages for CS Service and Delivered to BTS for CS Service

A4

Measurement point of SM Pagings Discarded on LAPD Link and CS Pagings Discarded on LAPD Link

Formula

This is an original counter without involving any formula.
