Analyzed Measurement of MRs

Measurement Counter

CR3557:CELL_TRAF_CH_TRAF_ULCELL

CR3558:CELL_TRAF_CH_TRAF_OLCELL

CR355A:CELL_TRAF_CH_TRAF

CS300:CELL_TRAF_CH_MR_NUM

CS3007:CELL_TRAF_CH_MR_NUM_FR

CS3008:CELL_TRAF_CH_MR_NUM_HR

CS301:CELL_SIG_CH_MR_NUM

CS3010:CELL_SIG_CH_MR_NUM_SD

CS3017:CELL_SIG_CH_MR_NUM_FR

CS3018:CELL_SIG_CH_MR_NUM_HR

Description

CR3557 refers to Traffic Volume of TCHs (Underlaid Subcell), that is, the average number of busy TCHs of the underlaid subcell.

CR3558 refers to Traffic Volume of TCHs (Overlaid Subcell), that is, the average number of busy TCHs of the overlaid subcell.

CR355A refers to Traffic Volume of TCHs, that is, the average number of busy TCHs.

CS300 refers to MRs on TCHs, that is, number of valid MRs received by the BSC from the TCH per granularity period.

CS3007 refers to MRs on TCHs (TCHF), that is, number of valid MRs received by the BSC from the TCHF per granularity period.

CS3008 refers to MRs on TCHs (TCHH), that is, number of valid MRs received by the BSC from the TCHH per granularity period.

CS301 refers to MRs on Signaling Channels, that is, number of valid MRs received by the BSC from the signaling channel (SDCCH or TCH) per granularity period.

CS3010 refers to MRs on Signaling Channels (SDCCH), that is, number of valid MRs received by the BSC from the SDCCH per granularity period.

CS3017 refers to MRs on Signaling Channels (TCHF), that is, number of valid MRs received by the BSC from the TCHF that carries signaling per granularity period.

CS3018 refers to MRs on Signaling Channels (TCHH), that is, number of valid MRs received by the BSC from the TCHH that carries signaling per granularity period.

Unit

Unit for traffic volume: Erl.

Unit for number of measurement reports: Integer number or integer.

Measurement Point

In the granularity period, the counters are calculated according to the following formulas.

Formula

Traffic Volume of TCHs (Underlaid Subcell) = [Mean Number of Busy TCHs (Underlaid Subcell)]

Traffic Volume of TCHs (Overlaid Subcell) = [Mean Number of Busy TCHs (Overlaid Subcell)]

Traffic Volume of TCHs = [Mean Number of Busy TCHs (TCHF) (900/850 Cell)] + [Mean Number of Busy TCHs (TCHF) (1800/1900 Cell)] + [Mean Number of Busy TCHs (TCHH) (900/850 Cell)] + [Mean Number of Busy TCHs (TCHH) (1800/1900 Cell)]

MRs on TCHs = [MRs on TCHs (TCHF)] + [MRs on TCHs (TCHH)]

MRs on TCHs (TCHF) = [MRs on TCHs (TCHF) (M900/850 Cell)] + [MRs on TCHs (TCHF) (M1800/1900 Cell)]

MRs on TCHs (TCHH) = [MRs on TCHs (TCHH) (M900/850 Cell)] + [MRs on TCHs (TCHH) (M1800/1900 Cell)]

MRs on Signaling Channels = [MRs on Signaling Channels (SDCCH)] + [MRs on Signaling Channels (TCHF)] + [MRs on Signaling Channels (TCHH)]

MRs on Signaling Channels (SDCCH) = [MRs on Signaling Channels (SDCCH) (M900/850 Cell)] + [MRs on Signaling Channels (SDCCH) (M1800/1900 Cell)]

MRs on Signaling Channels (TCHF) = [MRs on Signaling Channels (TCHF) (M900/850 Cell)] + [MRs on Signaling Channels (TCHF) (M1800/1900 Cell)]

MRs on Signaling Channels (TCHH) = [MRs on Signaling Channels (TCHH) (M900/850 Cell)] + [MRs on Signaling Channels (TCHH) (M1800/1900 Cell)]
