Number of Cell Reselections by MS at Short Interval

Measurement Counter

H9008: MOBILE_SHORT_INTV_OUTCELL_NUM

Description

This measurement provides the number of cell reselections by the MS at short interval in a granularity period.

Unit

Integer number or integer.

Measurement Point

The BSC records the time of the establishment of the MS context in a cell and the time of the FLUSH LL procedure initiated by the MS, and calculates the time interval between the establishment of the MS context and the occurrence of FLUSH LL procedure. This time interval is called the period during which the MS camps on a cell. In a granularity period, if the period during which the MS camps on a cell is smaller than or equal to 20 seconds, the counter is incremented by one.

The MS context is used to record the information associated with the MS.

Formula

This is an original counter without involving any formula.
