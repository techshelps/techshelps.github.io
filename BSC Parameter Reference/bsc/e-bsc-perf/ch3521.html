Failed Outgoing Inter-RAT Inter-Cell Handovers (TDD)

Measurement Counter

CH3521:CELL_INTERRAN_TDD_OUTCELL_HO_FAIL

Description

The outgoing inter-RAT inter-cell handover (TDD) may fail due to various reasons.

This measurement provides the number of failed outgoing inter-RAT inter-cell handover requests (TDD) initiated by the BSC. The measurement is based on the originating cell and involves handovers on various channels: SDCCH, TCHF, and TCHH.

Unit

Integer number or integer.

Measurement Point

At point A, B, C, D, E, and F, as shown in Figure 1 and Figure 2, the counter is measured when an outgoing inter-RAT inter-cell handover (TDD) fails.

Figure 1 Measurement of the counters related to failed outgoing inter-RAT inter-cell handover (excluding directed retry) (TDD)

Figure 2 Measurement of the counters related to failed outgoing inter-RAT inter-cell handover (directed retry) (TDD)

Formula

Failed Outgoing Inter-RAT Inter-Cell Handovers (TDD) = [Failed Outgoing Inter-RAT Inter-Cell Handovers (SDCCH) (Excluding Directed Retry) (TDD)] +

[Failed Outgoing Inter-RAT Inter-Cell Handovers (TCHF) (TDD)] +

[Failed Outgoing Inter-RAT Inter-Cell Handovers (TCHH) (TDD)]
