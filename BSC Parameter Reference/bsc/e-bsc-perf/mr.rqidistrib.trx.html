RQI Measurement per TRX

Description

This measurement unit provides the counters related to the RQI distribution on a TRX.

NOTE:

RQI is the acronym for Radio Quality Indication, it is the C/I ratio of a call and reflects the interference to a call. A measurement report contains RQI only when the call is on the AMR TRX. RQI is used in intra-cell handovers between a full-rate channel and a half-rate channel.

Measurement Object

TRX.
