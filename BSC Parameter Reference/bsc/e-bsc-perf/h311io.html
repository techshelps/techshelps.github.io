Number of Outgoing Internal Inter-Cell Handover Commands (Overlaid to Underlaid)

Measurement Counter

H311IO: CELL_INBSC_OUT_CELL_HO_CMD_OLCELL_ULCELL

Description

When a handover between different concentric cells is performed under the same BSC, the performance of the handover is measured. Taking the originating cell as the measurement object, this measurement provides the number of commands for handovers from the overlaid subcell of the originating cell to the underlaid subcell of the target cell. The handovers involve the handover between traffic channels, handover between signaling channels, and directed retry.

Unit

Integer number or integer.

Measurement Point

As shown in Figure 1, after receiving an MR from the MS, the BSC initiates a handover from the overlaid subcell of the originating cell to the underlaid subcell of the target cell. This counter is measured at B when the BSC sends a Handover Command message to the MS.

Figure 1 Outgoing internal inter-cell handover procedure

Formula

This is an original counter without involving any formula.
