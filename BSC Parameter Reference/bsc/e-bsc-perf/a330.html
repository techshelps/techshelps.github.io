Delivered Paging Messages for CS Service

Measurement Counter

A330:CELL_PAGES_CS

Description

When an MS is paged for CS services, the BSC receives a CS paging message from the MSC or PCU, and sends a PAGING CMD message to the corresponding cell based on the CI contained in the message. This counter is measured based on the cell. This counter provides the number of pagings over the Abis interface. Refer to GSM Rec. 08.58 and 04.08.

Unit

Integer number or integer

Measurement Point

This counter is measured when the BSC sends a PAGING COMMAND for CS service to the cell under the BTS, at A3 as shown in Figure 1.

Figure 1 CS paging procedure

A1

Measurement point of MSC-Initiated Paging Requests for CS Service received by the BSC from the MSC over the A interface

A2

Measurement point of SGSN-Initiated Paging Requests for CS Service received by the BSC from the MSC over the Gs interface

A3

Measurement point of Delivered Paging Messages for CS Service and Delivered to BTS for CS Service

A4

Measurement point of SM Pagings Discarded on LAPD Link and CS Pagings Discarded on LAPD Link

Formula

This is an original counter without involving any formula.
