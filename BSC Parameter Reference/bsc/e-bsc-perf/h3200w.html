Description

The BSC uses the handover algorithm to decide whether a handover is required based on the measurement reports. If the originating cell and the target cell are controlled by the same BSC, an internal inter-cell handover is initiated by the BSC. The specific counter provides the number of incoming internal inter-cell handover requests in the target cell in corresponding conditions. Together with the Incoming Internal Inter-Cell Handover Responses and the Failed Incoming Internal Inter-Cell Handovers, this counter determines the incoming internal inter-cell handover performance. Together with the counters related to the outgoing internal inter-cell handover, this counter determines the internal inter-cell handover performance.
