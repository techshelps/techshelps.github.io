Call Duration Measurement per BSC

Meaning

Call Duration Measurement per BSC is used to measure the call access duration and call establishment duration of all the calls controlled by a BSC.

Measurement Object

BSC.

Measurement Counter

The following table lists the measurement counters related to Call Duration Measurement per BSC.

Counter

Refer to Section...

AA032:BSC_CALL_ACCESS_DUR_AVG

Mean Duration of Call Access

AA033:BSC_CALL_ESTB_DUR_AVG

Mean Duration of Call Establishment
