Total Number of Failed Adjustments on HDLC Resources in HubBTS

Measurement Counter

L1103:BS_ADJ_IN_HUBBTS_FAIL_SUM

Description

In Abis over IP or Abis over HDLC mode, if the HubHDLC that bears the ESL link is available but another HubHDLC is faulty or congested, the adjustment on the channels is triggered. During the adjustment on the channels, the HubHDLC that bears the ESL link suddenly becomes faulty or all HubHDLCs are congested. The adjustment on the channels fails.

This measurement counter provides the total number of failed adjustments on HDLC resources in the HubBTS.

Unit

Integer number or integer.

Measurement Point

In a HubBTS, if the HubHDLC that bears the ESL link is faulty or all the HubHDLCs are congested, the adjustment on the channels fails. In this case, the counter is incremented by one.

Formula

This is an original counter without involving any formula.
