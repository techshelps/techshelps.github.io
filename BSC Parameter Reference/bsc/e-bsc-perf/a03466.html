Attempted Outgoing Internal Inter-Cell VGCS TCH Handovers

Measurement Counter

A03466: CELL_GCC_INTRABSC_OUTCELL_HO_REQ

Description

The internal inter-cell VGCS TCH handover is generally initiated because of the receive level, receive quality, frequency offset, quick PBGT, TA, load sharing, MSC intervention, rapid receive level drop, better cell, or OM. The originating cell and the target cell are two different cells in one BSC. This measurement provides the number of HO REQ messages sent by the originating cell (serving cell).

Unit

Integer number or integer.

Measurement Point

This counter is incremented by one when the originating cell (serving cell) sends an HO REQ message to the target cell after the handover algorithm decision. The orginating cell and the target cell are two different cells. In the signaling procedure, this counter is measured at point A in Figure 1.
