Measurement of Speech Disconnections per Cell

Description

The measurement unit "Measurement of Speech Disconnections per Cell" is used to measure the proportion of speech disconnections because of bad quality, after the BSC receives the Clear CMD message from the MSC.

Measurement Object

Cell

Measurement Counter

The following table lists the measurement counters related to Measurement of Speech Disconnections per Cell.
