Average Length of Uplink LLC PDU

Measurement Counter

AL9404: CELL_LLC_UP_LLC_PDU_AVG_LEN

Description

This measurement provides the average length of uplink LLC PDUs in a granularity period.

Unit

byte.

Measurement Point

None.

Formula

Average Length of Uplink LLC PDU = [Total Bytes of Uplink LLC PDUs Sent]/[Number of Uplink LLC PDUs sent]
