Number of Times CS Services Pre-empt Resources for PS Services

Measurement Counter

R3181: CELL_PS_PREEMPT_CS_TCH_TIMES

Description

This counter is used to measure the number of times of the circuit service pre-emption of the packet service.

Unit

Integer number or integer.

Measurement Point

This counter is measured when the packet service pre-empts the circuit service.

Formula

This is an original counter without involving any formula.
