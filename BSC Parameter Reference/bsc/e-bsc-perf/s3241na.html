The BTS Power Level of Non-AMR Call

Measurement Counter

S3241NA: CELL_NONAMR_BTS_PWR_LEV

Description

This measurement provides the total BTS power levels of the non-AMR calls in the cell within a granularity period.

Unit

Integer number or integer.

Measurement Point

When the BSC receives a valid measurement report or preprocesses the measurement report from the BTS in the measured cell, the BTS power levels are accumulated if the measurement report contains the BTS power level of a non-AMR call.

Formula

This is an original counter without involving any formula.
