Success Rate of Internal Intra-Cell Handover (Overlay to Underlay)

Measurement Counter

TH3031: CELL_INTRACELL_HO_SUCC_RATE_OLCELL_ULCELL

Description

This measurement counter provides the ratio of successful internal intra-cell handovers from the overlay to the underlay of a concentric cell. It is the ratio of Successful Internal Intra-Cell Handovers (Overlay to Underlay) to the Internal Intra-Cell Handover Requests (Overlay to Underlay).

Unit

%.

Measurement Point

None.

Formula

Success Rate of Internal Intra-Cell Handover (Overlay to Underlay) = [Successful Internal Intra-Cell Handovers (Overlay to Underlay)] x {100}/[Internal Intra-Cell Handover Requests (Overlay to Underlay]
