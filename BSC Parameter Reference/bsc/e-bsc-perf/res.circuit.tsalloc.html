A Interface Circuit Capability Measurement TSALLOC

Description

A Interface Circuit Capability Measurement TSALLOC provides the measurement counters related to the number of times and the time duration of each timeslot being occupied in the trunk circuit over the A interface, and is helpful to A interface circuit troubleshooting. The number of times and time duration of TS1 to TS31 being occupied in each trunk circuit are measured. TS0 for synchronization is not measured.

Measurement Object

TSALLOC.
