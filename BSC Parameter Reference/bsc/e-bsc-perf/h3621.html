Failed Incoming Inter-RAT Inter-Cell Handovers (TDD)

Measurement Counter

H3621:CELL_INTERRAN_TDD_INCELL_HO_FAIL

Description

The incoming inter-RAT inter-cell handover (TDD) may fail due to various reasons.

This measurement provides the total number of failed incoming inter-RAT inter-cell handovers (TDD). This measurement is based on the target cell under control of the target BSC and involves handovers on various channels: SDCCH, TCHF, and TCHH.

Unit

Integer number or integer.

Measurement Point

As shown in Figure 1, this counter is measured at A, B, C, and D when the incoming inter-RAT inter-cell handover (TDD) fails.

Figure 1 Measurement of the counters related to failed incoming inter-RAT inter-cell handovers (TDD)

Formula

Failed Incoming Inter-RAT Inter-Cell Handovers (TDD) = [Failed Incoming Inter-RAT Inter-Cell Handovers (SDCCH) (TDD)] + [Failed Incoming Inter-RAT Inter-Cell Handovers (TCHF) (TDD)] + [Failed Incoming Inter-RAT Inter-Cell Handovers (TCHH) (TDD)]
