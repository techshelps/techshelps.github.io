Analyzed Measurement of Duration of Maximum Power

Measurement Counter

RS327B:CELL_TIME_WHEN_MAX_DOWN_PWR_AVR

RS327A:CELL_TIME_WHEN_MAX_UP_PWR_AVR

Description

RS327B:CELL_TIME_WHEN_MAX_DOWN_PWR_AVR provides the ratio of the Duration of Maximum Downlink Power to the Number of Power Control Messages in a granularity period. Great value of this counter suggests bad coverage or improper settings of power control parameters.

RS327A:CELL_TIME_WHEN_MAX_UP_PWR_AVR provides the ratio of the Duration of Maximum Uplink Power to the Number of Power Control Messages in a granularity period. Great value of this counter suggests low rank of the uplink receive level or improper settings of power control parameters.

Unit

%.

Measurement Point

In the granularity period, the counters are calculated according to the following formulas.

Formula

Ratio of Maximum Downlink Power Duration (%) = {100} x [Duration of Maximum Downlink Power]/([Number of Power Control Messages] x {48}/{100})

Ratio of Maximum Uplink Power Duration (%) = {100} x [Duration of Maximum Uplink Power]/([Number of Power Control Messages] x {48}/{100})
