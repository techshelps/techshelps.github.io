Successful Internal Intra-Cell Handovers (Overlay to Underlay)

Measurement Counter

CH3031: CELL_INTRACELL_HO_SUCC_OLCELL_ULCELL

Description

This measurement counter provides the total number of successful internal intra-cell handovers from the overlay to the underlay of a concentric cell. It is equal to the Internal Intra-Cell Handover Requests (Overlay to Underlay) minus the Failed Internal Intra-Cell Handovers (Overlay to Underlay).

Unit

Integer number or integer.

Measurement Point

None.

Formula

Successful Internal Intra-Cell Handovers (Overlay to Underlay) = [Internal Intra-Cell Handover Requests (Overlay to Underlay)] - [Failed Internal Intra-Cell Handovers (Channel Unavailable) (Overlay to Underlay)] - [Failed Internal Intra-Cell Handovers (Other Causes) (Overlay to Underlay)]
