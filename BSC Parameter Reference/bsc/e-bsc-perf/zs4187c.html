Ratio of MRs on Uplink TCHH (Receive Quality Rank 7) per BSC

Measurement Counter

ZS4187C:TRX_HR_UL_RX_QLTY_7_RATIO

Description

This measurement provides the ratio of MRs, of which the receive quality rank is 7, on uplink TCHH per BSC.

Unit

%.

Measurement Point

BSC counters are calculation counters. This counter is measured according to the formula below when the measurement period ends. For details on the measurement point, see "Measurement Point" of each counter in the formula.

Formula

Ratio of MRs on Uplink TCHH (Receive Quality Rank 7) per BSC=

[Number of MRs on Uplink TCHH (Mean Receive Quality Rank 7) per BSC] x {100}

/

([Number of MRs on Uplink TCHH (Mean Receive Quality Rank 0) per BSC] +

[Number of MRs on Uplink TCHH (Mean Receive Quality Rank 1) per BSC] +

[Number of MRs on Uplink TCHH (Mean Receive Quality Rank 2) per BSC] +

[Number of MRs on Uplink TCHH (Mean Receive Quality Rank 3) per BSC] +

[Number of MRs on Uplink TCHH (Mean Receive Quality Rank 4) per BSC] +

[Number of MRs on Uplink TCHH (Mean Receive Quality Rank 5) per BSC] +

[Number of MRs on Uplink TCHH (Mean Receive Quality Rank 6) per BSC] +

[Number of MRs on Uplink TCHH (Mean Receive Quality Rank 7) per BSC])
