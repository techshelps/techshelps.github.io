Average Throughput of Packets Sent over an IPPM Link

Measurement Counter

L01037: IPPM_PKTS_MEANSTX

Description

This measurement provides the average rate of packets sent over the downlink IPPM from the BSC to the BTS in a granularity period.

Unit

packet/s.

Measurement Point

The counter is measured on the BSC side, as shown in Figure 1.

Figure 1 IPPM performance measurement point

Formula

IPPM_PKTS_MEANSTX = [IPPM_PKTS_TX]/{GP}/{60}

IPPM_PKTS_MEANSTX specifies the average rate of packets sent over an IPPM link.

IPPM_PKTS_TX specifies the number of packets sent over an IPPM link.
