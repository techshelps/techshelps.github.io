Times of Neighbor Cell Ranking Nth in MR

Measurement Counter

S374:CELL_NCELL_SORT_FIRST_IN_MR_TIMES

S375:CELL_NCELL_SORT_SECOND_IN_MR_TIMES

S376:CELL_NCELL_SORT_THIRD_IN_MR_TIMES

S377:CELL_NCELL_SORT_FOURTH_IN_MR_TIMES

S378:CELL_NCELL_SORT_FIFTH_IN_MR_TIMES

S379:CELL_NCELL_SORT_SIXTH_IN_MR_TIMES

Description

This measurement provides the number of times that the neighbor cell ranks Nth in the measurement report. N ranges from 1 to 6, mapping to FIRST, SECOND, THIRD, FOURTH, FIFTH, and SIXTH respectively.

Unit

Integer number or integer.

Measurement Point

After the BSC receives a valid MR from the MS, if a neighbor cell ranks Nth in the MR, the corresponding counter based on the serving cell, neighbor cell, and rank is incremented by one.

Formula

This is an original counter without involving any formula.
