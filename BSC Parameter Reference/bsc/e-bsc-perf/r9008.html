Number of PDCHs Out of Synchronization

Measurement Counter

R9008: OUT_OF_STEP_NUM

Description

This measurement provides the number of times that the PDCHs are out of synchronization in a granularity period.

Unit

Integer number or integer.

Measurement Point

Every time the PDCH is out of synchronization, the counter is incremented by one. The reason why the PDCH is out of synchronization may be the transition of the BTS frame number, change of the transmission delay, PS cell reset, or BTS reset.

Formula

This is an original counter without involving any formula.
