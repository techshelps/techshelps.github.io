Control-Plane Packets Received Over HDLC Path

Measurement Counter

SD002: BTSHDLC_RX_CTRL_PACK_NUM

Description

For the 3012 series base stations, this measurement provides the number of control-plane packets received over the HDLC paths between the TMU and the PTU, and the HDLC paths between the PTU and the PEU. The control-plane packets described here are received from the TMU or from the BSC. For the NG series base stations, this measurement provides the number of control-plane packets received over the HDLC paths between PTUs, and the HDLC paths between the PTU and the PEU. The control-plane packets described here are received from the transmission interface board or from the BSC.

Unit

Integer number or integer.

Measurement Point

For the 3012 series base stations, the PTU measures the control-plane packets received over the HDLC paths between the TMU and the PTU, and the HDLC paths between the PTU and the PEU. For the NG series base stations, the transmission interface board measures the control-plane packets received over the HDLC paths between PTUs, and the HDLC paths between the PTU and the PEU. The BTS periodically reports the result, and the BTSM disassembles the packets and processes the measurement results.

Formula

This is an original counter without involving any formula.
