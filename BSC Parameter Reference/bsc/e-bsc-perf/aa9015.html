Average Duration of Uplink GPRS TBF (s)

Measurement Counter

AA9015: UP_GPRS_TBF_AVG_TIME

Description

This measurement provides the average duration of uplink GPRS TBF in a cell in a granularity period.

Unit

s.

Measurement Point

On the basis of Total Duration of Uplink GPRS TBF (ms) and Number of Uplink GPRS TBF Normal Releases, the value of the counter can be obtained. The time unit of the PCU internal clock is tick. A second is equal to 100 ticks.

Formula

Average Duration of Uplink GPRS TBF (s) = [Total Duration of Uplink GPRS TBF (ms)]/[Number of Uplink GPRS TBF Normal Releases]/1000
