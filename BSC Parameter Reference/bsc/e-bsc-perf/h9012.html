Number of Network Controlled Cell Reselection Attempts due to Traffic Load

Measurement Counter

H9012: NC_CELL_LOAD_RESELECT_ATMPT

Description

This measurement provides the number of cell reselection attempts controlled by the network due to traffic overload in a granularity period.

Unit

Integer number or integer.

Measurement Point

Based on the measurement report sent by the MS and the condition of the cell that the MS camps on, the BSC determines whether to initiate the cell reselection. Every time a network-controlled cell reselection message is sent due to traffic overload in the cell, the counter is incremented by one. The parameters involved in the cell load are as follows: Cell Load Reselection Allowed and Load Reselect Level Threshold.

Formula

This is an original counter without involving any formula.
