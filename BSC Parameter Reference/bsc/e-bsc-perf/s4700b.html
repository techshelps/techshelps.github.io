Description

The measurement of the preceding counters provides the number of times that the BTS reports the EMR or pre-processed EMR with the MEAN_BEP ranging from 0 to 31 over the TCHH.

Unit

Integer number or integer.

Measurement Point

After receiving an EMR or pre-processed EMR from the BTS over the TCHH, the BSC decodes the MEAN_BEP in the downlink MR, and the specific counter is incremented by one.

Formula

This is an original counter without involving any formula.
