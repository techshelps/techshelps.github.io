Failed Reconnections to Old Channels upon Internal Outgoing Cell Handover Failure per BSC

Measurement Counter

ZCH912C:BSCRPT_INTRABSC_OUTCELL_HO_FAIL_RECONN_FAIL

Description

This measurement provides the number of outgoing internal inter-cell handover failures due to timer expiry in all the cells controlled by a BSC. The types of channel include SDCCH, TCHH, and TCHF.

In the outgoing internal inter-cell handover procedure (including directed retry), if the timer expires before completion of handover, it indicates that outgoing internal inter-cell handover fails. This measurement provides the number of outgoing internal inter-cell handover failures in the source cell according to the channel type and the cross-band handover type.

The handover can be performed between GSM900/850 and GSM900/850, between GSM1800/1900 and GSM1800/1900, between GSM900/850 and GSM1800/1900, or between GSM1800/1900 and GSM900/850.

Unit

Integer number or integer.

Measurement Point

This counter is measured according to the following formula when the measurement period ends. For details on the measurement point, see "Measurement Point" of each counter in the formula.

Formula

Failed Reconnections to Old Channels upon Internal Outgoing Cell Handover Failure per BSC =

Failed Outgoing Internal Inter-Cell Handovers (Timer Expired) (SDCCH) (Excluding Directed Retry) +

Failed Outgoing Internal Inter-Cell Handovers (Timer Expired) (TCHF) (Signaling Channel) +

Failed Outgoing Internal Inter-Cell Handovers (Timer Expired) (TCHH) (Signaling Channel) +

Failed Outgoing Internal Inter-Cell Handovers (Timer Expired) (TCHF) (Traffic Channel) +

Failed Outgoing Internal Intra-Cell Handovers (Timer Expired) (TCHH) (Signaling Channel) Per BSC +

Failed Outgoing Internal Inter-Cell Handovers (Timer Expired) (Directed Retry) per BSC
