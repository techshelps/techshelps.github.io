Handover KPI Measurement per BSC

Description

Handover KPI Measurement per BSC is used to measure the numbers of internal/external intra-cell handover requests, internal/external intra-cell handover requests commands, and successful internal/external intra-cell handovers, and the respective success rates.

Measurement Object

BSCRPT.
