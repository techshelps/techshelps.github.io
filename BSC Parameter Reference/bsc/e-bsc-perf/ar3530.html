Mean Duration of Busy State for All Channels (SDCCH/TCHF/TCHH/TCH)

Measurement Counter

AR3530:CELL_CH_BUSY_TIME_SD_AVR

AR3531:CELL_CH_BUSY_TIME_SD_AVR

AR3532:CELL_CH_BUSY_TIME_HR_AVR

R3544: CELL_CHAN_BUSY_TIME_TCH_AVE

Description

This measurement provides the average duration of the busy TCHs, SDCCHs, TCHFs, or TCHHs configured in a cell of the BSC in a granularity period. This counter indicates the average access frequency of the MS in a cell in a granularity period in terms of the duration of the busy channels.

Unit

s.

Measurement Point

None.

Formula

Mean Duration of Busy State for Channels (SDCCH) =

[Busy State Duration of Channels (SDCCH)]/(([Number of Dynamically Configured Channels (SDCCH) (900/850 Cell)]/[Sampled Number of Configured Channels]) + ([Number of Dynamically Configured Channels (SDCCH) (1800/1900 Cell)]/[Sampled Number of Configured Channels]))

Mean Duration of Busy State for Channels (TCHF) =

[Busy State Duration of Channels (TCHF)]/(([Number of Dynamically Configured Channels (TCHF) (900/850 Cell)]/[Sampled Number of Configured Channels]) + ([Number of Dynamically Configured Channels (TCHF) (1800/1900 Cell)]/[Sampled Number of Configured Channels]))

Mean Duration of Busy State for Channels (TCHH) =

[Busy State Duration of Channels (TCHH)]/(([Number of Dynamically Configured Channels (TCHH) (900/850 Cell)]/[Sampled Number of Configured Channels]) + ([Number of Dynamically Configured Channels (TCHH) (1800/1900 Cell)]/[Sampled Number of Configured Channels]))

Mean Duration of Busy State for Channels (TCH) =

([Duration of Busy State for Channels (TCHH)] + [Duration of Busy State for Channels (TCHF)])/(([Number of Dynamically Configured Channels (TCHH) (900/850 Cell)]/[Attemps Number of Configured Channels]) + ([Number of Dynamically Configured Channels (TCHH) (1800/1900 Cell)]/[Attemps Number of Configured Channels]) + ([Number of Dynamically Configured Channels (TCHF) (900/850 Cell)]/[Attemps Number of Configured Channels]) + ([Number of Dynamically Configured Channels (TCHF) (1800/1900 Cell)]/[Attemps Number of Configured Channels]))
