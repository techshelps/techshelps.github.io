Total Transmission Duration of Uplink EGPRS Background Service

Measurement Counter

L9453: CELL_LLC_TX_UP_EGPRS_BKGND_LLC_PDU_TRANS_DURATION

Description

This measurement provides the total transmission duration of all the uplink EGPRS background services in a cell in a granularity period.

Unit

ms.

Measurement Point

Every time the transmission of the background service is complete on the uplink EGPRS TBF, the transmission duration is used as the transmission duration of the uplink EGPRS TBF background service. In a granularity period, the total transmission duration of all background services on the uplink EGPRS TBFs is used as the value of the counter.

Formula

This is an original counter without involving any formula.
