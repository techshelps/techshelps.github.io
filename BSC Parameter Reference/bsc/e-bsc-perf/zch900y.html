Attempted Internal Inter-Cell Handovers per BSC (900/850 to 1800/1900)

Measurement Counter

ZCH900Y:BSCRPT_INTRABSC_INTERCELL_HO_REQ_900_1800

Description

This measurement provides the number of attempted internal inter-cell handovers of all the cells controlled by a BSC (900/850-1800/1900). The BSC uses the handover algorithm to decide whether a handover is required or not on the basis of the measurement reports submitted by the MS. If a handover is required, and if the source cell (current cell) and the target cell are two different cells controlled by a BSC, the BSC initiates an internal inter-cell handover. The counters indicate the number of incoming internal inter-cell handover requests in the source cell.

The channel involved in the measurement can be the SDCCH or the TCH.

This counter shows the frequency of inter-cell handovers initiated by the BSC. According to the counter Successful Internal Inter-Cell Handovers per BSC (900/850 to 1800/1900), the internal intra-cell handover per BSC can be well understood.

The handover frequency band range is 900/850 to 1800/1900.

Unit

Integer number or integer.

Measurement Point

This counter is measured according to the following formula when the measurement period ends. For details on the measurement point, see "Measurement Point" of each counter in the formula.

Formula

Attempted Internal Inter-Cell Handovers per BSC (900/850 to 1800/1900) = Incoming Internal Inter-Cell Handover Requests (SDCCH) (900/850-1800/1900) + Incoming Internal Inter-Cell Handover Requests (TCH) (900/850-1800/1900)
