Incoming Internal Inter-Cell Handover Detection Messages Received by BSC

Measurement Counter

CH324:CELL_INTRABSC_INCELL_HO_DETECT

Description

This measurement provides the total number of HO DETECT messages received by the BSC in the target cell from the BTS, including those on the SDCCHs, TCHFs, and TCHHs on various combinations of frequency bands before and after the handover.

Unit

Integer number or integer.

Measurement Point

As shown in Figure 1, this counter is measured in the target cell when the BSC receives from the BTS the HO DETECT message in the target cell.

Figure 1 Incoming internal inter-cell handover procedure

A

Measurement point of Incoming Internal Inter-Cell Handover Requests

B

Measurement point of Incoming Internal Inter-Cell Handover Responses

C

Measurement point of Incoming Internal Inter-Cell Handover Detection Messages Received by BSC

D

Measurement point of Successful Incoming Internal Inter-Cell Handovers

Formula

Incoming Internal Inter-Cell Handover Detection Messages Received by BSC =

[Incoming Internal Inter-Cell Handover Detection Messages Received by BSC (SDCCH)] +

[Incoming Internal Inter-Cell Handover Detection Messages Received by BSC (TCHF)] +

[Incoming Internal Inter-Cell Handover Detection Messages Received by BSC (TCHH)]
