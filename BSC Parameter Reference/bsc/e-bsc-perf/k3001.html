Failed SDCCH Seizures due to Busy SDCCH

Measurement Counter

K3001: CELL_KPI_SD_CONGEST

Description

This measurement provides the number of failed SDCCH seizures due to no idle SDCCH when the MS or MSC requests an SDCCH from the BSS in an immediate assignment procedure or a handover procedure.

Unit

Integer number or integer.

Measurement Point

(1) In the immediate assignment procedure, when the MS requests an SDCCH but the cell has no idle SDCCH available, this counter is incremented by one. See measurement point A in Figure 1.

Figure 1 Immediate assignment procedure

(2) In the internal handover procedure, if the handover occurs on the SDCCH, this counter is incremented by one when the MS fails to seize an SDCCH in the target cell because of no idle SDCCH available. See measurement point A in Figure 2.

Figure 2 Internal SDCCH handover procedure

(3) In the external handover procedure, if the handover occurs on the SDCCH, this counter is incremented by one when the MS fails to seize an SDCCH in the target cell because of no idle SDCCH available. See measurement point A in Figure 3.

Figure 3 External SDCCH handover procedure

Formula

Failed SDCCH Seizures due to Busy SDCCH = [Channel Assignment Failures (All Channels Busy or Channels Unconfigured) in Immediate Assignment Procedure (SDCCH)] + [Failed Internal Intra-Cell Handovers (No Channel Available) (SDCCH) per BSC] + [Failed Incoming Internal Intra-Cell Handovers (No Channel Available) (SDCCH) per BSC] + [Failed Incoming External Intra-Cell Handovers (No Channel Available) (SDCCH) per BSC]
