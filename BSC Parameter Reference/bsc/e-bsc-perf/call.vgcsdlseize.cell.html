VGCS Downlink Seizure

Description

VGCS Downlink Seizure is used to measure the number of busy VGCS downlink TCHs and the traffic volume on the VGCS downlink TCHs.

Measurement Object

Cells.

Measurement Counter

The following table lists the measurement counters related to VGCS Downlink Seizure.

Counter

Refer to Section...

A03455: CELL_VGCS_DL_CHAN_BUSY_NUM_TCH

Number of Busy VGCS Down Link TCH

A03456: CELL_KPI_VGCS_DL_TRAF_ERL

Traffic Volume of VGCS Down Link TCH
