Success Rate of Internal Intra-Cell Handover (Underlay to Overlay)

Measurement Counter

TH3032: CELL_INTRACELL_HO_SUCC_RATE_ULCELL_OLCELL

Description

This counter provides the ratio of successful internal intra-cell handovers from the underlay to the overlay of a concentric cell. It is the ratio of Successful Internal Intra-Cell Handovers (Underlay to Overlay) to the Internal Intra-Cell Handover Requests (Underlay to Overlay).

Unit

%.

Measurement Point

None.

Formula

Success Rate of Internal Intra-Cell Handover (Underlay to Overlay) = [Successful Internal Intra-Cell Handovers (Underlay to Overlay)] x {100}/[Internal Intra-Cell Handover Requests (Underlay to Overlay]
