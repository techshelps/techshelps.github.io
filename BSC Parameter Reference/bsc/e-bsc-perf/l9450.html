Total Transmission Duration of Uplink GPRS Interactive Service THPx

Measurement Counter

L9450: CELL_LLC_TX_UP_GPRS_INTACT_THP1_LLC_PDU_TRANS_DURATION

L9451: CELL_LLC_TX_UP_GPRS_INTACT_THP2_LLC_PDU_TRANS_DURATION

L9452: CELL_LLC_TX_UP_GPRS_INTACT_THP3_LLC_PDU_TRANS_DURATION

Description

This measurement provides the total transmission duration of all uplink GPRS interactive services THPx in a cell in a granularity period.

Unit

ms.

Measurement Point

Every time the transmission of the interactive service THPx is complete on the uplink GPRS TBF, the transmission duration is used as the transmission duration of the uplink GPRS TBF interactive service THPx. In a granularity period, the total transmission duration of all interactive services THPx on the uplink GPRS TBFs is used as the value of the counter. Note: THPx indicates THP1-THP3.

Formula

This is an original counter without involving any formula.
