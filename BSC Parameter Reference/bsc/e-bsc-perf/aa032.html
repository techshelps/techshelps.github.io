Mean Duration of Call Access

Measurement Counter

AA032:BSC_CALL_ACCESS_DUR_AVG

Description

Mean Duration of Call Access provides the duration from the time the BSC receives the CHANNEL REQUIRED message from the BTS to the time the BSC sends the ASSIGNMENT COMPLETE message to the MSC during a call.

Unit

100 ms.

Measurement Point

Accumulated Duration of Call Access is the accumulated duration from the time the BSC receives the CHANNEL REQUIRED message from the BTS to the time the BSC sends the ASSIGNMENT COMPLETE message to the MSC within the granularity period.

The value of Sampling Times of Call Access is added by one when the BSC sends a ASSIGNMENT COMPLETE message to the MSC.

Formula

Mean Duration of Call Access = [Accumulated Duration of Call Access]/[Sampling Times of Call Access]
