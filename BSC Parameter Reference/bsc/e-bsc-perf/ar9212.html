Average PPCH Queue Length

Measurement Counter

AR9212: CELL_RADIO_PPCH_AVG_QUEUE_LEN

Description

This measurement provides the average length of the paging message queues on the PPCH in the BSC in a granularity period. If the measurement value is always high, you can infer that the PS services are busy. In this case, you are advised to configure more PCCCHs.

Unit

byte.

Measurement Point

In a granularity period, Average PPCH Queue Length is calculated on the basis of Total Sampled PPCH Queue Lengths and Sampling Times of PDCH Queue Length.

Formula

Average PDTCH Queue Length = Total Sampled PPCH Queue Lengths/Sampling Times of PDCH Queue Length
