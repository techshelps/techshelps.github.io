Channel Assignment Requests in Incoming External Inter-Cell Handover Procedure (TCH)

Measurement Counter

R3202E:CELL_INTERBSC_INCELL_HO_CH_ALLOC_REQ_TIMES_TCH_PREF_ULCELL

R3203E:CELL_INTERBSC_INCELL_HO_CH_ALLOC_REQ_TIMES_TCH_PREF_OLCELL

Description

The counters are used to measure the number of TCH assignment requests of a concentric cell in the incoming external inter-cell handover procedure. The measurements are based on the requirement for the attributes of the concentric cell.

The previous counters show the utilization of the underlaid subcell and overlaid subcell of a concentric cell. If the ratio of assignment overflows to assignment requests is high, the underlaid subcell or the overlaid subcell is congested. The traffic load of the underlaid subcell and that of the overlaid subcell should be regulated.

Unit

Integer number or integer.

Measurement Point

In the incoming external inter-cell handover procedure, the measurements are triggered when a concentric cell requests for a TCH. The measurements are based on the requirement for the attributes of the concentric cell.

Formula

This is an original counter without involving any formula.
