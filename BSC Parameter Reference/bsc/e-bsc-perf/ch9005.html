Number of Failed Handovers from Overlaid Subcell to Underlaid Subcell

Measurement Counter

CH9005: OLCELL_INTO_ULCELL_FAIL_NUM

Description

This measurement provides the number of failed handovers from overlaid subcell to underlaid subcell in a granularity period.

Unit

Integer number or integer.

Measurement Point

Number of Failed Handovers from Overlaid Subcell to Underlaid Subcell is equal to Number of Overlaid Subcell to Underlaid Subcell Handover Requests subtracted by Number of Successful Handovers from Overlaid Subcell to Underlaid Subcell. The handover failure may be caused by the absence of PDCHs in the target cell.

Formula

Number of Failed Handovers from Overlaid Subcell to Underlaid Subcell = [Number of Overlaid Subcell to Underlaid Subcell Handover Requests] - [Number of Successful Handovers from Overlaid Subcell to Underlaid Subcell]
