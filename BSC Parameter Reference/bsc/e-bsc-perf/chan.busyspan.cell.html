Channel Busy Duration Measurement per Cell

Description

The Channel Busy Duration Measurement per Cell measurement unit is used to measure the following items:

  • Duration of busy state of SDCCH and TCH in the cell

  • Duration of the state of no available SDCCHs and TCHs

  • This measurement unit indicates the use of SDCCH and TCH.

Measurement Object

CELL.
