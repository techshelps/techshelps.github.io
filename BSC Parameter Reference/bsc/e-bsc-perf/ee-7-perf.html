Table 1 Explanation of terms

Term

Explanation

Measurement set

The measurement set corresponds to the level 1 contents.

One measurement set includes one or multiple measurement units.

Measurement unit

The measurement unit corresponds to the level 2 contents.

One measurement unit includes one or multiple measurement counters. One measurement unit belongs to only one measurement set.

Measurement counter

The measurement counter corresponds to the level 3 contents.

The measurement counter is a basic unit of performance measurement. One measurement counter belongs to only one measurement unit.
