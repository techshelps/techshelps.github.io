Logical Port Measurement(LOGICPORT)

Overview

Logical port is a virtual port based on the physical port. The shaping of the logical port implements the sharing of one physical port by multiple virtual ports and ensures that the traffic feature meets subscription requirements. Logical Port Measurement is used to measure the running conditions of all the logical ports in the BSC.
