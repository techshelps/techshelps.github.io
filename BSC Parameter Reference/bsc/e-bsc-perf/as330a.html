Analyzed Measurement of Receive Level During Contentric Cell Handover Initiation

Measurement Counter

AS330A:CELL_HO_UL_LEV_OLCELL_ULCELL_AVG

AS332A:CELL_HO_DL_LEV_OLCELL_ULCELL_AVG

AS330B:CELL_HO_UL_LEV_OLCELL_ULCELL_AVG

AS332B:CELL_HO_DL_LEV_OLCELL_ULCELL_AVG

Description

After receiving a valid MR from the MS or a pre-processed MR from the BTS, the BSC makes a handover decision. If an handover from the OL subcell to the UL subcell is required, the counter Uplink Receive Level during Concentric Cell Handover Initiation (Overlay to Underlay) is incremented by the uplink receive level in the MR, and the counter Downlink Receive Level during Concentric Cell Handover Initiation (Underlay to Overlay) is incremented by the downlink receive level in the MR. In a granularity period, the value of Mean Uplink Receive Level during Concentric Cell Handover Initiation (Overlay to Underlay) is the result of dividing the accumulative Uplink Receive Level during Concentric Cell Handover Initiation (Overlay to Underlay) by Internal Intra-Cell Handover Requests (Overlay to Underlay), and the value of Mean Downlink Receive Level during Concentric Cell Handover Initiation (Overlay to Underlay) is the result of dividing the accumulative Downlink Receive Level during Concentric Cell Handover Initiation (Overlay to Underlay) by Internal Intra-Cell Handover Requests (Overlay to Underlay).

These counters reflect the receive level in the concentric cell when a handover occurs.

Unit

dBm.

Measurement Point

At the end of the granularity period, this counter is calculated by using the following formula.

Formula

Mean Uplink Receive Level during Concentric Cell Handover Initiation (Overlay to Underlay) = Uplink Receive Level during Concentric Cell Handover Initiation (Overlay to Underlay)/Internal Intra-Cell Handover Requests (Overlay to Underlay)

Mean Downlink Receive Level during Concentric Cell Handover Initiation (Overlay to Underlay) = Downlink Receive Level during Concentric Cell Handover Initiation (Overlay to Underlay)/Internal Intra-Cell Handover Requests (Overlay to Underlay)

Mean Uplink Receive Level during Concentric Cell Handover Initiation (Underlay to Overlay) = Uplink Receive Level during Concentric Cell Handover Initiation (Underlay to Overlay)/Internal Intra-Cell Handover Requests (Underlay to Overlay)

Mean Downlink Receive Level during Concentric Cell Handover Initiation (Underlay to Overlay) = Downlink Receive Level during Concentric Cell Handover Initiation (Underlay to Overlay)/Internal Intra-Cell Handover Requests (Underlay to Overlay)
