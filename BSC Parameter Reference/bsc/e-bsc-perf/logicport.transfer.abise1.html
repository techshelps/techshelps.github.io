Abis Transmission Performance Measurement

Description

The measurement unit Abis Transmission Performance Measurement is mainly used to measure the transmission quality of the Abis interface. It indicates the transmission quality through the measurement on the rate of error seconds, rate of severe error seconds, and rate of error blocks received and sent over the Abis port. The transmission quality deteriorates when the measurement values increase.

Measurement Object

ABIS_E1.
