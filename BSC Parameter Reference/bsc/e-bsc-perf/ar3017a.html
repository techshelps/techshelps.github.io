Analyzed Measurement of Dynamically Configured Channels (TCHF)

Measurement Counter

AR3017A:CELL_CH_NUM_FR_900_AVR

AR3017B:CELL_CH_NUM_FR_1800_AVR

CR3017:CELL_CH_NUM_FR_AVR

Description

AR3017A:CELL_CH_NUM_FR_900_AVR provides the mean number of configured TCHFs in the GSM900, GSM900/DCS1800, GSM850, GSM850/DCS1800, and GSM850/PCS1900 cells controlled by the BSC in the granularity period. This number includes the number of TCHFs that are converted from dynamic PDCHs and TCHHs.

AR3017B:CELL_CH_NUM_FR_1800_AVR provides the mean number of configured TCHFs in the DCS1800 and PCS1900 cells controlled by the BSC in the granularity period. This number includes the number of TCHFs that are converted from dynamic PDCHs and TCHHs.

CR3017:CELL_CH_NUM_FR_AVR provides the mean number of configured TCHFs in the cells on all frequency bands controlled by the BSC in real-time measurement. This number includes the number of TCHFs that are converted from dynamic PDCHs and TCHHs.

Unit

Integer number or integer.

Measurement Point

In the granularity period, the counters are calculated as follows.

Formula

Mean Number of Dynamically Configured Channels (TCHF) (900/850 Cell) = Total Number of Dynamically Configured Channels (TCHF) (900/850 Cell)/Number of Sampling Times

Mean Number of Dynamically Configured Channels (TCHF) (1800/1900 Cell) = Total Number of Dynamically Configured Channels (TCHF) (1800/1900 Cell)/Number of Sampling Times

Mean Number of Available Channels (TCHF) = Mean Number of Available Channels (TCHF) (900/850 Cell) + Mean Number of Available Channels (TCHF) (1800/1900 Cell)
