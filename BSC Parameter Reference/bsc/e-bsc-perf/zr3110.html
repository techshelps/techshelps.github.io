TCH Channel Alloc Failed(No Usable Channel) Times In Imm Assignment Per BSC

Measurement Counter

ZR3110:BSCRPT_IMM_ASS_CHAN_ALLOC_FAIl_TIMES_NO_USABLE_CHAN_TCH

Description

This measurement provides the number of TCH assignment failures because no channel is available during the immediate assignment in a BSC.

Unit

Integer number or integer.

Measurement Point

This counter is measured according to the following formula when the measurement period ends. For details on the measurement point, see "TCH Channel Alloc Failed(No Usable Channel) Times In Imm Assignment".

Formula

TCH Channel Alloc Failed(No Usable Channel) Times In Imm Assignment Per BSC = TCH Channel Alloc Failed(No Usable Channel) Times In Imm Assignment

(That is, the total of the original counter TCH Channel Alloc Failed(No Usable Channel) Times In Imm Assignment in all the cells of the BSC.)
