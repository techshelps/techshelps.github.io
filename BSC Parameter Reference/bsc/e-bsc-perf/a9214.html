Total Duration of Uplink EGPRS TBF (ms)

Measurement Counter

A9214: UP_EGPRS_TBF_TOTAL_TIME

Description

This measurement counter provides the total duration of the uplink EGPRS TBF in a granularity period.

Unit

ms.

Measurement Point

The duration of an EGPRS TBF refers to the period that starts when the EGPRS TBF is successfully established and ends when the EGPRS TBF is normally released. The measurement value is obtained by accumulating the duration of uplink EGPRS TBFs that are normally released in a granularity period.

Formula

This is an original counter without involving any formula.
