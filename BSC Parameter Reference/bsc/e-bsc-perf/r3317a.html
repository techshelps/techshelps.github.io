Channel Activation Measurement of the Assignment Procedure

Measurement Counter

R3317A: CELL_ASS_CH_ACT_TCHF

R3318A: CELL_ASS_CH_ACT_TCHH

R3317B: CELL_ASS_CH_ACT_NACK_TCHF

R3318B: CELL_ASS_CH_ACT_NACK_TCHH

R3317C: CELL_ASS_CH_ACT_EXP_TCHF

R3318C: CELL_ASS_CH_ACT_EXP_TCHH

Description

In the stable signaling or service status, the BSC receives an ASS REQ message from the MSC. If the request type is voice or data and the BSC determines that the assignment procedure should follow, the BSC assigns a TCH and sends a CHANNEL ACTIVATION message to the BTS. At the same time, the counter R3317A "Channel Activation Attempts in Immediate Assignment Procedure (TCHF)" or R3318A "Channel Activation Attempts in Immediate Assignment Procedure (TCHH)" is incremented by one for the cell where the TCH resides based on the channel type.

In the assignment procedure, the BSC assigns a TCH and sends a CHANNEL ACTIVATION message to the BTS. If the BTS returns a CHANNEL ACTIVATION NEGATIVE ACKNOWLEDGE message indicating the channel activation failure, the counter R3317B "CHAN ACTIV NACK Messages Sent by BTS in Immediate Assignment Procedure (TCHF)" or R3318B "CHAN ACTIV NACK Messages Sent by BTS in Immediate Assignment Procedure (TCHH)" is incremented by one for the cell where the TCH resides based on the channel type.

In the assignment procedure, the BSC assigns a TCH, sends a CHANNEL ACTIVATION message to the BTS and starts an internal timer (5 s by default) to wait for a CHANNEL ACTIVATION ACKNOWLEDGE message. If the BSC does not receive the CHANNEL ACTIVATION ACKNOWLEDGE message after the timer expires, the counter R3317C "Channel Activation Timeouts in Immediate Assignment Procedure (TCHF)" or R3318C "Channel Activation Timeouts in Immediate Assignment Procedure (TCHH)" is incremented by one for the cell where the TCH resides based on the channel type.

Unit

Integer number or integer.

Measurement Point

Upon receipt of an ASS REQ message from the MSC, the BSC assigns a TCH and sends a CHANNEL ACTIVATION message to the BTS. At this time, the counter R3317A or R3318A is incremented by one. If the BTS returns a CHANNEL ACTIVATION NEGATIVE ACKNOWLEDGE message, the counter R3317B or R3318B is incremented by one. After sending a CHANNEL ACTIVATION message, the BSC starts the timer and waits for a response from the BTS. If the BSC does not receive any response after the timer expires, the counter R3317C or R3318C is incremented by one.

Formula

This is an original counter without involving any formula.
