Failed SDCCH Seizures due to Busy SDCCH per BSC

Measurement Counter

ZK3001:BSCRPT_KPI_SD_CONG

Description

In an immediate assignment procedure or a handover procedure, the BSC triggers the counter when the MS or the MSC requests an SDCCH from the BSS but no SDCCH is available.

Unit

Integer number or integer.

Measurement Point

This counter is measured according to the following formula when the measurement period ends. For details on the measurement point, see "Failed SDCCH Seizures due to Busy SDCCH".

Formula

Failed SDCCH Seizures due to Busy SDCCH per BSC = Failed SDCCH Seizures due to Busy SDCCH

(That is, the total of the original counter Failed SDCCH Seizures due to Busy SDCCH in all the cells of the BSC.)
