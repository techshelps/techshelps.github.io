Downlink BER Probability Variance

Measurement Counter

S4808B:CELL_DL_SIG_CVBEP

Description

After receiving an EMR or pre-processed EMR from the BTS, the BSC decodes the CV_BEP, and this counter is incremented by the value of the CV_BEP. This counter is used in the calculating of the Average Downlink BER Probability Variance.

Unit

None.

Measurement Point

After receiving an EMR or pre-processed EMR from the BTS in the serving cell, the BSC decodes the MEAN_BEP in the downlink MR, and this counter is measured.

Formula

This is an original counter without involving any formula.
