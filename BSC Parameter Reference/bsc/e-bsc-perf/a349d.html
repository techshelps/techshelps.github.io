Average TA In RACH Failure (Too High TA)

Measurement Counter

A349D: CELL_RACH_FAIL_AVER_TA_IN_TOO_HIGH_TA

Description

This measurement provides the average TA (too high TA) once the RACH is not successfully decoded.

Unit

None

Measurement Point

None.

Formula

Average TA In RACH Failure (Too High TA) = [TA Accumulation In RACH Failure (Too High TA)]/[RACH Failures (Too High TA)]
