Number of Intra-BSC Incoming Cell Handover Successes During BTS Loopback

Measurement Counter

CH320b:CELL_INTRABSC_INCELL_HO_SUCC_BTS_LOOP

Description

This measurement provides the total number of intra-BSC incoming cell handover successes during BTS loop. It is calculated by subtracting the Failed Internal Intra-Cell Handovers (BTS LOOP) from the Internal Intra-Cell Handover Requests (BTS LOOP).

Unit

Integer number or integer.

Measurement Point

As shown in Figure 1, this counter is measured at D when the BSC receives the HO CMP message from the MS during the intra-BSC incoming cell handover (BTS loop).

Figure 1 Incoming internal inter-cell handover procedure

A

Measurement point of Incoming Internal Inter-Cell Handover Requests

B

Measurement point of Incoming Internal Inter-Cell Handover Responses

C

Measurement point of Incoming Internal Inter-Cell Handover Detection Messages Received by BSC

D

Measurement point of Successful Incoming Internal Inter-Cell Handovers

Formula

Number of Intra-BSC Incoming Cell Handover Successes During BTS Loopback =

[Number of Intra-BSC Incoming Cell Handover Requests During BTS Loopback] -

[Number of Intra-BSC Incoming Cell Handover Failures During BTS Loopback]
