Average LLC PDU Bytes per Downlink TBF

Measurement Counter

AL9507: DOWN_TBF_AVG_TX_BYTE

Description

This measurement provides the average bytes of LLC PDUs sent by the downlink TBF in a cell in a granularity period. The average bytes of LLC PDUs sent by the downlink TBF for the FTP service are more than those for the WAP service.

Unit

byte.

Measurement Point

None.

Formula

Average LLC PDU Bytes per Downlink TBF = [Total Bytes of LLC PDUs Sent]/[Total Number of Downlink TBFs]
