Failed Handovers from Overlaid Subcell to Underlaid Subcell due to Busy Channels in Underlaid Subcell

Measurement Counter

R3225K:CELL_OLCELL_TO_ULCELL_HO_ULCELL_OVERFLOW_TIMES

Description

In the handover from the overlaid subcell to the underlaid subcell, this measurement provides the number of failed handovers because the underlaid subcell has no idle channel available. If the result of this measurement is too high, the traffic load of the underlaid subcell and that of the overlaid subcell should be regulated.

Unit

Integer number or integer.

Measurement Point

In the handover from the overlaid subcell to the underlaid subcell, the measurement is triggered when the BSC detects that the underlaid subcell has no channel to be assigned.

Formula

This is an original counter without involving any formula.
