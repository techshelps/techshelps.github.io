Description

This measurement provides the ratio of MRs with the RQI ranging from N to M to all MRs with RQI. N ranges from 0 to 45; M ranges from 1 to 55.

Unit

%.

Measurement Point

In the granularity period, this counter is measured by using the following formula.

Formula

Rate of Measurement Reports with RQI Equal to N-N+1 = [Number of Measurement Reports with RQI Equal to N-N+1]/([Number of Measurement Reports (RQI = 0 to 1)] + [Number of Measurement Reports (RQI = 1 to 2)]
