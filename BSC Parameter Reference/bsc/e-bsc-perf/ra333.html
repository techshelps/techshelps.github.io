Success Rate of Call Establishment

Measurement Counter

RA333: CELL_CALL_ESTB_SUCC_RATE

Description

This measurement indicates the proportion of the ASS CMP messages that the BSC sends to the MSC to the EST IND messages that the BSC receives from the BTS.

Unit

%.

Measurement Point

At the end of the granularity period, this counter is calculated by using the following formula.

Formula

Success Rate of Call Establishment =

([Successful Assignments] -

([Assignment Requests (SDCCH)] +

[Assignment Requests (Signaling Channel) (TCH)] -

[Failed Assignments (Signaling Channel)] +

[Assignment Requests (TCH Assigned Repeatedly over A Interface)] -

[Failed Assignments (TCH Assigned Repeatedly over A Interface)])) x {100}

/([SDCCH Seizures for Speech Service] +

[TCHF Seizures for Speech Service] +

[TCHH Seizures for Speech Service])
