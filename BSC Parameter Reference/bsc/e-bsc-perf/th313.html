Success Rate of Outgoing Internal Inter-Cell Handover

Measurement Counter

TH313:CELL_INTRABSC_OUTCELL_HO_SUCC_RATE

Description

This measurement provides the success rate of outgoing internal inter-cell handover. The counter is calculated by dividing the Successful Outgoing Internal Inter-Cell Handover by the Outgoing Internal Inter-Cell Handover Requests.

Unit

%.

Measurement Point

None.

Formula

Success Rate of Outgoing Internal Inter-Cell Handover = [Successful Outgoing Internal Inter-Cell Handover] x {100}/[Outgoing Internal Inter-Cell Handover Requests]
