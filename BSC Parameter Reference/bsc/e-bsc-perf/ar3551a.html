Mean Number of Busy TCHs (TCHF/TCHH) in Cells of Different Types

Measurement Counter

AR3551A:CELL_CH_BUSY_NUM_FR_TRAF_900_AVR

AR3551B: CELL_CH_BUSY_NUM_FR_TRAF_1800_AVR

AR3552A: CELL_CH_BUSY_NUM_HR_TRAF_900_AVR

AR3552B: CELL_CH_BUSY_NUM_HR_TRAF_1800_AVR

Description

AR3551A:CELL_CH_BUSY_NUM_FR_TRAF_900_AVR provides the mean number of busy TCHs (TCHF) in the GSM900, GSM900/DCS1800, GSM850, GSM850/DCS1800, and GSM850/PCS1900 cells controlled by the BSC at each sampling point.

AR3551B:CELL_CH_BUSY_NUM_FR_TRAF_1800_AVR provides the mean number of busy TCHs (TCHF) in the DCS1800 and PCS1900 cells controlled by the BSC at each sampling point.

AR3552A:CELL_CH_BUSY_NUM_HR_TRAF_900_AVR provides the mean number of busy TCHs (TCHH) in the GSM900, GSM900/DCS1800, GSM850, GSM850/DCS1800, and GSM850/PCS1900 cells controlled by the BSC at each sampling point.

AR3552B:CELL_CH_BUSY_NUM_HR_TRAF_1800_AVR provides the mean number of busy TCHs (TCHH) in the DCS1800 and PCS1900 cells controlled by the BSC at each sampling point.

Unit

Integer number or integer.

Measurement Point

In the granularity period, this counter is calculated according to the following formula.

Formula

Mean Number of Busy TCHs (TCHF) (900/850 Cell) = [Number of Busy TCHs (TCHF) (900/850 Cell)]/[Number of Attempts to Scan Channel States]

Mean Number of Busy TCHs (TCHF) (1800/1900 Cell) = [Number of Busy TCHs (TCHF) (1800/1900 Cell)]/[Number of Attempts to Scan Channel States]

Mean Number of Busy TCHs (TCHH) (900/850 Cell) = [Number of Busy TCHs (TCHH) (900/850 Cell)]/[Number of Attempts to Scan Channel States]

Mean Number of Busy TCHs (TCHH) (1800/1900 Cell) = [Number of Busy TCHs (TCHH) (1800/1900 Cell)]/[Number of Attempts to Scan Channel States]
