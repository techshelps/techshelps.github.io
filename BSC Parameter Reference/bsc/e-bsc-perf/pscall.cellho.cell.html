Intra-cell Handover Measurement

Description

The PSCALL.CellHo.Cell measurement unit provides the counters related to cell handover in a granularity period and indicates the traffic load of the PS services in a cell. It is used as a reference for establishing the PS service model.

Measurement Object

Cell.
