Failed Incoming Inter-RAT Inter-Cell Handovers

Measurement Counter

H362:CELL_INTERRAN_INCELL_HO_FAIL

Description

The incoming inter-RAT inter-cell handover may fail due to various reasons.

This measurement provides the total number of failed incoming inter-RAT inter-cell handovers. This measurement is based on the target cell under control of the target BSC and involves handovers on various channels: SDCCH, TCHF, and TCHH.

Unit

Integer number or integer.

Measurement Point

At points A, B, C, and D, as shown in Figure 1, the counter is measured when the incoming inter-RAT inter-cell handover fails.

Figure 1 Measurement of counters related to the failed incoming inter-RAT inter-cell handover

Formula

Failed Incoming Inter-RAT Inter-Cell Handovers = [Failed Incoming Inter-RAT Inter-Cell Handovers (SDCCH)] +

[Failed Incoming Inter-RAT Inter-Cell Handovers (TCHF)] +

[Failed Incoming Inter-RAT Inter-Cell Handovers (TCHH)]
