AMR Measurement per Cell

Description

At present, the complete counters such as Call Drop Ratio on TCH and Call Proportion exist in the BSC6000 performance counters. The existing measurement points, measurement methods, and counter descriptions of Call Drop Ratio on TCH and Call Proportion remain unchanged. The measurement counter of AMR is added separately. The AMR measurement is performed at the same measurement point with the original measurement counters.
