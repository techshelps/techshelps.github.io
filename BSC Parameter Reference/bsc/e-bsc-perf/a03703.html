Ratio of Bad Speech Quality Disconnections

Measurement Counter

A03703: CELL_SPEECH_BAD_QUAL_DISC_RATE

Description

This measurement provides the ratio of speech disconnections because of bad quality.

Unit

%

Measurement Point

None.

Formula

Ratio of Bad Speech Quality Disconnections (%) = [Bad Speech Quality Disconnections]*{100}/[Normal Speech Disconnections]
