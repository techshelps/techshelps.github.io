Sampling Times of PDCH Measurement on DSP

Measurement Counter

R9504: DSP_SAMPLE_TIMES_PDCH

Description

This measurement provides the number of times that the activated PDCHs on a DSP are sampled in a granularity period.

Unit

Integer number or integer.

Measurement Point

In a granularity period, the number of PDCHs activated on a DSP is sampled every five seconds. The counter is incremented by one every time the sampling is performed.

Formula

This is an original counter without involving any formula.
