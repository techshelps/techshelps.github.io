Number of Successful Outgoing Internal Inter-Cell Handovers (Underlaid to Overlaid)

Measurement Counter

H313OI: CELL_INBSC_OUT_CELL_HO_SUCC_ULCELL_OLCELL

Description

When an internal concentric cell handover is performed, the performance of the handover between different concentric cells is measured. This measurement provides the number of successful handovers from the underlaid subcell of the originating cell to the overlaid subcell of the target cell. The measurement is performed on the basis of the originating cell and covers the handover between traffic channels, handover between signaling channels, and directed retry.

Unit

Integer number or integer.

Measurement Point

None.

Formula

Number of Successful Outgoing Internal Inter-Cell Handovers (Underlaid to Overlaid) = [Number of Outgoing Internal Inter-Cell Handovers Commands (Underlaid to Overlaid)] - [Number of Failed Outgoing Internal Inter-Cell Handovers (Underlaid to Overlaid)]
