Channel Activation Measurement of the Internal Intra-Cell Handover Procedure

Measurement Counter

R3330A: CELL_INTRA_CELL_HO_CH_ACT_SD

R3330B: CELL_INTRA_CELL_HO_CH_ACT_NACK_SD

R3330C: CELL_INTRA_CELL_HO_CH_ACT_EXP_SD

R3337A: CELL_INTRA_CELL_HO_CH_ACT_TCHF

R3338A: CELL_INTRA_CELL_HO_CH_ACT_TCHH

R3337B: CELL_INTRA_CELL_HO_CH_ACT_NACK_TCHF

R3338B: CELL_INTRA_CELL_HO_CH_ACT_NACK_TCHH

R3337C: CELL_INTRA_CELL_HO_CH_ACT_EXP_TCHF

R3338C: CELL_INTRA_CELL_HO_CH_ACT_EXP_TCHH

Description

If the BSC determines that handover is required according to the measurement report and the target cell is the current cell after the MS seizes a channel, the BSC sends a CHANNEL ACTIVATION message to the BTS of the target cell and at the same time one of the following three counters is incremented by one for the cell where the target TCH resides based on the type of the channel assigned: R3330A "Channel Activation Attempts in Internal Intra-Cell Handover Procedure (SDCCH)", R3337A "Channel Activation Attempts in Internal Intra-Cell Handover Procedure (TCHF)", or R3338A "Channel Activation Attempts in Internal Intra-Cell Handover Procedure (TCHH)".

In the intra-cell TCH handover procedure, the BSC sends a CHANNEL ACTIVATION message to the BTS. If the BTS returns a CHANNEL ACTIVATION NEGATIVE ACKNOWLEDGE message indicating the channel activation failure, one of the following three counters is incremented by one for the cell where the target TCH resides based on the type of the channel assigned: R3330B "CHAN ACTIV NACK Messages Sent by BTS in Internal Intra-Cell Handover Procedure (SDCCH)", R3337B "CHAN ACTIV NACK Messages Sent by BTS in Internal Intra-Cell Handover Procedure (TCHF)", or R3338B "CHAN ACTIV NACK Messages Sent by BTS in Internal Intra-Cell Handover Procedure (TCHH)".

In the intra-cell TCH handover procedure, the BSC sends a CHANNEL ACTIVATION message to the BTS and starts an internal timer (5 s by default) to wait for a CHANNEL ACTIVATION ACKNOWLEDGE message. If the BSC does not receive the CHANNEL ACTIVATION ACKNOWLEDGE message after the timer expires, one of the following three counters is incremented by one for the cell where the target TCH resides based on the channel type: R3330C "Channel Activation Timeouts in Internal Intra-Cell Handover Procedure (SDCCH)", R3337C "Channel Activation Timeouts in Internal Intra-Cell Handover Procedure (TCHF)", or R3338C "Channel Activation Timeouts in Internal Intra-Cell Handover Procedure (TCHH)".

Unit

Integer number or integer.

Measurement Point

If the MS hands over after seizing a channel and the target cell is the current cell, the BSC sends a CHANNEL ACTIVATION message to the BTS of the target cell and at the same time the counter R3330A, R3337A, or R3338A is incremented by one. If the BTS returns a CHANNEL ACTIVATION NEGATIVE ACKNOWLEDGE message, the counter R3330B, R3337B, or R3338B is incremented by one. After sending a CHANNEL ACTIVATION message, the BSC starts the timer and waits for a response from the BTS. If the BSC does not receive any response after the timer expires, the counter R3330C, R3337C, or R3338C is incremented by one.

Formula

This is an original counter without involving any formula.
