Call Drops on TCH per BSC (Signaling Channel)

Measurement Counter

ZK3022:BSCRPT_KPI_TCH_DROPS_SIG

Description

This measurement provides the number of call drops when the TCH is used as signaling channel.

Unit

Integer number or integer.

Measurement Point

This counter is measured according to the following formula when the measurement period ends. For details on the measurement point, see "Call Drops on TCH (Signaling Channel)".

Formula

Call Drops on TCH per BSC (Signaling Channel) = Call Drops on TCH (Signaling Channel)

(That is, the total of the original counter Call Drops on TCH (Signaling Channel) in all the cells of the BSC.)
