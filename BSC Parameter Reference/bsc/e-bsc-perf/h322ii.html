Number of Failed Incoming Internal Inter-Cell Handovers (Overlaid to Overlaid)

Measurement Counter

H322II: CELL_INBSC_INTO_CELL_HO_FAIL_OLCELL_OLCELL

Description

When a handover between different concentric cells is performed under the same BSC, the performance of the handover is measured. Taking the target cell as the measurement object, this measurement provides the number of unsuccessful handovers from the overlaid subcell of the originating cell to the overlaid subcell of the target cell. The handovers involve the handover between traffic channels, handover between signaling channels, and directed retry.

Unit

Integer number or integer.

Measurement Point

As shown in Figure 1, after receiving the MR from the MS, the BSC initiates a handover from the overlaid subcell of the originating cell to the overlaid subcell of the target cell. This counter is measured at C when the timer set to wait for a Handover CMP message expires, or at D when the BSC receives a Ho Fail message.

Figure 1 Incoming internal inter-cell handover procedure

Formula

This is an original counter without involving any formula.
