Signal Strength of Neighbor Cell Ranking Nth in MR

Measurement Counter

S380:CELL_NCELL_SORT_FIRST_IN_MR_LEVEL

S381:CELL_NCELL_SORT_SECOND_IN_MR_LEVEL

S382:CELL_NCELL_SORT_THIRD_IN_MR_LEVEL

S383:CELL_NCELL_SORT_FOURTH_IN_MR_LEVEL

S384:CELL_NCELL_SORT_FIFTH_IN_MR_LEVEL

S385:CELL_NCELL_SORT_SIXTH_IN_MR_LEVEL

Description

This measurement provides the accumulated signal strength of the neighbor cell that ranks Nth in the measurement report. N ranges from 1 to 6.

Unit

dBm.

Measurement Point

After the BSC receives a valid MR from the MS, the measurement is performed in the serving cell and the neighbor cell. If the neighbor cell ranks Nth in the MR, the signal strength is accumulated.

Formula

This is an original counter without involving any formula.
