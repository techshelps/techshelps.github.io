LAPD Link Measurement at the BTS

Description

LAPD Link Measurement at the BTS is used to measure the number of various frames sent between the BSC and the BTS.

Measurement Object

LAPD Link

Measurement Counter

The following table lists the measurement counters related to LAPD Link Measurement at the BTS.
