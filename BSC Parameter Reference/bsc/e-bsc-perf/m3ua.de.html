M3UA DestEntity Measurement

Description

The M3UA DestEntity Measurement involves the measurement of the M3UA Destination Inaccessible Times and M3UA Destination Inaccessible Duration.

Note that "inaccessible" here indicates the measurement between entities but not links.

Measurement Object

M3UADE
