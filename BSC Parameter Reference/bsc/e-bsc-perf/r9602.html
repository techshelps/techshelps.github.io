Sampling Times of GPRS Uplink Priority Service

Measurement Counter

R9602: CELL_PDCH_UP_GPRS_TBF_PRI_SAMPLE_NUM

Description

In a granularity period, the BSC searches for GPRS uplink-preferred services in a cell at a predefined sampling interval. This measurement provides the total sampling times of GPRS uplink-preferred services in a cell in a granularity period.

Unit

Integer number or integer.

Measurement Point

If there are GPRS uplink-preferred services in a granularity period, the counter is incremented by one.

Formula

This is an original counter without involving any formula.
