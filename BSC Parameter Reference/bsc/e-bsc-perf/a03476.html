Successful Internal Inter-Cell VGCS TCH Handover Rate

Measurement Counter

A03476: CELL_GCC_INTRABSC_INTERCELL_HO_SUCC_RATE

Description

This measurement provides the ratio of successful internal inter-cell VGCS TCH handover (classified into the incoming internal inter-cell handover and the outgoing internal inter-cell handover).

Unit

%.

Measurement Point

For the measurement of this counter, refer to the following counters:

Successful Incoming Internal Inter-Cell VGCS TCH Handovers

Successful Outgoing Internal Inter-Cell VGCS TCH Handovers

Attempted Incoming Internal Inter-Cell VGCS TCH Handovers

Attempted Outgoing Internal Inter-Cell VGCS TCH Handovers

Formula

Successful Internal Inter-Cell VGCS TCH Handover Rate = ([Successful Incoming Internal Inter-Cell VGCS TCH Handovers] + [Successful Outgoing Internal Inter-Cell VGCS TCH Handovers]) x {100}/([Attempted Incoming Internal Inter-Cell VGCS TCH Handovers] + [Attempted Outgoing Internal Inter-Cell VGCS TCH Handovers])
