Interference Band Measurement per TRX

Description

Interference band is the interference level contained in the RF RES IND message that the BTS reports to the BSC when the channel is idle. There are five levels. The level range of each band is set through the Local Maintenance Terminal (LMT).

The higher rank suggests higher interference level.

Interference Band Measurement per TRX refers to the measurement of idle channels that are in the interference bands.
