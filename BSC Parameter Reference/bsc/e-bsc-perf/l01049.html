Rate of Errored Seconds of Sending over Abis Port

Measurement Counter

L01049:ABISE1_TESR

Description

This measurement provides the rate of Errored Seconds of Sending over Abis Port to Duration of Error Blocks over Abis Port. This measurement is relevant to Errored Seconds of Sending over Abis Port and Duration of Error Blocks over Abis Port.

Unit

%.

Measurement Point

In a granularity period, the BSC accumulates Errored Seconds of Sending over Abis Port and Duration of Error Blocks over Abis Port. When the granularity period is over, this counter is calculated.

Formula

Rate of Errored Seconds of Sending over Abis Port=([Errored Seconds of Sending over Abis Port]/[Duration of Error Blocks over Abis Port])*{100}
