Failed Incoming External Inter-Cell Handovers

Measurement Counter

CH342:CELL_INTERBSC_INCELL_HO_FAIL

Description

The incoming external inter-cell handover may fail due to various reasons.

This measurement provides the number of failed incoming external inter-cell handovers. The measurement is performed based on the originating cell and involves handovers on the SDCCH and TCH and handovers between frequency bands as listed in Table 1.
