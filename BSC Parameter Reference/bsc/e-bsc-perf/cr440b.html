Successful Immediate Assignments

Measurement Counter

CR440B: TRX_IMM_ASS_SUCC

Description

In the immediate assignment procedure, the MS successfully seizes the channel assigned by the BSC. The MS reports an SABM frame on the assigned channel to the BTS to notify the BTS that the radio link layer has entered the status of multiframe link establishment. The BTS returns a UA frame to the MS and at the same time sends an ESTABLISH INDICATION message to the BSC to notify the BSC that a link is normally established on the air interface. Upon receipt of the ESTABLISH INDICATION message, the BSC considers that the immediate assignment is successful and the counter CR440B "Successful Immediate Assignments" is incremented by one. The assigned channel can be an SDCCH or TCH. The counter covers the number of ESTABLISH INDICATION messages received on any channel of the measured TRX.

Unit

Integer number or integer.

Measurement Point

As shown in Figure 1, each time the BSC receives an EST IND message from the BTS (see measurement point C), the counter is incremented by one.

Figure 1 Immediate assignment procedure

Formula

Successful Immediate Assignments = Successful Immediate Assignments (SDCCH) + Successful Immediate Assignments (TCH)
