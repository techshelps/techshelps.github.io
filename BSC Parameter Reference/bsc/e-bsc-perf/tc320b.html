Success Rate of Intra-BSC Incoming Cell Handover During BTS Loopback

Measurement Counter

TC320b:CELL_INTRABSC_INCELL_HO_SUCC_RATE_BTS_LOOP

Description

This measurement provides the success rate of intra-BSC incoming cell handover during BTS loopback. It is calculated by dividing the Number of Intra-BSC Incoming Cell Handover Successes During BTS Loopback by Number of Intra-BSC Incoming Cell Handover Requests During BTS Loopback.

Unit

%.

Measurement Point

None.

Formula

Success Rate of Intra-BSC Outgoing Cell Handover During BTS Loopback = [Number of Intra-BSC Outgoing Cell Handover Successes During BTS Loopback] x {100}/[Number of Intra-BSC Incoming Cell Handover Requests During BTS Loopback]
