Internal Handover Success Ratio per Cell

Measurement Counter

RH303B: CELL_INTRABSC_HO_SUCC_RATE

Description

This measurement provides the success rate of intra-BSC handovers due to any cause, including the inter-cell handovers and the intra-cell handovers.

Unit

%.

Measurement Point

At the end of the granularity period, this counter is calculated by using the following formula.

Formula

Internal Handover Success Ratio per Cell =

([Successful Internal Intra-Cell Handovers per BSC] +

[Successful Incoming Internal Inter-Cell Handovers]) x {100}

/

([Internal Intra-Cell Handover Requests] +

([Incoming Internal Inter-Cell Handover Requests])
