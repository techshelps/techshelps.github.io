PIUEtherPort Measurement per BSC

Description

The PIUEtherPort Measurement per BSC involves the measurement of the HDLC Channel Maximum Sent Rate, HDLC Channel Average Sent Rate, HDLC Channel Maximum Received Rate, HDLC Channel Average Received Rate, HDLC Channel Total Sent Packets, HDLC Channel Total Received Packets, HDLC Channel Total Sent Bytes, HDLC Channel Total Received Bytes, HDLC Channel Total Sent Discarding Packets, HDLC Channel Total Received Discarding Packets, and HDLC Channel Average Configuration Bandwidth.

Measurement Object

BSCHDLCCHANNEL
