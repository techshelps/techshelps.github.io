Unsuccessful Internal Inter-Cell VGCS TCH Handovers (Timing Advance Out of Range)

Measurement Counter

A03470: CELL_GCC_INTRABSC_INTERCELL_HO_FAIL_TA_ILLEGAL

Description

After the internal inter-cell VGCS TCH handover procedure is started, the originating cell sends a HANDOVER COMMAND message to the MS. After the MS successfully accesses the target cell, the target cell requests the originating cell to clear the original resources. Thus, the internal inter-cell handover procedure is complete. If the MS cannot access the target cell due to invalid TA (TA out of range), then the internal inter-cell handover procedure fails. This measurement provides the number of unsuccessful internal inter-cell VGCS TCH handovers due to invalid TA.

Unit

Integer number or integer.

Measurement Point

In the internal inter-cell VGCS TCH handover procedure, the MS sends a HANDOVER FAILURE (0x08: Handover impossible, timing advance out of range) message to the originating cell if the MS fails to access the target cell. This counter is incremented by one when the originating cell receives a HANDOVER FAILURE message from the MS. The measurement point of this counter is A in the unsuccessful internal inter-cell VGCS TCH handover procedure, as shown in Figure 1.
