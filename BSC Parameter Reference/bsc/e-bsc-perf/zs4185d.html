Ratio of MRs on Downlink TCHH (Receive Quality Rank 5) per BSC

Measurement Counter

ZS4185D:TRX_HR_DL_RX_QLTY_5_RATIO

Description

This counter measures the ratio of MRs, of which the receive quality rank is 5, on downlink TCHH per BSC.

Unit

%.

Measurement Point

BSC counters are calculation counters. This counter is measured according to the formula below when the measurement period ends. For details on the measurement point, see "Measurement Point" of each counter in the formula.

Formula

Ratio of MRs on Downlink TCHH (Receive Quality Rank 5) per BSC=

[Number of MRs on Downlink TCHH (Mean Receive Quality Rank 5) per BSC] x {100}

/

([Number of MRs on Downlink TCHH (Mean Receive Quality Rank 0) per BSC] +

[Number of MRs on Downlink TCHH (Mean Receive Quality Rank 1) per BSC] +

[Number of MRs on Downlink TCHH (Mean Receive Quality Rank 2) per BSC] +

[Number of MRs on Downlink TCHH (Mean Receive Quality Rank 3) per BSC] +

[Number of MRs on Downlink TCHH (Mean Receive Quality Rank 4) per BSC] +

[Number of MRs on Downlink TCHH (Mean Receive Quality Rank 5) per BSC] +

[Number of MRs on Downlink TCHH (Mean Receive Quality Rank 6) per BSC] +

[Number of MRs on Downlink TCHH (Mean Receive Quality Rank 7) per BSC])
