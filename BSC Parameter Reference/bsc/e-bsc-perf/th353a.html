Success Rate of Inter-System Outgoing Cell Handover During BSC Loopback

Measurement Counter

TH353a:CELL_INTERRAN_OUTCELL_HO_SUCC_RATE_BSC_LOOP

Description

This measurement provides the success rate of inter-system outgoing cell handover during BSC loopback. It is calculated by dividing the Number of Inter-System Outgoing Cell Handover Successes During BSC Loopback by the Number of Inter-System Outgoing Cell Handover Requests During BSC Loopback.

Unit

%.

Measurement Point

None.

Formula

Success Rate of Inter-System Outgoing Cell Handover During BSC Loopback = [Number of Inter-System Outgoing Cell Handover Successes During BSC Loopback] x {100}/[Number of Inter-System Outgoing Cell Handover Requests During BSC Loopback]
