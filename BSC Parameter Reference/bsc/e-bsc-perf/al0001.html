Description

The counters AL00n:BS_A_INTFACE_BUSY_TSn_SUMAVR provide the average busy time of timeslot n during a certain period, that is, the average time of timeslot n used in the transmission of speech signals during the granularity period.

(01 <= n <= 31)

Unit

s.

Measurement Point

The BSC measures the sampled status data of each timeslot (timeslot 1 to timeslot 31). The average busy time of a timeslot can be obtained by dividing the total busy time of the timeslot by the number of times that the timeslot is busy.

Formula

Average Busy Time of TSn = [Total Busy Time of TSn]/[Number of Times When TSn Is Busy]
