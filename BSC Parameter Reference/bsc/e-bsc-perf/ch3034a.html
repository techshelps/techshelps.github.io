Successful Internal Intra-Cell Handovers (Dynamic Conversion, TCH-SDCCH)

Measurement Counter

CH3034A: CELL_INTRACELL_HO_SUCC_TCH_SD

Description

This counter indicates the total number of successful internal intra-cell handovers for a dynamic conversion from the TCH to the SDCCH. It is equal to the Internal Intra-Cell Handover Requests (Dynamic Conversion, TCH-SDCCH) minus the Failed Internal Intra-Cell Handovers (Dynamic Conversion, TCH-SDCCH).

Unit

Integer number or integer.

Measurement Point

None.

Formula

Successful Internal Intra-Cell Handovers (Dynamic Conversion, TCH-SDCCH) = [Internal Intra-Cell Handover Requests (Dynamic Conversion, TCH-SDCCH)] C [Failed Internal Intra-Cell Handovers (Dynamic Conversion, TCH-SDCCH)]
