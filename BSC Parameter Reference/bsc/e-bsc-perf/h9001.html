Number of Overlaid Subcell to Underlaid Subcell Handover Requests

Measurement Counter

H9001: OLCELL_INTO_ULCELL_REQ

Description

Upon receipt of the measurement report from the MS, the BSC determines the location of the MS (overlaid or underlaid subcell) based on the receive level and TA value. If the cell supports the handover from the overlaid subcell to the underlaid subcell and the MS is located in the overlaid subcell, then the BSC initiates the overlaid-to-underlaid handover.

Unit

Integer number or integer.

Measurement Point

Upon receipt of the measurement report from the MS, the BSC determines the location of the MS (overlaid or underlaid subcell) based on the receive level and TA value. If the cell supports the handover from the overlaid subcell to the underlaid subcell and the MS is located in the overlaid subcell, then the BSC initiates the overlaid-to-underlaid handover. The counter is incremented by one.

Formula

This is an original counter without involving any formula.
