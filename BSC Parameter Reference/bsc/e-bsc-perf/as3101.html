VAC Compress Rate of Cell

Measurement Counter

AS3101: CELL_VOICE_ACT COMP_RATE

Description

This measurement provides the compression rate of active voice frames.

Unit

None.

Measurement Point

In the granularity period, this counter is calculated according to the following formula.

Formula

VAC Compress Rate of Cell =

([Number of Uplink Frames with FR AMR of 4.75 kbit/s in the Customized MR of Cell]

+ [Number of Uplink Frames with FR AMR of 5.15 kbit/s in the Customized MR of Cell]

+ [Number of Uplink Frames with FR AMR of 5.90 kbit/s in the Customized MR of Cell]

+ [Number of Uplink Frames with FR AMR of 6.70 kbit/s in the Customized MR of Cell]

+ [Number of Uplink Frames with FR AMR of 7.40 kbit/s in the Customized MR of Cell]

+ [Number of Uplink Frames with FR AMR of 7.95 kbit/s in the Customized MR of Cell]

+ [Number of Uplink Frames with FR AMR of 10.20 kbit/s in the Customized MR of Cell]

+ [Number of Uplink Frames with HR AMR of 4.75 kbit/s in the Customized MR of Cell]

+ [Number of Uplink Frames with HR AMR of 5.15 kbit/s in the Customized MR of Cell]

+ [Number of Uplink Frames with HR AMR of 5.90 kbit/s in the Customized MR of Cell]

+ [Number of Uplink Frames with HR AMR of 6.70 kbit/s in the Customized MR of Cell]

+ [Number of Uplink Frames with HR AMR of 7.40 kbit/s in the Customized MR of Cell]

+ [Number of Uplink Frames with FR AMR of 12.20 kbit/s in the Customized MR of Cell]

+ [Number of Uplink Frames Among the FR Frames in the Customized MR of Cell]

+ [Number of Uplink Frames Among the EFR Frames in the Customized MR of Cell]

+ [Number of Uplink Frames Among the HR Frames in the Customized MR of Cell])/(([Duration of Busy State for Channels (TCHF)] + [Duration of Busy State for Channels (TCHH)]) x 50)
