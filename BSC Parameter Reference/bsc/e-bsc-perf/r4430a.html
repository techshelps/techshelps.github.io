Measurement of Attempted Handovers

Measurement Counter

R4430A: TRX_HO_CMD_SD

R4439A: TRX_HO_CMD_TCH

Description

In the SDCCH handover procedure, the BSC activates a channel in the target cell, receives a CHANNEL ACTIVATION ACKNOWLEDGE message, and then sends an HO CMD message through the source cell to the MS. At this time, the counter R4430A "Attempted Handovers (SDCCH)" is incremented by one for the TRX where the SDCCH of the target cell resides.

In the TCH handover procedure, the BSC sends an HO CMD message through the source cell to the MS after receiving a CHANNEL ACTIVATION ACKNOWLEDGE message from the target cell. At this time, the counter R4439A "Attempted Handovers (TCH)" is incremented by one for the TRX where the SDCCH of the target cell resides.

Unit

Integer number or integer.

Measurement Point

The two counters are involved in the Intra-Cell Handover Measurement, Incoming Internal Inter-Cell Handover Measurement, Incoming External Inter-Cell Handover Measurement, and Incoming Inter-RAT Inter-Cell Handover Measurement. See the corresponding sections for the flow charts. Each time the BSC sends an HO CMD message to an MS, the counter is incremented by one.

Formula

This is an original counter without involving any formula.
