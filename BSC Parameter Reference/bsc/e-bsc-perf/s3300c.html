Description

After receiving a valid MR from the MS or a valid pre-processed MR from the BTS, the BSC makes a handover decision. If a handover is required, the BSC measures the specific counter based on the uplink receive level (0-7) and the uplink receive quality (0-7). This counter indicates the performance of the handover in the serving cell.

Unit

Integer number or integer.

Measurement Point

After receiving a valid MR from the MS or a valid pre-processed MR from the BTS, the BSC makes a handover decision. If a handover is required, the specific counter is incremented by one based on the uplink receive level and the uplink receive quality.

Formula

This is an original counter without involving any formula.
