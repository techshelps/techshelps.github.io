Number of Failed Network Controlled Cell Reselections

Measurement Counter

H9014: NC_CELL_RESELECT_FAIL_ATMPT

Description

This measurement provides the number of failed cell reselection attempts controlled by the network in a granularity period.

Unit

Integer number or integer.

Measurement Point

Based on the measurement report sent by the MS and the condition of the cell that the MS camps on, the BSC determines whether to initiate the cell reselection. A network-controlled cell reselection attempt will be triggered due to deteriorated downlink transmission quality, traffic overload in the cell, or high receive level in a neighboring cell. Every time a failed message is received by the BSC, the counter is incremented by one.

Formula

This is an original counter without involving any formula.
