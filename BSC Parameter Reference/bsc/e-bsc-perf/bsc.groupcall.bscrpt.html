VGCS/VBS Measurement per BSC

Description

The measurement unit VGCS/VBS Measurement per BSC is used to measure the VGCS/VBS of all the cells controlled by a BSC.

The following lists the measurement counters related to VGCS/VBS Measurement per BSC:
  • The times of VGCS/VBS assignment requests includes the total times of VGCS/VBS assignment requests, successful times of VGCS/VBS assignment requests, and failed times of VGCS/VBS assignment requests because of various reasons.
  • The VGCS/VBS traffic volume includes the uplink and downlink traffic volume.
  • The number of VGCS/VBS channel handovers includes the total number of attempted VGCS/VBS channel handovers, number of successful VGCS/VBS channel handovers, and number of failed VGCS/VBS channel handovers because of various reasons.
  • The number of VGCS/VBS call drops and call drop rate on the uplink.
  • Handover success rate.
  • Successful assignment preemption rate and successful assignment rate.

Measurement Object

BSCRPT.
