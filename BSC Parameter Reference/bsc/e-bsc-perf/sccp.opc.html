SCCP OSP Measurement

Description

This measurement unit is used to collect statistics of the SCCP faults related to the signaling point and measure the signaling performance. When a message is sent from the signaling point at the local end or a message is received from the signaling point at the peer end, the counters related to this measurement unit are measured.
