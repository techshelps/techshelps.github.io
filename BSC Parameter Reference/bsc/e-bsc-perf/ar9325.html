Average Allocated Seizure Duration of All Available PDCHs (s)

Measurement Counter

AR9325: AVR_ALL_AVAIL_PDCH_ALLOC_TIME

Description

This measurement provides the average duration in which all available PDCHs in a cell are occupied in a granularity period. This measurement indicates whether the PS services are busy. You can reconfigure the channel according to this measurement. For the busy cell, you can configure more PDCHs or increase the value of Maximum Ratio Threshold of PDCHs.

Unit

s.

Measurement Point

None.

Formula

Average Allocated Seizure Duration of All Available PDCHs (s) = Total Allocated Seizure Duration of All Available PDCHs (ms)/Sampling Times of PDCH Measurement/1000
