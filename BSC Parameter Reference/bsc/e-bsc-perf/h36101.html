Measurement of Incoming Inter-RAT Inter-Cell Handover Responses (TDD)

Measurement Counter

H36101:CELL_INTERRAN_TDD_INCELL_HO_REQ_ACK_SD

H36171:CELL_INTERRAN_TDD_INCELL_HO_REQ_ACK_TCHF

H36181:CELL_INTERRAN_TDD_INCELL_HO_REQ_ACK_TCHH

Description

The specific counter provides the number of incoming inter-RAT inter-cell handover responses based on the type of the allocated target channel (SDCCH/TCHF/TCHH) when the BSC returns an HO REQ ACK message to the MSC during the incoming inter-RAT inter-cell handover (TDD). Together with Incoming Inter-RAT Inter-Cell Handover Requests (TDD) and Failed Incoming Inter-RAT Inter-Cell Handovers(TDD), this counter indicates the performance of the incoming inter-RAT inter-cell handover (TDD).

The channel involved in the measurement can be the SDCCH, TCHF, or TCHH.

Unit

Integer number or integer.

Measurement Point

As shown in Figure 1, the specific counter is measured at B, based on the type of the destination channel, when the BSC returns an HO REQ ACK message to the MSC.

Figure 1 Measurement of the counters related to incoming inter-RAT inter-cell handover (excluding directed retry) (TDD)

A

Measurement point of Incoming Inter-RAT Inter-Cell Handover Requests (TDD)

B

Measurement point of Incoming Inter-RAT Inter-Cell Handover Responses (TDD)

C

Measurement point of Incoming Inter-RAT Inter-Cell Handover Detection Messages (TDD)

Measurement point of Successful Incoming Inter-RAT Inter-Cell Handovers (TDD)

Formula

This is an original counter without involving any formula.
