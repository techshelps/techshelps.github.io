Failed Incoming External Inter-Cell Handovers (1800/1900-900/850)

Measurement Counter

RH3034F: CELL_INTERBSC_IN_CELL_HO_FAIL_TIMES_1800_900

Description

The incoming external inter-cell handover may fail due to various reasons.

This measurement provides the total number of failed incoming external inter-cell handovers initiated by the BSC. The measurement is performed in the target cell and covers failed handovers on the SDCCH and the TCH. In addition, the frequency band of the originating cell can be DCS1800 or PCS1900, and the frequency band of the target cell can be GSM900, GSM900&DCS1800, GSM850, GSM850&DCS1800, or GSM850&PCS1900.

Unit

Integer number or integer.

Measurement Point

This counter is measured according to the following formula.

Formula

Failed Incoming External Inter-Cell Handovers (1800/1900-900/850)

= [Failed Incoming Internal Inter-Cell Handovers (SDCCH) (1800/1900-900/850)] +

[Failed Incoming Internal Inter-Cell Handovers (TCH) (1800/1900-900/850)]
