Received Signaling-Plane Packets

Measurement Counter

S4002A: TRX_RX_SIGNALING_PACK_NUM

Description

This measurement provides the number of signaling-plane packets that the TRX receives from the BSC over the HDLC path when transmission optimization is implemented in the BTS.

Unit

Integer number or integer.

Measurement Point

The TRX performs the measurement after receives the signaling-plane packets. The BTS periodically reports the results, and the BTSM disassembles the packets and processes the measurement results.

Formula

This is an original counter without involving any formula.
