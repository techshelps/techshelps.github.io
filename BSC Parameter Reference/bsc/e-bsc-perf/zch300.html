Internal Intra-Cell Handover Requests per BSC

Measurement Counter

ZCH300:BSCRPT_INTRACELL_HO_REQ

Description

This measurement provides the number of internal intra-cell handover requests of all the cells controlled by a BSC. The channel involved in the measurement can be the SDCCH, TCHF, or TCHH.

The handover causes consist of the following: upper quality, downlink quality, uplink strength, downlink strength, timing advance, cell load, rapid level drop, MSC intervention, OM intervention, EDGE coverage, and other causes.

Unit

Integer number or integer.

Measurement Point

This counter is measured according to the following formula when the measurement period ends. For details on the measurement point, see "Measurement Point" of each counter in the formula.

Formula

Internal Intra-Cell Handover Requests per BSC = Internal Intra-Cell Handover Requests

(That is, the total of the original counter Internal Intra-Cell Handover Requests in all the cells of the BSC.)
