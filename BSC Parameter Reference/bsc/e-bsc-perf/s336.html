Mean Downlink Receiving Level during Edge Handover Initiation

Measurement Counter

S336: CELL_EDGE_HO_DL_LEV_AVG

Description

This measurement provides the average downlink receive level during edge handover in a specific period. Together with Mean Downlink Receiving Level during Handover Initiation and Mean Uplink Receiving Level during Edge Handover Initiation, this counter is helpful in edge handover troubleshooting and setting of Edge HO UL RX_LEV Threshold and Edge HO DL RX_LEV Threshold. This measurement is performed in the originating cell.

Unit

dBm.

Measurement Point

Refer to the following counters: Downlink Receive Level during Edge Handover Initiation, Cell Outgoing Handover Commands (Uplink Strength), and Cell Outgoing Handover Commands (Downlink Strength).

Formula

Downlink Receiving Level during Edge Handover Initiation/[Outgoing Internal Inter-Cell Handover Requests (Uplink Strength)] + [Outgoing Internal Inter-Cell Handover Requests (Downlink Strength)]+[Outgoing External Inter-Cell Handover Requests (Uplink Strength)]+[Outgoing External Inter-Cell Handover Requests (Downlink Strength)]
