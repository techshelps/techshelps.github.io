Traffic Volume on Signaling Channels (TCH)

Measurement Counter

R3550N:CELL_SIG_CH_TRAF_TCH

Description

This measurement provides the traffic volume on the signaling channels (TCH), that is, the average number of busy TCH channels for carrying signaling that are scanned in the granularity period.

Unit

Erl.

Measurement Point

In the granularity period, this counter is calculated according to the following formula.

Formula

Traffic Volume on Signaling Channels (TCH) = ([Number of Busy Signaling Channels (TCHF)] + [Number of Busy Signaling Channels (TCHH)])/[Number of Attempts to Scan SDCCH States]
