Number of Times the CV_BEP in the Downlink Code Streams on the TCHF Is N

Measurement Counter

S4800A:TRX_FR_NBR_CV_BEP_0

S4801A:TRX_FR_NBR_CV_BEP_1

S4802A:TRX_FR_NBR_CV_BEP_2

S4803A:TRX_FR_NBR_CV_BEP_3

S4804A:TRX_FR_NBR_CV_BEP_4

S4805A:TRX_FR_NBR_CV_BEP_5

S4806A:TRX_FR_NBR_CV_BEP_6

S4807A:TRX_FR_NBR_CV_BEP_7

Description

The measurement of the preceding counters provides the number of times that the BTS reports the EMR or pre-processed EMR with the CV_BEP ranging from 0 to 7 over the TCHF.

Unit

Integer number or integer.

Measurement Point

After receiving an EMR or pre-processed EMR from the BTS over the TCHF, the BSC decodes the CV_BEP in the downlink MR, and the specific counter is incremented by one.

Formula

This is an original counter without involving any formula.
