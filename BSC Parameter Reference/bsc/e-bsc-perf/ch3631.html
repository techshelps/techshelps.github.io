Successful Incoming Inter-RAT Inter-Cell Handovers (TDD)

Measurement Counter

CH3631:CELL_INTERRAN_TDD_INCELL_HO_SUCC

Description

This measurement provides the total number of successful incoming inter-RAT inter-cell handovers (TDD). It is calculated by subtracting the Failed Incoming Inter-RAT Inter-Cell Handovers (TDD) from the Incoming Inter-RAT Inter-Cell Handover Requests (TDD).

Unit

Integer number or integer.

Measurement Point

As shown in Figure 1, this counter is measured at D when the BSC receives the HO CMP message from the MS during the incoming inter-RAT inter-cell handover (TDD).

Figure 1 Measurement of the counters related to incoming inter-RAT inter-cell handover (excluding directed retry) (TDD)

A

Measurement point of Incoming Inter-RAT Inter-Cell Handover Requests (TDD)

B

Measurement point of Incoming Inter-RAT Inter-Cell Handover Responses (TDD)

C

Measurement point of Incoming Inter-RAT Inter-Cell Handover Detection Messages (TDD)

D

Measurement point of Successful Incoming Inter-RAT Inter-Cell Handovers (TDD)

Formula

Successful Incoming Inter-RAT Inter-Cell Handovers (TDD) = [Incoming Inter-RAT Inter-Cell Handover Requests (TDD)] - [Failed Incoming Inter-RAT Inter-Cell Handovers (TDD)]
