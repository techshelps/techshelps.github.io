Successful TCH Seizures on the TRX in the Overlaid Subcell

Measurement Counter

M338a:CELL_CALL_TCH_SUCC_OVERLAY

Description

This measurement provides the number of successful seizures of the TCH carried on the TRX in the overlaid subcell of a concentric cell.

Unit

Integer number or integer.

Measurement Point

In the assignment procedure, if the TRX is in the overlaid subcell of a concentric cell and a TCH is seized during a call, this counter is incremented by one when the BSC receives an ASS CMP message from the MS.

In the incoming inter-cell handover procedure, if the TRX is in the overlaid subcell of a concentric cell and a TCH is seized during a call, this counter is incremented by one when the BSC receives an HO CMP message from the MS.
