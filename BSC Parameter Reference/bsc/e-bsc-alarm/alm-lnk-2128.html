Check the current alarms

1) Right click the APMU board?

2) Select Board Warning on the shortcut menu.3) The Board Alarm Information dialog box is displayed.

The green one means normal state. The red one means there is an alarm. Click a red grid to show the description of the specific alarm in the right pane.
