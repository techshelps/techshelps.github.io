Query the charging current-limiting coefficient and the battery capacity.

To query the current-limiting coefficient and the battery capacity,do as follows:

1) Log in to the BSC6000 Local Maintenance Terminal.

2) Click a site in the BSC6000 management tree.

The Site Device Panel tab page is displayed.

3)Right-click the DPMU and choose Set/Query Power Module Parameters on the shortcut menu.

4) The Current Limiting Coefficient of Battery Charging and Volume of Assembled Battery can be viewed in the Battery Information tab page.
