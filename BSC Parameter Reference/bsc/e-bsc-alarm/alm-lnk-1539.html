Reset the BTS board

1) Log in to the LMT and then choose Alarm Maintenance > Browse Alarm. Then, view the Site No. in the alarm location information.

2) Select the corresponding site on the Management Tree tab page.

3) On the Site Device Panel tab page, right-click the BTS board to be reset and then choose Reset Board from the shortcut menu.
