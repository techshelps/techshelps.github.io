Replace the faulty TRX

Switch off the faulty TRX, remove the RF cables (feeders) of the TRX, and then remove the TRX from the slot. Then insert the new TRX into the slot and tighten the screws. Ensure that the RF cables (feeders) are properly connected and that the TRX is properly installed, and then turn on the switch.
