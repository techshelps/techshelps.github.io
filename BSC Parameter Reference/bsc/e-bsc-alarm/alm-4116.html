4116 TRX Voltage Alarm

Description

This alarm is reported when the output voltage of the power unit inside the Carrier Module is abnormal, causing the Carrier Module unable to run normally.

Alarm ID Alarm Severity Alarm Type
4116 Major Fault

Impact on the System

The DTRU cannot run normally when this alarm is generated and other unpredicted alarms may be generated.

System Actions

  • When this alarm occurs, the system shuts off the power amplifier automatically.
  • For the DTRU of main BCCH, DTRU mutual aid is started.

Possible Causes

  • Faulty hardware of DTRU;
  • The -48V voltage is abnormal.

Procedure

  1. Check if the -48V power supply is normal

    Shut down the corresponding air-break switch of the faulty DTRX. Pull out the power cables of the DTRU. Test the voltage of the power supply to see whether it is normal (within the range of -40 V to -60 V)

    Y=> Go to step 3.

    N=> Go to step 2.

  2. Test the input voltage of the lighting protection module of the power supply
    1. BTS3012:

      Test the input voltage of the lighting protection module of the power supply to see whether it is normal (normal range: from -40V to -60V).

      Y=> Test the voltages of the lighting protection module of the power supply, EMI filter, BusBar power switch, and the corresponding air-break switch to check whether there is any contact problem, and replace the faulty equipment.

      N=> Please contact Huawei Customer Service Center.

    2. BTS3012AE:

      Test the voltage at the BUSBAR power switch

      Test whether the voltage at the BUSBAR power switch is normal (normal range: from -40V to -60V) and see whether there is any contact problem.

      Y=> Go to step 3.

      N=> Please contact Huawei Customer Service Center.

    3. BTS3006C:

      2) Test the voltage at the power switch

      Test whether the voltage at the power switch is normal (normal range: from -40V to -60V) and see whether there is any contact problem.

      Y=> Go to step 3.

      N=> Please contact Huawei Customer Service Center.

  3. Replace the faulty TRX

  4. Replace the faulty TRX [/#2001]. Check if the alarm is cleared.

    Y=> The alarm is cleared. The Alarm Handling is completed.

    N=> The alarm is not cleared. Please contact Huawei Customer Service Center.
