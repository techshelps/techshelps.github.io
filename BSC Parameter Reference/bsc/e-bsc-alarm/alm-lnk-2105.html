Reset the site in Level 4

To reset the site, do as follows:

[1]Log in to the BSC6000 Local Maintenance Terminal.

[2]Right-click a site in the BSC6000 management tree and choose Reset site Leveled on the shortcut menu.

The Reset Site in Levels dialog box is displayed.

[3]Click Level 4 in the Level option and click Reset.
