Reset the NFCB through the hardware or software

1) Log in to the BSC6000 Local Maintenance Terminal.

2) Click a site in the BSC6000 management tree.

The Site Device Panel tab page is displayed.

3)Right-click the NFCB and choose Reset Board on the shortcut menu.

The Reset Board dialog box is displayed.

4)Click Reset.
