ALM-2226 BTS Maintenance Alarm

Description

The alarm is triggered when one of the following phenomena occurs: the TRX or CDU detects an erroneous ID defined by the DIP switches on the TRB panel, the DTRU does not match the PSU, and the BTS3X is configured with old DTRUs.

Impact on the System

None.

System Actions

None.

Possible Causes

  • The DIP switches are not correctly set.
  • The TMU is faulty.
  • The DTRUs are configured in the BTS when the AC/DC PSU is used.
  • Faulty kind of DTRUs are configured in the BTS.

Procedure

  1. Check whether the DIP switches S1?S4 on the TRB panel are correctly set based on the following table.

    note:

    1 ang s3 indicates cabinet number 0?7.OFF stands for 1 and ON for 0.

    A cabinet number is denoted by a binary numeral. F_ID3/ID6 is the most significant bit (MSB) and F_ID0/ID3 the least significant bit (LSB).

    To indicate cabinet number 5 (0101), for example, set F_ID3/ID6 to ON, F_ID2/ID5 to OFF, F_ID1/ID4 to ON and F_ID0/ID3 to OFF.

    note:

    s2 and s4 indicates frame number 0?3.OFF stands for 1 and ON for 0.

    A frame number is denoted by a binary numeral and specified only by B_ID1/ID2 and B_ID2/ID7. B_ID2/ID7 is the most significant bit (MSB) and B_ID1/ID2 the least significant bit (LSB).

    The frames are numbered 0?3 from the bottom up.

    To indicate frame number 2 (10), for example, set B_ID2/ID7 to OFF and B_ID1/ID2 to ON.

    Y=> The alarm may be caused by TMU hardware failure. Go to step 2.

    N=> Modify the relevant settings. The alarm handling ends.

  2. Replace the TMU.

    Replace the TMU and check whether the alarm is cleared.

    Y=> The alarm handling ends.

    N=> Huawei Customer Service Center.

  3. Check the PSU type.

    Check whether the PSU is of AC/DC PSU. (character such as AC/DC or DC/DC is labeld on the pane of the PSU)

    Y=> Check whether the DTRU is configured in the BTS. If there is DTRU in the BTS, remove the DTRU from the slots and delete the relevant configuration on the LMT.

    N=> No DTRU is configured in the BTS. Go to step 4.

  4. Replace the old DTRU.

    The BTS3X does not support old DTRU. Replace the old DTRU with the new kind of DTRU. Check whether the alarm is cleared.

    Y=> The alarm is cleared. The alarm handling is complete.

    N=> Please contact Huawei Customer Service Center.
