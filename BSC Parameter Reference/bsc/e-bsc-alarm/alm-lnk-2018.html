Turn on the power amplifier of the TRX corresponding to the CDU

1) Log in to the BSC6000 Local Maintenance Terminal.

2) Right-click a TRX and choose Modify Management Status on the shortcut menu.

The Modify Management Status dialog box is displayed.

3) Choose Unlock in the Modified Status drop down list.

4) Click Apply.
