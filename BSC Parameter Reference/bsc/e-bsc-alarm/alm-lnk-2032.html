Replace the faulty EMU

To replace the EMU board, perform the following steps:

1) Remove the faulty EMU.

2) Install the new EMU in the slot of the faulty one.

3) Check the replaced board.

4) Check the indicator on the panel of the new board.

If the green LED is ON, it means that the EMU board is operating normally.
