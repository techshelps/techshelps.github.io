ALM-2152 TRX Temperature Abnormal Alarm

Description

For the common TRX, if the temperature of the power amplifier exceeds 85℃, this alarm is reported. For the DETR and PETR TRXs, if the temperature of the power amplifier exceeds 95℃, this alarm is reported. For other EDGE TRXs, if the temperature of the power amplifier exceeds 90℃, this alarm is reported.

Impact on the System

The faulty TRX runs abnormally and all the services on the TRX are interrupted upon occurrence of this alarm.

System Actions

None.

Possible Causes

  • The heat dissipation system is abnormal.
  • The room temperature is too high.
  • The hardware of the TRX is faulty.

Procedure

  1. Check the Heat Dissipation System

    Check whether the ALM-2354 Fan Alarm, the ALM-2480 BTS3012A Air-Conditioner Alarm or the ALM-2492 BTS3012A Heat-Exchanger Alarm is reported. If any of these alarms is reported, power off the TRX board, clear the alarm. Then, power on the TRX board ten minutes after you clear the alarm. Check whether the alarm is cleared.

    Y=> The alarm is cleared. The alarm handling is complete.

    N=> The alarm is not cleared. Go to step 2.

  2. Check the Working Environment of the BTS

    Check whether the environment temperature is higher than 50℃. If the temperature is higher than 50℃, power off the TRX board, take corresponding measures to make the environment temperature below 25℃, and then power on the TRX board. Check whether the alarm is cleared.

    Y=> The alarm is cleared. The alarm handling is complete.

    N=> The alarm is not cleared. Go to step 3.

  3. Replace the TRX Board

    Replace the TRX board , and check whether the alarm is cleared.

    Y=> The alarm is cleared. The alarm handling is complete.

    N=> The alarm is not cleared. Contact the Huawei Customer Service Center.
