To query the temperature and humidity thresholds, do as follows:

1) Log in to the BSC6000 Local Maintenance Terminal.

2) Right-click the faulty site and choose Monitor Environment on the shortcut menu.

The Monitor Environment dialog box is displayed.

3) Click Get Temperature and Humidity and click Apply.

The result is displayed in the lower portion of the Monitor Environment window.
