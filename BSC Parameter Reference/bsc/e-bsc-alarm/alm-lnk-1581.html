Reset the Board that Fails the Synchronization

1) Log in to the LMT. Then, view the Subrack No. and Slot No. in the Location Info to locate the board to be reset.

2) Click the BSC6000 on the Management Tree tab page.

3) On the BSC Device Panel tab page, right-click the board to be reset and choose Query BSC Board Information from the shortcut menu to view the board status.

a. If the boards work in active/standby mode, reset the standby board, and then reset the active board.

b. If the board works in independent mode, block the related services on the cells and the TRXs, and then reset the board.

Note: After the board is reset, unblock the related services on the cells and the TRXs.
