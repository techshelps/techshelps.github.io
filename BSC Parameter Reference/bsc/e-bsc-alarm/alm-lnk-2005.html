Modify the Data

1) Log in to the BSC6000 Local Maintenance Terminal.

2) Select a cell under the BTS3012 and click the Cell Attributes tab.

3) Double-click a TRX under the Assigned TRXs at the bottom of the Cell Attributes page.

The Browse TRX Attributes dialog box is displayed.

4) Choose Channel Attributes tab and modify the parameters in the Channel Configure option.
