ALM-2124 TRX Close HPA Alarm

Description

When the temperature of the BTS3012A basic cabinet exceeds 52 too high and the function of "Derate upon TRX high temperature" in the software configuration is set to "Yes", the system will enter automatic protection state, turn off the power amplifiers of some TRXs and report this alarm.

Impact on the System

If the function "Derate upon TRX high temperature" in the software configuration is set to "Yes", the services of the system will partially be affected because it automatically turns off the power amplifiers of some TRXs.

System Actions

None.

Possible Causes

  • The internal temperature of the BTS3012A basic cabinet reaches 52°C.
  • The temperature detection circuit is faulty.
  • There is VSWR alarms on the combiner and divider to which the TRX is connected.

Procedure

  1. Query the temperature inside the cabinet.

    On the LMT or local maintenance terminal, check whether the temperature inside the BTS3012A cabinet is higher than 52°C .

    Y=> Go to step 3;

    N=> Go to step 2;

    NOTE:

    It takes about two minutes for the maintenance terminal to display the actual temperature of the board.

  2. Check the combiner and divider.

    Check whether a VSWR alarm is generated on the combiner and divider. If the VSWR alarm is reported, ensure that the antenna is correctly connected, or replace the combiner and divider. Check whether the alarm is cleared.

    Y=> The alarm handing ends;

    N => Go to step 3.

  3. Reset the BTS.

    On the LMT or local maintenance terminal, perform the fourth level reset for the BTS3012A. Check whether the alarm is cleared.

    Y=> The BTS3012A has false alarm. The alarm handing ends;

    N => The BTS3012A temperature detecting circuit might be faulty. Contact Huawei Customer Service Center.

  4. Query air-conditioner/heat-exchanger alarm.

    On the LMT, check whether the ALM-2480 BTS3012A Air-Conditioner Alarm or ALM-2492 BTS3012A Heat-Exchanger Alarm is reported. Clear the alarm according to the handling suggestions in the ALM-2480 BTS3012A Air-Conditioner Alarm or ALM-2492 BTS3012A Heat-Exchanger Alarm. Check whether the alarm is cleared.

    Y=> The alarm handing ends;

    N => Go to step 5.

  5. Observe the ambient temperature.

    Check whether the ambient temperature of the cabinet is higher than 55°C for the air-conditioner cabinet or higher than 45°C for the heat-exchanger cabinet.

    Y=> Go to step 6;

    N => The temperature detecting circuit or temperature control part in the BTS3012A might be faulty. Contact Huawei Customer Service Center.

  6. Lower the ambient temperature.

    A BTS3012A air-conditioner cabinet can work normally under the ambient temperature of 55°C. A BTS3012A heat-exchanger cabinet can work normally under the ambient temperature of 45°C. If the actual ambient temperature of the cabinet exceeds the relevant temperature range, take some measures to lower the ambient temperature, for example, prevent the cabinet from direct sunlight. When the ambient temperature of the cabinet remains under the normal temperature range for a while, check whether the alarm is cleared.

    Y=> The alarm handing ends;

    N => The temperature detecting circuit or temperature control part in BTS3012A might be faulty.Contact Huawei Customer Service Center.
