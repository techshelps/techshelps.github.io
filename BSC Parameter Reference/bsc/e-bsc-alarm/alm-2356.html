ALM-2356 Battery high temperature alarm

Description

It reports the battery high temperature alarm when the temperature of the storage batterie's surface overrun the limited.

Attribute

Alarm ID Alarm Severity Alarm Type
2356 Major Fault

Parameters

ID Name Meaning
1 Site No.

Number of the site where the faulty board or module is located

Value range: 0?2047
2 Site type

Type of the site where the faulty board or module is located

3: BTS30

5: BTS312

10: BTS3012A

11: BTS3006A
3 Board ID

Type of the faulty board or module

86: PMU
4 Board No.

Number of the faulty board or module

0: PMU 1
5 Sub No.

Alarm sub-category number

The BTS30 fan alarm has four sub-branches. The sub-branches are numbered from 0 to 3. The sub branch number of the only fan is 0, and the other sub branches are reserved.

There are four routes of fan alarms associated with the BTS312, number 0 to 3 (facing the front door of the cabinet).

0: Right-hand fan of the middle subrack of the cabinet

1: Left-hand fan of the middle subrack of the cabinet

2: Fan above the cabinet

3: Reserved

There are six routes of fan alarms associated with the BTS3012A, number 0 to 6 (facing the front door of the cabinet).

0: Fan of the middle subrack away from the auxiliary cabinet

1: Fan of the middle subrack near the auxiliary cabinet

2: Fan subrack above the cabinet

3: Fan subrack of the auxiliary cabinet

4: Reserved

6: Reserved
6 Cell ID.

Index of the cell where the board or module is located

0-2047: Cell index of the faulty cell in the data configuration

65536: None

Impact on the System

None.

System Actions

None

Possible Causes

  • The storage battery over loading.
  • The high temperature of batteries' circumambience.

Procedure

  1. Check whether the battery over loading.

    Y=> Close part of the correlative board.The alarm handling ends.

    NOTE:

    Meanwhile, check whether the devices in the cabinet are damaged. Huawei Customer Service Center. if damaged.

    N=> Go to Step 2.

  2. Check whether the temperature of batteries' circumambience being high.

    Ventilate the battery.View whether the alarm is cleared.

    Y=> The alarm handling ends.

    N=> Please contact Huawei Customer Service Center.
