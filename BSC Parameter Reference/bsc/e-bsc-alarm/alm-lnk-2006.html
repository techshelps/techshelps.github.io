Check the Power Level of the TRX in the data configuration

1)In the management tree of the LMT, right-click the cell where the TRX is located under the BSC6000 node.

2)Double click the TRX needs to be checked in Site Device Panel.

3)Choose Device Attributes tab in the Browse TRX Attributes.

4)Check the Power Level and Power Type.
