Check the channel configuration on the BTS side

1) Log in to the SMT.

2) In the Site Maintenance Terminal System window, select "Channel" in the left pane and then double-click "Channel Attributes Management" in the right pane.

3) Click "Refresh".

4) The SMT obtains the latest data from the BTS. You can perform the refresh operation to confirm the data accuracy.
