Check if The parameters of the CCCH in the system information must match with the channel attributes configured.

1) Log in to the BSC6000 Local Maintenance Terminal.

2) Select a cell and click the Cell Attributes tab.

3) Click Idle Mode under the Advanced Attributes.

The Browse Idle Parameter dialog box is displayed.

4) Check whether the parameters of the CCCH are configured correctly.

Note:

1) The parameters of the CCCH in the system information must match with the channel attributes configured.

2) The parameters of the CCCH must be allocated in timeslots 0, 2, 4, and 6 in sequence; in case timeslot 2 is not CCCH, timeslots 4 and 6 cannot be CCCH either.
