ALM-2542 TRX/CDU Power- Off Alarm

Description

This alarm is triggered when all of the TRXs and the CDUs of the BTS are powered down because the AC power supply for the BTS3012A shuts off and the output voltage from the battery is under the normal range.

Attribute

Alarm ID Alarm Severity Alarm Type
2542 Major Fault

140:OSSU

Impact on the System

When this alarm occurs, the BTS cannot communicate with the other devices.

System Actions

All of the TRXs and the CDUs are powered down.

Possible Causes

  • The TMU shuts down the power for the TRXs and the CDUs since the power stored in the battery is almost used up.
  • There is no mains input.
  • The temperature of the cabinet is too high.

Procedure

  1. Check the working status of the storage battery.

    Judge whether the battery is in normal operation according to the data given in the table below, which shows the correspondence relation among the current, power and the life-span after a recharge.

    Configuration Power (W) Three hours (life-span) Four hours (life-span) Six hours (life-span) Eight hours (life-span)

    S1/1/1 1.220 260Ah 300Ah 400Ah 500Ah

    S2/2/2 1.650 300Ah 400Ah 500Ah 650Ah

    S3/3/3 2.100 400Ah 500Ah 650Ah 800Ah

    S4/4/4 2.520 500Ah 650Ah 800Ah -

    Y=>The alarm handling ends. The alarm occurs because the system starts the hierarchical power supply mode for protection.

    N=> Please contact Huawei Customer Service Center.The alarm may be caused by error in BTS software configuration or fault of the battery.

  2. Check the temperature inside the cabinet.

    Check if the ALM-2376 Over Temperature Alarm is reported. Handle the alarm according to the suggestions in ALM-2376 Over Temperature Alarm. Check if the alarm is cleared.

    Y=>The alarm handling ends.

    N=>Please contact Huawei Customer Service Center.

    If the ALM-2376 Over Temperature Alarm is not reported, go to Step 3).

  3. Check the air conditioner or the heat exchanger.

    Check if the ALM-2480 BTS3012A Air-Conditioner Alarm or ALM-2492 BTS3012A Heat-Exchanger Alarm is reported. Handle the alarm according to the suggestions in ALM-2376 Over Temperature Alarm or ALM-2492 BTS3012A Heat-Exchanger Alarm. Check if the alarm is cleared.

    Y=>The alarm handling ends.

    N=>Please contact Huawei Customer Service Center.

    If the ALM-2480 BTS3012A Air-Conditioner Alarm or ALM-2492 BTS3012A Heat-Exchanger Alarm is not reported, Please contact Huawei Customer Service Center.
