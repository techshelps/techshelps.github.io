Stop Get The Compress Log of BTS to OMU(STP BTSCMPLOG2OMU)

Function

An OM command that stops the task of obtaining BTS compressed logs and saving them to the GOMU/GBAM.

Note

1. The MML client must communicate properly with the BSC.

2. The BSC must communicate properly with the BTS.

Parameters

None.

Example

Stop obtaining the compressed logs of the BTS:
STP BTSCMPLOG2OMU:;
