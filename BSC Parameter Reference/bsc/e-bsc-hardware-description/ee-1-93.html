Components of the BSC Subrack

This describes the components of the BSC subrack. The BSC subrack consists of the fan box, slots, front cable trough, and backplane.

Structure of the BSC Subrack

The BSC subrack is designed in compliance with IEC60297 standards. The BSC subrack is 19 inches wide and 12 U high. Figure 1 shows the structure of the BSC subrack.

Figure 1 Structure of the BSC subrack

(1) Fan box (2) Hanger (3) Guide rail
(4) Front cable trough (5) Board (6) Grounding screw
(7) Port for DC power input (8) Input port for the monitoring signals of the power distribution box (9) Cover of the DIP switch
