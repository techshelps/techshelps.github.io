BSC Cabinet

The BSC uses the Huawei N68E-22 cabinet. The cabinet is designed in compliance with IEC60297 and IEEE standards.

  • Appearance of the BSC Cabinet
    The dimensions of the BSC cabinet are as follows: 2,200 mm (height) x 600 mm (width) x 800 mm (depth). The BSC cabinets are classified into single-door cabinets and double-door cabinets.
  • Classification of the BSC Cabinet
    Based on functions, the BSC cabinets are classified into the GBCR and the GBSR.
