LEDs on BSC Parts

This describes the LEDs on the power distribution box and the panels.

  • LEDs on the Front Panel of the BSC Common Power Distribution Box
    The LEDs on the front panel of the BSC common power distribution box are labeled RUN and ALM.
  • LEDs on the Front Panel of the BSC High-Power Distribution Box
    The LEDs on the front panel of the BSC high-power distribution box are labeled RUN and ALM.
  • LEDs on the GDPUC(DPUa) Panel
    The LEDs on the GDPUC panel are labeled RUN, ALM, and ACT respectively.
  • LEDs on the GDPUP(DPUd) Panel
    The LEDs on the GDPUP panel are RUN, ALM, and ACT.
  • LEDs on the GDPUX(DPUc) Panel
    The LEDs on the GDPUX panel are RUN, ALM, and ACT.
  • LEDs on the GEHUB/GEPUG(PEUa) Panel
    The physical boards of the GEHUB and GEPUG are the same. After being loaded with different software, the physical boards perform different functions. These boards have the same types of LEDs on the panels: RUN, ALM, and ACT.
  • LEDs on the GEIUA/GEIUB/GEIUP/GEIUT(EIUa)Panel
    The physical boards of the GEIUA, GEIUB, GEIUP, and GEIUT are the same. After being loaded with different software, the physical boards perform different functions. These boards have the same types of LEDs on the panels: RUN, ALM, and ACT.
  • LEDs on the GFGUA/GFGUB/GFGUG(FG2a) Panel
    The physical boards of the GFGUA, GFGUB, and GFGUG are the same. After being loaded with different software, the physical boards perform different functions. These boards have the same types of LEDs on the panels: RUN, ALM, ACT, LINK (for Ethernet port), and ACT (for Ethernet port).
  • LEDs on the GGCU Panel
    There are three LEDs on the GGCU panel: RUN, ALM, and ACT.
  • LEDs on the GOGUA/GOGUB(GOUa) Panel
    The physical boards of the GOGUA and GOGUB are the same. After being loaded with different software, the physical boards perform different functions. These boards have the same types of LEDs on the panels: RUN, ALM, and ACT.
  • LEDs on the GOIUA/GOIUB/GOIUP/GOIUT(OIUa) Panel
    The physical boards of the GOIUA, GOIUB, GOIUP and GOIUT are the same. After being loaded with different software, the physical boards perform different functions. There are four LEDs on the panel of each of these boards: RUN, ALM, ACT, and LOS.
  • LEDs on the GOMU Panel
    There are five LEDs on the GOMU panel: RUN, ALM, ACT, OFFLINE, and HD.
  • LEDs on the GSCU Panel
    There are four types of LEDs on the GSCU panel: the RUN, ALM, and ACT LEDs, and the LEDs of the Ethernet ports.
  • LEDs on the GTNU Panel
    There are three LEDs on the GTNU panel: RUN, ALM, and ACT.
  • LEDs on the GXPUM/GXPUT/GXPUI(XPUa) Panel
    There are four types of LEDs on the GXPUM/GXPUT/GXPUI panel: the RUN, ALM, and ACT LEDs, and the LEDs of the Ethernet ports.
  • LEDs on the MDMC Panel
    There are two LEDs on the MDMC panel: RUN and ALM.
  • LEDs on the PAMU Panel
    There are two LEDs on the PAMU panel: RUN and ALM.
