LEDs on the GEIUA/GEIUB/GEIUP/GEIUT(EIUa)Panel

The physical boards of the GEIUA, GEIUB, GEIUP, and GEIUT are the same. After being loaded with different software, the physical boards perform different functions. These boards have the same types of LEDs on the panels: RUN, ALM, and ACT.

Table 1 describes the LEDs on the GEIUA/GEIUB/GEIUP/GEIUT panel.

Table 1 LEDs on the board

LED

Color

Status

Meaning

RUN

Green

On for 1s and off for 1s

The board is working.

On for 0.125s and off for 0.125s

The board is loading data.

On

There is power input but the board is faulty.

Off

There is no power input or the board is faulty.

ALM

Red

Off

There is no alarm.

On or blinking

There is an alarm.

ACT

Green

On

The board works in active mode.

Off

The board works in standby mode.
