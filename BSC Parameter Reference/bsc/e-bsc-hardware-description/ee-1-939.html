GBCR (Configuration Type B)

This describes the components, cable connections, and engineering specifications of the GBCR that is configured with the GOMU.

  • Components of the GBCR (Configuration Type B)
    This describes the outer components and inner components of the GBCR.
  • BSC High-Power Distribution Box
    Each BSC cabinet must be configured with a power distribution box, which is installed at the top of the BSC cabinet.
  • Cable Connections of the GBCR (Configuration Type B)
    The cables of the GBCR consist of the power cables, PGND cables, and signal cables.
  • Technical Specifications of the GBCR (Configuration Type B)
    The technical specifications of the GBCR consist of structural specifications and electrical specifications.
