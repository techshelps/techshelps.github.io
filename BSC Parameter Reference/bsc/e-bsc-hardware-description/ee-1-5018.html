BSC Fan Box

The fan box is used to dissipate the heat generated by the BSC. Each subrack must be configured with one fan box.

CAUTION:

If you reassemble the fan box when the system is running, ensure that the operation lasts for less than three minutes. If your operation exceeds three minutes, excessive heat may lead to faults in the boards.
