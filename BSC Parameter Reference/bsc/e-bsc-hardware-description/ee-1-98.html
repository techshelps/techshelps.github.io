Technical Specifications of the BSC Subrack

The technical specifications of the BSC Subrack consist of the dimensions, height of the available space, weight of the subrack, and power consumption of the fully configured subrack.

Technical Specifications of the GMPS

Table 1 lists the technical specifications of the GMPS.

Table 1 Technical specifications of the GMPS

Specification

Value

Dimensions

530.6 mm (height) x 436 mm (width) x 480 mm (depth)

Height of the available space in the subrack

12 U (1 U = 44.45 mm = 1.75 inch)

Weight

Empty subrack: 25 kg; subrack configured with boards: ≤ 57 kg

Power consumption of the fully configured subrack

780 W
