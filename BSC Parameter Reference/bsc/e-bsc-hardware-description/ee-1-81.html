Distribution of Power Switches in the GBCR (Configuration Type A)

Each of the six power outputs of the power distribution box at the top of the GBCR supplies power to a component in the GBCR in a fixed way.

Figure 1 shows the mapping between the six power control switches on the power distribution box and the components in the GBCR. Table 1 lists the mapping between the power control switches and the components.
