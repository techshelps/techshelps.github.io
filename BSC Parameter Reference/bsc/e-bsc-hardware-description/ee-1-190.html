BSC Power Cable

The BSC power cables are classified into external power cables and internal power cables. The power cables are mandatory, which consist of -48 V power cables and RTN power cables.

The BSC external power cables need to be installed on site. They are used to lead the power from the power distribution frame (PDF) to the power distribution box in the BSC. The BSC internal power cables are installed before delivery. They are used to lead the power from the power distribution box to each board and module in the BSC.
