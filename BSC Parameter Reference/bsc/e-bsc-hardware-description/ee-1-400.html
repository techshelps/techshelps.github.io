Functions of the GBAM

The GBAM serves as a bridge between the LMT and the BSC.

The GBAM performs the following functions:

  • Controls the communication between the LMT computer and boards, facilitates the data configuration of the LMT, and filters the performance and alarm data
  • Processes the commands issued by the LMT computer/M2000, and then forwards the commands to the boards in the BSC for processing
  • Filters the results from the BSC boards, and then returns the results to the LMT computer
