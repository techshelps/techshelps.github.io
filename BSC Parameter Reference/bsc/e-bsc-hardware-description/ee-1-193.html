DIP Switches on BSC Parts

This describes the DIP switches on the subracks and the boards.

  • DIP Switches on the BSC Subrack
    This describes the DIP switch on the BSC subrack. The DIP switch on the BSC subrack is used to define the numbering of the BSC subrack.
  • DIP Switches on the GEHUB/GEPUG(PEUa)
    The physical boards of the GEHUB and GEPUG are the same. After being loaded with different software, the physical boards perform different functions. They have the same types of DIP switches: S2, S4, S6, S8, and S10.
  • DIP Switches on the GEIUA/GEIUB/GEIUP/GEIUT(EIUa)
    The physical boards of the GEIUA, GEIUB, GEIUP, and GEIUT are the same. After being loaded with different software, the physical boards perform different functions. They have the same types of DIP switches: S1, S3, S4, S5, and S6.
  • DIP Switch on the MDMC
    There is a DIP switch labeled S2 on the MDMC.
  • DIP Switch on the PAMU
    There is a DIP switch labeled SW1 on the PAMU.
  • DIP Switch on the PFCU
    There is a DIP switch labeled SW1 on the PFCU.
