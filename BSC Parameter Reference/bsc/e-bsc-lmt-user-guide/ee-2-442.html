Maintaining the BSC Devices

This describes how to query the status of the devices, how to reset the devices, and how to perform switchover.

  • Maintaining the Server
    This describes how to maintain the core processes and the board and data files in the server.
  • Maintaining a Subrack
    You can maintain a subrack by querying its properties and resetting it.
  • Maintaining a BSC Board
    This describes how to maintain the status, information, properties, and standby board of a BSC board.
  • Managing the BSC Board Clocks
    This describes how to maintain the BSC board clocks, BSC clock references, the frequency offset of the 2 MHz clock on the GGCU, and the line clock.
  • Maintaining the Fan Box
    This describes how to query the attributes of the fan box and reset the fan box.
  • Maintaining the Power Distribution Box
    This describes how to reset the power distribution box. When the power distribution box incurs an error, you can reset it to restore its power monitoring function and overvoltage protection function without powering off the cabinet.
  • Maintaining the EAC
    This describes how to maintain the EAC.
  • Managing the DSP
    This describes how to manage the DSP by querying, modifying, and configuring the DSP information.
