Resetting a BTS Board

This describes how to reset a faulty BTS board.

Prerequisites

  • The LMT runs normally.
  • The communication between the LMT and the BSC is normal.
  • The communication between the BSC and the BTS is normal.

Context

CAUTION:

Be careful to reset a BTS board if the standby board of the BTS board is not in position or faulty. Otherwise, the services carried by the BTS board may be disrupted.

Resetting a board may affect the existing services, so reset the board when the traffic volume is low, for example, in the early morning.

Procedure

  • Through GUI
    1. Choose BTS Maintenance > Maintain Board > Reset Board. A dialog box is displayed, as shown in Figure 1.

      Figure 1 Reset Board dialog box

    2. Set Site, Subrack, and Slot, and then click Reset.
  • Through MML
    1. Run the RST BTSBRD command on the LMT to reset the BTS boards.
