Introduction to LMT

This provides the definition and functions of LMT. It also introduces the operation and maintenance system of the BSC and the configuration requirements of the LMT PC.

  • LMT-Related Definitions
    This describes the differences among the LMT, the LMT PC, and the LMT software.
  • Functions of the LMT
    This describes the functions of the LMT. On the LMT, you can maintain and configure data for the BSC and also for the BTSs that are connected to the BSC.
  • BSC6000 OM Networking
    This describes the networking of the BSC6000 OM system. The BSC6000 OM system consists of the server, LMT, and centralized OM system iManager M2000.
  • Configuration Requirements of the LMT PC
    This describes the configuration requirements of the LMT PC in terms of hardware, software, and communications capability.
