Resetting a TRX

This describes how to reset the faulty TRX of a specified BTS.

Prerequisites

  • The LMT runs normally.
  • The communication between the LMT and the BSC is normal.
  • The communication between the BSC and the BTS is normal.

Context

WARNING:
  • When the TRX to be reset is the DTRU, resetting one TRX may affect the normal operation of the other TRX in the same TRX mode.
  • Resetting a TRX may affect the services of the BTS. Therefore, perform this task with caution.

Procedure

  • Through GUI
    1. Choose BTS Maintenance > Maintain TRX > Reset TRX. A dialog box is displayed, as shown in Figure 1.

      Figure 1 Reset TRX dialog box

    2. Set Site and TRX, and then click Reset.
  • Through MML
    1. Run the RST TRX command on the Local Maintenance Terminal to reset BTS TRXs.
