Maintaining the BSC Interfaces

This describes how to maintain the BSC interfaces, namely, the Ater, A, Abis, Pb, and Gb interfaces.

  • Maintaining the Ater Interface
    This describes how to maintain the Ater interface.
  • Maintaining the A Interface
    This describes how to maintain the circuits and E1/T1 links of the A interface.
  • Maintaining the Abis Interface
    This describes how to maintain the configuration information of the Abis interface.
  • Maintaining the Pb Interface
    This describes how to maintain the E1/T1 links, signaling links, and circuits of the Pb interface.
  • Maintaining the Gb Interface
    This describes how to maintain the links of the Gb interface.
  • Setting/Querying IP Communication Modes on Interfaces
    This describes how to set/query IP communication modes on the A, Abis, and Gb interfaces through MML commands.
