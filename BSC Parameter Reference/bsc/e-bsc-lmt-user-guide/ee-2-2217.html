Managing the Inventory Information of the BSC

Inventory management refers to the management based on the equipment archive information. Through inventory management, you can know the running equipment and inventory equipment. You can perform inventory management on either the Local Maintenance Terminal or the M2000.

  • Uploading the Inventory Management File
    This describes how to upload the inventory management file.
  • Exporting the Inventory Management File
    This describes how to export the inventory management file to IP address of the server\BSC6000\data\mtndata\inventory\ivt. Exporting the inventory management file is also referred to as generating the inventory management file.
