Clearing BSC EAC Alarms

This describes how to clear the smoke alarms, infrared alarms, and door status alarms of the BSC EAC.

Prerequisites

  • The LMT runs normally.
  • The communication between the LMT and the BSC is normal.
  • The environment monitoring instrument runs normally.

Procedure

  1. On the LMT, choose BSC Maintenance > Maintain Device > Clear BSC EAC Alarms. A dialog box is displayed, as shown in Figure 1.

    Figure 1 Clear BSC EAC Alarms dialog box

  2. Select the Subrack of the BSC EAC, select the type of alarms to be cleared, and then click Clear.

    NOTE:
    Clearing BSC EAC alarms can remove other than clear the alarms displayed on the BSC6000 Local Maintenance Terminal. You must handle the alarms manually on site.

  3. Click Cancel.
