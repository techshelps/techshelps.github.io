Locking the Local Maintenance Terminal

This describes how to manually lock the Local Maintenance Terminal to avoid insecure operations on the Local Maintenance Terminal after operators leave.

Prerequisite

The Local Maintenance Terminal is started.

Context

You can lock the Local Maintenance Terminal through one of the following three methods.

Procedure

  • Choose System > Lock.
  • Press Ctrl+Alt+M.
  • Click the icon on the Local Maintenance Terminal.

Result

The Terminal Locked dialog box is displayed after the Local Maintenance Terminal is manually locked.
