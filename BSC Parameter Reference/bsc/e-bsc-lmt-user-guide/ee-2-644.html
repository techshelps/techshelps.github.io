Existing Convert Management System

This describes how to exit the convert management system on the LMT connected to the alarm box when the alarm box need not provide audible and visual indications.

Prerequisite

The convert management system runs normally.

Procedure

  1. You can exit the convert management system by the following three means:

    • Choose Operate > Exit.
    • Click .
    • Right-click in the task bar. When the communication at the serial port is exceptional, the icon is displayed as . Choose Exit.

  2. Click OK to exit the system.
