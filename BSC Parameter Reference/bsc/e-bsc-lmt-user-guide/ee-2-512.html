Maintaining the BTS

This describes how to remotely maintain the BTS through the LMT. The operations involve maintaining the BTS version, devices, and boards.

  • Concepts Related to BTS Maintenance
    This describes the basic concepts of BTS maintenance, including the logical objects, BTS attributes, and cell forcible handover mode. You can learn the parameters associated with the BTS attributes and forced cell handover.
  • Maintaining the BTS Versions
    This describes how to manage version software and ensure the compatibility of hardware version and software version by downloading, configuring, loading, and activating the version software. The version software is associated with the BTS and the BTS boards.
  • Maintaining the BTS Devices
    Maintaining the BTS devices involves maintaining the BTS battery, power module, auxiliary equipment, and RET antenna. You can perform the operations associated with the BTS equipment to ensure that the BTS operates normally. The operations involve querying, testing, viewing, and resetting.
  • Maintaining BTS Boards
    Maintaining BTS boards involves querying the information about the BTS boards, maintaining the clock, testing the board performance, setting the board parameters, and resetting the boards.
