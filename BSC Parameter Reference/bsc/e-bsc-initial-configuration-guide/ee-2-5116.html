Customized Alarm Ports of the DBS3900/BTS3900/BTS3900A Base Station

This describes the mapping between internal alarm IDs of the BTS and environment alarm ports and cables of each monitoring device. The monitoring devices are the EMUA, UPEU, UEIU, APMI, and FMUA.

Environment Alarm Ports of the EMUA

On the LMT, the EMUA is displayed as "DEMU". The EMUA is connected to the MON1 port of the UPEU or the UEIU through the RS485 signal cable.

Table 1 describes the relation between the alarm IDs and the environment alarm ports/cables of the EMUA.
NOTE:

Alarm ID in all the tables of this section is the BTS internal alarm ID. The BSC alarm ID should be planned separately.
