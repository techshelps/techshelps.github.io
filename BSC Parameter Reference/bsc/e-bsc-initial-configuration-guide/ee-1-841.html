Mixed Configuration of TRXs

The current Huawei BTSs support the mixed configuration of the single-transceivers and double-transceivers as well as the mixed configuration of the double-transceivers of different types. The principles of mixed configuration are as follows:

  • Only the mixed configuration of the single-transceiver of the BTS312 and the double-transceiver of the BTS3012 is supported.
  • The BTS3012 and BTS3012AE support the mixed configuration of DTRU A and DTRU B.
  • The BTS3012, BTS3012AE, and BTS3012 II support the mixed configuration of the DTRU and QTRU.
