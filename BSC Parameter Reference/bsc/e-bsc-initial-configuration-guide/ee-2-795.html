Configuring the BSC Boards

This describes how to add BSC boards or configure the attributes of the BSC boards. When the BM and TC are configured in different subracks, the GDPUC/GDPUX, GEIUA/GOIUA, GEIUT/GOIUT, GEIUB/GOIUB, GEHUB, GFGUB/GOGUB, GXPUM, GXPUT, GGCU, GTNU, and GSCU must be configured.

  • Configuring the GDPUC
    This describes how to add a GDPUC and configure its attributes in BM/TC separated mode.
  • Configuring the GDPUX
    This describes how to add a GDPUX in the GMPS/GEPS/GTCS and configure its attributes.
  • Configuring the GEIUA/GOIUA
    This describes how to add a GEIUA/GOIUA in the GTCS and configure its attributes in BM/TC separated mode. The GEIUA and GOIUA are responsible for the E1/T1 transmission and STM-1 transmission on the A interface respectively.
  • Configuring the GEIUT/GOIUT
    This describes how to add a GEIUT/GOIUT in the GMPS/GEPS/GTCS and configure its attributes in BM/TC separated mode. The GEIUT and GOIUT are responsible for the E1/T1 transmission and STM-1 transmission between the GMPS/GEPS and the GTCS.
  • Configuring the GEIUB/GOIUB
    This describes how to add a GEIUB or GOIUB in the GMPS or GEPS and configure its attributes. The GEIUB and GOIUB are responsible for the E1/T1 transmission and STM-1 transmission on the Abis interface respectively.
  • Configuring the GEHUB
    This describes how to add a GEHUB in the GMPS/GEPS and configure its attributes. The GEHUB is responsible for the transmission optimization on the Abis interface.
  • Configuring the GFGUB/GOGUB
    This describes how to add a GFGUB/GOGUB in the GMPS/GEPS and configure its attributes. The GFGUB/GOGUB is responsible for the IP transmission on the Abis interface.
  • Configuring the GGCU
    This describes how to configure the attributes of the GGCUs through the LMT. The GGCUs are displayed in slots 12 and 13 of the GMPS by default on the LMT.
  • Configuring the GSCU
    This describes how to configure the attributes of the GSCUs through the LMT. The GSCUs are displayed in slots 6 and 7 of each subrack by default on the LMT.
  • Configuring the GTNU
    This describes how to configure the attributes of the GTNUs through the LMT. The GTNUs are displayed in slots 4 and 5 of each subrack by default on the LMT.
  • Configuring the GXPUM
    This describes how to add GXPUMs in slots 0 and 1 of the GMPS/GEPS and configure their attributes.
  • Configuring the GXPUT
    This describes how to add GXPUTs in slots 2 and 3 of the GMPS/GEPS and configure their attributes.
  • Configuring the GXPUI
    This describes how to add GXPUIs in idle slots of the GMPS/GEPS and configure their attributes.
