NVIDIA Surround Configuration

NVIDIA® Surround (and NVIDIA® 3D Vision Surround for systems with 3D Vision) combines multiple displays to act as one larger display to allow a panoramic view of full-screen games or your desktop. The controls also let you adjust the displays to compensate for monitor bezel gaps for more realistic viewing of full-screen games.

NVIDIA Surround is supported only on Windows 7 and later Windows operating systems, and requires at least two SLI-ready GeForce 200 series or later graphics cards installed OR one Kepler-based NVIDIA graphics card.
