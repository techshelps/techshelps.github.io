Manage Updates

The Manage Updates link on the Manage 3D Settings page appears either with systems using NVIDIA's power-saving GPU technology or if NVIDIA Updates is installed. Clicking the link opens the NVIDIA Updates dialog where you can manage NVIDIA Updates, including whether the driver downloads small files to ensure that the latest programs take advantage of your NVIDIA GPU.

Note: For the purpose of prioritizing future driver features and optimizations, the driver may collect the following types of non-personal information: CPU, GPU, operating system, and other system information. Such information will only be collected on an anonymous, aggregated basis and can at no time be linked to your personal identity, IP address, hostname, username or any other personally identifiable information.
