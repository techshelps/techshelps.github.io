Getting Started with XML Digital Signatures

Before you use IXMLDigitalSignature or IXMLDigitalSignatureEx interface to sign an XML document, you must have a digital certificate installed on your machine. To obtain a digital certificate, you must either create or have access to a certificate authority (CA). You also need to install CAPICOM 2.0 on your machine.

The following topics describe how perform the necessary steps to get started with XML Digital Signatures.

  • Setting Up a Certificate Authority
  • Requesting a Digital Certificate
  • Issuing a Certificate for a Pending Request
  • Installing a Digital Certificate
  • Installing Platform SDK and Configuring Visual C++
  • Downloading and Installing CAPICOM 2.0
