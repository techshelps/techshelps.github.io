Microsoft XML Core Services (MSXML) 5.0 for Microsoft Office - Digital Signatures

Starter Kit

[This feature was first implemented for MSXML 5.0.]

This starter kit is meant to provide you with a quick introduction to the XML Digital Signature (XMLDSig) feature in MSXML 5.0 and later. It briefly discusses some of the important underlying technologies, such as Public Key Infrastructure (PKI), encryption, and digital signatures.
