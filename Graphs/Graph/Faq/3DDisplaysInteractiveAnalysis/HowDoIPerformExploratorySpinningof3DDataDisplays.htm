How do I perform exploratory spinning of 3D data displays?

Click the  toolbar button to display the Point of View Settings and Exploratory Spin dialog, which is used for rotation, spinning (for analytic or exploratory purposes), and interactive adjustment of the point of view for three-dimensional displays.

Click the Analytic exploratory spin options button to set the display into continuous rotation in clockwise or counter-clockwise directions.

Note that specific adjustments of the viewpoint and perspective (e.g., for an exact reproduction of a display) can also be made in the Point of View options pane of the Graph Options dialog.
