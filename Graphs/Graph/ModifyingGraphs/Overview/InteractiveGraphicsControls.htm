Interactive Graphics Controls

Located at the bottom of graphs, you'll find the interactive graphics controls, which enable you to adjust the transparency of the plot areas and markers, and to scroll and pan in order to interactively scale the graph. More controls are located in 3D graphs to enable interactive rotation. Click the wrench icon adjacent to the sliders to display the Graph Options dialog.
