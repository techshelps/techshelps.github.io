Graphs - Categorized Graphs - Missing and 'Out of Range' Data Plots

Select Missing/Range Data Plots from the Graphs - Categorized Graphs menu to display the Categorized Missing and 'Out of Range' Data Plots dialog.

When you create categorized missing data/range plots, a series of standard 2D missing data/range plots, one for each category of cases identified by the X or X and Y category variables (or identified by the multiple subset criteria) is produced.

See also, Conceptual Overviews - What Are Categorized Graphs?.
