Graphs - 2D Graphs - Means w/Error Plots

Select Means w/Error Plots from the Graphs menu to display the Means with Error Plots dialog. This plot visualizes means of the dependent variable(s) for subsets of cases specified by the user. Each mean marker is accompanied by a set of whiskers representing error bars that is the confidence intervals around the mean. These plots are typically used to compare marginal means across groups and visualize the reliability of these means. .
