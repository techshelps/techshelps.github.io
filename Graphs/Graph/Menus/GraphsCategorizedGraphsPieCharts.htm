Graphs - Categorized Graphs - Pie Charts

Select Pie Charts from the Graphs - Categorized Graphs menu to display the Categorized Pie Charts dialog.

When you create categorized pie charts, a series of standard pie charts, one for each category of cases identified by the X or X and Y category variables (or identified by the multiple subset criteria) is produced.

See also, Conceptual Overviews - What Are Categorized Graphs?.
