Unable to upgrade your installation of Microsoft Office 2003

If you cannot upgrade your installation of Microsoft Office 2003 to Microsoft Office 2010, the installation of Office 2003 might be corrupted.

Repair your installation of Office 2003

Windows Vista

  1. In Control Panel, click Programs, and then click Programs and Features.

     Note   In Classic view, double-click Programs and Features.

  2. Click Microsoft Office 2003, and then click Change.
  3. Click Repair.
  4. After the repair process has completed, try to install Office 2010 again.

Windows XP

  1. In Control Panel, click Add or Remove Programs, and then click Change or Remove Programs.

    2.  Note   In Classic view, double-click Add or Remove Programs.

  2. Click Microsoft Office 2003, and then click Change.
  3. Click Repair.
  4. After the repair process has completed, try to install Office 2010 again.

If you still have problems upgrading, you might need to remove Office 2003 from your computer. This might require that you have access to the original installation discs for Office 2003.
