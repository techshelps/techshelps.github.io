LabelLineStyle Constants

The VtChLabelLineStyle constants provide options for displaying lines connecting a label and series.

The following table lists the valid constants for VtChLabelLineStyle:

Constants Description
VtChLabelLineStyleNone No line connects the label and series.
VtChLabelLineStyleStraight A straight line connects the label and series.
VtChLabelLineStyleBent A bent line connects the label and series.
