SDI Output

Serial Digital Interface (SDI) is a digital, uncompressed high quality video format used for film and video post production and broadcast applications.

The NVIDIA Quadro® FX 4000 SDI converts composited video and graphics to uncompressed 8-bit and 10-bit SDI output.

Later NVIDIA Quadro® SDI solutions convert composited video and graphics to uncompressed 8-bit, 10-bit, or 12-bit SDI output.
