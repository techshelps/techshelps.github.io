Introducing a Delay in the SDI Output

In the SDI Signal Settings dialogue box, you can introduce a slight delay in the house-sync synchronised SDI output. For example, if delivery of video from other equipment is delayed because of greater cable length, you can introduce a delay in the SDI output from this card so that both deliveries are in sync.
