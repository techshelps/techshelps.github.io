If you need support outside the United States and Canada

Microsoft Technical Support Worldwide

If you are outside the United States and have a question about a Microsoft product, first:

  • Check the information in this Help file or the product manual.

  • Check the readme files that come with your product disks or discs. These files provide general information that became available shortly before the product was released.

  • Go to Support Online from Microsoft Technical Support at http://support.microsoft.com/support.

If you cannot find a solution, you can receive information on how to obtain technical support by contacting the Microsoft subsidiary office in your area.

Important   Phone numbers for Microsoft Subsidiaries are subject to change. For the most recent listing, see the options and phone number section on the Support Online Web site at http://support.microsoft.com/support. If there is no Microsoft subsidiary office in your country/region, contact the establishment from which you obtained your Microsoft product.
