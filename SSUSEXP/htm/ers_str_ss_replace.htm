<Local file> is writable, replace?

This warning has the following cause and solution:

  • There is a writable file in your working folder with the same name as the one you are attempting to get or check out.

    When you get or check out a file, a new copy is written to your working folder. If you already have a writable copy, you may have made changes that will be overwritten. In this situation, VSS gives you some options:

    • Click Yes to overwrite the working folder file.

    • Check Yes All to have your choices in this dialog box apply to all other files you currently have writable copies of.

    • Click No to skip the operation for this file.

    • Click No All to skip the operation for all files you have writable copies of.

    • Click Cancel to cancel the operation.
