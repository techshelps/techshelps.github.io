Add Files, Folders, and Projects to VSS

See Also

Before you can add files to the VSS database, you must have a project to place the files in. When you begin working with VSS, the first thing to do is create one or more projects.

After you have created a project, you can add files or folders from your computer to the VSS database.

For information on how to add files, folders, and projects to VSS, refer to:
