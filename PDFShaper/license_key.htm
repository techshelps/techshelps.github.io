License Key

As soon as your order for PDF Shaper is complete, you will receive a license key via e-mail, which should be entered into the program to unlock it for use.

 

Entering your license key:

1. Select the license key and press Ctrl+Insert to copy it to the Clipboard (or right mouse click and Copy).

2. Run the program and press Shift+Insert to paste your license key (or right mouse click and Paste).

3. Press the Register button and restart the program.
