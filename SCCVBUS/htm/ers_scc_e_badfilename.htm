The filename '<File>' contains illegal characters. The file will be ignored.

This error has the following cause and solution:

  • The filename you've specified contains one or more invalid characters.

    When adding files to VSS, they must meet the following requirements.

    • 255 characters or less in length.

    • The complete path cannot exceed 259 characters, including separators.

    • May not begin or end with a space.

    • May not begin with a $.

    • May contain 1 or more file extensions (e.g., MyFile.Ext1.Ext2.Ext3.Txt).

    • Legal characters from 32-255 characters in length, but not: ?”/\<>*|:
