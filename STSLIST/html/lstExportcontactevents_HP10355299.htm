Export contact or event information

Export contact information

In the Standard view and the Datasheet view of a contact list, you can export an entire contact list or an individual contact from a contact list to Microsoft Outlook.

Export a contact list

  1. In the ribbon, click the List tab, and then in the Connect & Export group, click Connect to Outlook.
  2. Outlook prompts you to confirm connecting the contact list to Outlook. Click Yes.

Once you have exported a contact list to Outlook, any changes that are made to the contact list on Microsoft SharePoint Foundation 2010 will be automatically forwarded and updated in the contact list in Outlook. These changes are displayed the next time you reload the contact list in Outlook.

Export an individual contact

  1. Do one of the following:
    • In Standard view, click the drop-down arrow in the Last Name cell for the contact you want to export, and then click Export Contact.
    • In Datasheet view, right-click the contact you would like to export. Click Export Contact.
  2. You will be prompted to confirm downloading a virtual business card file (with file name extension vcf). Click Open.

This exports the contact to the default Outlook contacts list Contacts. Subsequently, you can decide whether to save the contact in Contacts, or in another contact list.

Export event information

You can export an entire event list or an individual event from an event list to Outlook. To view a calendar as an event list, in the Ribbon, under List Tools, click List, and then click All Events in the Current View drop-down list.

Export an event list

  1. In the ribbon, click the List tab, and then in the Connect & Export group, click Connect to Outlook.
  2. Outlook prompts you to confirm connecting the calendar in Microsoft SharePoint Foundation 2010 to Outlook. Click Yes.

After you have exported an event list to Outlook, any changes that are made to the event list on Microsoft SharePoint Foundation 2010 will be automatically forwarded and updated in the Outlook calendar. These changes are displayed the next time you reload the calendar in Outlook.

Export an individual event

  1. Do one of the following:
    • In Datasheet view, right-click anywhere on the row for the event you would like to export, and then click Export Event.
    • In Standard view, click the event you would like to export, and then, in the Custom Commands tab, click Export Event.
  2. You will be prompted to confirm downloading an Internet calendar file (with file name extension ics). Click Open.

This exports the event to the default Outlook calendar Calendar. Subsequently, you can decide whether to save the event in Calendar, or in another calendar.
