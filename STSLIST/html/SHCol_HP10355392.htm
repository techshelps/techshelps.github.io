Show or hide a column

Show a hidden column

A thicker vertical line to the right of a column header indicates that the column next to it is hidden.

  • To unhide a column, select the visible columns on either side of the hidden column, right-click the selection, and click Unhide.
  • To unhide all hidden columns in the list, right-click the top-left corner of the list, and click Unhide.

Hide a column

  • To hide a column, right-click the header of the column that you want to hide, and click Hide.
