TRIMENDS

Removes leading and trailing spaces, such as whitespace and non-breaking space before and after the text. Use TRIMENDS on text strings that you have received from another application, and that may have extra spacing before and after the strings.

 Note   TRIMENDS preserves spacing within the text string. To alter spacing within the text string, use the TRIM function. For more information, see the Help topic about the TRIM function.

Syntax

TRIMENDS(text)

Text   The string from which you want spaces removed.

Example

FormulaDescription (Result)
=TRIMENDS (“ Record sales ”)Removes leading and trailing spaces from the text in the formula (Record sales)
