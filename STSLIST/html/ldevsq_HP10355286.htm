DEVSQ

Returns the sum of squares of deviations of data points from their sample mean.

Syntax

DEVSQ(number1,number2, ...)

Number1,number2, ...   are 1 to 30 arguments for which you want to calculate the sum of squared deviations.

Remarks

  • The arguments must be numbers or column references that contain numbers.
