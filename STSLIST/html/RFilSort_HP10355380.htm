Show All

Remove a filter or sort order

To see if a column is filtered, click the drop-down arrow in the column header. If a column has a filter, an entry other than Show All will be selected in the drop-down list.

To remove a filter from a column

  1. Click the drop-down arrow in the column header.
  2. Click Show All in the list.

To remove all filters and sorting orders

  • On the task pane toolbar, click Remove Filter/Sort.
