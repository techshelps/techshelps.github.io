Show or hide aggregate values

  • To show or hide aggregate values for all columns in the list, in the ribbon, click the List tab, and then in the Datasheet group, click Show Totals.

    The Total row appears at the bottom of the list. It displays the sum of values for numeric columns, and if there are no numeric columns in the list, a count of non-empty rows in the last column in the list. If the row was visible when you clicked Show Totals , it will be hidden.

  • To show or hide aggregate values for one or more columns in the list, do the following:
    1. Click a cell in the Total row.
    2. Click the down arrow that appears in the cell.
    3. In the drop-down list of aggregate functions, select None to hide the total value. Select a function other than None to display a total value.
